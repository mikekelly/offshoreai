Setting up the structure

A Jersey trust governed by Jersey proper law gives you the Article 9 firewall and Article 9A reserved powers. The classic mix is:

  1. A Jersey discretionary trust as the central wrapper;
  2. Underlying holdco companies per asset class;
  3. A US blocker for US-situs equities.

Key cautions

  • Forced-heirship recognition is decided by the trust's proper law.
  • Substance obligations attach to the underlying companies.
Article 9(4) directs the Court to disregard foreign heirship rights for a Jersey-proper-law trust.

See the firewall concept file at trusts/firewall.md.
