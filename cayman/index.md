# Cayman Islands — Adjacent Jurisdiction

## Why this section exists

The **Cayman Islands** is the **largest offshore financial
centre** globally by some measures, particularly for **fund
domiciliation**. Jersey practitioners encounter Cayman matters
in:

- **Hedge fund** and **private equity** structures (Cayman
  dominant);
- **Bonds** and **structured finance** issuances;
- **Cross-jurisdictional** trust / fund / insurance matters;
- **Large** corporate transactions;
- **Specific** sectors where Cayman is preferred.

This section provides **high-level** Cayman coverage. For full
Cayman practice, engage **Cayman attorneys**.
