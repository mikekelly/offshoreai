# Cayman Financial Regulation — Overview

## Regulatory architecture

Cayman regulation rests on:

- **CIMA** as integrated regulator;
- **Multiple sectoral statutes**;
- **Specific Rules and Guidance Notes**;
- **AML / CFT** framework;
- **Specific** AML / CFT regime.

## Principal statutes

### Banking

- **Banks and Trust Companies Act** — banks and trust
  companies;
- **Basel-aligned** prudential standards.

### Insurance

- **Insurance Act 2010** — comprehensive;
- **Specific** captive regulations.

### Funds

- **Mutual Funds Act** — open-ended funds;
- **Private Funds Act 2020** — closed-ended funds;
- **Specific** fund subcategories.

### Investment business

- **Securities Investment Business Act (SIBA)**;
- **Specific** investment-business categories.

### Trust services

- **Banks and Trust Companies Act** — trust licences;
- **Specific** trust regulation.

### AML / CFT

- **Proceeds of Crime Act**;
- **AML Regulations**;
- **CIMA Guidance Notes**;
- **Equivalent in approach** to Jersey POCL / MLO.

### VASPs

- **Virtual Asset (Service Providers) Act 2020**;
- **Specific** crypto / digital asset regulation;
- **AML / CFT** integration.

### Substance

- **International Tax Co-operation (Economic Substance) Act
  2018**;
- **Substance requirements**;
- **Annual reporting**.

## Comparison with other offshore jurisdictions

| Aspect | Jersey | Guernsey | IoM | Cayman |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Umbrella | FSL 1998 + sectoral | Multiple sectoral | FSA Act 2008 + sectoral | Multiple sectoral |
| Funds | CIFL 1988 | PoIL 2020 | CIS Act 2008 | **Mutual Funds + Private Funds Acts** |
| Insurance | IBL 1996 | Insurance Business Law 2002 | Insurance Act 2008 | Insurance Act 2010 |
| Regulator | JFSC | GFSC | FSA | **CIMA** |
| Hedge fund market | Some | Some | Some | **Dominant** |

## Authorisation process

Common features:

- **Application** to CIMA;
- **Fit-and-proper** assessment;
- **Capital** requirements;
- **Systems and controls**;
- **Local presence**;
- **Ongoing** requirements;
- **Specific** sectoral rules.

## Enforcement

CIMA tools:

- **Civil penalties** (Administrative Fines Regulations);
- **Public notices**;
- **Conditions** on licence;
- **Suspension** of licence;
- **Revocation** of licence;
- **Criminal** prosecution for serious offences;
- **Specific** Cayman court process.

## Cooperation

CIMA cooperates with:

- **SEC, FCA, PRA, ECB** — major regulators;
- **CFATF** — Caribbean Financial Action Task Force;
- **IOSCO, IAIS** — international standards;
- **CIMA MoUs** with sister regulators;
- **Specific** cooperation in cross-border investigations.

## Industry

Key Cayman industry:

- **Cayman Finance** — sector promotion;
- **Cayman Islands Bankers Association**;
- **Cayman Islands Insurance Managers Association**;
- **Cayman Islands Fund Administrators Association
  (CIFAA)**;
- **Society of Trust and Estate Practitioners (STEP) Cayman
  Branch**;
- **Cayman Islands Law Society**.
