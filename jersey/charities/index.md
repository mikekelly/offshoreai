# Charities — Jersey

## What this section is about

This section covers **Jersey charity law** — what counts as a
charity, how charities are registered and regulated, who
supervises them, and how charitable purposes are defined.

The principal statute is the **Charities (Jersey) Law 2014
(CL)**, supplemented by the **Charities (Jersey) Order** and
the **Charity Commissioner's guidance**.

## Charitable vehicles in Jersey

A Jersey charity can be structured as:

- **Charitable trust** — under the Trusts (Jersey) Law 1984;
- **Charitable foundation** — under the Foundations (Jersey)
  Law 2009;
- **Charitable company** — under the Companies (Jersey) Law
  1991 (typically limited by guarantee);
- **Charitable unincorporated association** — informal
  structure for small charities.

The **legal form** is separate from the **charitable status**
— a vehicle of any of these forms can apply for charity
registration.
