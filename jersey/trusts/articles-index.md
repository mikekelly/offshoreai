# Trusts (Jersey) Law 1984 — Articles Index

A one-line description of every Article in the
**[Trusts (Jersey) Law 1984][tjl]**, in statutory order, with a link to
the deep concept file for that Article where one exists. The deep
files live in `jersey/trusts/` and are named by concept rather than
Article number where the Article has a strong conceptual identity
(e.g., Article 9 → [`firewall.md`](firewall.md)); the frontmatter of
each concept file records which Article(s) it covers.

Articles marked **[repealed]** are listed for completeness; the
historical text remains in older consolidations on
[jerseylaw.je][tjl].

dedicated concept file. Articles 47B–47J are consolidated into a
single mistake-regime file at [article-47-set-aside.md](article-47-set-aside.md).
