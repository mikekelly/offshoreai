# Trusts — Jersey

The Jersey trust is one of the offshore-finance industry's flagship
wealth-structuring vehicles. The governing statute is the **[Trusts
(Jersey) Law 1984][tjl]**, which codifies (and in places modifies)
common-law principles of trusts, supplemented by case-law of the Royal
Court and the Court of Appeal.

## Statute layer — Trusts (Jersey) Law 1984
