# DL 2013 Article 20 — Discrimination in Education

Article 20 prohibits **discrimination in education**.

## Scope

Discrimination prohibited in:

### Schools

- **Admissions** policies;
- **Treatment** of pupils within school;
- **Behaviour management**;
- **Exclusions**;
- **Provision** of educational facilities;
- **Access** to specific subjects / programs.

### Further and higher education

- **Admissions**;
- **Treatment** within institution;
- **Specific** programs;
- **Specific** scholarship / award allocations.

### Vocational training

- **Specific** training programs;
- **Apprenticeship** opportunities;
- **Specific** sector-specific training.

## Forms

All forms of discrimination apply (DL Articles 4-10):

- **Direct** and **indirect**;
- **Harassment** (including by pupils);
- **Victimisation**;
- **Reasonable adjustments** for disability.

## Specific exemptions

### Single-sex schools

- **Permitted** in specific configurations;
- **Subject to** specific Article 46 rules;
- **Specific** Jersey schools relying on the exemption.

### Religious schools

- **Specific** religious-character exemptions on religion-of-
  pupil decisions;
- **Subject to** specific rules.

### Specialist schools for disability

- **Permitted** specifically for disabled pupils;
- **Reasonable** distinction from mainstream education.

## Pupil-on-pupil

Schools have specific responsibilities for:

- **Pupil-on-pupil harassment**;
- **Bullying** with discrimination dimension;
- **Specific** safeguarding obligations;
- **Tribunal** claims against school.

## Disability adjustments in education

Specific reasonable-adjustment duty for educational
institutions:

- **Physical accessibility**;
- **Auxiliary aids** (specific equipment);
- **Modified** procedures and exams (extra time, separate
  room);
- **Specific** Jersey practices.

## Tribunal jurisdiction

Education discrimination claims:

- **Tribunal** jurisdiction;
- **Specific** procedures;
- **Tight** time limits (typically 6 months);
- **Specific** Jersey case law developing.
