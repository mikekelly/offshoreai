# Founder / Entrepreneur — Use Cases

## Who this section is for

You are a **founder, entrepreneur, or owner-operator** placing
a Jersey company above an operating business — typically as:

- A **TopCo** above a UK / EU / US operating group;
- An **IP-holding** vehicle for licensing across jurisdictions;
- A **pre-IPO** holding structure for a future listing;
- A **capital-raising** vehicle for equity or debt issuance.

Your day involves some combination of: choosing the right
Jersey vehicle type; understanding the tax position; navigating
investor expectations; engaging with Jersey corporate-services
providers; planning for exit or listing.
