# International Lawyer — Use Cases

## Who this section is for

You are a **lawyer qualified outside Jersey** — typically
**English**, but possibly **US, EU, or other jurisdiction** —
running a matter with a Jersey-law element. You need Jersey-law
information to:

- **Advise** your client on the Jersey aspects of a broader
  matter;
- **Instruct** Jersey counsel effectively;
- **Anticipate** issues that you (as a non-Jersey lawyer) might
  not see;
- **Understand** the boundaries of what you can do without
  Jersey-qualified support.

Common matter types: cross-border family litigation involving
Jersey trusts; M&A involving Jersey TopCos; contentious trust
disputes spanning multiple jurisdictions; financial-restructuring
cases involving Jersey entities.
