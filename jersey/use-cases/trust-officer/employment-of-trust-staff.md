# Employment of Trust Staff — Compliance Across Jersey Laws

## The question

Your TCB firm employs **Jersey staff** — administrators,
secretaries, lawyers, accountants. What employment law
considerations apply, particularly given the regulated nature
of the business?

## The short answer

- **Employment (Jersey) Law 2003** applies to all staff
  (terms, pay, leave, dismissal);
- **Discrimination (Jersey) Law 2013** applies (race, sex,
  sexual orientation, age, disability, etc.);
- **CHWL status** must be verified before engagement;
- **JFSC fit-and-proper** for principal persons (MLRO,
  Compliance Officer, senior managers);
- **AML / CFT** training mandatory under MLO 2008;
- **Data protection** (DPL 2018) for employee personal data;
- **Confidentiality** undertakings for client information.

## The proper answer

### Employment contract essentials

Following **Employment Law 2003 Article 3** — written
particulars within 4 weeks. For TCB staff, **enhanced** terms
typically:

- **Confidentiality** — strict obligations regarding client
  data;
- **Conflicts of interest** — disclosure and management;
- **Restrictive covenants** — post-termination
  non-competition, non-solicitation;
- **JFSC notification** obligations if departing;
- **AML / CFT** acknowledgment of duties;
- **Bonus / incentive** terms appropriately structured.

See [`../../employment/el-article-3.md`](../../employment/el-article-3.md).

### CHWL status verification

Before hiring:

- **Verify** the candidate's residential / employment
  status (Entitled, Licensed, Entitled for work, Registered);
- **Registered** workers require **employer sponsorship**;
- **Specific** Population Office permissions for senior /
  specialist roles;
- **Sectoral quotas** in some configurations.

See [`../../immigration-residency/chwl-article-19.md`](../../immigration-residency/chwl-article-19.md).

### JFSC fit-and-proper

For **principal persons** (typically MLRO, Compliance
Officer, directors, controllers):

- **JFSC notification** before engagement;
- **Personal questionnaire** completion;
- **Fit-and-proper** assessment by JFSC;
- **Ongoing** fit-and-proper requirement.

See [`../../financial-regulation/fsl-article-9.md`](../../financial-regulation/fsl-article-9.md).

### AML / CFT training

Under MLO 2008 Article 19:

- **Mandatory** AML / CFT training;
- **At onboarding** and refresher annually;
- **Tailored** to the role;
- **Documented** completion.

See [`../../aml-cft/mlo-part-6-training.md`](../../aml-cft/mlo-part-6-training.md).

### Discrimination law compliance

The **Discrimination Law 2013** prohibits discrimination on
protected characteristics:

- **Recruitment** — no discriminatory questions or selection
  criteria;
- **Terms** — equal pay, equal opportunities;
- **Promotion** — non-discriminatory criteria;
- **Termination** — fair and non-discriminatory.

See [`../../discrimination/index.md`](../../discrimination/index.md).

### Data protection

Under DPL 2018:

- **Lawful basis** for processing employee personal data
  (typically legal obligation plus contract);
- **Privacy notice** to employees;
- **Specific** rules for special category data (health,
  ethnicity if collected);
- **International transfers** if group-wide HR systems
  outside Jersey;
- **Breach notification** if HR data breached.

See [`../../data-protection/index.md`](../../data-protection/index.md).

### Working time and leave

- **Statutory working time** limits;
- **Statutory holiday** entitlement;
- **Family-friendly leave** (maternity, paternity, adoption,
  parental);
- **Specific** sectoral practices.

See [`../../employment/el-part-4-leave.md`](../../employment/el-part-4-leave.md).

### Termination considerations

For TCB firms, termination procedures must address:

- **EL 2003** procedural requirements (unfair dismissal);
- **JFSC notification** if termination of principal person;
- **Confidentiality** continuing obligations;
- **Restrictive covenants** enforcement;
- **AML / CFT** records retention;
- **Specific** outgoing-employee CDD considerations.

See [`../../employment/el-part-5-termination.md`](../../employment/el-part-5-termination.md).

## Specific scenarios

### Hiring a senior MLRO

- **Fit-and-proper** notification to JFSC;
- **Comprehensive contract** including JFSC liaison
  obligations;
- **Specific** access to confidential AML files;
- **Specific** indemnities;
- **Specific** termination provisions.

### Disciplining for AML / CFT breach

- **Investigation** under disciplinary procedure;
- **Specific** AML / CFT considerations;
- **JFSC notification** if material breach;
- **Specific** termination considerations for serious
  breaches.

### Maternity leave coverage

- **Statutory** entitlements respected;
- **Cover** for AML / regulated functions during leave;
- **Specific** considerations for client continuity;
- **JFSC notification** if material change in MLRO
  arrangements.

### Cross-border secondment

- **Specific** Jersey employment law continues;
- **Foreign jurisdiction** rules also apply;
- **Specific** tax considerations for secondee;
- **Specific** social-security considerations.

## What to do next

For new TCB staff hire:

1. **Verify** CHWL status;
2. **Conduct** background checks (specific to role);
3. **Issue** comprehensive employment contract per Article
   3 EL;
4. **Notify JFSC** if principal person;
5. **Provide** AML / CFT training;
6. **Update** firm's AML / CFT and HR procedures.

For existing staff matter:

- **Apply** standard procedures consistently;
- **Document** decisions thoroughly;
- **Take advice** for senior / regulated-role matters;
- **Coordinate** HR and compliance functions.

## Pitfalls

- **Hiring without status verification** — CHWL breach
  exposure;
- **Inadequate training** of new staff in AML / CFT;
- **Discriminatory** procedures (race, sex, age, etc.);
- **Inadequate JFSC notification** for principal persons;
- **Inadequate** confidentiality undertakings;
- **Inadequate** restrictive covenants;
- **Data protection** failures with employee data.
