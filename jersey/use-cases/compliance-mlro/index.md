# Compliance Officer / MLRO — Use Cases

## Who this section is for

You are a **Compliance Officer** or **Money Laundering Reporting
Officer (MLRO)** at a JFSC-regulated business — trust company,
bank, fund administrator, investment manager, insurer, or
similar. Your day involves some combination of: running the AML
programme; receiving and assessing internal disclosures; filing
SARs; conducting CDD reviews; managing sanctions screening;
liaising with the JFSC and FIU; preparing for audits and
inspections.

This section answers the **questions you actually have**, day-
to-day. Each question links into the
[AML/CFT topic graph](../../aml-cft/index.md) for the
authoritative rule and into
[financial-regulation](../../financial-regulation/index.md) for
the supervisory context.
