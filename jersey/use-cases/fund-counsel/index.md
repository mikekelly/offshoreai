# Fund Counsel / GP — Use Cases

## Who this section is for

You are **counsel or principal** for a Jersey **fund vehicle**
(JPF, Expert Fund, Listed Fund, public fund) or its general
partner / manager / GP. Your day involves some combination of:
selecting the right fund regime; structuring the fund vehicle;
admitting investors; running the fund's compliance; dealing with
the JFSC on registrations; navigating cross-border marketing
(AIFMD/NPPR); handling tax classification for investors.

This section answers the **questions you actually have** as a
fund counsel or GP, day-to-day. Each question links into the
[funds topic graph](../../funds/index.md) for the authoritative
regime and into the
[financial-regulation section](../../financial-regulation/index.md)
for the regulatory framework.
