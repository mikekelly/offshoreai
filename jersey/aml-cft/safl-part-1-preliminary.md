# SAFL 2014 Part 1 — Preliminary

## Article 1 — Interpretation

Key definitions:

- **"Designated person"** — a person designated under
  Article 3-6 (UN, UK, EU, or autonomous Jersey listing) or
  by reference to lists maintained by such authorities;
- **"Funds"** — financial assets and economic benefits of
  every kind (cash, securities, deposits, claims, dividends,
  interest, etc.);
- **"Economic resources"** — assets of every kind, tangible
  and intangible, that may be used to obtain funds, goods,
  or services;
- **"Asset-freezing"** — preventing dealings with funds /
  economic resources of designated persons;
- **"Making available"** — directly or indirectly providing
  funds, economic resources, or services to a designated
  person;
- **"Minister"** — the Minister responsible for sanctions
  (typically Chief Minister or External Relations);
- **"Sanctions list"** — the consolidated list of sanctions
  targets maintained by the JFSC under the Law.

## Article 2 — Application

The Law applies to:

- **Any person in Jersey** (individual or legal);
- **Jersey companies, foundations, partnerships** anywhere
  they operate;
- **Jersey nationals** wherever resident;
- **Persons doing business** in or with Jersey;
- **Any conduct** taking place in Jersey or by Jersey-
  connected persons.

The territorial scope is **broad**, capturing both onshore
Jersey activity and Jersey-connected offshore activity.
