# POCL 1999 Article 35A — Protected Disclosures

Article 35A provides **statutory protection** for persons
making POCL disclosures (SARs and similar).

## Protected acts

A person making a **disclosure** under POCL:

- **Authorised disclosure** (Article 32A);
- **Internal disclosure** to MLRO;
- **External SAR** to FIU;
- **Article 36** information sharing within regulated sector;

is **protected** from various consequences.

## Forms of protection

### Civil liability

- **No action** lies against the discloser;
- For **breach of confidence**;
- For **defamation**;
- For **breach of contractual confidentiality**;
- **Subject to** good faith of disclosure.

### Contractual obligations

- The discloser is **not in breach** of any contractual
  confidentiality obligation by making the disclosure;
- **Specifically** addresses banker / customer
  confidentiality, professional confidentiality, etc.

### Employment

- The discloser **cannot be dismissed**, disciplined, or
  detrimentally treated by their **employer** for making the
  disclosure;
- **Specific** employment-protection mechanism;
- **Whistleblower** protection in AML / CFT context.

## Conditions

The protection is conditional on:

- **Good faith** of the disclosure;
- **Reasonable basis** for the suspicion;
- **Compliance** with statutory procedures;
- **No** malicious or reckless disclosure.

## Why this matters

Without protection:

- **Employees** would face risk of suit / dismissal for
  reporting concerns;
- **Reporting culture** would be chilled;
- **AML / CFT regime** would be less effective;
- Article 35A enables the **disclosure infrastructure**.

## Specific contexts

### MLRO making external SAR

- **Protected** under Article 35A;
- **Specific** SAR processing protections.

### Employee internal disclosure to MLRO

- **Protected** from employer retaliation;
- **Specific** confidential channel for disclosure.

### Regulated firm sharing under Article 36

- **Protected** from civil liability;
- **Specific** good-faith requirement.

## Complementary regimes

The Article 35A protection works with:

- **Public Interest Disclosure (Jersey) Law 2008** —
  broader whistleblower regime;
- **Specific** Jersey employment protections;
- **JFSC** whistleblowing mechanisms;
- **Specific** sector-specific protections.
