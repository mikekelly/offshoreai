# Crime (International Co-operation) (Jersey) Law 2001 — Articles Index

Systematic scaffold for the **Crime (International Co-
operation) (Jersey) Law 2001 (CICL)** — Jersey's principal
framework for **cross-border mutual legal assistance (MLA)**
in criminal matters.

The Law has 6 Parts and ~50 active Articles.
