# CICL 2001 Articles 6-12 — Specific MLA Powers

## Article 6 — Production orders

The Royal Court may order **production of documents**:

- **Specified documents** in possession of named persons;
- **Within** a specified timeframe;
- **Subject to** privilege protections;
- **Subject to** confidentiality safeguards;
- **Enforceable** through contempt.

## Article 7 — Search and seizure

The Royal Court may issue **search warrants**:

- **Specifying** premises to be searched;
- **Specifying** items to be sought;
- **Authorising** entry and seizure;
- **Subject** to procedural protections;
- **Executed** by States Police.

## Article 8 — Witness compulsion

The Royal Court may **compel** a witness:

- To **attend** for evidence-taking;
- To **answer** questions on oath;
- To **produce** documents;
- **Subject to** privilege and other protections;
- **Failure to comply** is contempt.

## Article 9 — Compulsory examination

A formal examination procedure:

- **Witness on oath**;
- **Counsel** for the foreign authority may attend;
- **Witness** may be represented;
- **Record** of evidence made;
- **Transmitted** to the foreign authority through the AG.

## Article 10 — Service of foreign documents

The AG may arrange **service in Jersey** of:

- **Foreign criminal process** documents (summonses,
  indictments);
- **Foreign civil process** for foreign criminal-related
  proceedings;
- **Court orders** of foreign courts.

Service must comply with **Jersey procedural safeguards**.

## Article 11 — Refusal grounds

The AG may refuse assistance:

- **Dual criminality** not satisfied;
- **Political offence** exception;
- **Discriminatory** purpose;
- **Public policy** concerns;
- **Sovereignty** concerns;
- **Disproportionate** request;
- **Conflict** with Jersey proceedings;
- **Specific** treaty grounds.

The AG's refusal decision is **reviewable** by the Royal
Court.

## Article 12 — Costs

Costs of MLA cooperation:

- **Typically borne** by the requesting state for
  substantial costs;
- **Routine** costs absorbed by Jersey;
- **Specific** arrangements under bilateral treaties;
- **Confiscation proceeds** sharing arrangements
  separately.
