# Money Laundering (Jersey) Order 2008 — Articles Index

This is the **systematic-completeness scaffold** for the Money
Laundering (Jersey) Order 2008. The Order has 6 Parts and
~25 articles (with significant amendments since 2008). Coverage
is delivered through **Part-cluster files** with individual
article files for the most-cited provisions.
