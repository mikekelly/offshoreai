# MLO 2008 Article 16 — Nominated Officer (MLRO)

Article 16 requires every supervised business to designate a
**nominated officer** — the **Money Laundering Reporting
Officer (MLRO)** in practice.

## The provision

A supervised business must designate a **nominated officer** to:

- **Receive internal disclosures** from staff;
- **Decide** whether to make external SARs;
- **Make** external SARs to the FIU;
- **Oversee** the AML / CFT compliance programme;
- **Liaise** with the JFSC and FIU;
- **Train** and supervise staff in AML obligations;
- **Report** to the board on AML matters.

## Fit and proper

The MLRO must be:

- **Fit and proper** under JFSC standards;
- **Sufficiently senior** to perform the role with authority
  (typically a director or senior manager);
- **Sufficiently independent** to make MLRO decisions without
  conflict;
- **Sufficiently knowledgeable** about AML / CFT;
- **Sufficiently resourced** to perform the role;
- **JFSC-approved** through fit-and-proper notification.

## JFSC approval / notification

For **JFSC-regulated firms**:

- MLRO is **notified** to the JFSC;
- **Fit-and-proper** assessment applies;
- Changes are **notified** to the JFSC;
- **Annual reporting** confirms MLRO arrangements.

For **non-financial regulated sector** (lawyers, accountants,
etc.):

- MLRO designation is **maintained internally** with
  oversight by the relevant supervisory body.

## Deputy MLRO

Best practice (and JFSC expectation):

- Designate a **deputy MLRO** to act in MLRO's absence;
- Deputy must be **fit and proper**;
- **Procedures** for deputy authority documented;
- **Cover** during MLRO leave / illness / departure.

## Reporting line

The MLRO typically reports:

- To the **board** or **senior management committee**;
- **Annually** on AML / CFT compliance;
- **Ad hoc** on material matters (SAR trends, JFSC visits,
  significant breaches);
- With **direct access** to the chair / CEO if needed.

## Independence and protection

The MLRO must have:

- **Independence** from operational pressure to suppress SARs;
- **Authority** to make external disclosure without senior-
  management sign-off (though notification is appropriate);
- **Protection** under POCL Article 35A from retaliation;
- **Resources** to perform the role (analyst support,
  systems).

## MLRO annual report

Best practice (and JFSC expectation):

- **Annual report** to the board;
- Covering:
  - **BWRA** review and updates;
  - **CDD** statistics (new, refreshed, exited);
  - **Internal disclosures** and SARs;
  - **JFSC** engagement;
  - **Training** completion;
  - **Breaches** and remediation;
  - **Plan** for the year ahead.
