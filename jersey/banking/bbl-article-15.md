# BBL 1991 Article 15 — Liquidity Requirements

Article 15 governs **liquidity requirements** for Jersey banks.

## Liquidity Coverage Ratio (LCR)

- **High-quality liquid assets** ≥ 100% of expected 30-day
  net cash outflow under stress;
- **HQLA composition**: cash, central-bank reserves, high-
  rated sovereign debt, certain other instruments;
- **Run-off** rates assumed for funding sources;
- **Daily** monitoring;
- **Aligned** with Basel III LCR.

## Net Stable Funding Ratio (NSFR)

- **Available stable funding** ≥ 100% of **required stable
  funding**;
- **Calibrated** over a **1-year horizon**;
- **Designed** to ensure funding profile is sustainable;
- **Aligned** with Basel III NSFR.

## Internal liquidity adequacy

Beyond regulatory minimums:

- **Internal liquidity adequacy assessment (ILAAP)**;
- **Stress testing** under various scenarios;
- **Contingency funding plan** documented and tested;
- **Diversified** funding sources.

## Intraday liquidity

- **Adequate** intraday cash for settlement;
- **Monitoring** systems;
- **Limits** for material exposures.

## Reporting

Banks must report:

- **LCR** daily;
- **NSFR** quarterly;
- **Liquidity** stress test outcomes;
- **Funding profile** changes.

## JFSC supervision

The JFSC:

- **Reviews** liquidity arrangements as part of supervision;
- **Sets** additional firm-specific liquidity requirements
  where warranted;
- **Stress-test** scenarios prescribed;
- **Specific** intervention powers.
