# Banking — Jersey

Deposit-taking in Jersey is regulated under the **[Banking Business
(Jersey) Law 1991][bbl]** and the codes of practice issued by the
JFSC. The sector is dominated by branches and subsidiaries of large
international banking groups, reflecting Jersey's long-standing
**"top-50" policy** of welcoming banks that ranked among the world's
fifty largest (the policy has been moderated over time but the
emphasis on systemically significant parent groups persists).
