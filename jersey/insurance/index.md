# Insurance — Jersey

Insurance underwriting in Jersey is regulated under the **[Insurance
Business (Jersey) Law 1996][insl]**, with **Category A** and
**Category B** permits issued by the JFSC. Jersey is a notable
domicile for captive and "rent-a-captive" insurance, which often makes
use of the **cell-company** structures under Part 18D of the
Companies (Jersey) Law 1991. See
[companies/cell-companies.md](../companies/cell-companies.md).
