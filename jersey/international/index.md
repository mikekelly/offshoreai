# International — Jersey

This section covers Jersey's place in the international tax and
regulatory order: the **UK relationship** (Crown link plus customs
union); the **post-Brexit EU relationship** (Protocol 3 fell away,
UK–EU TCA applies for goods); the **automatic-exchange frameworks**
(CRS and FATCA); the **bilateral tax treaties** (DTAs and TIEAs);
and Jersey's participation in the **OECD inclusive framework** (Base
Erosion and Profit Shifting projects and **Pillar Two** in particular).
