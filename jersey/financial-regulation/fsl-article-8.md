# FSL 1998 Article 8 — Application for Registration

Article 8 specifies what an applicant must include in a
registration application.

## Application content

A complete application includes:

### Applicant identity

- **Corporate name** and registration details;
- **Constitutional documents** (memorandum, articles,
  equivalent);
- **Group structure chart** with ultimate beneficial owners;
- **Shareholder details** (ownership, control percentages);
- **Class of business** sought and **scope** within class.

### Business plan

- **Proposed activities** in detail;
- **Target markets** (jurisdictions, customer types);
- **Distribution channels** and **marketing approach**;
- **Volume projections** (3-5 years);
- **Pricing model**;
- **Competitive analysis**;
- **Strategic** rationale.

### Principal persons

For each director, senior officer, controller (10%+
shareholder), MLRO, Compliance Officer:

- **Personal questionnaire** completion;
- **CV** and **qualifications**;
- **Employment history** (typically 10+ years);
- **Regulatory history** (any prior approvals / sanctions);
- **References**;
- **Police-record / financial-soundness** declarations;
- **Conflicts** disclosure.

### Resources

- **Capital** structure and source;
- **Liquidity** plan;
- **Office** premises in Jersey;
- **Staff** count and qualifications;
- **IT systems** and resilience;
- **Outsourcing** arrangements.

### Governance

- **Board composition** and effectiveness;
- **Committee** structure;
- **Risk management** framework (three lines of defence);
- **Internal audit** (in-house or co-sourced);
- **Compliance** function;
- **Reporting** lines and escalation.

### Compliance

- **AML / CFT** programme aligned with MLO 2008 and JFSC
  Handbook;
- **Sanctions** screening;
- **Risk-based** approach documentation;
- **Customer-onboarding** procedures;
- **Transaction monitoring**;
- **Record keeping**.

### Operational matters

- **Auditor** engagement letter;
- **Banker** arrangements;
- **PI insurance**;
- **Business continuity** planning;
- **Cyber resilience**.

### Fees

- **Application fee** prescribed by JFSC schedule;
- **Annual fees** schedule (post-registration);
- **Specific charges** for inspections etc.

## Pre-application engagement

In practice:

- **Months** of pre-application engagement with JFSC for
  material new businesses;
- **Initial meeting** scoping the application;
- **Iterative** feedback on draft application;
- **Concept** approval before full submission in complex
  cases;
- **Coordination** with **home regulator** for foreign-group
  applicants.

## Application timeline

Typical timeline:

- **TCB / FSB / Investment Business** new application: **4-9
  months** from full submission to grant;
- **Simple** applications (limited scope, established group):
  faster;
- **Complex** applications (novel structure, new entrant):
  longer;
- **Conditional grants** common — registration with
  conditions to be met before commencement.
