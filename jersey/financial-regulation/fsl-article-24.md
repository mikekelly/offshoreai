# FSL 1998 Article 24 — Civil Penalties

Article 24 enables the JFSC to impose **civil financial
penalties** for FSL breaches.

## The penalties regime

Under the **Civil Financial Penalties (Financial Services
Business) (Jersey) Order 2015** (issued under FSL):

- **Three bands** of penalty severity (Band 1, 2, 3);
- **Calibrated** to severity, duration, customer harm,
  cooperation;
- **No statutory upper limit** at Band 3;
- **Aggravating / mitigating** factors considered.

## Penalty bands

### Band 1 (lesser breaches)

- **Typically** £10k - £100k;
- **Specific** procedural / minor breaches;
- **Less material** customer impact.

### Band 2 (material breaches)

- **Typically** £100k - £500k;
- **Material** compliance failures;
- **Substantive** harm or risk.

### Band 3 (most serious)

- **Typically** £500k+;
- **No statutory upper limit**;
- **Specific** seven-figure fines for major breaches;
- **Material** customer harm or systemic failures.

## Aggravating factors

- **Wilful** or **negligent** conduct;
- **Customer harm** suffered;
- **Repeated** breaches;
- **Concealment**;
- **Scale** of breach;
- **Senior management** awareness without action.

## Mitigating factors

- **Cooperation** with JFSC;
- **Self-reporting**;
- **Remediation** of breach and effects;
- **First-offender** status;
- **Customer compensation**;
- **Specific** procedural mitigation.

## Process

The civil penalty process:

1. **JFSC investigation**;
2. **Decision Notice** with proposed findings and penalty;
3. **Right** to make representations;
4. **Decision Making Committee** (independent from
   investigators);
5. **Reasoned** decision;
6. **Right of appeal** to Royal Court;
7. **Specific** appeal procedure.

## Public statements

Civil penalties typically accompanied by:

- **Public statement** naming the firm;
- **Findings** of breach;
- **Specific** lessons for industry;
- **Reputational** sanction beyond financial.

## Individual penalties

Individuals (senior managers) can be:

- **Personally penalised** in some configurations;
- **Disqualified** from future regulated roles;
- **Publicly named** in JFSC statements;
- **Specific** Jersey individual-accountability themes
  developing.
