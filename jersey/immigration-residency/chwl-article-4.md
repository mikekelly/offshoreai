# CHWL 2012 Article 4 — Acquiring Entitled Status

Article 4 sets out routes to **Entitled** status — the
highest CHWL category.

## Routes to Entitled

- **Born in Jersey** of parent(s) with Entitled status;
- **10+ years** of continuous Jersey residence;
- **Specific routes** for children of Jersey nationals;
- **Marriage / civil partnership** to Entitled spouse with
  continued residence;
- **Specific** other prescribed routes.

## Continuous residence requirement

- **10 years** typically;
- **Continuous** — limited absences permitted;
- **Specific** rules for what breaks continuity;
- **Documentation** of residence.

## Effect of Entitled status

- **Unrestricted** housing rights;
- **Unrestricted** employment rights;
- **No** sponsorship needed;
- **Specific** automatic rights.

## Loss

Entitled status can be **lost** through:

- **Extended absence** (typically 12 months without break);
- **Voluntary surrender**;
- **Specific** other prescribed events.
