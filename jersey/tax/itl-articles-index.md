# Income Tax (Jersey) Law 1961 — Articles Index

This is the **systematic-completeness scaffold** for the Income
Tax (Jersey) Law 1961. The Law has 24 active Parts and ~170+
active Articles (numbering is non-contiguous due to insertions,
repeals, and lettered articles).

This article-by-article wiki is at an **early stage** — the
**conceptual files** in the tax section
([`zero-ten.md`](zero-ten.md), [`personal-income-tax.md`](personal-income-tax.md),
[`distributions-and-deemed-distributions.md`](distributions-and-deemed-distributions.md),
etc.) cover most of the substance practitioners need, organised
by **concept** rather than by **Article number**.

This index is for **future expansion** as deep article files
are written.
