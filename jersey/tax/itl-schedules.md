# ITL Schedules

The Income Tax (Jersey) Law 1961 has several **Schedules**
providing supporting and specific provisions.

## Schedule A1 — Trading company definitions

- **Definitions** for trading-company classification;
- **Specific** criteria for trading vs investment;
- **Anti-avoidance** for misclassification.

## Schedule 1A — Long-Term Care contribution

- **LTC contribution** collection;
- **Specific** mechanics integrated with income-tax collection;
- See [`long-term-care.md`](long-term-care.md).

## Schedule 2 — Benefit exemptions

- **Specific** employment benefits exempt from tax;
- **Standard** Jersey-employer benefit categories.

## Schedule 3 — Benefit valuation

- **Mechanics** for valuing taxable benefits in kind;
- **Specific** company-car, accommodation, etc. valuations.

## Schedule 3A — Non-resident landlord rental income

- **Withholding regime** for non-resident landlord rental
  income;
- **Tenant / agent** withholding obligations;
- **Annual** reconciliation.

## Schedule 5 — Transitional provisions

- **Specific** transitions for major changes to the Law;
- **Pre / post** effective-date treatment.

## Schedule 7 — Married / civil partnership taxation (2025 onwards)

- **2025 reform** of marriage / civil partnership tax;
- **Independent** treatment by default;
- **Joint election** available;
- **Specific** transitional provisions.

## Other schedules

- **Various** specific-purpose schedules supplementing
  substantive articles;
- **Annual budget** adjustments may add / amend schedules.

## Practical considerations

- **Schedules** are integral to the Law — not optional
  appendices;
- **Specific** mechanics often in schedules;
- **Check** relevant schedule when interpreting articles.
