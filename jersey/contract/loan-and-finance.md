# Loan and Finance Contracts under Jersey Law

## Framework

Jersey loan and finance contracts sit at the intersection
of:

- **General contract law** (Pothier, formation, performance);
- **Banking regulation** (Banking Business (Jersey) Law 1991);
- **Security regimes** — hypothec for real property,
  Security Interests Law 2012 for intangibles / movables;
- **LMA / industry standard forms** routinely used.

## Loan agreements

Standard structure:

- **Facility** type (term loan, revolving, RCF, working
  capital);
- **Commitment** and drawdown mechanics;
- **Interest** rate (fixed / floating, benchmark, margin);
- **Repayment** schedule;
- **Prepayment** rights and break costs;
- **Representations** and warranties;
- **Covenants** (financial, information, general);
- **Events of default**;
- **Boilerplate** (governing law, jurisdiction, notices).

## LMA-style documents in Jersey

The Loan Market Association standard forms are routine in
Jersey:

- **Generally adapted** for Jersey nuances;
- **Choice of English law** common (with Jersey security);
- **Specific** Jersey carve-outs for security and
  enforcement;
- **Jersey advocates** review for Jersey-law issues.

## Security packages

Typical security in Jersey finance:

- **Hypothec** over Jersey real property (Loi 1862);
- **Security interests** under SIL 2012 (movables /
  intangibles / receivables);
- **Share security** under SIL 2012;
- **Account control** (bank accounts);
- **Guarantees** (group / personal);
- **Specific** Jersey trust / partnership structures.

## Hypothec over real property

- **Conventionnelle** hypothec — by contract passed in Royal
  Court;
- **Judiciaire** — by Royal Court judgment;
- **Légale** — by operation of law;
- **Registration** in the Public Registry;
- See [`../property/hypothec.md`](../property/hypothec.md).

## Security Interests (Jersey) Law 2012

For non-real-property security:

- **Movables**, **intangibles**, **receivables**, **shares**;
- **Attachment** and **perfection** rules;
- **Registration** at the Security Interests Register;
- **Priority** rules;
- See [`../property/security-interests-law-2012.md`](../property/security-interests-law-2012.md).

## Covenants

Standard covenants in Jersey-context loans:

### Financial covenants

- **Leverage** ratio;
- **Interest coverage**;
- **Cash flow** tests;
- **Specific** Jersey calculation conventions.

### Information covenants

- **Financial statements** delivery;
- **Compliance certificates**;
- **Notice** of default / material events;
- **AML / tax** information.

### General covenants

- **Negative pledge**;
- **Restrictions** on disposals, mergers, change of
  business;
- **Maintenance** of regulatory permissions;
- **Specific** Jersey regulatory undertakings.

## Events of default

Standard EoDs:

- **Non-payment**;
- **Breach** of other obligations;
- **Misrepresentation**;
- **Cross-default**;
- **Insolvency / désastre** (Jersey-specific);
- **Material adverse change**;
- **Illegality** / sanctions.

## Acceleration and enforcement

On default:

- **Acceleration** of principal and interest;
- **Demand** for repayment;
- **Enforcement** of security;
- **Specific** Jersey procedures (Royal Court for hypothec
  enforcement);
- **Specific** SIL 2012 enforcement mechanics.

## Interest and default interest

- **Compound interest** subject to penal-clause review;
- **Default interest** — see [`penalty-and-liquidated-damages.md`](penalty-and-liquidated-damages.md);
- **Specific** Jersey usury / consumer-protection considerations.

## Regulatory overlay

Jersey-licensed banking activity:

- **BBL 1991** — deposit-taking licensing;
- **JFSC** supervision;
- **AML / sanctions** compliance;
- **Specific** Jersey rules on cross-border lending.

## Specific Jersey contexts

### Acquisition finance

- **CJL** financial-assistance provisions (Articles 56-58);
- **Whitewash** procedure (where applicable);
- **Specific** Jersey corporate-benefit considerations;
- **Hypothec** over target real-property assets.

### Real-estate finance

- **Hypothec** as principal security;
- **Royal Court** passing required;
- **Specific** Jersey enforcement procedures;
- **Receivership** options.

### Fund finance

- **NAV / subscription** facilities;
- **JPF / Expert Fund** specifics;
- **Security** over limited-partner commitments;
- **Specific** Jersey LP security rules.

### Trust-context finance

- **Trustee** borrowing — TJL considerations;
- **Trustee** indemnity from trust assets;
- **Specific** Jersey case law on trustee creditworthiness;
- **Beneficiary** consent / *Saunders v Vautier* considerations.

## Cross-border finance

- **Choice of law** — see [`choice-of-law.md`](choice-of-law.md);
- **Multi-jurisdiction** security packages;
- **Specific** Jersey enforcement of foreign judgments;
- **Sanctions** carve-outs increasingly important.

## Drafting recommendations

- **Use LMA / industry standard** as base;
- **Adapt** for Jersey specifics (security, désastre,
  hypothec, regulatory);
- **Engage** Jersey advocates for security and enforcement
  opinions;
- **Address** sanctions, AML, regulatory expressly;
- **Calibrate** financial covenants to Jersey business model.

## Pitfalls

- **Importing** UK templates without Jersey adaptation;
- **Missing** hypothec procedure for Jersey real property;
- **Drafting** default interest as penalty;
- **Ignoring** désastre vs winding-up distinction in EoDs;
- **Missing** corporate-benefit / financial-assistance
  considerations for group guarantees;
- **Failing** to align Jersey security with foreign-law
  loan agreement.
