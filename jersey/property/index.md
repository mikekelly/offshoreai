# Property Law — Jersey

## What this section is about

This section covers **Jersey property law** — the rules
governing ownership and transfer of real and personal
property in Jersey.

Jersey property law is **distinctly Norman-customary** in
character, with significant **statutory overlays**:

- **Customary law** governs much of real property — contracts
  passed before the Royal Court, hypothecs, rentes,
  servitudes;
- **Statutes** cover specific areas:
  - **Loi (1880) sur la propriété foncière** — the principal
    real-property statute;
  - **Loi (1862) sur les Hypothèques** — security over real
    property;
  - **Loi (1991) sur la Vente** — sales;
  - **Security Interests (Jersey) Law 2012** — security over
    personal property.
