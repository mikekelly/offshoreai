# Qualified Member of a Foundation Council

Every Jersey foundation must have at least one **qualified
member** on its council — a council member regulated by the
**JFSC** under the **trust company services** regime.

## What a qualified member is

A qualified member is a person who is:

- **Registered with the JFSC** under the Financial Services
  (Jersey) Law 1998 for trust company business; or
- **A licensed professional** (lawyer, accountant) authorised
  to act in this capacity under prescribed regulations; or
- **An employee or director** of such a regulated person, acting
  in that capacity.

The qualified member is the **Jersey-regulated anchor** of the
foundation's governance.

## Why the qualified member is mandatory

The qualified-member requirement serves several functions:

- **Jersey substance** — ensures the foundation has Jersey-
  regulated touch points and is not a pure foreign vehicle;
- **AML / CDD compliance** — the qualified member is responsible
  for ensuring CDD on the founder, beneficiaries, and
  contributing parties;
- **Regulatory oversight** — JFSC has visibility into the
  foundation through its regulatory hold on the qualified
  member;
- **Professional governance** — ensures at least one council
  member with **professional training** and **fiduciary duties**;
- **Continuity** — a regulated trust company can provide
  enduring governance even if founder-appointed members
  resign.

## Practical implementation

In most Jersey foundations:

- The qualified member is a **Jersey trust company** (e.g. one
  of the major bank-owned or independent trust companies);
- The trust company acts through a **nominated employee** or
  **corporate-director** representative;
- The trust company provides **administration services**
  (record-keeping, statutory filings, council meeting support);
- Fees are charged on standard trust-company tariffs.

## Duties

The qualified member has the **same duties** as other council
members (act in good faith, in best interests of the foundation,
etc.), plus:

- **AML / CDD responsibilities** under the AML/CFT regime;
- **CRS / FATCA classification** responsibilities;
- **JFSC reporting** obligations applicable to its trust-company
  business generally.

## What happens if the qualified member departs

A vacancy must be **filled promptly**:

- The remaining council appoints a replacement (subject to charter
  / regulations);
- If no qualified member is in place for a prolonged period, the
  JFSC may take enforcement action;
- The foundation **cannot operate without** a qualified member.
