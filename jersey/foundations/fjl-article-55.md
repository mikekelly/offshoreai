# FJL 2009 Article 55 — Citation

Article 55 is the **citation** provision of the Foundations
(Jersey) Law 2009.

## The provision

The Law is cited as the **"Foundations (Jersey) Law 2009"**.

## Commencement

The Law came into force on **17 July 2009**.

## Significance

The Law's introduction:

- **Established** the Jersey foundation as a legal vehicle;
- **Modernised** Jersey's offering for civil-law clients;
- **Complemented** the existing trust regime under TJL 1984;
- **Brought** Jersey in line with other offshore jurisdictions
  offering foundations (Bahamas 2004, Mauritius 2012, etc.).

## Amendments since

The FJL has been **amended** to address:

- **Specific** procedural refinements;
- **Substance Law 2019** integration;
- **FS(DPI)(J)L 2020** BO integration;
- **AML / CFT** updates;
- **Specific** FATF / Moneyval feedback.

## Related legislation

The FJL operates with:

- **Trusts (Jersey) Law 1984** — comparing / contrasting
  vehicle;
- **Companies (Jersey) Law 1991** — body-corporate analogues;
- **FSL 1998** — qualified-member regulation;
- **POCL 1999 / MLO 2008** — AML / CFT;
- **Income Tax (Jersey) Law 1961** — tax treatment of
  foundations;
- **Substance Law 2019** — substance for in-scope
  activities;
- **FS(DPI)(J)L 2020** — BO register integration.

## International standing

Jersey foundations are:

- **Internationally recognised**;
- **Reputation** of Jersey carries through;
- **Specific** characterisation in foreign tax laws;
- **Acceptance** by counterparties globally;
- **Distinct** from trusts but functionally complementary.
