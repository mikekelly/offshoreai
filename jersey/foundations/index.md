# Foundations — Jersey

A Jersey foundation is a civil-law-flavoured vehicle introduced by the
**[Foundations (Jersey) Law 2009][fl]**. It is a body corporate (like
a company) but has no shareholders; instead it is governed by a
**council** under a **charter** (a public document) and **regulations**
(a private document), oversight by a **guardian** is mandatory, and one
council member must be a Jersey-regulated **qualified member**.
([Carey Olsen — Jersey foundations][co]; [JFSC — About foundations][jfsc-fdn])
