# Beneficial Ownership Register — Jersey

Jersey maintains a **central register of beneficial ownership
and significant persons** under the **Financial Services
(Disclosure and Provision of Information) (Jersey) Law 2020**.

## Scope

The register captures beneficial-ownership information for:

- **Jersey companies** (since the 2020 Law, which built on earlier
  arrangements);
- **Jersey foundations**;
- **Jersey limited partnerships and LLPs** in scope;
- **Other Jersey entities** as specified.

## What is recorded

For each entity:

- **Beneficial owners** — natural persons who ultimately own or
  control the entity (typically 25%+ ownership or control);
- **Significant persons** — natural persons who can exercise
  significant influence over the entity;
- **Identification details** for each beneficial owner /
  significant person (name, date of birth, nationality,
  residential address);
- **Nature and extent of ownership / control**;
- **Effective dates** of beneficial-ownership changes.

## Public vs private elements

- **The central register itself** is **not publicly accessible** —
  access is restricted to **law enforcement, the JFSC, Revenue
  Jersey**, and authorised foreign authorities through
  cooperation channels;
- A **limited public element** — "**Public Register of Persons
  with Significant Control**" — may be available for certain
  categories of entity (subject to evolving policy).

## Enforcement

Failure to provide accurate beneficial-ownership information is
an **offence**:

- **Civil financial penalties** for non-compliance;
- **Criminal liability** for false / misleading information;
- **JFSC regulatory consequences** for in-scope licensed
  entities.

## Comparison with other jurisdictions

| Jurisdiction | Public register? |
|---|---|
| Jersey | Generally non-public (access restricted to authorities) |
| UK (PSC register) | Publicly accessible |
| Cayman | Authorities only |
| BVI | Authorities only (some categories changing) |
| Bermuda | Authorities only |

The **public vs private** question is one of the **most-debated
international tax-transparency** issues. Jersey's position
balances:

- **AML / law-enforcement effectiveness** (full information to
  authorities);
- **Privacy** of legitimate ownership arrangements;
- **International expectations** (subject to ongoing review by
  Moneyval, FATF, EU).
