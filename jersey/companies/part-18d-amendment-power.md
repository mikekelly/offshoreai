# Article 127YN — Power of States to Amend Part 18D

Article 127YN of the **[Companies (Jersey) Law 1991][cjl]** gives
the **States of Jersey** power to **amend Part 18D** (Cell
Companies) by Regulations.

## What Article 127YN covers

The States may by Regulations amend Part 18D, including:

- Adjusting cell-creation procedures;
- Refining ICC and PCC segregation rules;
- Modifying cell-transfer and conversion mechanisms;
- Updating naming conventions;
- Adapting accounting and winding-up modifications.

(See the consolidated text at [jerseylaw.je][cjl].)

## Why this matters

Cell-company practice evolves with market needs (funds,
insurance, securitisation). Article 127YN allows Jersey to keep
the regime current **without primary legislation each time**.
