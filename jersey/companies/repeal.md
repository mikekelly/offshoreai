# Article 223 — Repeal

Article 223 of the **[Companies (Jersey) Law 1991][cjl]**
**repealed** earlier legislation superseded by the 1991 Law.

## What Article 223 covers

The 1991 Law repealed (with appropriate transitional savings under
Article 221):

- The **Loi (1861) sur les Sociétés à Responsabilité Limitée**
  (the previous principal companies legislation);
- Various **amending statutes** in the period 1861–1991;
- **Related provisions** in other enactments that were no longer
  needed.

(See the consolidated text at [jerseylaw.je][cjl].)

## Why this matters

The 1991 Law is the **consolidated successor** to a long lineage
of Jersey corporate legislation. Article 223 formally closes the
old regime so that:

- There is **one principal companies statute** in force;
- **Historical research** may still reach back to the 1861 Loi
  for understanding pre-1991 events;
- **Current law** is uncluttered by superseded enactments.
