# Companies (Jersey) Law 1991 — Articles Index

One-line description of every Article in the
**[Companies (Jersey) Law 1991][cjl]**, in statutory order grouped
by Part, with a link to the deep concept file for that Article where
one exists. The Law is large (24 Parts including lettered Parts 18A,
18B, 18BA, 18C, 18D, 20A; ~300 active provisions) and the statute-
wiki is being built in successive batches.

Files marked **[repealed]** or *(planned: filename.md)* will be
filled in over the coming batches.

batches.
