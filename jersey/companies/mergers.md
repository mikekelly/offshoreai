# Part 18B — Statutory Mergers (Overview)

Part 18B of the **[Companies (Jersey) Law 1991][cjl]** is the
**statutory merger regime**: a directors-driven, court-free
mechanism by which **two or more bodies corporate** can be
combined into a **single surviving entity**, with assets,
liabilities, contracts and employees vesting **by operation of
law** in the merged body.

## When Part 18B is used

- **Group simplification** — collapsing parent-subsidiary or
  sister-company structures into a single entity;
- **Cross-border consolidation** — combining a Jersey company
  with a foreign body corporate (subject to JFSC consent and
  foreign-law recognition);
- **Inbound migration** — foreign body merges into a Jersey
  company (effectively a redomiciliation by merger);
- **Outbound migration** — Jersey company merges into a foreign
  body;
- **Post-acquisition integration** — buyer absorbs a Jersey
  target by merger rather than acquisition.

## Headline features

- **Court-free in the normal case** — directors give solvency
  certificates instead of a Court sanction (cf. scheme of
  arrangement);
- **Universal succession** — assets, liabilities, contracts and
  employees pass automatically (Article 127FN);
- **JFSC gating for cross-border** — Article 127FF consent is
  required where any merging body is not a Jersey company;
- **Court backstop where solvency is not certified** — Article
  127FD;
- **Member objection** in Court within 21 days — Article 127FB;
- **Creditor objection** in Court within 21 days — Article 127FE.

## The Part 18B chapters

| Chapter | Articles | Topic |
|---|---|---|
| 1 — General | 127A – 127C | Definitions, eligible bodies |
| 2 — Members | 127D – 127FB | Merger agreement, approval, member objection |
| 3 — Creditors | 127FC – 127FE | Creditor notice, Court application, creditor objection |
| 4 — Commission | 127FF – 127FI | JFSC consent for cross-border mergers |
| 5 — Registration | 127FJ – 127FM | Pre-registration documents, registration |
| 6 — Final | 127FN – 127GA | Effect of merger, offences, amendment power |

## Choosing between Part 18B merger and alternatives

| Outcome desired | Best route |
|---|---|
| 100% acquisition of a Jersey target with target board support | **Scheme of arrangement** (Article 125) or **Part 18B merger** |
| 100% acquisition without target board support | **Part 18 takeover offer** (squeeze-out at 90%) |
| Move corporate seat from Jersey to a foreign jurisdiction | **Outbound continuance** (Part 18C) or **outbound merger** (Part 18B with foreign merged body) |
| Split a business into separate companies | **Demerger** (Part 18BA) |
| Court-supervised compromise with creditors | **Scheme of arrangement** (Article 125) |
