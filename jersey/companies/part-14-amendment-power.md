# Article 85A — Power of States to Amend Part 14

Article 85A of the **[Companies (Jersey) Law 1991][cjl]** is the
**amendment-power** provision for Part 14 (Directors and
Secretary). The States may amend Part 14 by Regulations.

## What Article 85A says

The **States may amend this Part by Regulations**, providing
flexibility for legislative updates to provisions on directors,
secretaries, and related matters.

(See the consolidated text at [jerseylaw.je][cjl].)

## Why this matters

Director and secretary regulation is an area where:

- **OECD inclusive-framework** expectations evolve;
- **Beneficial-ownership and transparency** rules tighten over
  time;
- **Liability frameworks** may need updates to track international
  best practice.

The Article 85A power lets the States update Part 14 by
Regulations without primary-law amendment.

## Family of amendment powers

One of several Part-specific amendment-power Articles — see also
Articles 2B, 17D, 40C, 59, 66A, 113H, 115B, 124A, 127GA, 127YN,
194A.
