# Article 85 — Particulars of Secretaries

Article 85 of the **[Companies (Jersey) Law 1991][cjl]** specifies
the **particulars** that must be recorded for **secretaries** in
the register of directors and secretaries.

## What gets recorded

For each secretary:

- **Name** (natural-person name or corporate registered name);
- **Address** (residential or registered office, depending on
  whether natural or corporate);
- Other identifying particulars analogous to those required for
  directors.

(See the consolidated text at [jerseylaw.je][cjl].)

## In practice

In typical Jersey practice:

- The **registered agent** (a TCB) is the secretary, so the
  recorded particulars are the **TCB's registered office** and
  corporate details;
- For **listed Jersey companies** with in-house secretaries,
  the secretary's individual particulars are recorded.

## Pitfalls

- **Out-of-date** secretary particulars after the registered
  agent's office moves or its corporate structure changes.
