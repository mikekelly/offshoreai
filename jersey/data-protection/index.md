# Data Protection — Jersey

## What this section is about

This section covers **Jersey data protection law** — Jersey's
**GDPR-equivalent** framework governing the processing of
personal data.

The principal statutes are:

- **Data Protection (Jersey) Law 2018 (DPL)** — the core data
  protection regime, aligned with the EU GDPR;
- **Data Protection Authority (Jersey) Law 2018 (DPAL)** —
  establishing the **Information Commissioner (Jersey
  Office of the Information Commissioner — JOIC)** as
  supervisory authority.

Jersey's regime is **substantially aligned** with the EU GDPR
to maintain **adequacy** with the EU for international data
flows.
