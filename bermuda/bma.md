# BMA — Bermuda Monetary Authority

## What the BMA is

The **Bermuda Monetary Authority (BMA)** is Bermuda's
**integrated financial-services regulator**:

- **Established** in 1969 by the Bermuda Monetary Authority
  Act;
- **Independent** of government in day-to-day operation;
- **Funded** through industry fees;
- **Reports** to Bermuda Parliament.

## Functions

The BMA:

- **Licenses** financial-services businesses;
- **Registers** insurers, funds, banks, trust companies;
- **Supervises** ongoing compliance;
- **Investigates** breaches;
- **Enforces** regulatory standards;
- **Issues** Rules, Codes, and Guidance Notes;
- **Cooperates** with international regulators;
- **Issues** Bermuda dollar (currency board);
- **Administers** financial-services regulation.

## Sectors regulated

### Insurance (the flagship)

- **Insurance Act 1978**;
- **Multiple insurance classes** (1-5, special purpose,
  life);
- **Sophisticated** prudential framework;
- **Solvency II equivalent**;
- **NAIC qualified jurisdiction**.

### Banking

- **Banks and Deposit Companies Act 1999**;
- **Limited** number of banks;
- **Basel-aligned** prudential rules.

### Investment business / funds

- **Investment Business Act 2003**;
- **Investment Funds Act 2006**;
- **Specific** fund categories.

### Trust services

- **Trusts (Regulation of Trust Business) Act 2001**;
- **Trust company licensing**;
- **Specific** Bermuda rules.

### AML / CFT

- **Proceeds of Crime Act 1997**;
- **AML Regulations**;
- **BMA** AML / CFT Guidance.

### Digital assets

- **Specific** digital assets regulatory framework;
- **AML / CFT** integration.

## BMA's international standing

The BMA is **widely respected**:

- **Solvency II equivalence** with EU (only offshore
  jurisdiction with full equivalence);
- **NAIC qualified jurisdiction** for US reinsurance;
- **IOSCO MMoU** signatory;
- **IAIS** active member;
- **Sophisticated** professional staff;
- **Major international** influence in insurance regulation.

## BMA Insurance Rule Book

The BMA Insurance Rule Book:

- **Comprehensive** insurance regulation;
- **Class-specific** rules;
- **Solvency II-aligned** for Class 4 / Class 3B;
- **Specific** rules for captives, ILS, reinsurance;
- **Updated** to reflect international developments.

## Engagement

For Bermuda regulated entities:

- **BMA** authoritative on all regulatory matters;
- **Specific** BMA procedures;
- **Online** systems for filings;
- **Industry** consultations regularly;
- **Major** annual engagement events.
