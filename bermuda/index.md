# Bermuda — Adjacent Jurisdiction

## Why this section exists

**Bermuda** is the **leading global jurisdiction** for:

- **Captive insurance** (largest captive market globally);
- **Reinsurance** (one of the world's largest reinsurance
  hubs);
- **Specialty** insurance markets;
- **Insurance-linked securities** (ILS) / catastrophe bonds.

Jersey practitioners encounter Bermuda matters in:

- **Insurance** group structures;
- **Trust** structures with insurance components;
- **ILS** structures;
- **Specific** Bermuda corporate vehicles.

This section provides **high-level** Bermuda coverage. For
full Bermuda practice, engage **Bermuda counsel**.
