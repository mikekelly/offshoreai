# Bermuda Funds — Overview

## Principal legislation

- **Investment Funds Act 2006** — principal funds statute;
- **Specific** subsidiary regulations;
- **BMA Rules**.

## Fund categories

### Standard funds (authorised)

- **Retail** offerings;
- **Higher** regulatory tier;
- **Less common** in Bermuda.

### Class A exempt funds

- **Sophisticated investor** funds;
- **Minimum subscription** US$100,000;
- **Lighter** regulation;
- **Most common** Bermuda fund category.

### Class B exempt funds

- **Qualifying** investors;
- **Specific** sophistication tests;
- **Lighter** regulation;
- **Faster** establishment.

### Closed-ended funds

- **PE / VC / real estate** style;
- **Specific** Bermuda rules.

### Specialist funds

- **Specific** categories per investor type.

## Insurance-linked funds

Bermuda's **specialty** in funds:

- **ILS funds** invest in Bermuda cat bonds / ILS;
- **Reinsurance-related** investment funds;
- **Specific** Bermuda investment-services expertise;
- **Connection** to insurance sector.

## Fund vehicles

Bermuda funds typically use:

- **Bermuda exempted company**;
- **Bermuda LLC**;
- **Bermuda Limited Partnership** (for PE-style structures);
- **Segregated Accounts Company** (SAC) for umbrella
  vehicles.

## Comparison with other offshore

| Feature | Jersey | Cayman | Bermuda |
|---|---|---|---|
| Hedge funds | Some | **Dominant** | Limited |
| PE / VC | Significant | **Dominant** | Limited |
| Insurance-linked | Limited | Limited | **Specialty** |
| Public funds | CIFL 1988 | Mutual Funds Act | Investment Funds Act 2006 |
| AIFMD | NPPR | NPPR | NPPR |

Bermuda fund market is **smaller** than Jersey / Cayman but
has **specific specialty** in **insurance-linked** funds and
funds integrating with Bermuda's insurance sector.

## When to use Bermuda funds

Common scenarios:

- **ILS / cat bond** fund (specialty);
- **Insurance-linked** investment fund;
- **Existing** Bermuda group with fund needs;
- **Reinsurance** investment strategies;
- **Specific** Bermuda structural advantages.
