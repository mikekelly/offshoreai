The standard rate of GST in Jersey is **5%**, imposed by the **Goods and Services Tax (Jersey) Law 2007** and administered by Revenue Jersey. The rate has applied at 5% since being increased from 3% in 2011. Supplies are either taxed at 5%, zero-rated (e.g. exports), or exempt (e.g. certain finance/insurance supplies); a specialised **International Services Entity (ISE)** regime provides a flat-fee alternative for finance-services businesses serving non-Jersey clients.
