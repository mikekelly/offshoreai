## Is the Cayman LLC the right form?

For redomiciling a **Delaware LLC**, the Cayman LLC is generally the natural fit. The **Limited Liability Companies Act 2016** was specifically designed to mirror the Delaware LLC — same member/manager architecture, the LLC Agreement as the master document, limited liability for *all* members (no GP with unlimited liability), and minimal corporate formalities. That parity is the whole point of the form: a Delaware LLC can **convert directly** to a Cayman LLC, whereas redomiciling into a Cayman exempted company would mean converting to a corporate (share-based) form first. ([`knowledge/cayman/cayman-llc.md`](knowledge/cayman/cayman-llc.md))

The form is "particularly useful" precisely in your situation — a restructuring where a Delaware LLC is redomiciling to Cayman and Delaware-LLC parity matters. Migration in is permitted, and the corpus describes Cayman's continuation-in regime as meaningfully easier than many onshore jurisdictions. ([`knowledge/cayman/cayman-llc.md`](knowledge/cayman/cayman-llc.md); [`knowledge/cayman/exempted-company.md`](knowledge/cayman/exempted-company.md) §"Migration and exit")

**When you might *not* use the LLC:** if the entity is a fund-holding vehicle in a PE/VC structure, the **ELP** remains the dominant choice (GP/LP economics, market familiarity); the LLC is more commonly the **GP entity**, a JV/co-investment vehicle, or an SPV. And some investors/counterparties are still unfamiliar with the form and will need it explained. ([`knowledge/cayman/cayman-llc.md`](knowledge/cayman/cayman-llc.md); [`knowledge/cayman/exempted-limited-partnership.md`](knowledge/cayman/exempted-limited-partnership.md))

## How check-the-box works

The Cayman LLC is **eligible for US "check-the-box" classification** under the entity-classification regulations:

- **Default (no election):** as a foreign eligible entity owned by US tax persons, it is treated as a **partnership** if it has more than one member, or a **disregarded entity** if it has a single member. Both give flow-through treatment.
- **Elective:** the entity can affirmatively **elect to be treated as a corporation** instead — useful for **blocker / GILTI / Subpart F** planning.

This classification flexibility is one of the main reasons sponsors pick the LLC over the exempted company, which is a **per-se corporation** for US tax in most cases and does not get the same election. ([`knowledge/cayman/cayman-llc.md`](knowledge/cayman/cayman-llc.md) §"US tax treatment", §"Cayman LLC vs Cayman Exempted Company")

**Watch-outs flagged in the corpus:** the check-the-box *default* may not be the classification your structure actually wants, so confirm before completion; and the migration from Delaware carries **tax-residency and Pillar Two implications** that need careful handling. The Cayman LLC pays **no direct Cayman tax** and a 50-year tax undertaking is available. ([`knowledge/cayman/cayman-llc.md`](knowledge/cayman/cayman-llc.md) §"Pitfalls"; [`knowledge/cayman/tax.md`](knowledge/cayman/tax.md))
