The standard rate of GST in Jersey is **5%**, imposed by the **Goods and Services Tax (Jersey) Law 2007** and administered by Revenue Jersey. The rate rose from 3% to 5% in 2011 and remains flat — there is no separate higher or intermediate band; other supplies are either zero-rated (e.g. exports) or exempt (e.g. certain finance/insurance, housing, healthcare, education).
