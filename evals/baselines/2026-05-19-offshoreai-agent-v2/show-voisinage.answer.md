## What voisinage is

**Voisinage** is Jersey's Norman customary-law doctrine governing relations between neighbouring landowners. It obliges each owner to use their property in a way that does not cause **unreasonable harm** to neighbours — harm that **exceeds the ordinary inconveniences of neighbourhood** and lacks adequate justification. It is distinct from English nuisance: voisinage can apply on an **objective basis without proof of fault**, and it is not confined to enumerated categories like smell, smoke and noise. (`jersey/property/voisinage.md`)

## Does it apply to your neighbour problem?

It might — but only if the facts fit the threshold. The corpus identifies four elements you would need to be able to show:

1. **Harm** that is objectively identifiable;
2. **Caused by** the neighbour's use of their property;
3. **Exceeding** the ordinary inconveniences of neighbourhood living; and
4. **Without** adequate justification.

The doctrine has been applied to **excessive noise, smells (livestock, kitchens), smoke and fumes, vibrations, surface water and drainage, tree roots and overhanging branches, and light obstruction**, among others. Construction or commercial activities can qualify if they are excessive or prolonged rather than merely temporary. (`jersey/property/voisinage.md`)

Things that often **defeat** a claim: reasonable use of the property, the claimant's consent or acquiescence, statutory authority for the activity, and (in limited circumstances) prescription. Note that **planning permission does not by itself exclude voisinage** — regulatory compliance is only evidence of reasonable use, not a defence in itself. (`jersey/property/voisinage.md`)

## Practical next steps from the corpus

- **Document** the interference (logs, photographs, recordings);
- Build a **correspondence trail** with the neighbour;
- **Mediate** as a first step where possible;
- Consider **expert evidence** (acoustic, environmental) early;
- If litigation is needed, the route is an **Order of Justice in the Royal Court**, where the available remedies are **injunction, damages, and specific performance** of preventative measures;
- **Engage a Jersey advocate familiar with voisinage** — and avoid the common pitfall of assuming the English nuisance test transposes wholesale. (`jersey/property/voisinage.md`)
