## Recommendation

For a **closed-ended PE fund with a global LP base**, the corpus's decision matrix points to **Cayman ELP + Private Funds Act 2020 registration** as the default lean, not Jersey. Jersey's JPF becomes the better answer only if your LP base or marketing plan is **EU/UK-weighted**.

## Main factors

### 1. Vehicle form and LP familiarity

- **Cayman ELP** is described as the **dominant closed-ended PE/VC vehicle globally** — the LPA-driven contractual form most global LPs expect, with statutory safe-harbours for LP non-management, parallel-fund compatibility (Cayman ELP for non-US LPs alongside a Delaware LP for US LPs), and registration in 1–2 business days (`knowledge/cayman/exempted-limited-partnership.md`).
- **Jersey JPF** is structurally flexible (Jersey company, LP, unit trust, or PCC/ICC) and ships in **~48 hours** through the JFSC fast-track, but caps at **50 offers/investors at any time** and requires a JFSC-licensed administrator as the **Designated Service Provider** — a meaningful constraint if your LP count or marketing reach is larger (`knowledge/jersey/funds/jersey-private-fund.md`).

### 2. EU / UK marketing

This is the single biggest factor that flips the answer toward Jersey:

- **Cayman has no NPPR access** to EU member states (`knowledge/CROSS-JURISDICTIONAL-MAP.md`, "EU passport / NPPR routes" row).
- **Jersey AIFs are marketable into the EU via country-by-country NPPR** through Jersey's AIFMD-equivalent JFSC regime, and into the UK via the UK NPPR (`knowledge/jersey/funds/aifmd-nppr.md`). No full AIFMD third-country passport is in force.

If a material slice of LPs sits in the EU/UK, Jersey JPF or Expert Fund earns its place. If LPs are predominantly North American, Middle Eastern, or Asian, Cayman's lack of NPPR is irrelevant.

### 3. Regulatory regime

- **Cayman Private Funds Act 2020** brought closed-ended funds inside CIMA's perimeter — registration before launch, CIMA-approved auditor, valuation/custody/cash-monitoring policies, AML/CFT compliance, annual return. Lighter touch than EU AIFMD but more substantive than pre-2020 (`knowledge/cayman/private-funds-act.md`).
- **Jersey JPF** is the **light-touch** end of the Jersey spectrum: DSP substitutes for direct JFSC supervision of the fund itself; investors get a PPM and self-certify eligibility, not a public-fund prospectus (`knowledge/jersey/funds/jersey-private-fund.md`).

### 4. Tax

- **Cayman**: no direct tax on the ELP or partners; LPs taxed at home as if holding the underlying directly. Pillar Two MCIT 2025 (15% QDMTT) applies to in-scope groups (≥ EUR 750m revenue), not to typical PE fund LP economics (`knowledge/CROSS-JURISDICTIONAL-MAP.md`; `knowledge/cayman/exempted-limited-partnership.md`).
- **Jersey**: zero-ten regime with MCIT 2025 QDMTT layered on for in-scope groups (`knowledge/CROSS-JURISDICTIONAL-MAP.md`).

Both jurisdictions are tax-neutral at the fund-vehicle level for LPs; the Pillar Two overlay is functionally similar.

### 5. Economic substance

Both impose substance requirements on the GP / Investment Manager (Cayman Economic Substance Act 2018; Jersey Substance Law 2019). Paper-only presence is not viable in either jurisdiction — sponsor must plan real Cayman or Jersey footprint for the management function (`knowledge/cayman/exempted-limited-partnership.md`; `knowledge/CROSS-JURISDICTIONAL-MAP.md`).

### 6. The >50-investor question

Jersey's JPF investor cap (50) is the cleanest disqualifier. If you expect a wider LP base, you'd step up to a **Jersey Expert Fund** (or Listed Fund) rather than a JPF — at which point Cayman's ELP/PFA route becomes more directly comparable.

## How to decide

- **Global LP base, primarily non-EU/UK, expected wide LP count** → **Cayman ELP + PFA registration**.
- **Material EU/UK LP slice, ≤50 investors, want speed and DSP-supervised lighter touch** → **Jersey JPF as LP**.
- **EU/UK marketing required at scale (>50 investors)** → **Jersey Expert Fund** (out of scope of the files cited here; see `knowledge/jersey/funds/expert-fund.md`).

## Caveats

- All four cited corpus files are `status: draft` (not `stable`/`review`). Verify against primary sources — the ELP Act, Private Funds Act 2020, JFSC JPF Guide, and the AIF (Jersey) Order 2012 — before commitment.
- The corpus does not cover **CIMA fee levels, JFSC fee levels, or annual running-cost comparisons** between the two — get a current cost quote from administrators in both jurisdictions.
- Manager-level regulation (CIMA Investment Business / Excluded Persons in Cayman vs. JFSC AIFMR 2012 in Jersey) is a separate factor not detailed in the cited files.
