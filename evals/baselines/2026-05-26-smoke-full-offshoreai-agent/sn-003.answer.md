# Jersey Private Fund (JPF) vs Expert Fund

## What the JPF regime is

The **Jersey Private Fund (JPF)** is Jersey's **light-touch private-fund regime** for closed-investor, sophisticated-investor structures. Its headline features are an investor cap of **up to 50 offers/investors** at any time, **professional/eligible investors only**, **fast-track JFSC authorisation (typically ~48 hours)**, and reduced ongoing supervision. A JPF must appoint a **JFSC-licensed administrator as its Designated Service Provider (DSP)**, which carries CDD/AML, books and records, and administration responsibility — effectively substituting for direct JFSC oversight of the fund itself. JPFs can be structured as a Jersey company, limited partnership (most common), unit trust, or cell company, and are the default vehicle for PE funds, closed-ended real estate, family-and-friends pools, single-LP/club deals, and carry vehicles ([`knowledge/jersey/funds/jersey-private-fund.md`](knowledge/jersey/funds/jersey-private-fund.md)).

## How an Expert Fund differs

The **Expert Fund** is designed for **expert investors** — sophisticated parties meeting **net-worth** (typically minimum US$100,000 investment, or institutional/professional-client status) and investment-threshold criteria. Compared to a JPF, it has **no specific investor cap**, requires **more detailed disclosure**, takes **longer to authorise** (few days to weeks rather than 48 hours), and offers **wider expert-investor marketing scope** including AIFMD NPPR routes for EU marketing ([`knowledge/jersey/funds/expert-fund.md`](knowledge/jersey/funds/expert-fund.md)).

## Side-by-side

| Feature | JPF | Expert Fund |
|---|---|---|
| Investor cap | 50 | No specific cap (subject to expert criteria) |
| Investor qualification | Professional / eligible | Expert (net-worth or institutional) |
| Authorisation time | ~48 hours | Few days to weeks |
| Disclosure | Light PPM | More detailed offering memorandum |
| Marketing scope | Limited private placement | Wider expert-investor marketing / NPPR |

Source: [`knowledge/jersey/funds/expert-fund.md`](knowledge/jersey/funds/expert-fund.md).

## When to choose which

- **JPF** — PE funds, co-investments, closed-ended real estate, family-and-friends pools, single-LP/club deals, carry vehicles where the investor count fits within 50 ([`knowledge/jersey/funds/jersey-private-fund.md`](knowledge/jersey/funds/jersey-private-fund.md)).
- **Expert Fund** — single-strategy hedge funds with multiple expert investors, larger PE vehicles exceeding the JPF cap, liquid alternatives marketed to family offices/institutions, and funds needing AIFMD NPPR for EU marketing ([`knowledge/jersey/funds/expert-fund.md`](knowledge/jersey/funds/expert-fund.md)).
