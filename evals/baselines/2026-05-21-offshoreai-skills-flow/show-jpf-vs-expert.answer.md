## Recommendation: JPF, for a club deal

For a **club-deal PE fund**, the **Jersey Private Fund (JPF)** is the natural fit — the corpus lists "club deal structures," "co-investment vehicles," and private equity funds as the JPF's core use cases (`knowledge/jersey/funds/jersey-private-fund.md`). Club deals are small, sophisticated-investor groups, which sits well inside the JPF's design envelope.

## Why the JPF over the Expert Fund here

| Factor | JPF | Expert Fund |
|---|---|---|
| Investor cap | Up to **50** offers/investors | No specific cap (subject to expert criteria) |
| Investor qualification | Professional / eligible | "Expert" (e.g. ~US$100k min investment or net-worth threshold) |
| Authorisation | Fast-track JFSC, **~48 hours** | A few days to weeks; more disclosure |
| Disclosure doc | Light PPM | More detailed offering memorandum |
| Supervision | Light-touch via the **designated service provider (DSP)** | Streamlined but heavier than JPF |

(`knowledge/jersey/funds/jersey-private-fund.md`, `knowledge/jersey/funds/expert-fund.md`)

A club deal almost always involves a small number of known investors — comfortably within the **50-investor JPF cap** — so you gain the JPF's speed-to-market (~48h authorisation) and lighter ongoing supervision without needing the Expert Fund's higher investor ceiling. The Expert Fund earns its keep when you **exceed the JPF's 50-investor count** or want wider expert-investor marketing latitude (`knowledge/jersey/funds/expert-fund.md`).

## When you'd reconsider

- **You expect more than 50 investors / offers** → the Expert Fund's uncapped (but expert-qualified) base is the reason to switch.
- **EU marketing** → both can sit under AIFMD NPPR, but the Expert Fund file is the one flagged for NPPR-driven EU marketing; check `knowledge/jersey/funds/aifmd-nppr.md` (not read here) before committing to a marketing plan.

## Structuring note

A JPF is typically formed as a **Jersey limited partnership** (the most common PE structure), though a company, unit trust, or cell company are all available — the choice turns on investor tax preferences. It requires a Jersey-licensed administrator acting as the **DSP**, which handles investor CDD/AML and substitutes for direct JFSC oversight (`knowledge/jersey/funds/jersey-private-fund.md`).
