## What changes for a Jersey AIFM marketing into the EU under NPPR

AIFMD II (Directive (EU) 2024/927) takes effect 16 April 2026, but **NPPR access itself is preserved** — Jersey continues to meet the EU's tax-transparency and AML criteria, so the route to EU professional investors stays open. The changes are deltas on top of that route, not a removal of it (`knowledge/frontier/aifmd-ii-april-2026.md`).

**The two directly-applicable changes for a Jersey NPPR AIFM:**

1. **Expanded investor disclosures** — pre-marketing and ongoing disclosure obligations widen to cover liquidity management, sustainability factors (interlocking with SFDR), and loan-originating-fund disclosures where relevant. Expect PPM updates and refreshed transparency reporting.
2. **Enhanced Annex IV reporting** to ESMA via competent authorities — new data fields and more granular reporting on asset categories and risk exposures; systems are being upgraded for this, with first submissions under the new rules expected end-Q2 / early-Q3 2026.

**Indirect but potentially substantial — delegation:** AIFMD II tightens rules on EU AIFM delegation to third countries. An EU AIFM delegating portfolio management to a Jersey entity faces enhanced substance/oversight requirements and cannot become a "letter-box entity." This *reduces* the appeal of the "EU shell marketing + delegate everything to Jersey" model — and, counterintuitively, makes a **direct Jersey AIFM marketing under NPPR comparatively more attractive** than an EU-AIFM-with-CI-delegate structure (`knowledge/frontier/aifmd-ii-april-2026.md`).

**Jersey-side regulatory response:** the JFSC is **splitting its AIF Code of Practice** into an **EU AIF Code** (for NPPR-into-EU marketing, aligned to AIFMD II) and a **UK AIF Code** (for UK marketing). The EU AIF Code is expected to be finalised through 2026, so a Jersey AIFM should expect to comply with the new EU-facing code as it lands (`knowledge/frontier/aifmd-ii-april-2026.md`).

**Pitfalls flagged in the corpus:** don't treat AIFMD II as a small-deltas exercise (loan-originating-fund and substance rules are substantive); don't assume NPPR is wholly unaffected (disclosure and Annex IV have widened); and watch uneven member-state transposition — a fund marketed across several EU states may face different effective requirements (`knowledge/frontier/aifmd-ii-april-2026.md`).

The baseline mechanics of NPPR (JFSC AIFMD-equivalent registration, country-by-country notification, AIFMD-equivalent depository for EU-marketed AIFs, no third-country passport) are in `knowledge/jersey/funds/aifmd-nppr.md`.
