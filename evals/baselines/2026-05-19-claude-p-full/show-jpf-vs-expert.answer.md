For a **club-deal private-equity fund**, the Jersey corpus points clearly at the **Jersey Private Fund (JPF)** rather than the Expert Fund — with one decisive caveat about investor count.

**Why JPF is the default for a club deal**

The JPF concept file explicitly names "**Single-LP** or **club deal** structures" and "**Private equity** funds and **co-investment** vehicles" as the regimes the JPF is *designed for* — they're the first items on the "When to use a JPF" list (`jersey/funds/jersey-private-fund.md`, lines 45–51). The JPF gives you:

- Up to **50 offers / investors** at any time
- **Professional / eligible** investors only
- **~48-hour JFSC fast-track** authorisation
- A reduced-supervision regime where a **Jersey-licensed administrator (DSP)** carries the AML/CDD and books-and-records load on the fund's behalf
- Structural flexibility — typically a **Jersey limited partnership** for a PE/club-deal vehicle, but a company, unit trust, or cell company is available (lines 54–63)

A club deal — a small group of sophisticated co-investors backing one or a small number of deals — sits squarely inside the JPF's design envelope: closed-ended, professional-investor, low headcount, fast to launch.

**When you'd switch to the Expert Fund**

The Expert Fund regime exists for cases the JPF outgrows. The corpus's own comparison table (`jersey/funds/expert-fund.md`, lines 58–64) and its "When to use" list flag two main triggers:

- **Investor count exceeds 50** — the JPF's hard cap. The Expert Fund has "no specific cap" beyond the expert-investor qualification.
- **Wider marketing** to expert investors / family offices / institutions, including **AIFMD NPPR** EU marketing — the Expert Fund permits broader expert-investor distribution, the JPF is limited private placement.

The trade-off is more disclosure (a fuller offering memorandum rather than a light PPM) and a longer authorisation window ("few days to weeks" vs ~48 hours).

**Practical decision for your fund**

- If your club is **≤ 50 professional/eligible investors** and you're not running an active marketing programme into the EU, the **JPF** is the corpus's named default. Most Jersey PE club deals will land here.
- If you expect to **breach 50 investors**, or you need **NPPR marketing into EU member states**, escalate to the **Expert Fund**.
