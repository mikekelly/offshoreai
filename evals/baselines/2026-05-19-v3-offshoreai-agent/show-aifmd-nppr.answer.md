## Marketing a Jersey AIF into Germany under NPPR

Germany is one of the **stricter NPPR jurisdictions** in the EU, but the route is well-trodden for Jersey AIFs. The mechanics break down into two layers: Jersey-side AIFM registration and German-side BaFin notification.

### Layer 1 — Jersey-side prerequisites

Before you can notify any EU member state, the **AIFM must be JFSC-registered** under Jersey's AIFMD-equivalent regime and demonstrate substantive AIFMD-equivalent compliance:

- capital adequacy proportionate to AIFM size
- risk-management framework equivalent to AIFMD Art 15
- liquidity management for open-ended AIFs
- **AIFMD-equivalent depository** arrangements (a "lighter" Jersey depository alone is generally insufficient for EU-marketed AIFs — a combined arrangement is common)
- transparency to investors (annual report, periodic reporting)
- regulatory reporting to JFSC

(`jersey/funds/aifmd-nppr.md`; `jersey/use-cases/fund-counsel/aifmd-nppr-registration.md`)

### Layer 2 — Germany-specific NPPR notification

Germany's NPPR sits under **§330 KAGB** (the *Kapitalanlagegesetzbuch*) and is administered by **BaFin** as the competent authority. The corpus flags Germany as having "strict NPPR rules" with the following Germany-specific features:

- a **local fact-sheet** (Wesentliche Anlegerinformationen-style document) is required;
- a **local distributor** is sometimes required;
- **ongoing reporting** obligations apply after acceptance.

(`jersey/use-cases/fund-counsel/aifmd-nppr-registration.md`)

### Process

1. Confirm AIFMD-equivalent classification of the fund.
2. Complete JFSC AIFM registration in Jersey.
3. Stand up AIFMD-equivalent compliance + EU-grade depository.
4. Prepare the BaFin notification package (Germany-specific documentation).
5. Submit to BaFin; await clearance.
6. Market **only to German professional investors** within the NPPR limits.

### Key caveats

- **No third-country passport.** Full AIFMD passporting for Jersey AIFs was proposed but never implemented — NPPR remains the practical EU route (`jersey/funds/aifmd-nppr.md`).
- **Reverse solicitation is narrow.** If a German investor approaches the AIF unsolicited, no NPPR notification is needed, but BaFin scrutinises these claims and active outreach dressed up as reverse solicitation attracts enforcement risk (`jersey/use-cases/fund-counsel/aifmd-nppr-registration.md`).
- **UK is now a separate stream.** Marketing into the UK uses the UK's own NPPR with the FCA — Brexit split this from EU NPPR (`jersey/funds/aifmd-nppr.md`).
