## Headline

For a **closed-ended PE fund with a global LP base**, the corpus's decision framework points to **Cayman ELP registered under the Private Funds Act 2020** as the default lean (`knowledge/CROSS-JURISDICTIONAL-MAP.md`). Jersey's equivalent product is the **Jersey Private Fund (JPF)**, which is the corpus's preferred lean specifically when EU-marketing via NPPR is a primary requirement.

So the right question isn't "which is better" — it's "which of your facts dominate?" The main factors below tell you which way to lean.

## Factor 1 — Investor base and market familiarity

- **Cayman ELP** is the **dominant closed-ended fund vehicle globally** for PE/VC/RE/credit. Global LPs — US endowments, sovereigns, Asian institutionals, family offices — expect this form, and parallel structures (Delaware LP for US LPs + Cayman ELP for non-US LPs) are routine in mid- and large-cap PE (`knowledge/cayman/exempted-limited-partnership.md`).
- **Jersey JPF / LP** is well-known to European LPs but is not the global default. If your LP base is heavily US/Asian, the friction of explaining a JPF to LPs is non-trivial.

## Factor 2 — EU marketing

This is the strongest pro-Jersey factor. Jersey markets into individual EU member states via **AIFMD National Private Placement Regimes (NPPR)** (`knowledge/jersey/index.md`, funds section). Cayman has **no NPPR equivalent and no EU passport route** (`knowledge/CROSS-JURISDICTIONAL-MAP.md`). If a material slice of your LP base is in NPPR-friendly EU jurisdictions (Netherlands, Luxembourg, Nordics, etc.), Jersey is much easier; for that case the corpus would push you toward a JPF or Expert Fund rather than Cayman.

## Factor 3 — Investor cap

- **JPF: 50 offers/investors maximum** (`knowledge/jersey/funds/jersey-private-fund.md`). If your fund will exceed 50 LPs, you need a Jersey **Expert Fund** instead — which loses the 48-hour authorisation speed (`knowledge/jersey/funds/expert-fund.md`).
- **Cayman ELP under PFA 2020**: no equivalent cap.

For a global institutional PE fund with >50 LPs, this alone often pushes the decision Cayman-ward, or to a Jersey Expert Fund rather than JPF.

## Factor 4 — Speed and regulatory load

- **JPF**: ~48-hour JFSC authorisation; reduced ongoing supervision; a Jersey-licensed **designated service provider (DSP)** substitutes for direct JFSC oversight (`knowledge/jersey/funds/jersey-private-fund.md`).
- **Cayman ELP under PFA**: registration "typically achieved within days"; ongoing CIMA obligations include audited financials, valuation policy, custody/safekeeping, cash monitoring, AML — lighter than EU AIFMD but more substantive than pre-2020 Cayman (`knowledge/cayman/private-funds-act.md`).

Both are fast. JPF has a slight edge on speed and a lighter ongoing footprint *for sub-50-LP structures*.

## Factor 5 — Tax and substance

Both jurisdictions deliver tax-neutral pooling for PE. Differences:

- **Cayman**: no direct tax on the ELP or partners; Economic Substance Act 2018 applies to GP/Investment Manager; **MCIT 2025 (QDMTT) in force** for in-scope groups meeting the EUR 750m Pillar Two threshold (`knowledge/CROSS-JURISDICTIONAL-MAP.md`, `knowledge/cayman/exempted-limited-partnership.md`).
- **Jersey**: zero-ten regime (0% on fund vehicles); Substance Law 2019; **MCIT 2025 (QDMTT + Qualified IIR)** for in-scope groups; no UTPR (`knowledge/jersey/history/finance/trajectory.md`, `knowledge/CROSS-JURISDICTIONAL-MAP.md`).

Pillar Two reaches in-scope GP groups in either jurisdiction — it's not a differentiator unless your sponsor group is below the EUR 750m threshold and you want to keep it that way.

## Factor 6 — Trust law / wrapping considerations

If the fund sits inside a broader wealth-structuring or carry vehicle that needs trust-law protection, Jersey wins decisively (Article 9 firewall, Article 9A reserved powers — `knowledge/CROSS-JURISDICTIONAL-MAP.md`). But for the pure-fund question this is usually a separate decision at a different layer of the structure.

## Bottom line

| Your facts say… | Lean toward |
|---|---|
| Global LP base (US/Asia heavy), >50 LPs, no EU NPPR priority | **Cayman ELP + PFA 2020** |
| EU LPs material; NPPR access matters; ≤50 LPs; speed prized | **Jersey JPF** |
| EU LPs material but >50 LPs | **Jersey Expert Fund** |
| Family-office / club deal, ≤50 sophisticated investors | **Jersey JPF** |
| Carry vehicle that also wants Article 9 firewall wrap | **Jersey** structure |

## Caveats

- The `cayman/exempted-limited-partnership.md`, `cayman/private-funds-act.md`, `jersey/funds/jersey-private-fund.md` and `jersey/funds/expert-fund.md` files are all status `draft` in the corpus. Treat the comparative framing as reliable but verify specific numerical thresholds (fee levels, exact PFA carve-out conditions, expert-investor thresholds) against the primary statutes before relying on them.
