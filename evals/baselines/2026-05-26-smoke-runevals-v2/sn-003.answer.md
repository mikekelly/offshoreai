I'll pull the two relevant corpus files in parallel.## The Jersey Private Fund (JPF)

The **Jersey Private Fund** is Jersey's light-touch private-fund regime for closed, sophisticated-investor structures. Headline features: **up to 50 offers/investors**, professional/eligible investors only, **fast-track JFSC authorisation in ~48 hours**, reduced ongoing supervision, and a **JFSC-regulated administrator** acting as the **designated service provider (DSP)** that carries CDD/AML, books and records, and substitutes for direct JFSC oversight of the fund itself. Structurally flexible — Jersey company (often no-par-value), limited partnership (most common), unit trust, or PCC/ICC cell company. Typical uses: PE funds, co-investment vehicles, closed-ended real estate, family-and-friends partnerships, single-LP/club deals, and carry vehicles (`knowledge/jersey/funds/jersey-private-fund.md`).

## How an Expert Fund differs

The **Expert Fund** is aimed at **expert investors** — defined by net-worth/investment-threshold criteria (typically a US$100,000 minimum commitment, prescribed net-asset threshold, professional clients, or institutional investors). Compared with the JPF:

| Feature | JPF | Expert Fund |
|---|---|---|
| Investor cap | 50 | No specific cap (subject to expert criteria) |
| Investor qualification | Professional / eligible | Expert (net-worth/institutional) |
| Authorisation time | ~48 hours | Few days to weeks |
| Disclosure document | Light PPM | More detailed offering memorandum |
| Marketing scope | Limited private placement | Wider expert-investor marketing |

So the practical differences are scale (no hard 50-investor cap), investor qualification (Expert > eligible), more disclosure, slower authorisation, and broader marketing — including AIFMD NPPR routes into the EU. Use an Expert Fund when you need more than 50 investors, are running a single-strategy hedge fund with multiple expert LPs, a larger PE vehicle, or need NPPR-compliant EU marketing; otherwise the JPF is the default Jersey private-fund vehicle (`knowledge/jersey/funds/expert-fund.md`).
