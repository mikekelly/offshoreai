## JPF — almost certainly the right call for a club deal

For a **club-deal PE fund**, the **Jersey Private Fund (JPF)** is the default vehicle. The Expert Fund is the alternative only if you outgrow the JPF's investor cap or need broader marketing latitude.

### Why JPF fits a club deal

- **Investor count fits the structure.** Club deals are by definition a small group of co-investing professional/institutional LPs — well within the JPF's **cap of 50 offers/investors** (`knowledge/jersey/funds/jersey-private-fund.md`).
- **Speed.** Fast-track JFSC authorisation, **typically within 48 hours** of application — material when assembling a club around a specific deal opportunity with a closing window.
- **Investor qualification matches.** JPFs require **professional / eligible** investors only, which is the standard club-deal LP profile.
- **Structuring flexibility.** Can be a **Jersey limited partnership** (the most common PE structure), a no-par-value private company, a unit trust, or a PCC/ICC — pick whichever suits LP tax preferences.
- **Light-touch ongoing supervision** via the **designated service provider (DSP)** substitution model — a Jersey-licensed administrator carries CDD/AML and is JFSC-supervised under FSB registration, which substitutes for direct JFSC oversight of the fund.

The JPF file lists **"club deal structures"** as an explicit use case alongside PE funds and co-investment vehicles.

### When you should pivot to Expert Fund instead

The Expert Fund is appropriate where:

- The club exceeds **50 investors** (Expert Fund has no specific cap, subject to expert-investor criteria);
- You need **wider expert-investor marketing** rather than limited private placement;
- The vehicle is sized into the **AIFMD NPPR** route for EU marketing where a more detailed offering memorandum is wanted.

Trade-offs: longer authorisation (**days to weeks** vs ~48 hours), more detailed offering memorandum, and a higher investor-qualification bar (**expert** — typically US$100k minimum or net-asset thresholds) vs JPF's professional/eligible standard (`knowledge/jersey/funds/expert-fund.md`).

### Practical recommendation

For a club deal — small group of sophisticated co-investors around a defined opportunity — go **JPF as a Jersey limited partnership**, with a Jersey-licensed administrator as DSP. Reserve the Expert Fund for the case where investor count, marketing scope, or NPPR posture genuinely requires it.
