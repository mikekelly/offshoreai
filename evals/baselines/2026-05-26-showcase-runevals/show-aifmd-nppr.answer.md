## The route at a glance

Marketing a Jersey AIF into Germany goes through the **National Private Placement Regime (NPPR)** — country-by-country private-placement notification — because Jersey is non-EU and the third-country AIFMD passport was proposed but never implemented (`knowledge/jersey/funds/aifmd-nppr.md`).

## Step-by-step

1. **JFSC AIFM registration.** The Jersey AIFM must be registered under Jersey's **AIFMD-equivalent** regime, with capital adequacy, risk-management framework (equivalent to AIFMD Art 15), liquidity management for open-ended AIFs, AIFMD-equivalent depository arrangements, investor transparency, and JFSC regulatory reporting in place (`knowledge/jersey/use-cases/fund-counsel/aifmd-nppr-registration.md`).
2. **Confirm AIFMD-equivalent classification** of the fund and its compliance infrastructure (`knowledge/jersey/use-cases/fund-counsel/aifmd-nppr-registration.md`).
3. **Notify BaFin** — Germany's competent authority — under the German **§330 KAGB** NPPR rules. Germany is one of the **stricter** member states, alongside France (`knowledge/jersey/use-cases/fund-counsel/aifmd-nppr-registration.md`; `knowledge/jersey/funds/aifmd-nppr.md`).
4. **Germany-specific requirements:**
   - A **local fact-sheet** is required;
   - A **local distributor** is sometimes required;
   - **Ongoing reporting** obligations apply after acceptance (`knowledge/jersey/use-cases/fund-counsel/aifmd-nppr-registration.md`).
5. **Market only to professional investors** in Germany once cleared.

## Depository note

A Jersey AIF marketed to EU investors via NPPR typically needs **AIFMD-equivalent depository** arrangements (custody, oversight, cash-flow monitoring). Jersey allows a "lighter" depository for non-EU-marketed funds, but for EU NPPR marketing the full AIFMD-equivalent depository must be in place (`knowledge/jersey/funds/aifmd-nppr.md`; `knowledge/jersey/use-cases/fund-counsel/aifmd-nppr-registration.md`).

## Reverse solicitation

If a German investor approaches the AIF **unsolicited**, NPPR is not triggered — but the documentation has to genuinely demonstrate reverse solicitation; BaFin scrutinises overclaims and active outreach disguised as reverse solicitation attracts penalties (`knowledge/jersey/use-cases/fund-counsel/aifmd-nppr-registration.md`).

## AIFMD II — what changed from 16 April 2026

AIFMD II (Directive (EU) 2024/927) preserves Crown Dependencies' NPPR access but widens two things you'll feel in a German notification:

- **Expanded investor disclosures** (pre-investment and ongoing) covering liquidity management, sustainability factors, and loan-originating-fund specifics;
- **Enhanced Annex IV reporting** to ESMA via member-state authorities, with new data fields.

Germany is one of the **stricter transposers** of AIFMD II — expect tighter effective requirements than in Luxembourg or Ireland. Jersey's AIF Code is being **bifurcated** into an EU AIF Code (aligned with AIFMD II) and a UK AIF Code; the EU AIF Code is expected to finalise through 2026 (`knowledge/frontier/aifmd-ii-april-2026.md`).
