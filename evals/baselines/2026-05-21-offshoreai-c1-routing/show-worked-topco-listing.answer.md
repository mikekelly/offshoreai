## End-state structure

```
Public investors → [Jersey TopCo] (listed on LSE) → [UK Opco]
```

A Jersey holding company is inserted above your UK operating business; the public lists *the Jersey TopCo*, which owns 100% of the UK Opco.

## Step by step

**1. Incorporate the Jersey TopCo.** A public company limited by shares under the **Companies (Jersey) Law 1991** — typically 1–3 days to incorporate. You need at least two directors, a Jersey-resident registered office / corporate-services provider, IPO-ready memorandum and articles, and beneficial-ownership information lodged with the Registry (private register, not public). (`knowledge/jersey/companies/companies-law-1991.md`)

**2. Share-for-share exchange.** UK Opco shareholders exchange their UK shares for new TopCo shares (1:1 or another ratio), leaving TopCo owning 100% of the UK Opco. UK CGT on the exchange is generally deferred by **share-for-share rollover relief under TCGA 1992 s.135** (subject to anti-avoidance); **0.5% UK stamp duty** may arise on the transfer of UK shares (reliefs may apply for genuine reorganisations); no Jersey tax arises (no Jersey CGT). (`knowledge/jersey/use-cases/founder-entrepreneur/migration-pre-ipo.md`) — *the UK tax points here are UK law, outside the Jersey corpus; verify with UK tax counsel.*

**3. TopCo Jersey tax position.** Incorporated and managed-and-controlled in Jersey, the TopCo is Jersey-resident. Under the **zero-ten** regime (Income Tax (Jersey) Law 1961) a holding company pays the **0% standard rate** (10% applies only to financial-services companies; 20% to utility / property / quarrying / cannabis income). (`knowledge/jersey/tax/zero-ten.md`)

**4. Economic substance.** Under the **Taxation (Companies — Economic Substance) (Jersey) Law 2019**, a pure equity-holding company faces a **reduced substance test** — adequate people and premises in Jersey, but a lower bar than IP or financing/leasing companies. Pure-paper presence does not satisfy it. (`knowledge/jersey/tax/economic-substance.md`)

**5. Pillar Two.** If the consolidated group's annual revenue is **≥ EUR 750m**, Jersey's **MCIT (effective 1 January 2025)** applies a **QDMTT** topping the TopCo's Jersey income up to **15%**, keeping the top-up in Jersey rather than ceding it under another jurisdiction's IIR; UK Pillar Two runs in parallel. Below the threshold, Pillar Two doesn't engage and the 0% position continues. (`knowledge/jersey/international/pillar-two-mcit.md`)

**6. Capital structure.** CJL 1991 gives IPO-useful flexibility: **no-par-value shares** (standard), **multiple share classes** (founder / investor / public, including dual-class super-voting), **treasury shares**, **redeemable shares**, and **buy-backs**; capital reductions by special resolution + solvency statement (less court-intensive than the UK). (`knowledge/jersey/companies/companies-law-1991.md`)

**7. LSE listing.** Jersey companies are routinely accepted across the LSE segments. **UK Listing Rules** apply (with the DTRs, MAR, and Prospectus Regulation), and the **UK Takeover Code** applies to a Jersey-incorporated company with securities on a UK regulated market. A prospectus (or AIM admission document) is required, with Jersey legal opinions and corporate authorities in the disclosure; Jersey advocates work alongside UK securities counsel. (`knowledge/jersey/use-cases/founder-entrepreneur/listing-rule-overlay.md`)

**8. Post-IPO.** The **UK Corporate Governance Code** applies; the JFSC does not regulate the TopCo unless it carries on regulated business; Registry filings (annual return, beneficial ownership) continue. **Distributions** are made on a **solvency basis** under CJL 1991 (no retained-earnings test), with **0% Jersey withholding** on dividends to non-resident shareholders. (`knowledge/jersey/companies/companies-law-1991.md`)

## Main pitfalls
Stamp-duty leakage on the exchange if not structured for relief; keeping the TopCo's tax residence genuinely Jersey-based (real board decision-making in Jersey, to avoid UK central-management-and-control residence); Pillar Two if the group is in scope; FCA prospectus / sponsor / market-abuse requirements; and real (not paper) substance at TopCo level.
