## The route in outline

For a Jersey AIF marketed to German professional investors, the path is **Jersey AIFMD-equivalent registration → BaFin NPPR notification under §330 KAGB → marketing within NPPR limits**. There is no EU-wide passport available to Jersey AIFMs; the proposed third-country passport extension was never implemented, so country-by-country NPPR remains the practical route. [`jersey/funds/aifmd-nppr.md`](jersey/funds/aifmd-nppr.md); [`jersey/use-cases/fund-counsel/aifmd-nppr-registration.md`](jersey/use-cases/fund-counsel/aifmd-nppr-registration.md)

## Step 1 — Jersey-side AIFMD-equivalent registration

The AIFM must register with the JFSC under Jersey's AIFMD-equivalent regime and demonstrate substantive equivalence on:

- capital adequacy proportionate to AIFM size
- risk-management framework equivalent to AIFMD Art 15
- liquidity management (for open-ended AIFs)
- depository arrangements (AIFMD-equivalent for EU-marketed AIFs — Jersey allows a lighter depository for non-EU-marketed funds, but EU marketing via NPPR requires the full equivalent)
- transparency to investors (annual report, periodic reporting)
- regulatory reporting to JFSC

[`jersey/use-cases/fund-counsel/aifmd-nppr-registration.md`](jersey/use-cases/fund-counsel/aifmd-nppr-registration.md)

## Step 2 — German NPPR notification (BaFin, §330 KAGB)

Germany is one of the stricter NPPR jurisdictions. The corpus flags the following Germany-specific requirements:

- notification to **BaFin** under **§330 KAGB**
- a **local fact-sheet** required
- a **local distributor** sometimes required
- post-acceptance **reporting obligations**
- BaFin must recognise the Jersey AIFM regime as equivalent before clearance

[`jersey/use-cases/fund-counsel/aifmd-nppr-registration.md`](jersey/use-cases/fund-counsel/aifmd-nppr-registration.md)

## Step 3 — Marketing within NPPR limits

Once cleared, the AIF can be actively marketed **to German professional investors** only (not retail). Two adjacent points worth flagging:

- **Reverse solicitation** doesn't trigger NPPR, but BaFin scrutinises reverse-solicitation claims; active outreach disguised as reverse solicitation attracts penalties. Documentation must support genuine unsolicited approach.
- **UK marketing is a separate stream** post-Brexit (UK NPPR via FCA), so if marketing extends to the UK as well, expect two separate notification packages.

[`jersey/use-cases/fund-counsel/aifmd-nppr-registration.md`](jersey/use-cases/fund-counsel/aifmd-nppr-registration.md)
