Jersey accesses EU markets through each member state's **National Private Placement Regime (NPPR)** — there is no EU-wide passport available to Jersey AIFMs. For Germany specifically, the route is:

1. **Register the AIFM with the JFSC** under Jersey's AIFMD-equivalent regime and demonstrate substantive AIFMD-equivalent compliance (capital adequacy, risk management, conflicts of interest, depository arrangements, transparency to investors, regulatory reporting).
2. **Notify the AIF to BaFin** (Germany's regulator) under the German NPPR before marketing to German professional investors.
3. **Provide AIFMD-equivalent depository arrangements** — Jersey permits lighter depository functions for non-EU-marketed funds, but EU-marketed AIFs under NPPR require the full AIFMD-equivalent depository.

Marketing is restricted to **professional investors**, country-by-country — a German notification does not extend to other member states. ([`knowledge/jersey/funds/aifmd-nppr.md`](knowledge/jersey/funds/aifmd-nppr.md))

**Germany is one of the stricter NPPR jurisdictions.** The corpus flags Germany and France as having stricter NPPR rules than some other member states, so budget for additional German-specific requirements (BaFin notification detail, German depository expectations) beyond the baseline. ([`knowledge/jersey/funds/aifmd-nppr.md`](knowledge/jersey/funds/aifmd-nppr.md))

The **Expert Fund** is the common Jersey vehicle used for AIFMD-NPPR marketing. ([`knowledge/jersey/funds/aifmd-nppr.md`](knowledge/jersey/funds/aifmd-nppr.md))

### What changed in April 2026 — read this before you proceed

NPPR access **survives** AIFMD II (Directive (EU) 2024/927, most requirements effective **16 April 2026**), but the conditions tightened:

- **Expanded investor disclosures** — pre-marketing and ongoing disclosure obligations widened (liquidity management, sustainability/SFDR interaction, loan-originating-fund disclosures where applicable).
- **Enhanced Annex IV reporting** to ESMA via the competent authority — new data fields and more granular asset/risk reporting.
- **Germany is expected to transpose AIFMD II strictly** (alongside France), so the German disclosure and reporting requirements may be at the tighter end. ([`knowledge/frontier/aifmd-ii-april-2026.md`](knowledge/frontier/aifmd-ii-april-2026.md))

Jersey is also **splitting its AIF Code of Practice** into an **EU AIF Code** (AIFMD II-aligned, for NPPR marketing) and a **UK AIF Code** — the EU AIF Code is expected to be finalised through 2026, so the precise applicable Jersey code is in flux. ([`knowledge/frontier/aifmd-ii-april-2026.md`](knowledge/frontier/aifmd-ii-april-2026.md))
