# Isle of Man Funds — Overview

## Principal legislation

IoM funds are governed by:

- **Collective Investment Schemes Act 2008** — principal
  funds statute;
- **Specific** FSA Rules and Notes;
- **Companies Acts** for corporate fund vehicles;
- **Limited Partnerships Act** for LP vehicles.

## Fund categories

### Authorised Schemes

- **Retail** open-ended funds;
- **UCITS-equivalent**;
- **Highest** regulatory standards.

### Regulated Funds

- **Sophisticated investor** schemes;
- **Lighter** retail-protection regime;
- **Specific** investment freedoms.

### Specialist Fund

- **Specialist / sophisticated** investors only;
- **Wider** investment freedoms;
- **Reduced** regulation;
- **Similar to** Jersey Expert Fund.

### Qualifying Fund

- **Closed-circle** sophisticated investors;
- **Light** FSA scrutiny;
- **Faster** establishment;
- **Similar to** Jersey Private Fund.

### Listed Funds

- **Listed** on TISE or other recognised exchanges;
- **Closed-ended** typically;
- **Specific** rules.

## Comparison with Jersey

| Feature | Jersey | IoM |
|---|---|---|
| Public funds | CIFL 1988 | Authorised Schemes |
| Expert funds | Expert Fund Guide | Specialist Fund |
| Private funds | JPF | Qualifying Fund |
| Listed funds | Listed Fund Guide | Listed Schemes |
| Unregulated | UEIF, UETF | Limited equivalent |
| AIFMD | NPPR | NPPR |

Both jurisdictions are **competitive fund centres**. Specific
suitability varies by structure and target market.

## Service providers

IoM funds use:

- **Manager** (FSA-licensed);
- **Administrator**;
- **Custodian / depositary**;
- **Auditor**;
- **Specific** FSA-licensed providers.

## AIFMD / cross-border

- **No EU passporting** (IoM outside EU);
- **NPPR** for EU distribution;
- **FSA** opt-in AIFMD regime for EU-compatible managers.

## Pension fund speciality

IoM has a particular specialty in:

- **Pension** vehicle structuring;
- **International** pension schemes;
- **Specific** legislation for pensions;
- **Tax-advantaged** pension products.

## Substance

- **In-scope** for IoM Substance Law (mirror of Jersey
  Substance Law 2019);
- **Manager** typically substance-relevant;
- **Annual** substance return.
