# Isle of Man — Overview

## What the Isle of Man is

The **Isle of Man (IoM)** is a self-governing British Crown
Dependency located in the **Irish Sea**, between Great
Britain and Ireland:

- **Crown Dependency** — not part of UK, not part of EU;
- **Self-governing** for domestic matters;
- **UK Government** acts for defence and external relations;
- **Privy Council** as final appeal court;
- **Tynwald** as legislature (claims to be the world's
  oldest continuous parliament — 1,000+ years).

## Population and economy

- **Population**: approximately **85,000**;
- **Capital**: **Douglas**;
- **Currency**: **Manx pound** (par with GBP);
- **Economy**: dominated by **financial services**, **online
  gaming**, **insurance** (captive), **manufacturing** (niche
  technology / aerospace).

## Constitutional / political

The IoM:

- Has its own **legislature** (Tynwald);
- Has its own **judiciary**;
- Has its own **financial services regulator** (FSA);
- Has its own **tax system**;
- Has **historic** Norse / Celtic constitutional roots;
- **Operates** under a similar Crown-Dependency framework to
  Jersey / Guernsey but with distinct features.

## Tynwald

The **Tynwald** is the IoM's tripartite legislature:

- **House of Keys** (24 elected members);
- **Legislative Council** (11 members — President, Bishop,
  Attorney General, 8 elected by House of Keys);
- **Tynwald Court** (joint sitting);
- **Tynwald Day** ceremony at St John's annually;
- **Constitutional** continuity claim back over 1,000
  years.

## Financial-services sector

IoM is a **major offshore financial centre**:

- **Banking**: smaller than Jersey but significant;
- **Insurance**: substantial **captive** and **life
  insurance** market;
- **Funds**: established fund-services industry;
- **Trusts**: well-developed trust regime;
- **eGaming**: leading online-gaming jurisdiction (specific
  IoM speciality);
- **TCB**: similar to Jersey / Guernsey.

## Regulator

The **Isle of Man Financial Services Authority (FSA)**:

- **Independent** statutory regulator;
- **Successor** to the Financial Supervision Commission and
  Insurance and Pensions Authority (merged 2015);
- **Regulates** financial services including insurance,
  funds, banking, fiduciary;
- **Similar role** to Jersey JFSC / Guernsey GFSC.

## Distinctive IoM features

### Online gaming (eGaming)

- **Leading** online gaming jurisdiction;
- **Online Gambling Regulation Act 2001** plus subsequent
  legislation;
- **Gambling Supervision Commission** as regulator;
- **Major** revenue source and reputation driver.

### Insurance

- **Substantial** captive market (smaller than Guernsey but
  significant);
- **Specific** life insurance specialty (long-term wealth
  protection products);
- **Different** Solvency II equivalent regime.

### Tax (zero rate as default)

- IoM operates a similar **zero-corporate-tax** regime;
- **Some specifics** different from Jersey / Guernsey
  zero-ten.

### Pension funds

- **Pension** structures specialty;
- **Specific** IoM pension legislation.

## Comparison with Jersey

| Feature | Jersey | Isle of Man |
|---|---|---|
| Status | Crown Dependency | Crown Dependency |
| Population | ~107k | ~85k |
| Legislature | States Assembly | Tynwald |
| Banking | Larger | Smaller |
| Captives | Smaller | Significant |
| eGaming | Limited | **Major specialty** |
| Tax | Zero-ten with GST | Zero corporate (similar structure); VAT shared with UK |
| Regulator | JFSC | FSA |
| Customary law | Norman | Manx / Norse |
| Currency | Jersey pound | Manx pound |

## VAT / Customs

A **key difference** from Jersey:

- **IoM is in a VAT and Customs union with the UK**;
- IoM applies **UK VAT** (currently 20%) on goods and
  services;
- Different from Jersey's **5% GST**;
- Different from Guernsey's **no GST / VAT**;
- Significant tax structural difference for goods / services
  consumption.
