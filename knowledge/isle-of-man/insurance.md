# Isle of Man Insurance — Overview

## IoM as insurance jurisdiction

The Isle of Man is a **significant insurance jurisdiction**:

- **Established** captive market;
- **Specialty** in **life insurance** for wealth protection;
- **International** insurance hub;
- **FSA** as regulator;
- **Smaller** than Guernsey captive market but distinctive
  features.

## Principal legislation

- **Insurance Act 2008** — principal insurance statute;
- **Insurance Companies (Amendment) Regulations** — specific
  rules;
- **FSA Insurance Rule Book** — detailed regulation;
- **Captive Insurance** Companies Regulations.

## Categories

### Class 1 — Domestic insurance

- **IoM-resident** policyholders;
- **Domestic** market;
- **Higher** consumer protection.

### Class 2 — International / general

- **Non-IoM** policyholders;
- **General insurance** including captives;
- **Lower** retail consumer protection.

### Class 3 — Long-term / life

- **Life insurance** business;
- **IoM specialty**;
- **Specific** regulatory regime.

## Life insurance specialty

IoM has a **distinctive specialty** in:

- **Wealth-protection** life policies;
- **Inheritance-tax-planning** products;
- **International** unit-linked life;
- **Specific** products designed for non-IoM-resident
  policyholders;
- **Significant** premium income.

Life insurance is a **major** IoM industry, larger than
Jersey life insurance market.

## Captive insurance

- **Established** captive market;
- **Smaller** than Guernsey but significant;
- **Cell company** structures available;
- **Specific** captive Rules;
- **Range** of captive types (pure, sponsored, etc.).

## Cell companies

IoM permits **Protected Cell Companies (PCC)**:

- **Adopted** the concept from offshore peers;
- **Used** for insurance (especially captives);
- **Specific** IoM PCC Regulations.

## Regulator

The **Isle of Man Financial Services Authority (FSA)**:

- **Successor** to Insurance and Pensions Authority (merged
  2015);
- **Comprehensive** insurance supervision;
- **Different** from Jersey JFSC structurally but
  substantively aligned.

## Comparison with Jersey insurance

| Feature | Jersey | IoM |
|---|---|---|
| Statute | IBL 1996 | Insurance Act 2008 |
| Categories | Category A / B | Class 1 / 2 / 3 |
| Captive market | Smaller | Significant |
| Life market | Smaller | **Major specialty** |
| PCC | Yes | Yes |
| Regulator | JFSC | FSA |

## Why IoM for life insurance

- **Established** life-insurance industry;
- **Specialist** wealth-protection products;
- **Specific** tax / regulatory advantages for life products;
- **Major** international distribution.

## Why IoM for captive

- **Smaller market** than Guernsey but established;
- **Specific** UK customs union (VAT advantages in some
  configurations);
- **Specific** existing IoM advisory relationships;
- **Cost** considerations.
