# Isle of Man Companies — Overview

## Two parallel regimes

The IoM operates **two parallel** company-law regimes:

### Companies Act 1931 ("traditional")

- **Older** structure;
- **More** detailed requirements;
- **More** Companies House-style filings;
- **Familiar** to UK / common-law lawyers.

### Companies Act 2006 ("modern")

- **Simpler** structure;
- **Reduced** filings;
- **More flexible**;
- **Modelled** in part on BVI and similar offshore regimes;
- **Faster** to incorporate.

Companies can choose which Act they incorporate under. Both
remain in force in parallel.

## Company types

### Companies limited by shares

The standard vehicle in both Acts:

- **Limited liability** of shareholders;
- **Share capital** (par or no-par);
- **Standard** governance.

### Companies limited by guarantee

- **No share capital**;
- **Members guarantee** on winding-up;
- **Used** for non-profits.

### Protected Cell Companies (PCC)

- **Originated in offshore** broadly (Guernsey 1997);
- **IoM PCC Regulations** allow PCCs;
- **Used** for insurance, structured products, fund umbrella.

### Limited Liability Companies (LLC)

- **Specific** IoM provision;
- **US-style LLC** features (member-managed);
- **Specific** uses.

## Key features

### Share capital

- **Par-value or no-par-value**;
- **Different** specific rules between 1931 and 2006 Acts;
- **No** minimum capital requirements typically.

### Directors

- **At least one** director;
- **Standard** fiduciary duties;
- **Specific** IoM provisions on duties;
- **No** specific Jersey-style Article 74 framework.

### Constitutional documents

- **Memorandum** (1931 Act);
- **Articles of Association**;
- **Specific** simplified options under 2006 Act.

### Capital maintenance

- **Solvency-based** test for distributions;
- **Specific** IoM tests under each Act;
- **Similar** in principle to Jersey but different in
  specifics.

### Migration / continuance

- **Continuance in** to IoM from other jurisdictions;
- **Continuance out** to other jurisdictions;
- **Specific** IoM procedures;
- **Cross-jurisdictional** mobility supported.

### Registration

- **IoM Companies Registry**;
- **Online** registration system;
- **Public register**;
- **Specific** filing requirements per Act.

## Comparison with Jersey

| Feature | Jersey CJL 1991 | IoM 1931 Act | IoM 2006 Act |
|---|---|---|---|
| Share types | Par / no-par | Par / no-par | Par / no-par |
| Cell companies | PCC and ICC | PCC under specific regs | Cell structures |
| Director duties | Article 74 | Common-law plus statute | Specific 2006 Act provisions |
| Filings | Annual return | Detailed annual filings | Reduced filings |
| Incorporation speed | Days to weeks | Days to weeks | Faster |

## When to use IoM companies

Common scenarios:

- **eGaming** companies (IoM speciality);
- **Insurance** companies (especially captive / life);
- **Specific** holding structures;
- **2006 Act** preferred for simpler structures;
- **1931 Act** preferred for more traditional structures.

## Insolvency

IoM company insolvency:

- **Companies (Winding Up) Acts** (multiple);
- **Specific** IoM procedure;
- **Different** from Jersey désastre;
- **Court-supervised** winding-up.
