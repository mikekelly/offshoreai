# Isle of Man Financial Regulation — Overview

## Regulatory architecture

IoM regulation rests on:

- **FSA** as principal financial-services regulator;
- **Gambling Supervision Commission** for online gaming;
- **Multiple statutes** for specific sectors;
- **Rule Book** of detailed rules;
- **AML / CFT** framework.

## Principal statutes

### Banking

- **Banking Act 1998** and related;
- **Basel III** alignment.

### Insurance

- **Insurance Act 2008** — comprehensive insurance
  legislation.

### Investment business

- **Financial Services Act 2008** — investment business,
  funds, advisers.

### Fiduciary services

- **Financial Services Act 2008** plus specific
  legislation;
- **TCB-equivalent** activities.

### AML / CFT

- **Proceeds of Crime Act 2008**;
- **AML / CFT Code 2019**;
- **FSA AML / CFT Handbook**;
- **Equivalent in approach to Jersey POCL / MLO**.

### eGaming

- **Online Gambling Regulation Act 2001**;
- **Gambling Supervision Commission** (separate from FSA).

## Comparison with Jersey / Guernsey

| Aspect | Jersey | Guernsey | IoM |
|---|---|---|---|
| Umbrella | FSL 1998 | Multiple sectoral | Financial Services Act 2008 plus sectoral |
| Insurance | IBL 1996 | Insurance Business Law 2002 | Insurance Act 2008 |
| Trust regulation | FSL 1998 Article 9 | RFCB Law 2000 | Financial Services Act 2008 |
| Funds | CIFL 1988 | PoIL 2020 | Financial Services Act 2008 |
| Regulator | JFSC | GFSC | FSA |
| eGaming | Limited | Limited | **GSC separate** |

## Authorisation process

Common features across IoM regulated activities:

- **Application** to FSA;
- **Fit-and-proper** assessment;
- **Capital** requirements;
- **Systems and controls**;
- **Local presence**;
- **Ongoing** requirements;
- **Specific** Rule Book provisions.

## Enforcement

FSA tools:

- **Civil penalties**;
- **Public statements**;
- **Conditions** on licence;
- **Suspension** of licence;
- **Revocation** of licence;
- **Criminal** prosecution for serious offences.

## Cooperation

The FSA cooperates with:

- **JFSC**, **GFSC** — sister Crown Dependencies;
- **UK FCA / PRA**;
- **EU** regulators selectively;
- **Foreign regulators** through MoUs;
- **IOSCO, IAIS, FATF** international participation.

## Industry bodies

Key IoM industry:

- **Isle of Man Finance** — sector promotion;
- **Isle of Man Bankers Association**;
- **Isle of Man Insurance Association**;
- **Isle of Man Funds Association**;
- **eGaming Association**;
- **Society of Trust and Estate Practitioners (STEP)** —
  IoM chapter.
