# Isle of Man Tax — Overview

## Principal statute

The **Income Tax Act 1970** (Isle of Man) is the principal
tax statute, with extensive amendments.

## Corporate tax regime

IoM operates a **zero-rated corporate tax** system:

- **0%** — most companies (the general rate);
- **10%** — banking business;
- **10%** — land / property income from IoM;
- **20%** — specific other categories (some specific
  sectors).

The regime is **broadly aligned** with Jersey / Guernsey
zero-ten but with **specific differences**:

- **Banking** rate (10%) — same as Jersey;
- **Most other** corporate income at **0%** — similar but
  some specific Jersey 10% categories at 0% in IoM (or
  vice versa).

## Personal tax

### Income tax bands

- **10%** on first portion (specific threshold);
- **20%** on remainder;
- **Specific** allowances and deductions.

### Tax cap

IoM has a **personal tax cap**:

- **Maximum** tax liability per person per year
  (currently around **£200,000**, periodically reviewed);
- **Attractive** for very high earners;
- **Specific** rules on residence and structure;
- **Jersey** does not have an equivalent cap in the same
  form (HVR has different structure).

This is a **distinctive** IoM offering for high-net-worth
individuals.

## VAT (not GST)

A **major structural difference** from Jersey:

- **IoM in customs and VAT union** with the UK;
- **UK VAT rate (20%)** applies;
- **No** separate IoM VAT regime;
- **Different from** Jersey 5% GST or Guernsey's no-GST.

For businesses:

- **VAT recoverable** on business inputs (like UK businesses);
- **Cross-border** VAT planning relevant;
- **Specific** IoM-UK customs / VAT documentation.

## No other major taxes

Like Jersey / Guernsey:

- **No** capital gains tax;
- **No** inheritance tax;
- **No** wealth tax.

## Substance Law

IoM has equivalent **economic substance** legislation:

- **Aligned** with Jersey / Guernsey approach;
- **Same** activity categories;
- **Substance** requirements;
- **Annual** substance return.

## Pillar Two

IoM implements **Pillar Two** equivalent:

- **Multinational Top-up Tax** or similar mechanism;
- **15%** minimum effective rate for €750m+ groups;
- **Aligned** with OECD GloBE rules;
- **Coordinated** with Jersey / Guernsey approaches.

## CRS / FATCA

- **CRS** implementation;
- **FATCA IGA** with US;
- **CbCR** for in-scope groups;
- **Aligned** transparency standards.

## Tax treaties

IoM has:

- **Specific** bilateral tax treaties;
- **TIEAs** with multiple jurisdictions;
- **DTAs** with growing number of countries;
- **MLI** signatory.

## Specific IoM features

### Personal residence

- **Specific** IoM residence rules;
- **Attractive** for high-earners (tax cap);
- **Specific** statutory tests for residence.

### Pension tax

- **Tax-efficient** pension products specifically designed in
  IoM;
- **Specific** legislation;
- **International** distribution.

### Life insurance products

- **Tax** characteristics of life policies;
- **Specific** wealth-protection products;
- **International** distribution.

## Comparison with Jersey

| Feature | Jersey | IoM |
|---|---|---|
| Corporate (general) | 0% | 0% |
| Corporate (banking) | 10% | 10% |
| Corporate (specific) | 20% | 10% (land); various |
| Personal (general) | 20% | 10%/20% |
| Personal cap | None (HVR has structure) | £200k+ |
| GST / VAT | 5% GST | 20% VAT (UK customs union) |
| Pillar Two | MCIT | Equivalent |
| Substance | 2019 | Equivalent |
