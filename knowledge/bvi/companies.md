# BVI Companies — Overview

## Principal statute

The **BVI Business Companies Act 2004** is the principal
company-law statute.

## Company types

### BVI Business Company (BC)

The **dominant** vehicle — see [`business-company.md`](./business-company.md).

### Company limited by guarantee

- **No share capital**;
- **Members guarantee** on winding-up;
- **Used** for non-profits, social enterprises.

### Restricted purpose company

- **Specific** purpose stated in memorandum;
- **Subject to** specific BVI rules;
- **Used** for orphan SPVs, certain trust contexts.

### Segregated portfolio company (SPC)

- **BVI version** of cell company;
- **Cell-style** segregation;
- **Used** for insurance, fund umbrella;
- **Less prevalent** than Cayman SPC.

## Other vehicles

### BVI Limited Partnership

- **Limited Partnership Act**;
- **GP / LP** structure;
- **Used** for funds and joint ventures.

### Specific partnerships

- **General partnerships** under common law;
- **Limited Liability Partnerships (LLP)** under specific
  Act.

## Key features common to BVI vehicles

### Speed and cost

- **Hours** to incorporate;
- **Low** government fees;
- **Standard** form documentation;
- **Online** processes.

### Registered agent

- **Mandatory** for BCs;
- **BVI-licensed** registered agent;
- **Maintains** BO information;
- **Handles** filings and statutory requirements.

### Capital flexibility

- **No** minimum capital requirements;
- **Various** share classes;
- **Specific** redemption rules.

### Directors

- **At least one**;
- **Foreign** directors permitted;
- **Corporate** directors permitted (subject to specific
  rules);
- **Standard** fiduciary duties.

## Insolvency

BVI company insolvency:

- **Insolvency Act 2003** governs;
- **Specific** liquidation procedures;
- **Court-supervised** in contested cases;
- **Cross-border** cooperation through specific arrangements;
- **Recognition** of foreign insolvency proceedings.

## Comparison with Jersey

| Feature | Jersey CJL 1991 | BVI BC Act 2004 |
|---|---|---|
| Speed | Days to weeks | **Hours** |
| Cost | Higher | **Lower** |
| Number of companies | ~30k | **400,000+** |
| Sophistication | Higher per company | Volume |
| Filings | Annual return | Lighter |
| Director duties | Article 74 framework | BC Act provisions |
| Cell companies | PCC, ICC | SPC (less prevalent) |

## When to use BVI companies

Common scenarios:

- **Holding** companies for international investments;
- **SPV** in M&A or financing;
- **Standard** offshore vehicle;
- **Volume** company formation;
- **Speed**-critical transactions.
