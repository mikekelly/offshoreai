# British Virgin Islands — Overview

## What the BVI is

The **British Virgin Islands (BVI)** is a **British Overseas
Territory** in the **Caribbean**:

- **British Overseas Territory** (not Crown Dependency);
- **UK Parliament** can legislate (rarely does);
- **BVI Government** with its own legislature (**House of
  Assembly**);
- **Privy Council** as final appeal court;
- **Multiple islands**: Tortola (largest), Virgin Gorda, Anegada,
  Jost Van Dyke, others.

## Population and economy

- **Population**: approximately **30,000**;
- **Capital**: **Road Town** (on Tortola);
- **Currency**: **United States dollar (USD)** (uses USD as
  its currency);
- **Economy**: dominated by **financial services** (especially
  company formation) plus tourism.

## Constitutional / political

The BVI:

- Has its own **House of Assembly** (legislature);
- Has its own **judiciary** (with Privy Council final
  appeal);
- Has its own **financial-services regulator** (FSC);
- **No income tax**;
- **UK Government** retains responsibility for defence,
  external relations.

## Financial-services sector

The BVI is **dominated** by **corporate services**:

### Company formation

- **400,000+ active BVI Business Companies**;
- **Largest** offshore company-formation jurisdiction
  globally;
- **Standard** vehicle in international tax planning;
- **BC Act 2004** as principal statute;
- **Fast** and **cheap** to establish.

### Funds

- **Significant** fund-services industry;
- **Various** fund categories;
- **Specific** BVI fund regimes.

### Trusts

- **Established** trust regime;
- **VISTA trusts** as distinctive BVI offering;
- **Specific** BVI trust structures.

### Insurance

- **Smaller** insurance market;
- **Some** captive activity.

## Why BVI for company formation

Reasons for BVI dominance in company formation:

- **Speed** — companies incorporated in **hours**;
- **Cost** — very low fees;
- **Flexibility** — minimal share-capital and procedural
  requirements;
- **Anonymity** — significant historic privacy (now reduced
  through BO transparency);
- **Worldwide acceptance** of BVI BC as a standard vehicle;
- **Sophisticated** registered-agent infrastructure;
- **No** local presence requirements for most companies.

## Regulator

The **BVI Financial Services Commission (FSC)**:

- **Independent** statutory regulator;
- **Established** under Financial Services Commission Act
  2001;
- **Comprehensive** financial-services regulation;
- **Similar** role to other offshore regulators.

## Distinctive BVI features

### BVI Business Company (BC)

- The **flagship** vehicle;
- **Modernised** BVI BC Act 2004;
- **Replaced** older International Business Company (IBC);
- **Simple**, **flexible**, **fast**;
- See [`business-company.md`](./business-company.md).

### VISTA trusts

- **Virgin Islands Special Trusts Act 2003**;
- **Trustee** holds shares of an underlying company **without
  active management duties**;
- **Settlor's** preferences for company management can be
  given effect;
- **Distinctive** BVI offering.

### No income tax

- **No** corporate income tax;
- **No** personal income tax;
- **No** capital gains tax;
- **No** inheritance tax;
- **No** GST / VAT;
- **Annual fees** for companies;
- **Customs duties** as revenue.

## Comparison with Jersey

| Feature | Jersey | BVI |
|---|---|---|
| Status | Crown Dependency | BOT |
| Population | ~107k | ~30k |
| Tax | Zero-ten with GST | No income tax |
| Currency | Jersey pound | USD |
| Companies focus | Quality / depth | **Volume — 400k+ companies** |
| Trust regime | TJL 1984 | BVI Trustee Act + VISTA |
| Regulator | JFSC | FSC |
| Pillar Two | MCIT | MTT 2025 |
