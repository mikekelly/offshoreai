# British Virgin Islands — Adjacent Jurisdiction

## Why this section exists

The **British Virgin Islands (BVI)** is the **leading offshore
corporate jurisdiction** by **company numbers**:

- **400,000+** active BVI Business Companies;
- **Major** for **holding companies** and SPVs;
- **Standard** vehicle in international tax planning;
- **Bond / structured finance** prevalent;
- **Significant** in M&A as acquisition vehicles.

This section provides **high-level** BVI coverage. For full
BVI practice, engage **BVI counsel**.
