# BVI Financial Regulation — Overview

## Regulatory architecture

BVI regulation rests on:

- **FSC** as integrated regulator;
- **Multiple sectoral statutes**;
- **Rules and Guidance Notes** issued by FSC;
- **AML / CFT** framework.

## Principal statutes

### Companies

- **BVI Business Companies Act 2004** — BCs;
- **Specific** subsidiary regulations.

### Funds and investment business

- **Securities and Investment Business Act 2010 (SIBA)** —
  comprehensive investment-business and fund statute.

### Insurance

- **Insurance Act** — comprehensive insurance regulation.

### Banking

- **Banks and Trust Companies Act** — bank and trust
  licences.

### AML / CFT

- **Proceeds of Criminal Conduct Act**;
- **AML Regulations**;
- **FSC AML / CFT Code of Practice**.

### Substance

- **Economic Substance (Companies and Limited Partnerships)
  Act 2018**.

## Comparison with other offshore

| Aspect | Jersey | Cayman | BVI |
|---|---|---|---|
| Volume of regulated entities | Lower | Significant | **Highest (400k+)** |
| Sophistication per entity | Higher | Higher | Standardised |
| Funds | CIFL 1988 | Mutual Funds + Private Funds | SIBA |
| Companies | CJL 1991 | Companies Act | BC Act 2004 |
| Regulator | JFSC | CIMA | FSC |

## Authorisation process

Common features:

- **Application** to FSC;
- **Fit-and-proper** assessment;
- **Capital** requirements;
- **Systems and controls**;
- **Local presence** (registered agent);
- **Ongoing** requirements;
- **Specific** sectoral rules.

## Enforcement

FSC tools:

- **Civil penalties** (administrative fines);
- **Public statements**;
- **Conditions** on licence;
- **Suspension** / **revocation**;
- **Criminal** prosecution for serious offences.

## Cooperation

The FSC cooperates with:

- **SEC, FCA, PRA** — major international regulators;
- **CFATF** — Caribbean Financial Action Task Force;
- **IOSCO, IAIS** — international standards;
- **Specific** MoUs.

## Industry

Key BVI industry:

- **BVI Finance** — sector promotion;
- **Society of Trust and Estate Practitioners (STEP) BVI
  Branch**;
- **Specific** legal professional bodies;
- **Registered agents** trade association.
