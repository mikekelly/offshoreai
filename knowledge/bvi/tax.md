# BVI Tax — Zero-Tax Environment

## BVI has no income tax

Like Cayman, the BVI has **no income tax**:

- **No** corporate income tax;
- **No** personal income tax;
- **No** withholding tax;
- **No** capital gains tax;
- **No** inheritance / estate tax;
- **No** gift tax;
- **No** GST / VAT;
- **No** stamp duty on most transactions.

## Government revenue

BVI government revenue from:

### Annual company fees

- **Each BC** pays annual fee;
- **400,000+ companies** × annual fee = substantial
  government revenue;
- **Specific** fees per company type and capital size.

### Other fees

- **Companies Registry** fees;
- **FSC** licence fees;
- **Trust** licence fees;
- **Fund** annual fees.

### Customs duties

- **Goods** imports;
- **Tourism** related.

### Land taxes

- **Stamp duty** on real estate;
- **Limited** other property-related taxes.

### Specific transaction taxes

- **Specific** taxes on particular transactions;
- **Subject to** specific BVI law.

## No tax neutrality certificates needed

Unlike Cayman (which offers tax-exemption certificates), BVI
doesn't need them — there's **no tax** to be exempted from.

## Pillar Two — BVI implementation

The BVI implemented **Pillar Two** equivalent:

- **Multinational Top-up Tax (MTT)** from 2025;
- **15%** minimum effective rate for €750m+ groups;
- **Aligned** with OECD GloBE rules;
- **Significant** development for BVI (first income tax for
  in-scope groups).

## Economic Substance Act 2018

The **Economic Substance (Companies and Limited Partnerships)
Act 2018**:

- **Aligned** with Jersey / Cayman / Guernsey approach;
- **In-scope** activities require substance;
- **Annual** substance return;
- **Penalties** for non-compliance.

## CRS / FATCA

- **CRS** implementation;
- **FATCA IGA** with US;
- **CbCR** for in-scope groups;
- **Aligned** transparency standards.

## Tax treaty network

BVI has:

- **Limited** treaty network;
- **TIEAs** with multiple jurisdictions;
- **Limited** full DTAs;
- **MLI** signatory.

## Practical tax considerations

### BVI-side

- **No BVI income tax** (subject to MTT);
- **Annual** company fees;
- **Substance** if in-scope;
- **Specific** registration fees.

### Foreign side

- **CFC** rules in investor jurisdictions;
- **Specific** US tax considerations;
- **Specific** UK tax considerations;
- **Specific** other jurisdictions.
