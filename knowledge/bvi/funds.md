# BVI Funds — Overview

## Principal legislation

BVI funds are governed by:

- **Securities and Investment Business Act 2010 (SIBA)** —
  principal statute;
- **Mutual Funds Regulations**;
- **Private Investment Funds Regulations**;
- **Specific** subsidiary legislation.

## Fund categories

### Public funds

- **Retail** offerings;
- **Highest** regulatory tier;
- **Less common** in BVI.

### Professional funds

- **Sophisticated** investor base;
- **Minimum** investment (US$100,000);
- **Specific** professional-investor criteria;
- **Lighter** regulation than public funds.

### Private funds

- **50 or fewer** investors;
- **Light** regulation;
- **Specific** BVI rules.

### Approved funds

- **Limited** to 20 investors;
- **Approved** by FSC;
- **Lightest** ongoing regulation.

### Incubator funds

- **Maximum** 20 investors;
- **Maximum** $20m AUM;
- **2-year** maximum duration;
- **Startup** fund-launch vehicles;
- **Specific** BVI introduction.

### Closed-ended funds

- **Private Investment Funds Regulations**;
- **Closed-ended** vehicles (PE, VC, real estate);
- **Specific** registration requirements.

## Fund vehicles

BVI funds typically use:

### BVI Business Company (BC)

- **Most common** for funds;
- **Standard** corporate features;
- **Investor base** holds shares.

### Limited partnership

- **Common** for PE / VC;
- **GP / LP** structure;
- **Tax transparency** in home jurisdictions.

### SPC (Segregated Portfolio Company)

- **Cell-style** umbrella;
- **Less prevalent** than Cayman SPC.

## Comparison with Jersey

| Feature | Jersey | BVI |
|---|---|---|
| Public funds | CIFL 1988 | Public Funds Regulations |
| Expert / professional | Expert Fund | Professional Fund |
| Private | JPF | Private Investment Fund |
| Approved | Less specific | Approved Fund (20-investor cap) |
| Incubator | No equivalent | **Incubator Fund** |
| AIFMD | NPPR | NPPR |

## When to use BVI funds

Common scenarios:

- **Startup** fund manager (incubator fund);
- **Smaller** fund vehicles (approved fund);
- **Cost-efficient** mid-sized fund structures;
- **BVI BC** as fund vehicle;
- **Specific** BVI advantages.
