# BVI vs Jersey — Comparison

## Constitutional / political

| Feature | Jersey | BVI |
|---|---|---|
| Status | Crown Dependency | British Overseas Territory |
| Population | ~107k | ~30k |
| Legislature | States Assembly | House of Assembly |
| Top court | Royal Court | High Court (Eastern Caribbean) |
| Privy Council | Yes | Yes |
| Currency | Jersey pound | USD |
| Time zone | UTC/UTC+1 | UTC-4 |

## Tax

| Feature | Jersey | BVI |
|---|---|---|
| Corporate income | Zero-ten | **None** |
| Personal income | 20% | **None** |
| GST / VAT | 5% GST | **None** |
| CGT | None | None |
| IHT | None | None |
| Pillar Two | MCIT | MTT 2025 |
| Substance Law | 2019 | Economic Substance Act 2018 |

BVI has **no income tax** like Cayman. Tax revenue from
**annual company fees** and **specific stamp duties**.

## Companies

| Feature | Jersey CJL 1991 | BVI BC Act 2004 |
|---|---|---|
| Statute | CJL 1991 | BC Act 2004 |
| Number of companies | ~30k Jersey companies | **400,000+ BCs** |
| Speed to incorporate | Days to weeks | **Hours** |
| Cost | Higher | **Lower** |
| Annual fees | Significant | Lower per company |
| Capital | Par / no-par | Flexible |
| Cell companies | PCC and ICC | Limited (BVI cell-structured products differently) |
| Director duties | Article 74 framework | Specific BC provisions |

**BVI dominates in volume** of companies — Jersey has fewer
companies but **higher value-per-company** and more
sophisticated structures.

## Trusts

| Feature | Jersey | BVI |
|---|---|---|
| Statute | TJL 1984 | Trustee Act + amendments |
| Reserved powers | Article 9A | Specific BVI provisions |
| Firewall | Article 9 | Specific BVI provisions |
| Purpose trusts | Article 12 | Specific BVI provisions |
| **VISTA** | No equivalent | **Distinctive BVI offering** |

**VISTA trusts** (Virgin Islands Special Trusts Act 2003)
are the **distinctive BVI** trust feature:

- **Trustee holds shares** of underlying company;
- **No active management** duties for the trustee;
- **Settlor's wishes** for company management can be given
  effect;
- **Different** from standard trust where trustee actively
  manages.

## Funds

| Feature | Jersey | BVI |
|---|---|---|
| Public funds | CIFL 1988 | Public Funds Regulations |
| Private funds | JPF | Private Investment Funds Regulations |
| Closed-ended | Various | Closed-ended Fund Regulations |
| AIFMD | NPPR | NPPR |

Both have fund offerings; BVI fund market is smaller than
Cayman / Jersey but offers competitive options.

## Insurance

| Feature | Jersey | BVI |
|---|---|---|
| Captive market | Smaller | **Smaller still** |
| Specialty | Various | Less developed |

**BVI** has limited insurance specialty compared to Jersey /
Guernsey / Cayman / Bermuda.

## Banking

| Feature | Jersey | BVI |
|---|---|---|
| Sector | Significant | **Limited domestic banking** |
| International banks | Many | Limited |
| Specialty | Mixed | Mostly trust-services-related |

**BVI banking** is **less developed** than Jersey banking.

## Specific BVI features

### Volume of BCs

- **400,000+** active BVI Business Companies;
- **Standard** vehicle in international tax structures;
- **Industry** of registered agents serving the volume.

### Tortola registered agents

- **Specific** BVI registered-agent firms;
- **Tortola-based** services;
- **Volume** business model.

## When to choose BVI

Strong factors favouring BVI:

- **Volume** company formation;
- **Speed** and **cost** efficiency;
- **VISTA trust** for specific structures;
- **Standard vehicle** in international planning;
- **No tax** environment;
- **USD** denomination.

## When Jersey is the natural choice

Strong factors favouring Jersey:

- **Sophisticated** structures (PCC, ICC, schemes of
  arrangement);
- **Banking** sector depth;
- **Specific Jersey** legal advantages (Article 9A);
- **Crown Dependency** status preferred;
- **GBP** denomination;
- **EU / UK** proximity.

## Common combinations

BVI and Jersey often **combined**:

- **BVI** acquisition vehicle in M&A + **Jersey** topco;
- **BVI** SPV in structured finance + **Jersey** trust at
  top;
- **BVI** holding companies + **Jersey** management;
- **Specific** sectoral combinations.
