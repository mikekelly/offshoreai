# BVI Legal System — Overview

## Court structure

### Eastern Caribbean Supreme Court

BVI shares the **Eastern Caribbean Supreme Court (ECSC)**
with other Eastern Caribbean states:

- **High Court** sits in BVI as part of ECSC;
- **Court of Appeal** ECSC for multiple jurisdictions;
- **Commercial Division** with specialist commercial
  judges;
- **Privy Council** final appeal.

### High Court (BVI division of ECSC)

- **Principal civil and criminal** court for BVI;
- **Justices** of the ECSC;
- **Specific** procedural rules.

### Commercial Division

- **Specialist** commercial judges;
- **Major** BVI commercial cases;
- **Streamlined** procedures;
- **International** prestige.

### Magistrate's Court

- **Lower** court;
- **Minor** matters;
- **Specific** procedural rules.

### Privy Council

- **Final appeal** from ECSC;
- **Same** as Jersey / Cayman / IoM / Guernsey.

## Sources of law

BVI law sources:

1. **BVI statute** (BVI legislation);
2. **Case law** (ECSC, Privy Council);
3. **English common law** (highly applicable);
4. **Equity** principles;
5. **Specific** BVI statutory variations.

BVI law is **substantially common-law** in character:

- **English case law** directly applicable in many areas;
- **Less** customary or civil-law influence than Jersey;
- **Specific** BVI statutory provisions.

## Legal profession

### BVI lawyers

- **BVI Bar**;
- **Specific** admission requirements;
- **Cross-qualification** routes from UK / common-law
  jurisdictions;
- **Major** firms with significant BVI offices.

## Procedure

- **Civil Procedure Rules** (ECSC);
- **Modelled** on UK CPR with Eastern Caribbean adaptations;
- **Standard** common-law procedural approach;
- **Specific** Commercial Division rules.

## Specific BVI features

### Commercial Division depth

- **Major** offshore disputes heard here;
- **High-profile** cases;
- **Specialist** judges;
- **International** prestige.

### English law connection

BVI law and procedure are **substantially English-style**:

- **More direct** than Jersey;
- **Specific** BVI statutory variations.

## Comparison with Jersey

| Feature | Jersey | BVI |
|---|---|---|
| Top court | Royal Court | ECSC (BVI Division) |
| Head | Bailiff | Chief Justice (ECSC) |
| Lay judges | Jurats | None |
| Legal heritage | Norman customary | Common law (Caribbean) |
| Customary law role | Substantial | Minimal |
| English law | Persuasive | More directly applicable |
| Specialist division | Not formal | Commercial Division |
