# Bermuda Companies — Overview

## Principal statute

The **Companies Act 1981** (extensively amended) is the
principal company-law statute, supplemented by specific
sectoral acts:

- **Companies Act 1981** — general company law;
- **Limited Liability Company Act 2016** — LLC vehicles;
- **Segregated Accounts Companies Act 2000** — cell
  structures;
- **Specific** insurance / banking statutes.

## Company types

### Exempted company

The **principal vehicle** for international business:

- **Exempt** from local Bermuda business restrictions;
- **Cannot** carry on local business with Bermudians
  (specific exceptions);
- **Major** insurance / reinsurance companies use this form;
- **Standard** offshore exempted-company features.

### Ordinary / local company

- **Can** conduct local Bermuda business;
- **Subject to** specific local rules.

### Segregated Accounts Company (SAC)

- **Bermuda PCC equivalent**;
- **Multiple segregated accounts**;
- **Used** for insurance, fund umbrella, structured
  products.

### Limited Liability Company (LLC)

The **Bermuda LLC**:

- **Limited Liability Company Act 2016**;
- **US-style** LLC features;
- **Flexible** governance;
- **Increasingly used** in international structures.

### Permit company

- **Foreign company** permitted to do business in Bermuda;
- **Specific** registration.

## Key features

### Share capital

- **Authorised** capital concept;
- **Par-value or no-par-value**;
- **Specific** Bermuda rules.

### Directors

- **At least one** director (and at least one director must
  be ordinarily resident in Bermuda for some company types);
- **Specific** Bermuda fiduciary duties;
- **Standard** company-law approach.

### Constitutional documents

- **Memorandum** and **Bye-laws**;
- **Standard** Bermuda approach.

### Capital maintenance

- **Specific** Bermuda solvency rules;
- **Distributions** subject to solvency;
- **Specific** financial-assistance rules.

### Migration

- **Continuance in** to Bermuda;
- **Continuance out** to other jurisdictions;
- **Specific** Bermuda procedures.

### Listed companies

- **Bermuda Stock Exchange (BSX)** for listings;
- **Specific** listing rules;
- **Major Bermuda companies** listed on international
  exchanges.

## Bermuda Stock Exchange (BSX)

The **BSX**:

- **Securities exchange** in Bermuda;
- **Listed** debt securities, equities, funds;
- **Specific** listing rules;
- **Recognised** by international counterparts.

## Insolvency

Bermuda company insolvency:

- **Specific** Bermuda procedures;
- **Court-supervised** winding-up;
- **Cross-border** cooperation.

## Comparison with Jersey

| Feature | Jersey CJL 1991 | Bermuda Companies Act 1981 |
|---|---|---|
| Statute | CJL 1991 | Companies Act 1981 |
| Exempted | No specific category | **Yes** |
| LLC | LLP only | **LLC** Act 2016 |
| Cell companies | PCC, ICC | **SAC** (Segregated Accounts Company) |
| Director residence | No requirement | **Some Bermuda residence** for some company types |
| Specialty | Trust / commercial | **Insurance / reinsurance** |

## When to use Bermuda companies

Common scenarios:

- **Insurance / reinsurance** entities (overwhelming
  Bermuda preference);
- **ILS** vehicles (SPI);
- **Existing Bermuda group** structures;
- **Specific** US / EU insurance integration;
- **NAIC-qualified** counterparty preference.
