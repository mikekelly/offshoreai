# Bermuda Financial Regulation — Overview

## Regulatory architecture

Bermuda regulation rests on:

- **BMA** as integrated regulator;
- **Multiple sectoral statutes**;
- **BMA Rules** and Codes;
- **AML / CFT** framework;
- **Solvency II equivalent** for insurance.

## Principal statutes

### Insurance (the flagship)

- **Insurance Act 1978** — comprehensive insurance
  regulation;
- **Insurance Returns and Solvency Regulations**;
- **Specific** subsidiary regulations.

### Banking

- **Banks and Deposit Companies Act 1999**;
- **Basel-aligned** rules.

### Investment business / funds

- **Investment Business Act 2003**;
- **Investment Funds Act 2006**.

### Trust services

- **Trusts (Regulation of Trust Business) Act 2001**.

### AML / CFT

- **Proceeds of Crime Act 1997**;
- **AML Regulations**;
- **BMA AML / CFT Guidance**.

### Digital assets

- **Digital Asset Business Act 2018**;
- **Specific** crypto regulation;
- **Bermuda** an early adopter of comprehensive digital
  asset regulation.

### Substance

- **Economic Substance Act 2018**;
- **Aligned** with other offshore jurisdictions.

## Authorisation process

Common features:

- **Application** to BMA;
- **Fit-and-proper** assessment;
- **Capital** requirements (substantial for insurance);
- **Systems and controls**;
- **Local presence**;
- **Ongoing** requirements;
- **Specific** Rule Book provisions.

## Enforcement

BMA tools:

- **Civil penalties**;
- **Public statements**;
- **Conditions** on licence;
- **Suspension** / **revocation**;
- **Criminal** prosecution for serious offences.

## Cooperation

The BMA cooperates extensively with:

- **NAIC** (US insurance regulators);
- **EIOPA** (EU insurance regulator);
- **Major** insurance regulators globally;
- **IAIS** — international insurance supervisors;
- **IOSCO** — securities;
- **FATF / CFATF** — AML;
- **Specific** MoUs with major regulators.

## Industry

Key Bermuda industry:

- **Bermuda International Business Association**;
- **Insurance Association**;
- **Bermuda Investment Funds Association**;
- **Society of Trust and Estate Practitioners** Bermuda;
- **Major international** firms with Bermuda offices.
