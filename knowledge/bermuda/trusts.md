# Bermuda Trusts — Overview

## Principal legislation

Bermuda trust law:

- **Trustee Act 1975** (extensively amended);
- **Trusts (Special Provisions) Act 1989** — firewall and
  related;
- **Specific** other trust legislation;
- **Common law** overlay.

## Standard Bermuda trusts

### Express trusts

- **Common-law** foundation;
- **Specific** Bermuda statutory provisions;
- **Standard** trust principles.

### Charitable trusts

- **Recognised**;
- **Specific** Bermuda rules;
- **Tax advantageous**.

### Discretionary trusts

- **Standard** discretionary regime;
- **Specific** Bermuda approaches.

## Trusts (Special Provisions) Act 1989

The **TSPA** provides:

- **Firewall** protection from foreign forced-heirship and
  matrimonial claims;
- **Reserved powers** specifically allowed;
- **Specific** Bermuda safeguards;
- **Recognised** as one of the early offshore firewall
  statutes.

## Bermuda Trust Companies

Bermuda has a well-developed trust company industry:

- **Specialist** offshore trust providers;
- **Many international groups** with Bermuda trust operations;
- **Regulated by BMA** under Trusts (Regulation of Trust
  Business) Act 2001;
- **Substantial** international trust assets.

## Reserved powers

Bermuda law permits **wide reserved powers**:

- **Similar to** Jersey Article 9A;
- **Specific** Bermuda provisions;
- **Established** in commercial use.

## Information rights

- **Beneficiary information** rights;
- **Similar** to Schmidt v Rosewood approach;
- **Specific** Bermuda case law.

## Purpose trusts

Bermuda has purpose trust provisions:

- **Non-charitable purpose** trusts;
- **Enforcer** requirement;
- **Specific** Bermuda rules.

## Comparison with Jersey

| Feature | Jersey TJL 1984 | Bermuda Trustee Act |
|---|---|---|
| Statute | TJL 1984 | Trustee Act 1975 + amendments |
| Reserved powers | Article 9A | TSPA 1989 |
| Firewall | Article 9 | TSPA 1989 |
| Purpose trusts | Article 12 | Specific provisions |
| Customary | Norman | Common law |

Both **Bermuda** and **Jersey** are well-established trust
jurisdictions with **substantial international trust
business**. **Bermuda's specialty** in **insurance-related**
trust structures gives it specific advantages where insurance
integration is needed.

## When to use Bermuda trusts

Common scenarios:

- **Insurance group** structures with trust components;
- **Bermuda-based** wealth or counterparties;
- **Specific** Bermuda structural advantages;
- **NAIC qualified** counterparty preference for insurance
  trust integration;
- **Existing** Bermuda relationships.
