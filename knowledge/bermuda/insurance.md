# Bermuda Insurance — Global Insurance Hub

## Why Bermuda dominates

Bermuda is **the world's leading offshore insurance
jurisdiction**:

- **Established** since 1960s;
- **Largest** captive market globally;
- **Top 3** reinsurance market globally (with Lloyd's of
  London and Munich Re);
- **Specialty** insurance market depth;
- **ILS / cat bond** leader;
- **Sophisticated** professional infrastructure;
- **Solvency II equivalent**;
- **NAIC qualified** for US.

## Principal legislation

- **Insurance Act 1978** (extensively amended) — principal
  statute;
- **Insurance Returns and Solvency Regulations**;
- **Specific** subsidiary legislation;
- **BMA Rules**.

## Insurance classes

Bermuda has a **multi-class system**:

### Class 1

- **Pure captives** insuring only related-party risks;
- **Single parent** captives;
- **Simplest** category;
- **Lowest** regulatory tier.

### Class 2

- **Mid-sized captives**;
- **Some third-party** business possible (limited);
- **Specific** rules.

### Class 3

- **Standard category** for general insurers and reinsurers;
- **Subject to** comprehensive regulation;
- **Most** commercial insurers and reinsurers.

### Class 3A

- **Smaller** insurers (subset of Class 3);
- **Specific** rules.

### Class 3B

- **Larger** insurers (subset of Class 3);
- **Higher** capital requirements.

### Class 4

- **Largest** insurers / reinsurers;
- **Subject to** the most comprehensive regulation;
- **Specific** capital requirements for major operations;
- **Solvency II-equivalent** treatment.

### Class C, D, E

- **Long-term (life) insurance** classes;
- **Specific** rules per class size and complexity.

### Special Purpose Insurer (SPI)

- **For ILS / cat bond** transactions;
- **Fully collateralised** typically;
- **Specific** regulatory framework;
- See [`ils.md`](./ils.md).

## Captive insurance

Bermuda has the **largest** captive market globally:

- **Numerous** captives across industries;
- **Healthcare**, **manufacturing**, **financial services**,
  **utilities** all represented;
- **Sponsored** captives common;
- **Pure**, **group**, **agency** captives.

## Reinsurance

Bermuda is a **top 3 global reinsurance** market:

- **Property catastrophe** specialty;
- **Casualty** reinsurance;
- **Specialty** lines (cyber, terrorism, marine, aviation);
- **Major reinsurers** with Bermuda operations.

## Specialty insurance

Specialty lines specifically strong:

- **Catastrophe** insurance;
- **Cyber** insurance;
- **Political risk**;
- **Aviation / marine**;
- **Specific** industry-specific products.

## Solvency II equivalence

The BMA achieved **Solvency II equivalence** with EU:

- **Mutual recognition** for prudential purposes;
- **Bermuda insurers** can deal with EU counterparts more
  easily;
- **EU recognition** of Bermuda regulator and capital
  regime.

## NAIC qualified jurisdiction

For US-related insurance:

- **Bermuda is a qualified jurisdiction** for NAIC
  purposes;
- **US-based reinsurers** can use Bermuda reinsurance with
  reduced collateral requirements;
- **Significant** competitive advantage for Bermuda
  reinsurers.

## Capital and solvency

Bermuda insurance capital regime:

- **Risk-based** capital approach;
- **Bermuda Solvency Capital Requirement (BSCR)**;
- **Class-specific** capital calculations;
- **Sophisticated** modelling;
- **Aligned** with Solvency II principles.

## Comparison with other offshore

| Feature | Jersey | Guernsey | Cayman | Bermuda |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Captive market | Smaller | Significant | Significant | **Largest globally** |
| Reinsurance | Limited | Limited | Some | **Top 3 globally** |
| ILS | Limited | Limited | Some | **Leader** |
| Solvency II | Selective | Selective | Selective | **Full equivalence** |
| NAIC qualified | Not | Not | Not | **Yes** |
| Class regime | A/B | Specific | Class A-D | Class 1-4, C/D/E, SPI |

## Industry

Key Bermuda insurance industry:

- **Major** insurance / reinsurance groups headquartered
  (RenaissanceRe, Arch, AXIS, Hiscox Bermuda, others);
- **Specialist** broker firms (Aon, Marsh, others with major
  Bermuda offices);
- **Specialist** legal firms;
- **Specialist** accounting firms (Big 4 plus specialist
  insurance accountants);
- **Specialist** actuarial firms;
- **Bermuda Insurance Association**.
