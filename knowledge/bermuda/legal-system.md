# Bermuda Legal System — Overview

## Court structure

### Supreme Court of Bermuda

The **Supreme Court** is the principal civil and criminal
court:

- **Chief Justice** as head;
- **Multiple Puisne Judges**;
- **Specific** divisions (Civil, Criminal, Commercial);
- **Comparable** role to Jersey Royal Court.

### Bermuda Court of Appeal

- **Appeal** court;
- **Sits** periodically;
- **Specific** Court of Appeal judges typically drawn from
  UK / Commonwealth bench.

### Magistrates' Court

- **Lower** court;
- **Minor** matters.

### Privy Council

- **Final appeal** from Bermuda;
- **Same** as Jersey / Cayman / IoM / Guernsey / BVI.

## Sources of law

Bermuda law sources:

1. **Bermuda statute** (Bermuda legislation);
2. **Case law** (Supreme Court, Court of Appeal, Privy
   Council);
3. **English common law** (highly applicable);
4. **Equity** principles;
5. **Specific** Bermuda statutory variations.

Bermuda law is **substantially common law** with Bermuda
statutory variations.

## Legal profession

### Bermuda Bar

- **Bermuda Bar Association**;
- **Specific** admission requirements;
- **Cross-qualification** from UK / common-law jurisdictions;
- **Major firms** with Bermuda offices.

## Procedure

- **Supreme Court Rules**;
- **Modelled** on English RSC with Bermuda adaptations;
- **Standard** common-law procedural approach.

## Specific Bermuda features

### Insurance specialty

The Supreme Court has substantial **insurance-related**
litigation expertise:

- **Coverage** disputes;
- **Reinsurance** disputes;
- **Captive-related** matters;
- **ILS** disputes.

### Commercial expertise

- **Major** offshore commercial disputes heard;
- **Specific** Bermuda procedural rules;
- **Sophisticated** judiciary.

## English law comparison

Bermuda legal practice is **substantially English**:

- **English case law** directly applicable in many areas;
- **Specific** Bermuda statutory variations;
- **Less distinctive** procedural concepts than Jersey.

## Comparison with Jersey

| Feature | Jersey | Bermuda |
|---|---|---|
| Top court | Royal Court | Supreme Court of Bermuda |
| Head | Bailiff | Chief Justice |
| Lay judges | Jurats | None |
| Legal heritage | Norman customary | Common law |
| Customary law role | Substantial | Minimal |
| English law | Persuasive | More directly applicable |
| Specialty | Trust / commercial | **Insurance** |
