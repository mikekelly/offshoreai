# Cayman Companies — Overview

## Principal statute

The **Companies Act** (originally 1961, extensively amended)
is the principal company-law statute, supplemented by
specific Acts:

- **Companies Act** — general company law;
- **Limited Liability Companies Act 2016** — LLC vehicles;
- **Mutual Funds Act** — fund-specific corporate features;
- **Specific** insurance, banking acts.

## Company types

### Exempted company

The **principal international vehicle**:

- **Exempt** from local Cayman company business
  restrictions;
- **Cannot** transact local Cayman business with Cayman
  residents (with specific exceptions);
- **Can** do international business globally;
- **No income tax** exemption certificates available (20-30
  years);
- **Standard** corporate features (directors, shares, share
  capital).

### Ordinary company

- **Can** transact local Cayman business;
- **Subject to** specific local restrictions;
- **Less common** for international structures.

### Foreign company

- **Foreign-incorporated** companies registered to do
  business in Cayman.

### Segregated Portfolio Company (SPC)

- **Single legal entity** with **multiple segregated
  portfolios**;
- **Each portfolio** segregated from others statutorily;
- **Cayman equivalent** of PCC;
- **Widely used** for insurance, fund umbrella, structured
  products.

### Limited Liability Company (LLC)

The Cayman LLC (**Limited Liability Companies Act 2016**):

- **US-style LLC** features;
- **Member-managed** by default;
- **Flexible** governance and structure;
- **Specific** Cayman LLC features;
- **Increasingly popular** for joint ventures, private
  equity.

## Key features

### Share capital

- **Authorised** capital concept;
- **Par-value or no-par-value**;
- **Specific** Cayman rules on capital reductions, redemptions.

### Directors

- **At least one** director;
- **Foreign** directors permitted;
- **Specific** Cayman fiduciary duties;
- **Less developed** statutory framework than Jersey
  CJL Article 74.

### Constitutional documents

- **Memorandum** and **Articles of Association**;
- **Standard** approach.

### Capital maintenance

- **Specific** Cayman provisions;
- **Different** from Jersey's Article 115 cash-flow test;
- **Specific** Cayman solvency / financial-assistance rules.

### Migration

- **Continuance in** to Cayman from other jurisdictions;
- **Continuance out** to other jurisdictions;
- **Specific** Cayman procedures.

### Annual obligations

- **Annual return**;
- **Annual fees**;
- **Specific** filings;
- **Limited** financial-statement filing requirements (private
  companies generally don't file accounts).

## Cayman Companies Registry

- **Registry** maintained;
- **Online** access for some functions;
- **Different** transparency than Jersey JFSC registry;
- **Specific** BO register;
- **Specific** access rules.

## When to use Cayman companies

Common scenarios:

- **Fund manager / GP** entity;
- **Holding company** for international investments;
- **Joint venture** vehicle (LLC often preferred);
- **SPC** for cell-structured products;
- **Bond / structured finance** issuer.

## Insolvency

Cayman company insolvency:

- **Companies (Winding Up) Provisions** in Companies Act;
- **Court-supervised** winding-up;
- **Specific** Cayman procedures;
- **Cross-border** cooperation available.
