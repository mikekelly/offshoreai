# Cayman Legal System — Overview

## Court structure

### Grand Court

The **Grand Court** is the principal civil and criminal
court:

- **Chief Justice** as head;
- **Multiple Justices** for various divisions;
- **Specific** divisions (Financial Services Division, Family
  Division, etc.);
- **Comparable** role to Jersey Royal Court.

### Cayman Islands Court of Appeal

- **Appeal court**;
- **Sits** periodically;
- **Specific** Court of Appeal judges typically drawn from
  UK / Commonwealth bench;
- **Specific** procedural rules.

### Summary Court

- **Lower court** for minor matters;
- **Magistrates**;
- **Specific** procedural rules.

### Privy Council

- **Final appeal** from Cayman;
- **Same** as Jersey / Guernsey / IoM.

## Financial Services Division

A **specific Grand Court division** for financial-services
matters:

- **Specialist** judges;
- **Major** trust, fund, insolvency, corporate cases;
- **Streamlined** procedures for sophisticated cases;
- **International** prestige.

This division has handled many **high-profile** offshore
disputes.

## Sources of law

Cayman law sources:

1. **Statute** (Cayman legislation);
2. **Case law** (Grand Court, Court of Appeal, Privy Council);
3. **English common law** (highly applicable);
4. **Equity** principles;
5. **Specific** Cayman statutory provisions.

Cayman law is **more directly common-law** than Jersey law:

- **English case law** **directly applicable** in many
  cases;
- **Less** customary-law overlay than Jersey;
- **More** alignment with English commercial law.

## Legal profession

### Cayman attorneys

- **Cayman Bar**;
- **Specific** admission requirements;
- **Cross-qualification** from UK / common-law jurisdictions
  routes available;
- **Major firms** with significant Cayman operations.

### Bar associations

- **Cayman Islands Law Society**;
- **Specific** Bar rules and disciplinary procedures.

## Procedure

The **Grand Court Rules**:

- **Modelled** on English RSC / CPR;
- **Specific** Cayman adaptations;
- **Standard** common-law procedural approach;
- **Specific** Financial Services Division rules.

## English law comparison

Cayman legal practice is **closer to English** than Jersey:

- **Less** distinctive procedural concepts than Jersey
  Royal Court;
- **More** direct application of English commercial /
  trust / company law;
- **Specific** Cayman variations through statute.

## Comparison with Jersey

| Feature | Jersey | Cayman |
|---|---|---|
| Top court | Royal Court | Grand Court |
| Head | Bailiff | Chief Justice |
| Lay judges | Jurats | None |
| Legal heritage | Norman customary | Common law |
| Customary law role | Substantial | Minimal |
| English law | Persuasive | More directly applicable |
| Specialist division | Not formal | **Financial Services Division** |
| Procedural distinctness | Order of Justice, Representation, Doleance | Standard CPR-style |

## Specific Cayman features

### Confidentiality

Cayman has **strong** confidentiality protections:

- **Confidential Information Disclosure Act**;
- **Specific** restrictions on disclosure of business
  information;
- **Subject to** specific exceptions (criminal, regulatory).

### Hearing in private

- **In-camera** hearings more readily available;
- **Specific** confidentiality protections in fund / trust
  disputes;
- **Specific** Cayman approach.
