# CIMA — Cayman Islands Monetary Authority

## What CIMA is

The **Cayman Islands Monetary Authority (CIMA)** is Cayman's
**integrated financial-services regulator**:

- **Established** by the Monetary Authority Act 1996;
- **Independent** of government in day-to-day operation;
- **Funded** through fees;
- **Reports** to the Cayman Government / Parliament.

## Functions

CIMA:

- **Licenses** financial-services businesses;
- **Registers** funds and managers;
- **Supervises** ongoing compliance;
- **Investigates** breaches;
- **Enforces** regulatory standards;
- **Issues** Rule Book and guidance;
- **Cooperates** with international regulators;
- **Administers** the Currency Board (Cayman dollar issuance).

## Sectors regulated

### Banking

- **Banks Act**;
- **Basel III** alignment;
- **Domestic** and **international** banks.

### Insurance

- **Insurance Act 2010**;
- **Captive** and **commercial** insurance;
- **Specific** classes per Insurance Act.

### Funds

- **Mutual Funds Act**;
- **Private Funds Act 2020**;
- **Open-ended** and **closed-ended** funds;
- **Master / feeder** funds;
- **Specific** categories.

### Investment business / securities

- **Securities Investment Business Act (SIBA)**;
- **Specific** investment-business categories;
- **Fund managers** and investment advisers.

### Trust and corporate services

- **Banks and Trust Companies Act**;
- **Specific** trust company licensing;
- **Specific** corporate services regulation.

### VASPs / digital assets

- **Virtual Asset (Service Providers) Act 2020**;
- **Specific** crypto / digital asset regulation;
- **Specific** AML / CFT integration.

### AML / CFT

- **Proceeds of Crime Act**;
- **AML Regulations**;
- **CIMA Guidance Notes**;
- **Equivalent** approach to Jersey POCL / MLO framework.

## CIMA structure

- **Board of Directors**;
- **Managing Director**;
- **Multiple divisions** for different sectors;
- **Significant** professional staff;
- **Industry** consultations regularly.

## Comparison with sister regulators

| Feature | JFSC | GFSC | FSA (IoM) | CIMA |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Country | Jersey | Guernsey | IoM | Cayman |
| Status of country | Crown Dependency | Crown Dependency | Crown Dependency | British Overseas Territory |
| Year founded | 1998 | 1987 | 2015 (merged) | 1996 |
| Scope | TCB/FSB/Investment/banking/insurance | Sectoral statutes | Unified post-2015 | Comprehensive |
| Funds | CIFL 1988 | PoIL 2020 | CIS Act 2008 | Mutual Funds Act + Private Funds Act |

## CIMA's international participation

CIMA participates in:

- **IOSCO** — securities;
- **IAIS** — insurance;
- **Basel** — banking observer;
- **FATF / CFATF** — Caribbean Financial Action Task Force;
- **Specific** MoUs with major regulators (SEC, FCA, etc.).

## Practical engagement

For practitioners:

- **CIMA** authoritative for Cayman regulated entities;
- **Specific** CIMA procedures;
- **Online** systems for filings;
- **Direct** engagement on material matters;
- **Industry** representation important.
