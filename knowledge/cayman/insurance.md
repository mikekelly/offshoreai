# Cayman Insurance — Captive Centre

## Cayman as insurance jurisdiction

Cayman is a **significant** captive insurance jurisdiction:

- **Major** US-related captive market (healthcare risk, group
  captives);
- **Smaller** than Bermuda but substantial;
- **CIMA** as regulator;
- **Specialty** in healthcare and reinsurance.

## Principal legislation

- **Insurance Act 2010** — principal insurance statute;
- **Specific** captive insurance regulations;
- **CIMA Rules** for insurance.

## Categories

### Class A — General insurance (Cayman policyholders)

- **Cayman-resident** policyholders;
- **Domestic** market;
- **Higher** consumer protection.

### Class B — International insurance

- **Non-Cayman** policyholders;
- **Captives** and **reinsurance**;
- **Various** sub-categories.

### Class C — Special category

- **Specific** insurance structures.

### Class D — Reinsurance

- **Pure reinsurance** activities.

## Captive uses

### Healthcare captives

- **US healthcare** systems often use Cayman captives;
- **Liability** for medical professional risks;
- **Group** medical captives.

### Group captives

- **Multiple** member-companies;
- **Shared** captive vehicle;
- **Specific** Cayman tradition for these.

### Pure captives

- **Single parent** captives;
- **Standard** captive use.

### Reinsurance

- **Cayman reinsurers** for major risks;
- **Specific** reinsurance specialty.

## SPC (Segregated Portfolio Company)

- **Cayman PCC equivalent**;
- **Widely used** for cell captives;
- **Specific** Cayman SPC rules.

## Comparison with Jersey insurance

| Feature | Jersey | Cayman |
|---|---|---|
| Statute | IBL 1996 | Insurance Act 2010 |
| Captive market | Smaller | Significant |
| Healthcare captives | Some | **Major specialty** |
| PCC/SPC | PCC and ICC | SPC |
| Reinsurance | Some | Significant |
| Regulator | JFSC | CIMA |

## Cayman vs Bermuda for insurance

Cayman has a **substantial** captive market but **Bermuda
remains larger** in:

- **Total** captive numbers;
- **Premium** volumes;
- **Reinsurance** market depth.

**Cayman has advantages** in:

- **Cost** efficiency;
- **Healthcare** captive specialty;
- **Smaller** group / cell captives.
