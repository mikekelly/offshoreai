# Cayman Islands — Overview

## What the Cayman Islands is

The **Cayman Islands** is a **British Overseas Territory**
(not a Crown Dependency like Jersey) located in the
**Caribbean**:

- **British Overseas Territory** — different constitutional
  status from Jersey;
- **UK Parliament** can legislate for Cayman directly (rarely
  does);
- **Cayman Government** with its own legislature
  (**Parliament of the Cayman Islands**);
- **Privy Council** as final appeal court;
- **Three islands**: Grand Cayman (largest), Cayman Brac,
  Little Cayman.

## Population and economy

- **Population**: approximately **80,000**;
- **Capital**: **George Town** (on Grand Cayman);
- **Currency**: **Cayman Islands dollar (CI$)** pegged to USD
  at CI$1 = US$1.20;
- **Economy**: **dominated by financial services** (fund
  domiciliation, insurance, banking, trust services) plus
  tourism.

## Constitutional / political

The Cayman Islands:

- Has its own **Parliament** (legislature);
- Has its own **judiciary** (with Privy Council final
  appeal);
- Has its own **financial-services regulator** (CIMA);
- Has its own **tax system** (no income tax);
- **UK Government** retains responsibility for defence,
  external relations, and (in extremis) good governance.

## Financial-services sector

Cayman is the **world's largest offshore financial centre**
by certain measures:

### Fund domiciliation

- **Globally dominant** for **hedge funds**;
- **Major** for **private equity / venture capital**;
- **Mutual Funds Law** and related;
- **Speed** of fund establishment a key competitive
  advantage;
- **Sophisticated** legal and accounting professional
  infrastructure.

### Banking

- **Substantial** banking sector;
- **International** banks with significant Cayman presence;
- **Captive** banking;
- **Wholesale** market focus.

### Insurance / captives

- **Major** captive insurance jurisdiction (smaller than
  Bermuda or Vermont but significant);
- **Segregated Portfolio Companies (SPC)** — Cayman
  equivalent of PCC.

### Trusts / private wealth

- **STAR trusts** (Special Trusts — Alternative Regime);
- **Established** trust industry;
- **Specific** Cayman trust structures.

### Companies

- **Exempted companies** widely used for international
  business;
- **LLC** vehicles;
- **Specific** corporate structures.

## Regulator

The **Cayman Islands Monetary Authority (CIMA)**:

- **Integrated** financial-services regulator;
- **Established** under the Monetary Authority Law (1996);
- **Independent** of government in day-to-day operation;
- **Comprehensive** regulation of banks, insurance, funds,
  trust companies, etc.

## Distinctive Cayman features

### Mutual Funds Law

- **Specific** Mutual Funds Law for hedge / mutual funds;
- **Faster** registration than many other jurisdictions;
- **Sophisticated** investor categories.

### STAR trusts

- **Special Trusts — Alternative Regime** introduced 1997;
- **Non-charitable purpose** trusts with **enforcer**;
- **Used** for orphan structures, family-governance;
- **Similar** in function to Jersey purpose trusts but with
  specific Cayman features.

See [`star-trusts.md`](./star-trusts.md).

### Segregated Portfolio Companies (SPC)

- **Cayman** equivalent of PCC;
- **Widely used** for fund umbrella, insurance;
- **Specific** Cayman SPC rules.

### LLC

- **Limited Liability Company** vehicle;
- **US-style** LLC features;
- **Specific** Cayman LLC Law (2016).

### Tax

- **No income tax** (corporate or personal);
- **No** capital gains tax;
- **No** inheritance tax;
- **No** GST / VAT;
- **Indirect taxes** through customs duties, fees;
- **Comprehensive zero-tax** environment.

## Comparison with Jersey

| Feature | Jersey | Cayman |
|---|---|---|
| Status | Crown Dependency | British Overseas Territory |
| Population | ~107k | ~80k |
| Tax | Zero-ten plus GST | **No income tax**; customs / fees |
| Currency | Jersey pound | Cayman Islands dollar (CI$1=US$1.20) |
| Fund domicile | Significant | **Global #1 for hedge funds** |
| Trust regime | TJL 1984 | Cayman + STAR trusts |
| Regulator | JFSC | CIMA |
| Privy Council | Yes | Yes |
| Heritage | Norman | British colonial / commonwealth |
