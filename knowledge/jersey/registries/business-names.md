# Jersey Business Names Register

The **Business Names Register** is the public register of
**business names** used by Jersey businesses other than under
their legal name. It is maintained under the **Registration of
Business Names (Jersey) Law 1956**.

## When registration is required

A Jersey business **must register** a business name where:

- A **sole trader** trades under a name other than their personal
  name;
- A **partnership** trades under a name other than the partners'
  surnames;
- A **company** trades under a name other than its registered
  corporate name.

## What is registered

For each business name:

- The **business name**;
- The **principal place of business** in Jersey;
- The **identity of the proprietor** (sole trader, partners, or
  company);
- Other prescribed particulars.

## Public access

The register is **publicly searchable** — allowing counterparties
to verify who is behind a trading name.

## Why this matters

Trading-name registration:

- **Protects counterparties** by identifying who is legally
  responsible for the business;
- **Supports enforcement** of debts and contracts;
- **Prevents** misleading trading names;
- **Enables** AML / CFT due diligence on counterparties.
