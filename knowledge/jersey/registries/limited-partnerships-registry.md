# Limited Partnerships Registry

Jersey maintains registers for **partnership-form** vehicles
under three distinct laws:

## Limited Partnerships (Jersey) Law 1994

A **limited partnership (LP)**:

- **One or more general partners** with **unlimited
  liability**;
- **One or more limited partners** with liability capped at
  their contribution;
- **Not** a separate legal entity (unlike corporate forms);
- **General partner** acts on behalf of the partnership.

**Registration**:

- LP filed with JFSC;
- **Public register** entry;
- **Partnership agreement** typically private;
- **Annual filings** required.

**Common use**:

- **Investment fund** vehicles (especially private equity,
  venture capital);
- **Joint venture** vehicles;
- **Management** vehicles for family offices.

## Separate Limited Partnerships (Jersey) Law 2011

**Separate limited partnerships (SLP)**:

- Same as LP **except** has **separate legal personality**;
- **Can hold property** in own name;
- **Can sue / be sued** in own name;
- **Like a corporate**, distinct from partners.

**SLPs** combine **partnership tax transparency** (where
applicable) with **corporate** legal personality. Common for:

- **International** investment structures;
- **Cross-border** real estate;
- **Specific** fund configurations.

## Incorporated Limited Partnerships (Jersey) Law 2011

**Incorporated limited partnerships (ILP)**:

- **Body corporate** with separate legal personality;
- **General partner / limited partner** structure preserved;
- **Stronger** corporate veil than SLP;
- **Similar** features to corporate forms but partnership
  governance.

## Limited Liability Partnerships (Jersey) Law 2017

**Limited liability partnerships (LLP)**:

- **All partners** have limited liability;
- **No** unlimited-liability general partner;
- **Body corporate** with separate legal personality;
- **Designed** for professional firms (accountants, lawyers
  trading internationally) and similar uses.

## Registration features

Common registration features:

- **Names** with appropriate suffix ("LP", "SLP", "ILP",
  "LLP");
- **Registered office** in Jersey;
- **General partner / designated members** notified;
- **Annual** filings;
- **Specific** changes notified.

## Fund-vehicle use

Many Jersey funds use partnership-form:

- **Private equity** — LP structure typical;
- **Hedge funds** — sometimes corporate, sometimes LP;
- **Real estate funds** — LP or SLP;
- **Carry / co-invest** vehicles — typically LP.

## Substance considerations

LPs and similar are **in scope** for the Substance Law 2019
where they conduct relevant activities. **General partners**
typically substance-relevant.

## Tax characterisation

For Jersey tax:

- LPs **transparent** (partners taxed on their share);
- SLPs / ILPs / LLPs may be **transparent or opaque**
  depending on configuration;
- **MCIT** considerations for large multinational partnership
  structures.

For foreign tax:

- **Variable** — depends on the foreign jurisdiction's
  characterisation;
- **Check-the-box** election in US;
- **Specific** local rules.
