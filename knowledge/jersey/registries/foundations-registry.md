# Jersey Foundations Registry

The **Jersey Foundations Registry** is the public register of
Jersey foundations, operated by the JFSC under the **Foundations
(Jersey) Law 2009**.

## What the Registry holds

For every Jersey foundation:

- **Charter** — the foundation's constitutive document (public);
- **Council member identities**;
- **Guardian identity**;
- **Qualified member** (JFSC-regulated council member);
- **Registered office**;
- **Name and registered number**;
- **Annual confirmation** filings;
- **Major filings** — amendments to charter, change of council
  / guardian.

Crucially:

- The **regulations** (the foundation's internal rules) are
  **private** — not registered or publicly accessible;
- **Beneficial interests** are typically private.

## Public access

The Registry permits public searches similar to the Companies
Registry — names, basic identification details, charter, council
composition.

## The charter / regulations split

The split is a **distinctive feature** of the Jersey foundation
regime:

- **Public charter** — establishes the foundation's existence and
  basic identity;
- **Private regulations** — set out the operational and beneficial
  detail;
- **Public ↔ private** transparency balance — establishment is
  publicly verifiable, internal operations are privacy-protected.
