# Registry Access Procedures — How to Search Jersey Registers

## What this is about

This file describes **how** to search Jersey's public registers
— online portals, fee schedules, available information, search
limitations.

## The principal registers

### JFSC online registry

The **JFSC operates** the principal Jersey public registers:

- **Companies** register (Companies (Jersey) Law 1991);
- **Foundations** register (Foundations (Jersey) Law 2009);
- **Business names** register;
- **Limited partnerships**;
- **Incorporated limited partnerships**;
- **Limited liability partnerships**;
- **Public funds**;
- **Charities** register (separately by Charity Commissioner);
- **VASP** register.

**Search**: jerseyfsc.org → Registry → "Search Registry"

### Royal Court land registry

The **Royal Court Public Registry** maintains:

- **Real property** records (transactions, mortgages,
  hypothecs);
- **Specific** historic record types;
- Access through **Royal Court office**.

### Other registers

- **Vehicle** register — DVS;
- **Births / marriages / deaths** — Superintendent Registrar;
- **Beneficial ownership** central register (limited public
  access).

## Free vs paid information

### Free / minimal-charge access

- **Company name** confirmation;
- **Status** (active, dissolved);
- **Registered office** address;
- **Director names** (current);
- **Specific** basic details.

### Standard fee access

- **Full corporate** record;
- **Annual returns**;
- **Charges register**;
- **Memorandum and articles**;
- **Specific** document searches.

### Higher-fee access

- **Historical** records;
- **Charity** detailed records;
- **VASP** register details;
- **Bulk** data searches.

## Information available — example: company search

For a Jersey company search you typically obtain:

- **Company name** and any former names;
- **Registration number**;
- **Date of incorporation**;
- **Status** (active, dissolved, struck off);
- **Registered office** address;
- **Articles of association** (filed amendments);
- **Directors** (current and historical);
- **Secretary**;
- **Annual returns** filed;
- **Charges** registered;
- **Specific** filings (capital reductions, mergers).

## Information NOT generally available

The following are **NOT** generally available through public
registry search:

- **Beneficial ownership** detail (subject to specific rules
  on authorities' access);
- **Trust** details — Jersey trusts are **not registered**;
- **Foundation regulations** (the private constitutional
  document);
- **Bank account** details;
- **Tax filings**;
- **Trust deeds**;
- **Specific** confidential filings.

## Beneficial ownership

Jersey's **central register of beneficial ownership** under
the FS(DPI)(J)L 2020:

- **Authorities** have access;
- **Limited public access** (current Jersey position);
- **Specific** legitimate-interest access in some cases;
- **Compared with**: UK's PSC register has **full public
  access** to beneficial owners.

The Jersey position **may evolve** under international
pressure for greater transparency.

## Specific search scenarios

### KYC / due diligence on a Jersey company

- **JFSC registry** for basic corporate details;
- **Land registry** for real property holdings;
- **Charges register** for security interests;
- **Annual returns** for current status;
- **Direct CDD** request for beneficial ownership and trust
  information.

### Identifying a Jersey trust

- **Not** in public registers — trusts are not registered;
- **CDD** through service providers;
- **Specific** disclosure routes through court procedures;
- **Beneficial-ownership** register through authority channels.

### Litigation searches

- **Royal Court** filings (some public, some not);
- **Specific** notice of proceedings if filed;
- **Limited** general access to civil court files.

### Real-estate transactions

- **Land registry** for prior transactions;
- **Charges** over the property;
- **Planning consents** through Planning Department;
- **CHWL status** verification through Population Office.

## Authorised representative access

Some registry searches require:

- **Identification** of the searcher;
- **Specific purpose**;
- **Subscription** to specific services (for bulk users);
- **API access** for service providers.
