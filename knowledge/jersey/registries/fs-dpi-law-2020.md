# Financial Services (Disclosure and Provision of Information) (Jersey) Law 2020

The **Financial Services (Disclosure and Provision of
Information) (Jersey) Law 2020 (FS(DPI)(J)L)** is Jersey's
**central beneficial-ownership** legislation.

It establishes:

- The **central register** of beneficial owners;
- **Reporting obligations** on entities;
- **Access** rules (authorities; limited others);
- **Penalties** for non-compliance.

## What it covers

The Law applies to:

- **Jersey companies**;
- **Jersey foundations**;
- **Jersey limited partnerships** and similar;
- **Specific other** entity types prescribed.

## Beneficial owner definition

A **beneficial owner** is a natural person who:

- **Ultimately owns or controls** 25%+ of the entity (shares,
  voting rights, capital);
- **Otherwise exercises control** over the entity;
- Acts on behalf of someone who would meet either test.

For **trusts**:

- **Settlor**;
- **Trustees** (each);
- **Protector**;
- **Beneficiaries** (named, plus class);
- **Other** persons with effective control.

For **foundations**:

- **Founder**;
- **Council members** (each);
- **Guardian**;
- **Beneficiaries**;
- **Other** persons with effective control.

## Reporting obligations

Entities must:

- **Identify** their beneficial owners;
- **Maintain** internal BO register;
- **Report** to the central register;
- **Update** within prescribed period of changes;
- **Refresh** periodically.

## Access

### Authority access

Full access by:

- **JFSC**;
- **Comptroller of Revenue**;
- **States of Jersey Police**;
- **Attorney General**;
- **FIU**;
- **Foreign authorities** through MoU / treaty channels.

### Limited public access

Current Jersey position:

- **No general public** access (unlike UK PSC register);
- **Specific** legitimate-interest access in some configurations;
- **Subject to** international developments and pressures.

The **non-public** approach is a key Jersey policy choice
balancing:

- **Privacy** for legitimate users;
- **Transparency** for AML / tax purposes (via authorities);
- **International** expectations evolving.

## Penalties

For non-compliance:

- **Civil penalties** on the entity;
- **Civil penalties** on responsible persons (directors,
  council members);
- **Criminal offences** for serious / intentional breaches;
- **JFSC** enforcement action;
- **Strike-off** in extreme cases of persistent non-compliance.

## International coordination

The Law operates with:

- **UK BO register** (PSC register) — alignment;
- **EU 5th AMLD** transparency standards — Jersey aligns
  selectively;
- **FATF Recommendation 24** — Jersey compliance documented;
- **Specific** information sharing with foreign authorities.
