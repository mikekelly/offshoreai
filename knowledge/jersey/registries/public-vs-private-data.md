# Public vs Private Data in Jersey Registers

## What this is about

This file explains **what data is public and what is private**
in Jersey's various registers — useful for KYC, journalists,
researchers, and counterparties trying to understand what's
discoverable.

## Companies

### Public

- **Name** (current and former);
- **Registration number**;
- **Date of incorporation**;
- **Status** (active, dissolved);
- **Registered office** address;
- **Memorandum and articles** of association;
- **Directors** (current and historical);
- **Secretary**;
- **Annual returns**;
- **Material capital changes** (reductions, increases beyond
  authorised);
- **Charges** registered.

### Private (with authority access)

- **Beneficial owners** (FS(DPI)(J)L 2020 central register);
- **Shareholder identities** (not always public, depending on
  the company form);
- **Specific** AML / CDD information held by service providers;
- **Tax filings**.

### Private entirely

- **Internal** board records;
- **Internal** financial statements (subject to specific
  audit / filing requirements);
- **Commercial** contracts;
- **Customer / supplier** lists.

## Foundations

### Public

- **Name**;
- **Date of establishment**;
- **Status**;
- **Registered office**;
- **Council members**;
- **Qualified member**;
- **Charter** (the constitutional document);
- **Annual returns**;
- **Major filings**.

### Private

- **Regulations** (the private constitutional document);
- **Beneficiaries** (not in public charter / not in central
  register beyond authority access);
- **Distribution records**;
- **Letters of wishes**;
- **Internal** governance documents.

## Trusts

### Public

- **Nothing** about specific trusts is in any public Jersey
  register;
- Trusts are **not registered**;
- **Indirect** information through court records if litigation;
- **Specific** trust company business statistics aggregated.

### Private (with authority access)

- **Settlor, trustees, protector, beneficiaries** through the
  FS(DPI)(J)L 2020 BO register;
- **Trust deeds, distribution records** through CDD held by
  trustees and accessible by authorities through court / MLA.

## Charities

### Public

- **Name**;
- **Charitable purposes**;
- **Trustees**;
- **Annual returns** (for general section);
- **Material** changes.

### Private (restricted section)

- **Specific** beneficiary information;
- **Detailed** financial information;
- **Specific** family-charity arrangements.

## Real property

### Public

- **Property transactions** through Royal Court records;
- **Mortgages** and **charges**;
- **Specific** historic records;
- **Planning consents** through Planning Department.

### Private

- **Internal valuations**;
- **Property condition** reports;
- **Specific** lease terms in some cases;
- **Buyer / seller** identities not always public (subject to
  Royal Court process visibility).

## Funds

### Public funds (CIFL)

- **Name and authorisation status**;
- **Functionaries**;
- **Prospectus** (in many cases public);
- **Major** filings.

### Private funds (Expert / EIF / JPF)

- **Existence** typically in JFSC register;
- **Detailed terms** private;
- **Investor** identities not public;
- **Specific** offering materials private.

## VASP register

- **Existence** of registered VASP;
- **Categories** of permitted activities;
- **Status**.
