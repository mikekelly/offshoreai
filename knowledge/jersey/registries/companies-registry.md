# Jersey Companies Registry

The **Jersey Companies Registry** is the public register of
Jersey companies, operated by the **JFSC** as **Registrar of
Companies** under Part 23 of the Companies (Jersey) Law 1991.

## What the Registry holds

For every Jersey company:

- **Incorporation details** — name, date, registered number,
  company type;
- **Memorandum and articles** of association;
- **Annual confirmation statements**;
- **Director and secretary** identities;
- **Registered office** address;
- **Share-capital structure**;
- **Major filings** — special resolutions, mergers, continuances,
  cell creation;
- **Dissolution / strike-off** records.

## Public access

Most Registry records are **publicly accessible** for a fee:

- **Search** by company name or number;
- **Online portal** for routine searches;
- **Certificate of good standing** orders;
- **Document copy** orders.

Some information is **restricted** for privacy reasons (notably
director home addresses).

## Online filing

Most filings are made through the JFSC's **online portal**:

- **Incorporation applications**;
- **Annual confirmation statements**;
- **Director / secretary change notices**;
- **Share allotment** notices;
- **Other prescribed filings**.

Paper filing remains available but is increasingly the exception.
