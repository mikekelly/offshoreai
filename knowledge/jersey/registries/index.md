# Registries — Jersey

The Island operates several statutory registers, most of them within
the **JFSC**:

- The **Companies Registry** (incorporations, annual returns, name
  changes).
- The **Foundations Registry** (foundations under the 2009 Law).
- The **Business Names** register.
- The **Limited Liability Partnerships** and **Limited Partnerships**
  registers.
- The **Virtual Asset Service Providers (VASP)** register.
- The **Beneficial Ownership and Significant Persons** register
  required under the **Financial Services (Disclosure and Provision of
  Information) (Jersey) Law 2020** — not publicly accessible but
  available to law-enforcement and regulators.
- The **Public Register of Persons with Significant Control** — a
  more limited public element of the disclosure regime.

Outside the JFSC:

- The **Public Registry** of contracts (notably the *Hypothèques* or
  charges over Jersey land) at the Royal Court.
- The **Land Registry**, where freehold and qualifying interests in
  Jersey land are recorded.
- The **Charities Register**, maintained by the Charity Commissioner
  under the Charities (Jersey) Law 2014.
