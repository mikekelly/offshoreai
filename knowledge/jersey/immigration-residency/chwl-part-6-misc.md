# CHWL 2012 Part 6 — Miscellaneous

Part 6 consolidates administrative matters.

## Articles 39-43 — Regulations and Orders

The Minister may make **Orders** for:

- **Specific** status categories and acquisition rules;
- **Sectoral** quotas;
- **Specific** licence categories (HVR, essential worker);
- **Property categorisation**;
- **Forms and fees**;
- **Procedural** requirements.

## Articles 44-46 — Population Office

The **Population Office**:

- **Administers** the CHWL;
- **Headed by** the Population Director;
- **Reports** to the Chief Minister / specific Minister;
- **Liaises** with related bodies (planning, employment,
  social security).

## Article 47 — Cooperation

The Population Office cooperates with:

- **JFSC** for regulated entities;
- **Comptroller of Revenue** for tax matters;
- **Social Security** for benefits-status alignment;
- **Planning Department** for property categorisation;
- **Other** government departments.

## Article 48 — Annual review

Annual review of:

- **CHWL** operation and effectiveness;
- **Population statistics** and projections;
- **Sectoral** workforce composition;
- **Housing** market and affordability.

## Article 49 — Transitional

Transitional provisions:

- **Pre-2012** status categories (predecessor Law) migrated
  to current categories;
- **Specific** legacy arrangements;
- **Grandfathered** rights.

## Article 50 — Citation

The Law is cited as the **"Control of Housing and Work
(Jersey) Law 2012"**.

It came into force in **2012** and has been amended several
times:

- **2018** — specific procedural updates;
- **Specific** category refinements;
- **HVR** rule updates;
- **Specific** Brexit-related adjustments for cross-border
  workers.
