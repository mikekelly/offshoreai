# CHWL 2012 Part 3 — Housing Controls (residual)

Article 10 (restrictions on residential property) has its own
deep file. This cluster covers Articles 11-18.

## Article 11 — Consents

The Population Office may grant **consents** for non-Entitled
persons to occupy qualified property:

- **Essential workers** (sponsored by employer);
- **HVR** (Article 135A);
- **Specific** family circumstances;
- **Specific time-limited** scenarios.

Consents are:

- **Conditional** typically;
- **Time-limited**;
- **Revocable** in specific circumstances;
- **Reviewable** on changed circumstances.

## Article 12 — Lodgings registration

**Lodging houses** must be registered:

- **Operator** licensed by Population Office;
- **Standards** of accommodation;
- **Records** of occupants;
- **Periodic inspection**.

## Article 13 — Second-home rules

Specific rules for **second / holiday homes**:

- **Restrictions** on additional property acquisition;
- **Subject to** specific consent;
- **Specific** rural / coastal area protection;
- **Coordinated** with planning law.

## Article 14 — Family rules

Family arrangements:

- **Family of Entitled** person can typically occupy same
  property;
- **Specific** rules for adult children continuing in
  family home;
- **Inheritance** of property by status-appropriate family;
- **Specific** transitional treatment.

## Article 15 — Tenancy rules

Tenancy arrangements:

- **Landlord** must verify tenant status;
- **Tenancy agreements** subject to CHWL compliance;
- **Tenant** of qualified property must have proper status /
  consent;
- **Penalties** for landlords letting to non-qualifying
  tenants.

## Article 16 — Property transactions

Real-property transactions:

- **Conveyancing** through Royal Court (contract passing);
- **Status** verified at completion;
- **Notice** to Population Office for certain transactions;
- **Compliance** with CHWL specifically certified.

## Article 17 — Specific property categories

Specific Article-defined categories:

- **Social housing** (specific allocation rules);
- **Sheltered housing**;
- **Specific** historic / heritage property;
- **Commercial** property converted to residential (specific
  rules).

## Article 18 — Property records

The Population Office maintains:

- **Records** of property categorisation;
- **Consents** granted;
- **Occupation** of qualified property;
- **Available** for verification by parties to transactions.
