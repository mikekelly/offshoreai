# CHWL 2012 Article 3 — Status Categories

Article 3 establishes the **four-category** status system that
governs housing and employment access in Jersey.

## The four categories

### Entitled

The **highest** status:

- **Acquired** by:
  - Born in Jersey of parents with Jersey status;
  - Long-term residence (typically 10 years);
  - Other defined routes.
- **Full** housing rights (can occupy any residential
  property);
- **Full** employment rights (can take any job);
- **No** specific approval needed for housing or work
  beyond status itself.

### Licensed

A **special** category for:

- **Essential workers** (specific sectors, specific employer
  sponsorship);
- **High-Value Residents (HVR)** (Article 135A regime — see
  HVR-specific files);
- **Specific** categories prescribed by Order.

Licensed persons have:

- **Restricted** housing rights (specific consent typically
  needed);
- **Specific** employment in line with their licence (e.g.,
  bound to specific employer);
- **Time-limited** in many cases.

### Entitled for work

A category for:

- **Long-term residents** acquiring work entitlement (typically
  5 years' continuous residence);
- Some **categories** of qualified workers.

Entitled-for-work persons:

- **Restricted** housing rights;
- **Full** work rights.

### Registered

The **most restricted** category:

- **Newcomers** to Jersey;
- **Short-term** workers;
- **Specific** categories of registered persons;
- **Subject to** specific employer permission for work;
- **Significant** housing restrictions.

## Acquiring status

Movement between categories:

- **Through residence** — typically progresses with continuous
  Jersey residence;
- **Through specific** Population Office decisions;
- **Through marriage** to person of higher status (specific
  rules);
- **Through specific** programs (HVR);
- **Lost** through extended absence.

## Status determines housing and employment

The categories are **load-bearing**:

- **Housing** — only Entitled persons have unrestricted
  housing access; others have specific consents needed;
- **Employment** — only Entitled (and Entitled for work) have
  unrestricted work; others bound to specific employer /
  business;
- **Both** controls interlink through CHWL.

## Specific scenarios

### Newcomer arriving from UK

- Initial: **Registered** status;
- After **5 years** continuous Jersey residence: **Entitled
  for work** possible;
- After **10 years**: **Entitled** typically possible;
- **Specific** route depending on personal circumstances.

### High-Value Resident (HVR) under Article 135A

- **Licensed** status under specific HVR rules;
- **Allowed** to acquire qualified property;
- **Subject to** specific HVR-prescribed conditions;
- See [`high-value-residency.md`](high-value-residency.md).

### Inter-spouse rules

- **Marriage** to higher-status partner may accelerate status
  acquisition;
- **Specific** time and residency requirements still apply;
- **Specific** treatment of children.
