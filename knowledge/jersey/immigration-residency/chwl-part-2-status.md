# CHWL 2012 Part 2 — Status (residual)

Article 3 (categories) has its own deep file. This cluster
covers Articles 4-9.

## Article 4 — Acquiring entitled status

Routes to **Entitled** status:

- **Born in Jersey** of one or both Entitled parents;
- **10+ years** continuous Jersey residence;
- **Specific routes** for children of Jersey nationals;
- **Marriage** to Entitled spouse with continued residence;
- **Specific** other routes.

## Article 5 — Licensed status

**Licensed** status is granted by Population Office decision:

- **Essential workers** sponsored by specific employer in
  specific sector;
- **HVR** under Article 135A regime;
- **Specific** other categories (Crown servants, diplomats);
- **Conditions** and **duration** specified in licence.

## Article 6 — Entitled for work

**Entitled for work** typically requires:

- **5+ years** continuous Jersey residence;
- **No** intervening loss of status;
- **Application** to Population Office;
- **Approval** typically granted on threshold meeting.

## Article 7 — Registered status

**Registered** status is the **default** for newcomers:

- **Sub-categories** within Registered;
- **Permission to work** tied to specific employer / business;
- **Limited housing** access;
- **Time-limited** in many cases.

## Article 8 — Loss of status

Status can be **lost** through:

- **Extended absence** from Jersey (typically 12 months
  without break);
- **Voluntary surrender**;
- **Specific events** (loss of sponsoring employer for
  Licensed, etc.);
- **Lifestyle / behaviour** in extreme cases.

## Article 9 — Children

Status of children:

- **Born to Entitled parents** in Jersey — Entitled by birth;
- **Born to other-status parents** — same status as parent;
- **Acquired status** on parent's status change in some
  configurations;
- **Specific rules** for minors moving between statuses on
  parental status changes.
