# CHWL 2012 Part 4 — Employment Controls (residual)

Article 19 has its own deep file. This cluster covers
Articles 20-26.

## Article 20 — Business licences

Carrying on **business** in Jersey requires:

- **Business licence** under the Control of Housing and
  Work Law;
- **Specific** business categorisation;
- **Workforce composition** consistent with sectoral
  controls;
- **Quotas** on Registered staff in some sectors.

## Article 21 — Sectoral controls

Specific sectors have **dedicated controls**:

- **Construction** — specific permits, training requirements;
- **Hospitality** — seasonal worker permissions;
- **Agriculture** — seasonal worker permits;
- **Healthcare** — specific qualified-worker rules;
- **Financial services** — JFSC fit-and-proper plus CHWL
  status.

## Article 22 — Quotas

Some sectors / businesses are subject to **quotas**:

- **Maximum** number of Registered (newcomer) employees;
- **Pro rata** to total workforce;
- **Specific** roles must be filled by Entitled / Entitled
  for work where possible;
- **Quotas** managed by Population Office in conjunction
  with industry.

## Article 23 — Self-employment

Self-employment is **employment** for CHWL purposes:

- **Status** governs ability to self-employ;
- **Business** must be permitted activity for the status;
- **Business licence** required for substantive operations;
- **Specific** rules for sole-trader self-employment.

## Article 24 — Director / officer roles

**Directorships** and **officer roles**:

- For **commercial** business (e.g., director of an
  operating Jersey company) — status-governed;
- For **service-provider** directorships (TCB directors of
  client companies) — separately governed by JFSC TCB
  authorisation;
- **Specific** rules for non-resident directors.

## Article 25 — Workplace inspections

The Population Office may inspect:

- **Premises** of businesses;
- **Employment records**;
- **Worker status** documentation;
- **Compliance** with quotas;
- **Specific** sectoral checks.

## Article 26 — Cross-jurisdictional employment

Specific rules for:

- **Cross-border** workers (UK / France commuting);
- **Remote workers** with Jersey employer;
- **Temporary** assignments from foreign affiliates;
- **Specific** EU / UK arrangements where applicable.
