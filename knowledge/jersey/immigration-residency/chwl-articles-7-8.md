# CHWL 2012 Articles 7-8 — Registered Status and Loss of Status

## Article 7 — Registered status

**Registered** status is the **default** for newcomers:

- **Sub-categories** within Registered;
- **Permission to work** tied to specific employer / business;
- **Limited housing** access;
- **Time-limited** in many cases.

### Process

- **Newcomer** arrives in Jersey;
- **Employer** sponsors employment;
- **Population Office** registers the person;
- **Specific** workplace identified;
- **Specific** time limits per sectoral rules.

### Progression

A Registered person can typically:

- Apply for **Entitled for work** after **5 years**
  continuous residence;
- Apply for **Entitled** after **10 years** continuous
  residence;
- **Subject to** specific procedural requirements;
- **Documentation** of continuity.

## Article 8 — Loss of status

Status can be **lost** through:

### Extended absence

- **12 months or more** without break typically;
- **Specific** rules on what counts as absence;
- **Specific** exceptions for medical, educational reasons.

### Voluntary surrender

- **Specific** procedure;
- **Documented** decision;
- **Irrevocable** in most cases.

### Specific events

- **Loss of sponsoring employer** for Licensed (essential
  worker);
- **Specific** trigger events under regulations;
- **Specific** breach of conditions.

### Extreme misconduct

- **Specific** behaviour-based grounds;
- **Rare** in practice;
- **Specific** procedural protections.

## Reapplication

- **Specific** procedures for status renewal / reapplication;
- **Subject to** Population Office discretion;
- **Specific** considerations for previous status loss.
