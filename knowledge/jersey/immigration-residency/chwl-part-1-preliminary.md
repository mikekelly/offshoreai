# CHWL 2012 Part 1 — Preliminary

## Article 1 — Interpretation

Key definitions:

- **"Entitled"** — highest residential / employment status
  category;
- **"Licensed"** — second-tier category (essential workers,
  HVR);
- **"Entitled for work"** — third-tier (qualified residents);
- **"Registered"** — fourth-tier (work permits, restricted);
- **"Population Office"** / **"Population Director"** —
  administering body within government;
- **"Housing qualifications"** — formal status required to
  occupy qualified residential property;
- **"Employment permission"** — formal status required to
  work in Jersey;
- **"Qualified property"** — residential property restricted
  to certain status categories.

## Article 2 — Application

The Law applies to:

- **Residential property** in Jersey (specific categories);
- **Employment** in Jersey;
- **All persons** (Jersey citizens, UK citizens, foreign
  nationals);
- **Specific exemptions** (diplomatic, certain categories of
  Crown servants, etc.).

The Law operates **in addition to** UK immigration law (which
controls **entry** into the Common Travel Area including
Jersey) — CHWL addresses **internal Jersey** housing /
employment access.
