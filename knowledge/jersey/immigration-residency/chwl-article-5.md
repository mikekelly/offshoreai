# CHWL 2012 Article 5 — Licensed Status

Article 5 covers **Licensed** status.

## What Licensed status is

Licensed status is granted by **Population Office** for:

- **Essential workers** sponsored by specific employer in
  specific sector;
- **HVR (High-Value Residents)** under Article 135A regime;
- **Specific other categories** (Crown servants, diplomats);
- **Specific** other prescribed groups.

## Application

Application is to the **Population Office**:

- **Sponsoring employer** (for essential workers);
- **Individual application** (for HVR);
- **Specific** documentation;
- **Fit-and-proper** consideration;
- **Specific** discretionary factors.

## Effect of Licensed status

Licensed persons have:

- **Restricted** housing rights (specific consent typically
  needed);
- **Specific** employment rights in line with their licence
  (bound to specific employer for essential workers);
- **Time-limited** in many cases;
- **HVR-specific** tax regime if HVR Licensed.

## Conditions

Specific conditions apply:

- **Specific** job / employer for essential workers;
- **Specific** property purchase for HVR;
- **Specific** ongoing reporting;
- **Specific** revocation grounds.
