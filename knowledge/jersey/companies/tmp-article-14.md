# Takeovers Panel Law 2009 Article 14 — Compensation

Article 14 enables the Panel — through rules — to order
**compensation payments** by parties in breach of rules
requiring the payment of money.

## Key provisions

- Rules may **authorise the Panel** to order compensation
  for breach of money-payment rules.
- Compensation may be ordered in amounts the Panel "thinks
  just and reasonable."
- Compensation may include **compound interest**.

## Operational use

The most common application: where a bidder has failed to
pay the required Rule 9 mandatory-offer price to all
shareholders, or has paid different prices to different
shareholders contrary to equal-treatment rules. The Panel
orders compensation to bring the affected shareholders to
the position they would have been in had the rule been
complied with.

This is distinct from a private cause of action — see
[Article 16](./tmp-article-16.md), which clarifies that
breach of statutory duty does not create private causes of
action.
