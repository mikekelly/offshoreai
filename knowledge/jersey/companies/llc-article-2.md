# LLC Law 2018 Article 2 — Limited liability company

**The constitutional foundation** of the Jersey LLC.

## Key provisions

- LLCs may be **registered for lawful purposes**;
- An LLC has **legal personality separate from its members**;
- LLCs are **not bodies corporate** (subject to potential modification under [Article 2A](./llc-article-2a.md)).

## Operational significance

This formulation mirrors the **Delaware LLC** —separate legal personality without body-corporate character. Combined with the flexible operating-agreement framework, the Jersey LLC provides Delaware-LLC-equivalent functionality under Jersey law.
