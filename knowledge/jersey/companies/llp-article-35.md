# LLP Law 2017 Article 35 — Criminal liability of partners, directors and other officers

Article 35 makes **individuals personally liable** for
entity offences committed with their **consent, connivance,
or attributable to their neglect**.

## Key provisions

- Where an offence under the Law is committed by an LLP (or
  another entity), **partners, directors, and other
  officers** may be personally liable;
- The test is **consent, connivance, or neglect** —
  the individual contributed to the offence either
  actively or by failing in their oversight role;
- Liability of the entity is not displaced — both the
  entity and the individual may be prosecuted.
