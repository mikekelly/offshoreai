# LP Law 1994 Article 4 — Registration of declaration

Article 4 is the **mechanism by which an LP comes into
existence** — a signed declaration delivered to the
registrar, with the LP formally established on issue of a
certificate of registration.

## Provision

Before an LP is formed:

- A **signed declaration** must be delivered to the
  registrar;
- The registrar **issues a certificate of registration**;
- The LP exists from the date of the certificate.

## Operational significance

Unlike an ordinary partnership (which exists by virtue of
agreement between the parties), an LP requires
**registration** for its limited-liability status to exist.
Trading as a "limited partnership" before registration
would be misleading and an offence.
