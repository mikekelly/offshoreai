# LP Law 1994 Article 8 — Registered office

The LP must maintain a **Jersey registered office** with
prescribed records — partner details, declarations,
partnership agreements, contribution statements.

## Significance

The Jersey registered-office requirement is the **substance
anchor** for Jersey-LP status. An LP whose registered office
is not in Jersey is in breach. The records-on-site
requirement supports the public-inspection regime under
[Article 32](./lp-article-32.md).
