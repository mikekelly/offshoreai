# SLP Law 2011 Article 26 — Dissolution of partnership on death etc., of general partner

Sole / last GP's death or withdrawal triggers **automatic dissolution** unless LPs unanimously **elect replacement GPs within 90 days**. Parallels [LP Article 24](./lp-article-24.md).
