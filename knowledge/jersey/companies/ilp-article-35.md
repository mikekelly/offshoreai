# ILP Law 2011 Article 35 — Regulations

The **States may enact Regulations** addressing:

- **Partner disqualifications**;
- **Audit requirements**;
- Potentially incorporating **CJL 1991 and Foundations Law
  2009 provisions** by reference.

The cross-reference to CJL 1991 and Foundations Law 2009 is
useful — it permits the Regulations to import body-corporate
mechanics from the company / foundation statutes rather than
duplicating them in primary legislation.
