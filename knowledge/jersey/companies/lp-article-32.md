# LP Law 1994 Article 32 — Inspection and production of documents kept by registrar

**Public inspection rights** for registered documents. The
registrar may **certify copies** for evidentiary use.
Certain information may be restricted from public access per
the
[FSDI Law 2020](../registries/fs-dpi-law-2020.md)
beneficial-ownership framework.
