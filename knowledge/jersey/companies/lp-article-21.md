# LP Law 1994 Article 21 — Assignments

Partnership interest assignments require either:

- **Unanimous partner consent**; or
- **Partnership agreement provision** permitting assignment.

Assignees become limited partners **only on registration**.

## Operational significance

In PE LPs, the partnership agreement typically:

- Permits assignment with GP consent (not unanimous);
- Conditions assignment on transferee compliance with KYC,
  accredited-investor / professional-investor warranties,
  and tax representations;
- Allows secondary-market transactions to proceed without
  disruption to fund operations.

For traditional non-fund LPs, the unanimous-consent default
makes assignment harder — by design.
