# LP Law 1994 Article 6 — General and limited partners

Article 6 permits one person to hold both **GP status and
LP status** in the same LP simultaneously. The dual-status
person:

- Has the GP's unlimited liability for LP obligations in
  respect of the GP capacity; and
- Has the LP's capped liability (and entitlements) in
  respect of the limited-partner contribution.

## Operational use

Common in carry vehicles and family-office LPs where the
GP entity is also an LP investor in its own LP. The Article
6 dual-status structure removes any technical bar to this
arrangement.
