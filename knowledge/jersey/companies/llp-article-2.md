# LLP Law 2017 Article 2 — Limited liability partnership

Article 2 establishes the **foundational constitutional
provisions** of the Jersey LLP — what it is and what it does
as a legal entity.

## Key provisions

- A limited liability partnership is **registered** under
  the Law in accordance with the procedure in
  [Article 18](./llp-article-18.md);
- An LLP is a **distinct legal person**, separate from
  its members;
- The LLP **has perpetual succession** until cancelled or
  dissolved under the Law.

## Operational significance

The LLP's separate legal personality and perpetual
succession distinguish it from the
[Limited Partnerships Law 1994](./lp-1994-index.md) LP
which is a contractual arrangement without independent
legal personality. The LLP can:

- **Hold property** in its own name;
- **Contract** in its own name;
- **Sue and be sued** in its own name;
- **Continue across changes in partner composition**.
