# LP Law 1994 Article 27 — Settling accounts on winding up

On winding up, the **priority of payments** is:

1. **Creditor payments** in full;
2. **Distributions to partners** per the prescribed
   sequence (which the partnership agreement may modify).

Limited partners dealing with the LP in non-partner
capacity (e.g. as lenders under
[Article 15](./lp-article-15.md)) rank as **creditors** at
the first tier — ahead of partner-distribution priority.

## Operational significance

For PE / VC funds, the winding-up distribution sequence
reflects the partnership agreement's **distribution
waterfall**:

- Return of capital contributions (priority order per
  agreement);
- Hurdle / preferred returns;
- Carry / catch-up;
- Remaining distributions per allocations.

Article 27's statutory priority applies as the default;
the partnership agreement typically elaborates extensively.
