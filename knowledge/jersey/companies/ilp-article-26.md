# ILP Law 2011 Article 26 — [Deleted]

Article 26 was **deleted by R&O.138/2020**. It originally
covered annual return requirements; the regime was
consolidated / replaced by the
[FSDI Law 2020](../registries/fs-dpi-law-2020.md).
