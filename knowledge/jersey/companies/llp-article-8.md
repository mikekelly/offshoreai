# LLP Law 2017 Article 8 — Limited liability partnership secretary

Article 8 requires the LLP to **appoint a secretary** from
the date of registration. The secretary discharges
prescribed administrative functions on behalf of the LLP.

## Key provisions

- An LLP must, at all times from registration, have a
  **qualified secretary** appointed;
- The secretary's responsibilities include the records
  maintenance under [Article 9](./llp-article-9.md);
- The secretary's qualifications and obligations are set
  out in regulations / orders made under the Law.

## Operational significance

The secretary requirement was introduced in the 2017 Law to
replace the prior "designated partners" architecture from
the 1997 Law. The secretary is the operational locus of
LLP administration distinct from the partner population.
