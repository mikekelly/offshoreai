# LP Law 1994 Article 29 — Authority to sign

Creates **conclusive presumptions favouring non-partners**
regarding GP authority and document validity:

- A counterparty dealing with a GP can assume the GP has
  authority to sign on behalf of the LP;
- Documents signed by the GP are valid against the LP
  regardless of internal partnership-agreement limitations
  the counterparty was unaware of.

This protects counterparty certainty in dealings with LPs.
