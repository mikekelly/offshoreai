# LLP Law 2017 Article 40 — Rules of Court

Article 40 extends the **Court's rule-making authority** to
procedures under the Law — enabling the Royal Court to make
specific procedural rules for LLP-related applications
(dissolution, production-of-records, compliance-orders,
etc.).
