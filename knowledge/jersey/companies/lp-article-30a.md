# LP Law 1994 Article 30A — Annual administration fee

The Commission may collect a **published annual
administration fee** from LPs, payable by **28 February**
each year alongside the
[Article 5A annual confirmation](./lp-article-5a.md). Unpaid
fees are recoverable as debts.
