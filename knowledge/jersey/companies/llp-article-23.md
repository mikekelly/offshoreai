# LLP Law 2017 Article 23 — Cancellation of registration

Article 23 governs the **administrative termination** of an
LLP's registration:

- The **registrar cancels registration** on winding-up
  completion;
- The **certificate of cancellation is conclusive evidence**
  of the cancellation;
- The **Court may void cancellations within 10 years** if
  cancellation was inappropriate.
