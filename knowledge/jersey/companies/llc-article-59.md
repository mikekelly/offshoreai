# LLC Law 2018 Article 59 — Penalties

A person guilty of an offence under this Law is liable
to **imprisonment** for a term not exceeding the
prescribed maximum and / or a **fine** at the level
specified for that offence.

Penalty levels are set on conviction and may be
adjusted by Order under [Article 61](./llc-article-61.md).
