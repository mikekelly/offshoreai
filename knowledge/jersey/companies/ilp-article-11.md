# ILP Law 2011 Article 11 — Rights and obligations of general partner

The GP has **all partnership rights and powers** subject to:

- Restrictions on **impossible acts**;
- Restrictions on **unauthorised property dealings**;
- The requirement to obtain partner **consent before
  admitting new partners**.

Parallels [LP Article 11](./lp-article-11.md) but adapted
for the body-corporate ILP context.
