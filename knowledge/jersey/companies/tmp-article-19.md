# Takeovers Panel Law 2009 Article 19 — Recovery of fees, charges or levy

Article 19 treats unpaid fees, charges, or levy as
**recoverable debts** owed to the Panel.

## Key provisions

"An amount payable by any person or body by virtue of
Article 17 or 18 is a debt due from that person or body to
the Panel, and is recoverable accordingly."

## Operational significance

Translation: the Panel can sue for non-payment in the
ordinary civil-debt manner. This is distinct from the
sanctions / enforcement regime in
[Articles 13–15](./tmp-article-13.md) — Article 19 is the
**collections mechanism** for the Panel's funding base.
