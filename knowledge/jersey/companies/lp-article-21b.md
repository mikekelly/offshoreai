# LP Law 1994 Article 21B — Liability of partners following dissolution by registrar on continued default

Following registrar dissolution under
[Article 21A](./lp-article-21a.md) for continued default:

- **Limited-partner liability limitations are preserved** —
  the dissolution does not retroactively expose limited
  partners;
- **General partners bear reinstatement costs** where
  applicable.
