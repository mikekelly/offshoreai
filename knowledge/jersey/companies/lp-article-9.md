# LP Law 1994 Article 9 — Accounts and audit

The LP must maintain **sufficient accounting records**.
**Audit is optional** unless required by the partnership
agreement or regulations.

## Operational reality

For most Jersey LPs used as fund vehicles, audit is
typically conducted regardless of statutory requirement —
because:

- The fund's limited partners typically require audited
  accounts as an LP-agreement term;
- Regulatory regimes (CIF Law 1988, AIF Regulations 2012)
  may impose audit requirements at the fund level;
- The JFSC's
  [Code of Practice for Fund Services Business](../financial-regulation/cop-fsb-index.md)
  expects appropriate accounting standards.
