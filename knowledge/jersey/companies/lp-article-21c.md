# LP Law 1994 Article 21C — Limitation on exercise of powers and rights by general partner following dissolution

After dissolution, the general partner is restricted to:

- **Realising LP assets**;
- **Discharging LP liabilities**;
- **Distributing surplus**;
- **Reinstatement actions** under
  [Article 22](./lp-article-22.md).

The GP cannot continue to undertake new business or new
commitments — the LP exists only for winding-up purposes.
