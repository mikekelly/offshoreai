# ILP Law 2011 Article 16 — Return of limited partner's contribution

Contributions may only be **returned when the ILP is
solvent**. Improper returns are **repayable with interest**
for **six months post-receipt**. Parallels
[LP Article 17](./lp-article-17.md).
