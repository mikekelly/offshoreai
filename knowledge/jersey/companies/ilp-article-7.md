# ILP Law 2011 Article 7 — Name of partnership

The ILP's name must end with **"Incorporated Limited
Partnership"** or approved abbreviation (typically
**"ILP"**).

Limited partners' names cannot appear in the ILP's name
unless they are also general partners or the name was
previously carried.
