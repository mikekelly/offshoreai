# LLC Law 2018 Article 57 — Criminal liability of managers

Where an offence under this Law is committed by an LLC
with the **consent or connivance** of, or attributable
to **neglect** on the part of, a manager (or person
purporting to act as a manager), that person also
commits the offence.

Standard "officer attribution" provision aligned with
the Companies Law 1991 equivalent.
