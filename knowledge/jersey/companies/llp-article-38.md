# LLP Law 2017 Article 38 — Regulations

Article 38 empowers the **States** to make Regulations
prescribing:

- **LLP dissolution and winding-up** procedures (used
  alongside [Article 22](./llp-article-22.md));
- **Accounts preparation** standards;
- **Audit** procedures where applicable;
- Other matters as required for the Law's operation.
