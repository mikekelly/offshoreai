# SLP Law 2011 Article 46 — Relationship to other law

- **Customary partnership law** applies except where inconsistent;
- The **Limited Partnerships (Jersey) Law 1994 does NOT apply** to SLPs.

Parallel to [ILP Article 38](./ilp-article-38.md) — both ILP and SLP statutes exclude the LP 1994's application.
