# LLC Law 2018 Article 56 — Offences of giving false or misleading information

A person who **knowingly or recklessly** makes a
statement to the registrar that is false or misleading
in a material particular commits an **offence**.
Standard mens rea formulation; penalties under
[Article 59](./llc-article-59.md).
