# Article 113N — Delegation of Commission's Powers and Duties

Article 113N of the **[Companies (Jersey) Law 1991][cjl]** lets the
**JFSC delegate** its powers and duties relating to auditor
oversight to qualified persons or organisations.

## What Article 113N covers

The Commission may **delegate specified powers and duties** to
qualified persons or organisations while **retaining ultimate
oversight** responsibility.

(See the consolidated text at [jerseylaw.je][cjl].)

## Common delegations

The JFSC may delegate to:

- **Professional bodies** — for disciplinary handling of their
  members in the first instance;
- **Specialist audit-quality assurance** bodies;
- **External reviewers** for specific investigations.

The delegation **preserves the JFSC's ultimate authority** — the
JFSC can intervene, revoke the delegation, or take direct action
where appropriate.

## Pitfalls

- **Treating delegated bodies as primary regulators** — the
  JFSC retains ultimate responsibility.
