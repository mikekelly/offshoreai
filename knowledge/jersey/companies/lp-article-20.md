# LP Law 1994 Article 20 — Admission of additional limited partners

Admission of new limited partners requires:

- Compliance with the **partnership agreement** terms;
- **Registration in the partner register** maintained by
  the LP.

Common in fund vehicles where subsequent closings admit new
limited partners during the fund's investment period.
