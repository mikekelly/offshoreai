# Takeovers Panel Law 2009 Article 9 — Offence of disclosure in contravention of Article 8

Article 9 makes **unauthorised disclosure** of Article 8
information a **criminal offence**.

## Key provisions

- Disclosing information in contravention of Article 8 is
  an offence.
- **Defence**: the person was not aware the information
  came from the Panel **or** took reasonable steps to
  prevent disclosure.
- **Penalty**: imprisonment for a term of 2 years and a
  fine.
- **Officers** of companies and partnerships may be liable
  for corporate breaches.
- **Aiding or abetting** the offence is itself an offence.
- **Prosecution requires consent** of the Attorney General.
