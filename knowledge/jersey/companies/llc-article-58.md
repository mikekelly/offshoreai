# LLC Law 2018 Article 58 — Limitation of liability

The registrar, the JFSC, and persons acting on their
behalf incur **no civil liability** for anything done
or omitted in good faith in the discharge of their
functions under this Law.

Standard regulator-protection provision; mirrors the
JFSC immunity in the
[FSC Law 1998](../financial-regulation/jfsc-law-1998.md).
