# LP Law 1994 Article 15 — Dealings by limited partner with partnership

Limited partners may **transact with the LP** in capacities
beyond their partner role. For non-contribution claims (e.g.
loans, services) they **rank as creditors** rather than as
partners — placing them ahead of partner-distribution
priority on winding-up.
