# LLP Law 2017 Article 10 — Keeping and form of limited liability partnership records

Article 10 sets standards for record maintenance:

- Records must be **kept in a manner that prevents loss**
  and supports retrieval;
- Entries must be made with **accuracy and timeliness**;
- Electronic record-keeping is permitted subject to
  reliability standards.
