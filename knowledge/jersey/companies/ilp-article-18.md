# ILP Law 2011 Article 18 — Limited partner's liability to creditors

The **load-bearing safe-harbour Article** for the ILP — the
ILP analogue of [LP Article 19](./lp-article-19.md).

## The general rule

Limited partners are **generally not liable** for ILP debts,
**except** when they:

- **Participate in management** of the ILP; **and**
- The creditor **reasonably believed them to be general
  partners**.

The "reasonably believed" qualifier is **more protective**
to limited partners than the LP 1994 formulation — under
the ILP, limited partners can participate in management
without losing limited liability **unless** that
participation creates the creditor's reasonable belief that
they were GPs.

## Safe harbours

As with LP 1994, an extensive list of activities does not
constitute participation in management — LPAC service,
voting on prescribed matters, consultative roles,
derivative actions, etc. The specific list should be
verified against the current Article 18 text.
