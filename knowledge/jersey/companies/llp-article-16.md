# LLP Law 2017 Article 16 — Assignments, etc.

Article 16 restricts the assignment of partnership
interests:

- A partner cannot assign their partnership interest
  **except per the partnership agreement**;
- Permitted assignments occur in connection with **partner
  admission, retirement, or death** under
  [Article 15](./llp-article-15.md);
- The restriction protects the LLP's partner composition
  from unilateral partner action.
