# LLP Law 2017 Article 39 — Orders

Article 39 empowers the **Minister** to prescribe matters
requiring prescription by Order under the Law — subject to
**Commission consultation**.

## Key provisions

- Minister-level instrument (Order) for matters requiring
  Ministerial flexibility;
- Commission must be consulted before such Orders are made;
- Used for matters of operational detail (fees, forms,
  procedural rules).
