# ILP Law 2011 Article 4 — Registration of declaration

Registration mechanics: signed declaration → registrar →
certificate of registration. The certificate is **conclusive
evidence** of incorporation.

Parallels [LP Article 4](./lp-article-4.md), adapted for the
body-corporate ILP framework.
