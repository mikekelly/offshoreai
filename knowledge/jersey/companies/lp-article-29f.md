# LP Law 1994 Article 29F — Effect of continuance

Continuance does **not create a new partnership** — it
transfers the existing partnership's status to Jersey law.
Specifically:

- **Assets are preserved** (no transfer required);
- **Partnership interests** continue;
- **Pending legal proceedings** continue;
- **Contractual rights and obligations** continue.

This is the load-bearing legal-continuity provision —
without it, continuance would require asset-by-asset
transfer and contract novation.
