# SLP Law 2011 Article 12 — Partnership property

Partnership property is held **beneficially for all partners** according to the agreement. Partners are **collectively entitled** to property held in their names or a GP's name.

The SLP's "separate legal person without body-corporate character" means property can be held in the SLP's name or the GP's name on behalf of the SLP.
