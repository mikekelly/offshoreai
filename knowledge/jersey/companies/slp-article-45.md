# SLP Law 2011 Article 45 — Amendments to other enactments

References to "partnerships" in other Jersey laws are deemed to **encompass SLPs**, with corresponding terminology adjustments. Consolidating provision ensuring the SLP regime is properly recognised across the statutory landscape.
