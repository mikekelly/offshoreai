# LLC Law 2018 Article 42 — Ceasing of rights on assignment

On assignment of the **whole** of a member's LLC
interest, the assignor's rights and powers in respect
of the assigned interest **cease**, but the assignor
remains liable for contributions and other obligations
owed to the LLC unless the LLC agreement otherwise
provides.
