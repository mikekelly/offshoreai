# SLP Law 2011 Article 30 — Legal proceedings and service of documents

Actions involve the **SLP or GPs only**. Service valid at the **registered office**. **LPs may sue derivatively** on behalf of the SLP if GPs wrongfully refuse to act. Parallels [LP Article 28](./lp-article-28.md).
