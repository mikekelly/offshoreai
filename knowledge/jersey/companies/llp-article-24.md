# LLP Law 2017 Article 24 — Legal proceedings

Article 24 sets the procedural position for litigation
involving an LLP:

- An **LLP is a party to proceedings** in its own name;
- A judgment against the LLP is **enforced against LLP
  property** only;
- LLP creditors may pursue **partner interests** in narrow
  defined circumstances (e.g. claw-back under
  [Article 12](./llp-article-12.md)).
