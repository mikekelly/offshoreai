# LP Law 1994 Article 29A — Meaning of eligible foreign limited partnership

Defines an **"eligible foreign limited partnership"** for
continuance purposes:

- Formed under non-Jersey law;
- Has both **fully-liable partners** (GP-equivalent) and
  **liability-limited partners** (LP-equivalent) — i.e. the
  foreign vehicle has substantively the same structure as a
  Jersey LP.

This is the **scope-defining provision** for Part 2A
(continuance-in regime).
