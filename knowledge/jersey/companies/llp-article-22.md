# LLP Law 2017 Article 22 — Dissolution of limited liability partnership

Article 22 sets the **dissolution mechanism** for an LLP —
the procedural framework for ending the entity's legal
existence.

## Key provisions

- The **registrar may initiate dissolution proceedings**
  for:
  - Business inactivity (the LLP not carrying on
    business);
  - Regulatory non-compliance (failure to file required
    documents, non-payment of fees);
  - Other defined triggers.
- A **three-month cure period** is allowed before final
  dissolution.
- The substantive winding-up procedure (asset realisation,
  creditor payment, residual distribution) is governed by
  [Regulations](./llp-article-38.md) made under
  Article 38.
