# LP Law 1994 Article 21A — Cancellation of registration of declaration and dissolution

The registrar may cancel registration of an LP on:

- **Dissolution requests** by the LP;
- **Continuance into another legal form** (e.g. continuance
  out to another jurisdiction);
- **Continued partner default** in meeting Law obligations.

Cancellation triggers the legal dissolution of the LP.
