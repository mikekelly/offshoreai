# LLC Law 2018 Article 2A — Limited liability company that is a body corporate

Permits the States, by Regulations, to provide for **LLCs to be formed as body corporates**. Optional / opt-in variant — the default LLC under [Article 2](./llc-article-2.md) is non-body-corporate.

This enables the LLC framework to flex between non-body-corporate (default, parallels Delaware LLC) and body-corporate variants depending on use case. Useful where the LLC needs body-corporate treatment for specific accounting / tax / counterparty purposes.
