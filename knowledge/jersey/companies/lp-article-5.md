# LP Law 1994 Article 5 — Amendment of declaration

Article 5 requires the LP to **notify changes** to the
registrar **within 21 days** of any change to particulars
in the original declaration.

Common triggers: change of name, registered office, GP /
limited partner composition, partnership-agreement
particulars.
