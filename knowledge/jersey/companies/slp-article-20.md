# SLP Law 2011 Article 20 — Limited partner's liability to creditors

**The load-bearing safe-harbour Article** for the SLP — the SLP analogue of [LP Article 19](./lp-article-19.md) and [ILP Article 18](./ilp-article-18.md).

## General rule

Limited partners face **no creditor liability** unless they **participate in management** — in which case they become liable **as general partners** to persons dealing with the SLP.

## Comparison across the partnership family

| Vehicle | Safe-harbour formulation |
|---|---|
| [LP Article 19](./lp-article-19.md) | LP not liable except where management participation with creditor knowledge |
| [ILP Article 18](./ilp-article-18.md) | LP not liable except where management participation with reasonable creditor belief |
| **SLP Article 20 (this Article)** | LP not liable except where management participation; participating LP becomes liable as GP |

All three Articles permit extensive safe-harbour activities (LPAC service, voting on prescribed matters, etc.) without losing limited liability — the specific list should be verified against each Article's current text.
