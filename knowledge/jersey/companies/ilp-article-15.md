# ILP Law 2011 Article 15 — Limited partners' rights as between themselves

Limited partners rank **equally for capital return** and
**proportionally for profits**, unless the partnership
agreement provides differentiated rights. Parallels
[LP Article 16](./lp-article-16.md).
