# LP Law 1994 Article 23 — Winding up of limited partnership

The **general partner is required** to:

- **Complete the winding-up** of the LP (realising assets,
  discharging liabilities, distributing surplus);
- **File a cancellation request** with the registrar on
  completion.

The winding-up process follows the priorities in
[Article 27](./lp-article-27.md).
