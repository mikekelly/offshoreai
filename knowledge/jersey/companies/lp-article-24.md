# LP Law 1994 Article 24 — Winding up of limited partnership on death etc. of general partner

When the **sole general partner dies or is dissolved**:

- **Winding up commences automatically**, **unless**
- The limited partners **unanimously elect a replacement GP
  within 90 days**.

## Operational significance

This is one structural reason fund GPs are typically
**corporate entities** rather than individuals — the
corporate GP does not "die" and the LP does not face the
90-day replacement scramble.

Where there are multiple GPs, the loss of one does not
trigger winding-up; the other(s) continue.
