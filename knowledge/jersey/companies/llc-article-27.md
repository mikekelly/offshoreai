# LLC Law 2018 Article 27 — Delegation of rights and powers to manage

Members and managers may **delegate** management rights and powers. Delegations may be **revocable or irrevocable** per the LLC agreement.

The irrevocable-delegation option is distinctive — supports investment-manager arrangements where the manager has bargained-for security of mandate.
