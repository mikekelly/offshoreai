# Article 224 — Citation

Article 224 of the **[Companies (Jersey) Law 1991][cjl]** is the
**citation provision** — it states the short title by which the
Law is to be known.

## What Article 224 covers

The Law may be cited as the **"Companies (Jersey) Law 1991"**.

(See the consolidated text at [jerseylaw.je][cjl].)

## Why this matters

The **standard citation** appears at the start of every section
of this wiki. The Law is sometimes abbreviated:

- **"CJL 1991"** in practitioner shorthand;
- **"the 1991 Law"** in textual references;
- **"the Companies Law"** when context makes the jurisdiction
  clear.

The citation closes the principal Law before the Schedules.
