# LP Law 1994 Article 16 — Limited partners' rights as between themselves

Default rules between limited partners:

- **Parity** in contribution returns;
- **Pro-rata profit distribution**.

Both defaults are **modifiable by partnership agreement** —
LP agreements routinely vary the default to reflect carry
waterfalls, hurdle rates, preferred returns, and other
fund-economics arrangements.
