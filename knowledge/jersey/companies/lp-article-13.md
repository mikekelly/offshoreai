# LP Law 1994 Article 13 — Rights of limited partner

Limited partners have:

- **Inspection rights** to LP records;
- **Information rights** regarding partnership affairs;
- **No unilateral dissolution right** — LP cannot dissolve
  the LP on their own (this is a GP / Court matter).

Limited partners' lack of unilateral dissolution power is
the structural counterpart to their limited liability —
they are passive investors, not active managers.
