# Limited Liability Partnerships (Jersey) Law 2017 — Index

The **Limited Liability Partnerships (Jersey) Law 2017** creates
the Jersey **Limited Liability Partnership (LLP)** — a body-
corporate partnership where **every partner enjoys limited
liability** (subject to defined exceptions).

The LLP differs from the [LP / ILP / SLP](./lp-1994-index.md)
family by removing the General Partner / Limited Partner
asymmetry. In an LLP, **all partners are "members"** with
limited liability; there is no GP carrying unlimited liability
for the partnership's obligations.

This file is the structural entry point. Per-Article files are
scaffolded as stubs.

## Why an LLP

The Jersey LLP is the **default vehicle for Jersey
professional-services firms** — law firms, accountancy
practices, advisory firms — and for any business where:

- All partners want **limited liability**;
- The **partnership economic model** (capital accounts,
  partner profit shares, member-level tax) is preferred to a
  corporate model with shares and dividends;
- The structure benefits from **separate legal personality**
  (the LLP is a body corporate);
- Continuity through partner changes is wanted.

It is **not** typically used as a fund vehicle — the
GP / LP separation in the
[LP 1994](./lp-1994-index.md) regime is what funds need. The
LLP is for operating businesses, not investment-pooling
structures.

## How the LLP compares to the UK LLP and Cayman LLP

The Jersey LLP is conceptually similar to the UK LLP under the
**Limited Liability Partnerships Act 2000** and to the Cayman
LLP. Mechanical differences exist on:

- **Member contribution and withdrawal mechanics**;
- **The "claw-back" rules** for distributions taken when the
  LLP was insolvent or near insolvent;
- **Tax classification** in cross-border contexts;
- **Filing and registration** mechanics.

The substantive partner-protection logic is the same.

## Structural coverage

The Law has **5 Parts** and **44 Articles** (1–43 with 23A
inserted by amendment). Full per-Article wiki at
[`llp-articles-index.md`](./llp-articles-index.md):

- **Part 1 (Preliminary)** — Article 1.
- **Part 2 (Essentials of a limited liability partnership)**
  — Articles 2–12 (legal personality, LLP property, LLP
  liability, partner liability, name, registered office,
  secretary, records, accounts / audit, claw-back via
  specified solvency statement under
  [Article 12](./llp-article-12.md)).
- **Part 3 (Relations of partners with one another and
  third parties)** — Articles 13–17 (partnership agreement
  primacy, dealings, admission / retirement,
  assignments, agency).
- **Part 4 (Registration and dissolution)** — Articles
  18–23A (registration, declaration amendment, validity
  rule, dissolution, cancellation, economic-substance
  trigger).
- **Part 5 (Miscellaneous and general)** — Articles 24–43
  (legal proceedings, service, compliance orders,
  registrar, fees, inspection, offences, penalties,
  Regulations, Orders, customary-law preservation,
  citation).
