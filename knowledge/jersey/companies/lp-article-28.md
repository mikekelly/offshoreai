# LP Law 1994 Article 28 — Legal proceedings and service of documents

Three key provisions:

- **Proceedings restricted to general partners** — LPs do
  not litigate in their own name; the GP litigates on
  behalf of the LP;
- **Limited partner derivative actions** permitted when the
  GP wrongfully refuses to act — limited partners can step
  in to vindicate LP rights;
- **Service** is effected at the **registered office**.

## Operational significance

The derivative-action mechanism is a safe-harboured
activity under [Article 19](./lp-article-19.md) — limited
partners can bring derivative claims without losing
limited liability.
