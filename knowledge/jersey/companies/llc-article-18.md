# LLC Law 2018 Article 18 — Liabilities to third parties

**The load-bearing limited-liability shield** for LLC members and managers.

## Key provisions

Members and managers are **NOT personally liable** for LLC debts **unless** they **voluntarily assume such liability** (e.g. through a personal guarantee).

## Operational significance

This is the **defining feature** of the LLC vehicle — the limited-liability shield is stronger and simpler than the partnership safe-harbour mechanisms in [LP Article 19](./lp-article-19.md), [ILP Article 18](./ilp-article-18.md), and [SLP Article 20](./slp-article-20.md), because there is **no "participation in management" trigger** — members and managers can participate fully in LLC operations without losing the shield.

This makes the LLC suitable as a **single-vehicle solution** where the partnership model's GP-LP-split structure is unwieldy.
