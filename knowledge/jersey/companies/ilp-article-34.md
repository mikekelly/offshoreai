# ILP Law 2011 Article 34 — Criminal liability of partners, directors and other officers

**General partners, managing limited partners, and
officers** may be **personally liable** when corporate
offences involve their **consent, connivance, or neglect**.
Parallels [LP Article 36A](./lp-article-36a.md) but extends
to managing-limited-partner liability (consistent with the
limited-partner-management trigger in
[Article 18](./ilp-article-18.md)).
