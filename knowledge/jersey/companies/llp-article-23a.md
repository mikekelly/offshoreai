# LLP Law 2017 Article 23A — Economic substance test

Article 23A (inserted by amendment to align with the
[Taxation (Companies — Economic Substance) (Jersey) Law 2019](../tax/economic-substance.md))
permits the **Court to order compliance or dissolution** of
an LLP that fails the economic substance test.

## Key provisions

- The Court may, on application, order the LLP to **comply
  with substance requirements**;
- Where compliance is not achieved, the Court may order
  **dissolution**;
- Triggers application of the Substance Law 2019 to LLPs
  carrying on relevant activities.
