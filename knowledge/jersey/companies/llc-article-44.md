# LLC Law 2018 Article 44 — Rights and liabilities on becoming a member

On admission as a member, an assignee acquires the
**rights and powers** and is subject to the
**restrictions and liabilities** of a member as
specified in the LLC agreement and this Law — but is
not personally liable for member obligations incurred
**before** admission unless the LLC agreement provides
otherwise.
