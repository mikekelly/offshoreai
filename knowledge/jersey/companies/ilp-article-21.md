# ILP Law 2011 Article 21 — Winding up and dissolution

The **States may enact Regulations** governing winding up
and dissolution of both **solvent and insolvent ILPs**. The
detail lives in Regulations rather than primary statute —
parallel to the
[ILP Article 35](./ilp-article-35.md) Regulations-making
power.
