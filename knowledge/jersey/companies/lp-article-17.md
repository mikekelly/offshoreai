# LP Law 1994 Article 17 — Return of limited partner's contribution

Return of contribution to a limited partner is restricted
to **periods of solvency** (per
[Article 2](./lp-article-2.md)). Returns made when insolvent
are **repayable** — recoverable for **six months** or
longer per partnership agreement.

## Operational significance

This is the **capital-return solvency rule** — parallel to
the Article 14 profit-share rule. Together they ensure
that limited partners do not extract capital or returns
when the LP is unable to discharge creditor obligations.
