# Article 113H — Power to Amend Part 16

Article 113H of the **[Companies (Jersey) Law 1991][cjl]** is the
**amendment-power** provision for Part 16 (Accounts and Audits).

## What Article 113H says

> The States may amend this Part by Regulations.

(See the consolidated text at [jerseylaw.je][cjl].)

## Why this matters

Accounts and audit standards evolve — international accounting
frameworks, audit-quality requirements, professional-body rules.
Article 113H lets the States update Part 16 by Regulations.

## Family of amendment powers

One of several Part-specific amendment-power Articles. See
Articles 2B, 17D, 40C, 59, 66A, 85A, 115B, 124A, 127GA, 127YN,
194A.
