# ILP Law 2011 Article 38 — Relationship to other law

Two clarifications:

- **Customary partnership law** applies to ILPs **except
  where inconsistent** with the Law (standard preservation
  clause);
- The **Limited Partnerships (Jersey) Law 1994 does NOT
  apply** to ILPs.

The exclusion of LP 1994 is the structural separation —
ILPs are governed by their own statute, not by parallel
provisions in the LP 1994.
