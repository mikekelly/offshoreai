# LP Law 1994 Article 36A — Criminal liability for offences by bodies corporate

Where an LP-Law offence is committed by a corporate entity
(typically a corporate GP), **officers and managers** are
personally liable if the offence was committed with their
**consent or connivance**. Standard officer-liability
provision.
