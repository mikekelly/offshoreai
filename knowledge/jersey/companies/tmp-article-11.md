# Takeovers Panel Law 2009 Article 11 — Commission's duty to take appropriate steps to assist Panel

Article 11 is the **reciprocal mirror** of
[Article 10](./tmp-article-10.md) — it imposes on the
[JFSC](../financial-regulation/jfsc.md) a duty to take
appropriate steps to assist the Panel.

## Key provisions

- The Commission must co-operate with the Panel to assist
  it in the discharge of its functions.
- The Commission may **exercise its regulatory powers** in
  support of the Panel where appropriate.
- **Statutory restrictions on disclosure** (in other
  enactments) do not prevent the Commission disclosing
  business information to the Panel where necessary for
  the Panel's functions.

## Operational significance

This is the **statutory bridge** between the Panel and the
Jersey regulated population. The Commission's wide
supervisory information base — held under the
[FSL 1998](../financial-regulation/financial-services-jersey-law-1998.md),
the
[Banking Business Law 1991](../financial-regulation/banking-business-law-1991.md),
and other regulatory laws — can flow to the Panel where
relevant to a transaction, without breaching the
Commission's own confidentiality regimes.
