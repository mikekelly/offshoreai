# LLP Law 2017 Article 25 — Service of documents and other communications

Article 25 governs **service**:

- Service is **effected at the registered office** of the
  LLP;
- **Or at a partner's address** in defined circumstances;
- The **Minister may prescribe additional service methods**
  by Order.
