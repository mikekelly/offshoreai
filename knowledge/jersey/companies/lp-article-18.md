# LP Law 1994 Article 18 — Partner's liability to partnership

- **Limited partner liability**: for the difference between
  the **promised** contribution and the amount **actually
  contributed**;
- **General partner liability**: bears insufficient-asset
  liability — i.e. unlimited liability to the LP for any
  obligations the LP cannot meet from its own assets.

This is the **structural asymmetry** of the LP: limited
partners liable for what they promised but haven't paid;
general partners liable for everything the LP cannot
otherwise cover.
