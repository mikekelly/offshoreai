# LLC Law 2018 Article 48 — Appointment and functions of registrar

The **registrar of limited liability companies** is the
same person appointed as **registrar of companies**
under the Companies Law 1991 — i.e. the
[JFSC](../financial-regulation/jfsc.md) Registry
function.
