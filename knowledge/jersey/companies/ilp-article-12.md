# ILP Law 2011 Article 12 — Rights of limited partner

Limited partners have:

- **Inspection rights** for partnership records during
  business hours;
- **Entitlement to partnership information** on demand
  where appropriate.

Parallels [LP Article 13](./lp-article-13.md).
