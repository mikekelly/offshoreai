# LP Law 1994 Article 5A — Annual confirmation

Article 5A requires general partners to deliver **annual
confirmations** verifying partnership particulars between
**1 January and 28 February** each year.

The confirmation captures changes that occurred during the
year and confirms the state of the LP as at the end of the
year — operational mechanism for maintaining a current
registry record.
