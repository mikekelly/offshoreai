# LLC Law 2018 Article 21 — Management of limited liability company

**The fundamental governance choice.** LLC agreements may provide for:

- **Manager-managed**: appointed managers run the LLC;
- **Member-managed**: if no managers appointed, members run the LLC directly.

## Operational significance

This dual-mode framework mirrors the Delaware LLC's flexibility. Members can choose the model best suited to their use case:

- **Investment LLCs**: typically manager-managed, with the manager being a sponsor / GP-equivalent;
- **Small operating LLCs**: typically member-managed for simplicity;
- **Joint-venture LLCs**: typically manager-managed with member-board oversight.

The flexibility is what makes the LLC suitable for use cases that don't fit cleanly into Jersey company or partnership structures.
