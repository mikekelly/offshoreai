# LLP Law 2017 Article 26 — Order for compliance

Article 26 permits an **aggrieved party** to apply to the
Court for an order compelling compliance with requirements
of the Law.

## Key provisions

- An aggrieved party may apply to the Court;
- The Court may make orders compelling compliance with
  the Law's requirements;
- Typical use: applying for an order requiring the LLP or
  its secretary to comply with filing, record-keeping, or
  declaration-amendment requirements.
