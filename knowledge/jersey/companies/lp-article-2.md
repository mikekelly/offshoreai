# LP Law 1994 Article 2 — Meaning of "insolvent" and "solvent"

Article 2 defines the LP's solvency test for the Law's
purposes.

## Provision

A Jersey LP is **insolvent** when its general partner cannot
discharge the LP's debts from **partnership assets alone**
— i.e. without accessing the GP's personal assets.

Conversely, the LP is **solvent** when partnership assets
are sufficient to cover its obligations.

## Operational significance

The solvency definition is the **load-bearing concept** for
distributions / returns of capital. A profit distribution to
limited partners (Article 14) or return of contribution
(Article 17) is permitted only when the LP is solvent. The
test uses **partnership assets**, not GP assets in their
personal capacity.
