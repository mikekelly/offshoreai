# LP Law 1994 Article 22 — Application to reinstate dissolved limited partnership

The Court may **reinstate a dissolved LP** within **ten
years** on application by:

- Partners;
- Creditors;
- Other interested parties.

Reinstatement effectively undoes the dissolution and
restores the LP to its prior existence — subject to
conditions the Court may impose.
