# LP Law 1994 Article 10 — Contribution of limited partner

Limited partners may contribute **"any currency, any other
property or services"** to the LP — wide flexibility in
the form of contribution.

This flexibility supports the LP's use as a fund vehicle
where limited-partner contributions may include cash,
securities, real assets, or (in unusual cases) services.
