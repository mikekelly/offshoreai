# LLC Law 2018 Article 51 — Form of documents to be delivered to registrar

Documents delivered to the registrar must be in the
**form prescribed** by the registrar (paper or
electronic) and contain the **information specified**
in the form. The registrar may **refuse** non-compliant
filings.
