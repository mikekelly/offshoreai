# LLC Law 2018 Article 45 — LLC acquisition of member or manager interest

An LLC may **acquire**, by purchase, redemption or
otherwise, the LLC interest of a member or manager,
subject to the LLC agreement and the
[Article 37 solvency limitation](./llc-article-37.md).
