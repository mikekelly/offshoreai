# LP Law 1994 Article 12 — Enforcement of judgments against property of limited partnership

Article 12 restricts judgment enforcement against LP
property to cases where the judgment is **against the GP in
their partnership capacity**. Judgments against the GP
**personally** (i.e. not in respect of LP obligations)
cannot reach LP property.

This is the **load-bearing asset-segregation rule** that
protects LP property from GP personal creditors.
