# LLC Law 2018 Article 40 — Assignee right to exercise member rights

An assignee is **entitled to receive distributions** and
allocations of profits and losses to which the assignor
would otherwise be entitled, but **not** to exercise
other member rights (voting, information) absent
admission as a member.
