# LLC Law 2018 Article 38 — Assignment of LLC interest

An LLC interest is **assignable** in whole or in part,
subject to the LLC agreement. An assignment does **not
of itself entitle** the assignee to participate in
management or to become a member — see
[Articles 39–43](./llc-articles-index.md).
