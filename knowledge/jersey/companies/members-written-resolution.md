# Article 95ZB — Members' Power to Require Circulation of Written Resolution

Article 95ZB of the **[Companies (Jersey) Law 1991][cjl]** is the
**member-initiated** counterpart to Article 95ZA — members holding
sufficient shares can **require the company to circulate** their
proposed written resolution.

## What Article 95ZB says

Members holding **sufficient shares** may **require the company
to circulate** their proposed written resolution.

The company must send it to all members **within specified
timeframes**.

**Costs** may be recoverable from requesting members if
**requirements aren't met**.

(See the consolidated text at [jerseylaw.je][cjl].)

## Why this matters

The Article 95ZB mechanism is the **written-resolution analogue**
of [Article 89](./requisition-of-meetings.md) (requisition of
meetings):

- A **minority** of members can force consideration of a
  resolution they support;
- The mechanism bypasses **director gatekeeping** — directors
  cannot block a properly-requisitioned resolution;
- Operates as **shareholder activism** tool in larger Jersey
  companies.

The exact **share threshold** is specified by the Law (typically
similar to the 10% threshold for meeting requisitions, though
specific to written resolutions).

## Procedure

1. **Requisitioning members** submit the proposed resolution
   text and supporting statement to the company.
2. The **company circulates** to all eligible members within
   the statutory timeframe.
3. Members **respond in writing** within the deadline.
4. The resolution **passes** if it reaches the required
   threshold for that type of resolution.

## Costs

The Article addresses **costs**:

- If the **requisition is valid** and proper procedure is
  followed, the company typically bears the costs;
- If the **requisitioners fail to meet requirements** (e.g.,
  insufficient threshold, defective resolution), the
  requisitioners may be liable for costs.

## Pitfalls

- **Inadequate threshold** — requisition fails.
- **Defective resolution text** — directors may refuse to
  circulate, or the resolution may be invalid even if circulated.
- **Cost exposure** for unsuccessful requisitions.
