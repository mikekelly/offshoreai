# LLP Law 2017 Article 6 — Name of limited liability partnership

Article 6 governs the LLP's name. Key provisions:

- The LLP's name must end with **"Limited Liability
  Partnership"** or the abbreviation **"LLP"**;
- The registrar has **authority over misleading names** —
  may decline to register or may require change of a name
  the registrar considers misleading or inappropriate;
- Restrictions on use of similar names by non-LLPs.
