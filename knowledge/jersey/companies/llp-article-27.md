# LLP Law 2017 Article 27 — Appointment and functions of registrar

Article 27 establishes the registrar's role:

- The **companies registrar** (within the JFSC Registry)
  serves as the **LLP registrar**;
- The registrar **maintains the LLP register**;
- The registrar **may remove inaccurate or invalid material**
  per a published policy;
- The registrar's decisions are subject to applicable review
  / appeal mechanisms.
