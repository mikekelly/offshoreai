# LLP Law 2017 Article 28 — Fees, charges and forms

Article 28 establishes the **fees regime**:

- The Commission may collect **annual administration
  fees** and **additional charges**;
- Sets **deadlines and payment procedures**;
- Prescribes **forms** for registration and amendment
  filings.
