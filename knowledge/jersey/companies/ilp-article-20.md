# ILP Law 2011 Article 20 — Assignments

Limited partners may **assign interests** subject to the
partnership agreement. Assignees become partners **only
upon register entry** and assume predecessor liabilities
accordingly. Parallels [LP Article 21](./lp-article-21.md).
