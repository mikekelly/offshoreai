# LP Law 1994 Article 25 — Power of Court to order winding up

The Court may order winding up on grounds of:

- **Prejudicial conduct** by partners against another
  partner;
- **Oppression**;
- **Just and equitable** grounds;
- **Damage to Jersey's reputation** through the LP's
  activities.

The "damage to Jersey's reputation" ground is notable — it
gives the Court a tool to wind up LPs whose activities are
operationally legitimate but bring discredit on the
jurisdiction.
