# Takeovers Panel Law 2009 Article 17 — Fees and charges

Article 17 enables the Panel — through rules — to **levy
fees and charges** payable to it for the purpose of meeting
its expenses under the Law.

## Key provisions

- Rules may provide for **fees or charges** payable to the
  Panel.
- The purpose: **meeting any part of the Panel's expenses**
  under the Law.
- Panel expenses include:
  - Payments to or in respect of the **Takeover Appeal Board**;
  - **Repayment of borrowed money** and interest;
  - Maintaining a **reasonable reserve**.

## Operational significance

Article 17 funds the Panel's operations through
**transaction-level fees** charged to bidders, offerors,
and (in some cases) target companies. The fees are set in
rules; non-payment is a debt recoverable by the Panel under
[Article 19](./tmp-article-19.md).
