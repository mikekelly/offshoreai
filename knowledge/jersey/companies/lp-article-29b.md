# LP Law 1994 Article 29B — Eligible foreign limited partnership may continue in Jersey

An eligible foreign LP may **continue in Jersey** unless:

- **Prohibited by formation jurisdiction's law**;
- The foreign LP is subject to **disqualifying conditions**
  — winding up, deregistration, or other terminal state.

This is the LP-law analogue of corporate continuance under
[CJL 1991 Part 18C](./continuance.md) — allowing a foreign-
domiciled LP to "migrate" to Jersey as its governing
jurisdiction.
