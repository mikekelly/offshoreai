# LLP Law 2017 Article 34 — Offences of giving false or misleading etc. information

Article 34 creates **offences** for making false or
misleading statements:

- **False statements to the registrar** — knowingly or
  recklessly making false / misleading statements in
  filings;
- **Unauthorised use of "limited liability partnership"
  terminology** in business operations (i.e. holding out as
  an LLP without being registered as one);
- Penalties at the **Article 36** level.
