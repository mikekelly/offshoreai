# Takeovers Panel Law 2009 Article 20 — Panel as party to proceedings

Article 20 gives the Panel **legal capacity** to bring and
defend proceedings in its own name — even though the Panel
may not be a body corporate.

## Key provisions

- The Panel may **bring proceedings under this Law** in its
  own name.
- The Panel may also **bring or defend other proceedings**
  in its own name.
- This applies **whether or not** the Panel is an
  unincorporated body.

## Operational significance

The provision removes any procedural obstacle to the Panel
litigating — it does not need to identify particular
natural-person or corporate plaintiffs / defendants. This
matters particularly for:

- Court enforcement under [Article 15](./tmp-article-15.md);
- Debt recovery under [Article 19](./tmp-article-19.md);
- Judicial review and defensive litigation.
