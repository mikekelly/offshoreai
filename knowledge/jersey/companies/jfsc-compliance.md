# Article 113M — Commission to Ensure Compliance

Article 113M of the **[Companies (Jersey) Law 1991][cjl]** is the
**JFSC's general compliance-monitoring duty** for recognised
auditors.

## What Article 113M covers

The Commission bears **responsibility for**:

- **monitoring** recognised auditors' adherence to applicable
  rules;
- **taking enforcement action** when violations occur.

(See the consolidated text at [jerseylaw.je][cjl].)

## Compliance monitoring activities

The JFSC monitors auditors through:

- **Audit-quality reviews** — selective inspection of audit
  files;
- **Independence-monitoring** processes;
- **Coordination with professional bodies** on disciplinary
  matters;
- **Complaint investigation**;
- **Market-wide reviews** of audit-quality trends.

## Enforcement

Where violations are found, the Commission can:

- Issue **directions** or **conditions** on the auditor's
  registration;
- **Suspend** the auditor's registration;
- **Remove** the auditor from the register;
- **Refer** to professional bodies or, in serious cases, to the
  Attorney General for prosecution.
