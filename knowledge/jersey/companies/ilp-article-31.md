# ILP Law 2011 Article 31 — Form of partnership's records

Records may be kept in **bound books, electronic formats, or
other reproducible media**. Partnerships must **prevent loss,
falsification, and inaccuracy**. Parallels
[LP Article 34](./lp-article-34.md).
