# LLP Law 2017 Article 30 — Production and inspection of records where offence suspected

Article 30 empowers the Court — on **Attorney General
application showing reasonable cause** — to order
production or inspection of LLP records in an investigation
where an offence is suspected.

## Key provisions

- AG application required;
- Reasonable cause for offence suspicion required;
- Court order for production / inspection;
- Records may be obtained from the LLP, secretary, or
  partners.
