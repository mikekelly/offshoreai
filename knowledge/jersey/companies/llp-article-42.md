# LLP Law 2017 Article 42 — Customary law

Article 42 preserves the operation of **Jersey customary
partnership law** — applicable to LLP affairs **except where
inconsistent with express provisions of the Law**.

This is the standard Jersey-statute preservation clause —
the Law operates against the background of customary law,
displacing it only where expressly contradicted.
