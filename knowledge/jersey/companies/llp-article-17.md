# LLP Law 2017 Article 17 — Agency of partner in a limited liability partnership

Article 17 sets the **agency relationship**: each partner
acts as agent of the LLP binding it, but partners are **not
agents of each other**.

## Key provisions

- Each partner acts as **agent of the LLP** and can bind
  the LLP in transactions within the scope of the LLP's
  business;
- Partners are **not agents of one another**;
- The LLP is bound by partner acts within the scope of
  their authority; partner acts outside that scope do not
  bind the LLP unless ratified.

## Operational significance

This formulation differs materially from the **traditional
partnership** position (all partners jointly liable as
principals to each other) and reflects the LLP's separate-
legal-personality structure. A partner's wrongful act in
the course of LLP business may bind the LLP under
[Article 4](./llp-article-4.md) without making the other
partners personally liable under
[Article 5](./llp-article-5.md).
