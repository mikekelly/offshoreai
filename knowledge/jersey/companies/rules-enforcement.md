# Article 113O — Enforcement of Rules

Article 113O of the **[Companies (Jersey) Law 1991][cjl]** gives
the **JFSC enforcement powers** for breaches of audit rules.

## What Article 113O covers

The Commission may:

- **issue guidance**;
- **conduct investigations**;
- **impose sanctions** including:
  - **suspension** of registration;
  - **deregistration**.

(See the consolidated text at [jerseylaw.je][cjl].)

## The enforcement toolkit

The JFSC's options:

| Sanction | When used |
|---|---|
| **Guidance** | Minor breaches, where direction can prevent recurrence |
| **Direction** | Specific compliance requirements imposed on the auditor |
| **Public statement** | Naming an auditor for breach |
| **Civil financial penalty** | Where the Civil Financial Penalties regime applies |
| **Suspension** | Temporary removal from the register |
| **Deregistration** | Permanent removal — the most severe sanction |

## Process

The JFSC follows due-process expectations:

- **Investigation** with auditor cooperation;
- **Provisional findings** communicated to the auditor;
- **Auditor representations** considered;
- **Final decision** with reasons;
- **Appeal rights** to the Royal Court or other appropriate
  forum.
