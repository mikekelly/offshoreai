# Takeovers Panel Law 2009 Article 18 — Levy

Article 18 is the **alternative funding mechanism** to
Article 17 transaction fees. The Minister may, by Order,
impose a **levy** on specified persons or bodies.

## Key provisions

- The **Minister may** establish a levy through Order.
- Levy is payable by persons or bodies that:
  - Are capable of being **directly affected** by the
    Panel's functions; or
  - Have a **substantial interest** in those functions.
- The Order may specify **rates**, **periods**, and
  **payment terms**.
- The Panel must maintain **proper accounts** and prepare
  statements of account as specified.

## Operational significance

The levy mechanism contemplates funding from the **wider
market participant base** rather than transaction-by-
transaction fees. In UK Panel practice, a levy on
securities transactions of specified types serves this
purpose. The Jersey provision permits the same in principle;
the practical implementation depends on the Order in force.
