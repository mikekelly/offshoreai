# Limited Partnerships (Jersey) Law 1994 — Articles Index

Article-by-article enumeration of the
[Limited Partnerships (Jersey) Law 1994](https://www.jerseylaw.je/laws/current/l_22_1994)
— the foundational statute for Jersey LPs, by far the most
common Jersey fund vehicle. The Law has **3 Parts** plus a
new **Part 2A** (eligible foreign LP continuance) inserted
by amendment.
