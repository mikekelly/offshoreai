# LLP Law 2017 Article 9 — Records to be held by limited liability partnership secretary

Article 9 sets the secretary's record-holding obligations:

- The secretary must retain **accounting records for 10
  years**;
- The records must be **available for partner inspection**;
- The form and content of the records are governed by
  [Article 10](./llp-article-10.md).
