# LLP Law 2017 Article 7 — Registered office

Article 7 requires the LLP to maintain a **registered office
in Jersey** and specifies records that must be kept there.

## Key provisions

- An LLP must at all times have a **registered office in
  Jersey**;
- Specified records must be **kept at the registered
  office** — partner register, accounting records,
  partnership agreement, statutory filings;
- Changes to the registered office require notification to
  the registrar.

## Operational significance

The Jersey registered-office requirement is the **substance
anchor** for Jersey-LLP status. An LLP operating without a
Jersey registered office is in breach.
