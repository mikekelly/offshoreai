# LP Law 1994 Article 31A — Form of documents to be delivered to registrar

Where published forms are not available, documents may be
delivered in **registrar-acceptable forms**. Applicable fees
remain payable. Provides operational flexibility for filings
where the published-form schema does not yet exist.
