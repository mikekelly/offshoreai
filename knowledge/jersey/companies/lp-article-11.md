# LP Law 1994 Article 11 — Rights and obligations of general partner

The GP has **all rights and powers of an ordinary partner**
and holds partnership property as LP assets subject to the
partnership agreement.

## Operational significance

- The GP has **management authority** (limited partners do
  not — see [Article 19](./lp-article-19.md) safe harbours);
- Partnership property is **legal-titled in the GP**
  (typically) but held **on behalf of the LP**;
- The GP carries **unlimited liability** for the LP's
  obligations — this is the structural counterpart to the
  limited partners' capped liability.
