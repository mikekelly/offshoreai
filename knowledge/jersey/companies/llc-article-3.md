# LLC Law 2018 Article 3 — LLC agreement generally

**The load-bearing operational document.** The LLC agreement:

- Governs the **conduct of members and managers**;
- Is **binding on all parties** whether executed or not;
- Sets the **internal economic and governance terms** of the LLC.

## Operational significance

The LLC agreement is the **flexibility centrepiece** of the Jersey LLC framework. Unlike a Jersey company under [CJL 1991](./companies-law-1991.md) where directors' duties and share-rights are largely statutory, the LLC agreement can:

- Define **economic interests** with bespoke profit-share / loss-allocation / distribution waterfalls;
- Define **governance** with member-management or manager-management arrangements;
- Define **voting** on per-capita, financial-interest, or other bases;
- Define **classes** of members with differentiated rights;
- Customise or eliminate certain default fiduciary duties (see [Article 47](./llc-article-47.md)).

This **Delaware-LLC-style flexibility** is what makes the Jersey LLC distinctive from the CJL 1991 company.
