# LLP Law 2017 Article 18 — Registration of limited liability partnership

Article 18 sets the **registration procedure** by which an
LLP comes into existence:

- A **declaration** is submitted to the registrar with
  prescribed information (LLP name, registered office,
  secretary, initial partners, partnership-agreement
  particulars);
- The registrar **issues a certificate of registration**;
- Registration takes **effect from the date of the
  certificate**;
- The LLP exists as a legal person from registration —
  [Article 2](./llp-article-2.md) takes effect.
