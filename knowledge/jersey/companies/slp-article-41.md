# SLP Law 2011 Article 41 — Criminal liability of partners, directors and other officers

**GPs and participating LPs** are liable for entity offences committed with their **consent, connivance, or attributable to neglect**. The participating-LP extension is consistent with [Article 20](./slp-article-20.md)'s GP-liability conversion.
