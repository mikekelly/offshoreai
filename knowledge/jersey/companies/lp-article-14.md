# LP Law 1994 Article 14 — Share of profits

Limited partners are entitled to **profit shares only when
the LP remains solvent** (per [Article 2](./lp-article-2.md)
definition). Profit-share payments made when the LP is
insolvent are **repayable with interest**.

## Operational significance

This is the **claw-back mechanism** for LP distributions —
parallel in function to the company-law solvency-test rules
(CJL 1991 Article 115). Distributions while insolvent are
not protected and can be recovered for the benefit of
creditors.
