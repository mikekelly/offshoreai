# ILP Law 2011 Article 14 — Dealings by limited partner with partnership

Limited partners may **lend, borrow, and transact** with the
ILP. Non-GP limited partners **rank as creditors** for
non-contribution claims. Parallels
[LP Article 15](./lp-article-15.md).
