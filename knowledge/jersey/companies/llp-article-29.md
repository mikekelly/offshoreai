# LLP Law 2017 Article 29 — Inspection and production of documents kept by registrar

Article 29 governs **public access** to registered LLP
documents:

- The public **may inspect registered documents** held by
  the registrar;
- **Certified copies** are admissible as evidence;
- Certain information may be **restricted from public
  access** under prescribed circumstances (e.g.
  beneficial-ownership information under the
  [FSDI Law 2020](../registries/fs-dpi-law-2020.md)).
