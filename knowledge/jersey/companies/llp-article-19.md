# LLP Law 2017 Article 19 — Amendment of declaration

Article 19 requires the LLP to **notify changes** to its
registered declaration:

- Changes to be notified **within 28 days**;
- The registrar **issues certificates** for registered
  amendments;
- Common amendment triggers: change of name, registered
  office, partners, secretary, partnership-agreement
  particulars.
