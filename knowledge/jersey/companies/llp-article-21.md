# LLP Law 2017 Article 21 — Validity and proof of registration

Article 21 provides the **conclusive-proof** rule for LLP
registration:

- **Errors in the declaration do not invalidate
  registration**;
- The **certificate of registration is conclusive proof**
  that the LLP has been properly registered.

## Operational significance

This protects against the catastrophic outcome of LLP-
registration being challenged years later on a defect in
the original declaration. Once the certificate has issued,
the LLP exists as a legal person and counterparties can
rely on its registration without going behind the
certificate.
