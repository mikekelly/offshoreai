# LLP Law 2017 Article 36 — Penalties

Article 36 specifies the **penalty regime**:

- **Fines** — typically at the **level 3 standard scale**;
- **Imprisonment** — up to **2 years** for the more serious
  offences;
- Penalty levels are calibrated to the specific offence
  under the Law (false statements, unauthorised use of
  terminology, breach of compulsory production order, etc.).
