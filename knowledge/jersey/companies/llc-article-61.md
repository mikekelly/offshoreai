# LLC Law 2018 Article 61 — Orders

The **Minister** may by **Order** prescribe fees,
charges, forms, time periods, and other administrative
matters under this Law. Orders are the executive
(secondary legislation) route, contrasted with
Regulations under [Article 60](./llc-article-60.md).
