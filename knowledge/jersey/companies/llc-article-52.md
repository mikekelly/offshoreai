# LLC Law 2018 Article 52 — Inspection and production of documents

A person may, on payment of the prescribed fee,
**inspect** any document held by the registrar in
respect of an LLC and require a **certified copy** to
be produced. Certain documents (e.g. beneficial-owner
information under the FSDI Law 2020) are **not
publicly inspectable**.
