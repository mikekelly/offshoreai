# LP Law 1994 Article 1 — Interpretation

Article 1 sets out defined terms for the Law, including:

- **"general partner"** — a partner with unlimited liability
  for the LP's obligations, managing the LP;
- **"limited partner"** — a partner whose liability is
  capped at capital contributed (subject to safe harbours
  in [Article 19](./lp-article-19.md));
- **"limited partnership"** — an LP registered under this
  Law;
- **"registrar"** — the JFSC company registrar (per
  [Article 30](./lp-article-30.md));
- And other terms used throughout the Law.
