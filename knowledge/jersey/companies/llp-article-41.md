# LLP Law 2017 Article 41 — Professional rules not affected by Law

Article 41 preserves **professional-conduct rules** — the
Law does **not override** the professional duties of:

- **Solicitors and advocates** (under the Advocates and
  Solicitors (Jersey) Law 1997 and the Law Society's
  professional-conduct rules);
- **Accountants** and accountancy professional bodies;
- **Other professionals** subject to professional-body
  rules.

## Operational significance

A professional practising through a Jersey LLP — a legal
firm, an accountancy firm, an advisory firm — remains
subject to their professional-conduct rules. The LLP
structure does not insulate the individual from
professional discipline.
