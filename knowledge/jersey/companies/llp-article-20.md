# LLP Law 2017 Article 20 — [Deleted]

Article 20 was **deleted by R&O.138/2020**. It originally
covered annual return requirements.

The annual-return regime was subsequently consolidated /
replaced by the
[Financial Services (Disclosure and Provision of Information) (Jersey) Law 2020](../registries/fs-dpi-law-2020.md)
and related Orders.
