# LLP Law 2017 Article 15 — Admission and retirement of partners

Article 15 confines admission and retirement of partners to
the terms of the partnership agreement:

- New partners are **admitted only per the partnership
  agreement**;
- Retirement is **limited to the agreement's terms**;
- Changes are subject to the declaration-amendment
  procedure under [Article 19](./llp-article-19.md).
