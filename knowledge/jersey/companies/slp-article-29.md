# SLP Law 2011 Article 29 — Settling accounts on dissolution

Distribution priority on dissolution:

1. **Creditor debts** satisfied first;
2. Then ranked:
   - **GP non-capital claims**;
   - **LP capital**;
   - **LP profits**;
   - **GP capital**;
   - **GP profits**.

This ordering provides a default that the partnership agreement may modify (subject to creditor priority).
