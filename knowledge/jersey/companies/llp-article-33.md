# LLP Law 2017 Article 33 — Registration in the Public Registry

Article 33 addresses registration in the Jersey **Public
Registry of immovable property** — relevant where an LLP
acquires Jersey immovable property and a record of the
acquisition is required to be entered in the Public
Registry maintained by the Judicial Greffier.
