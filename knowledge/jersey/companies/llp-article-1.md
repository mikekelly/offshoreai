# LLP Law 2017 Article 1 — Interpretation

Article 1 sets out the defined terms used throughout the
Law.

## Key defined terms

- **"limited liability partnership"** — an LLP registered
  under the Law;
- **"partner"** — a person admitted as a partner in
  accordance with the partnership agreement;
- **"partnership agreement"** — the agreement between the
  partners governing the LLP's internal affairs;
- **"partnership interest"** — a partner's share in the
  capital, profits, and management of the LLP;
- **"property"** — property of the LLP;
- And others, with definitions consistent with the rest of
  the Law.
