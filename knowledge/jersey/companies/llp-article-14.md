# LLP Law 2017 Article 14 — Dealings by partners with limited liability partnership

Article 14 permits partners to **transact with the LLP** —
subject to the terms of the partnership agreement.

## Key provisions

- Partners may enter into transactions with the LLP;
- Such transactions are **governed by the partnership
  agreement**;
- Standard fiduciary considerations (avoidance of undue
  benefit, disclosure of interest) apply through the
  partnership agreement framework.
