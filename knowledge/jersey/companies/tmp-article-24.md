# Takeovers Panel Law 2009 Article 24 — Regulations and Orders

Article 24 is a **technical / supplementary provision**
permitting Regulations or Orders under the Law to include
transitional, consequential, incidental, or supplementary
provisions as deemed necessary.

## Key provisions

Orders or Regulations made under the Law **may contain**:

- **Transitional** provisions;
- **Consequential** provisions;
- **Incidental** provisions;
- **Supplementary** provisions.

## Operational significance

This is a standard enabling provision permitting the
delegated-legislation framework to operate flexibly. It is
the legal foundation for the Order appointing the Panel
under [Article 2](./tmp-article-2.md), the levy Order under
[Article 18](./tmp-article-18.md), and any subsequent
operational Orders.
