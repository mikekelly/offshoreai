# LLC Law 2018 Article 47 — Fiduciary and other duties

Article 47 is one of the **most distinctive provisions**
of the Jersey LLC Law and one of two strongest signals
(alongside [Article 3](./llc-article-3.md) on the LLC
agreement) that the Law is a deliberate Delaware-LLC
import.

## The default duty

Where the LLC agreement is silent, a member or manager
who exercises rights or powers in respect of the LLC
owes the LLC and the other members the **duty of good
faith**.

## The modification regime

The LLC agreement may **expand, restrict, or eliminate**
duties (including fiduciary duties) that a member or
manager would otherwise owe — **provided** that:

1. the **duty of good faith** cannot be eliminated; and
2. the LLC agreement may not eliminate liability for any
   act or omission that involves **fraud, wilful default,
   or gross negligence**.

Within those guard-rails, the LLC agreement may **replace
the default fiduciary duty framework with a contractually-
specified one** — the most consequential single feature
distinguishing the Jersey LLC from a Jersey company under
the Companies Law 1991, where directors' fiduciary duties
are non-modifiable.

## Comparison

| Regime | Default duty | Modification |
|---|---|---|
| **Jersey Company (CJL 1991)** | Statutory + customary fiduciary duties | Largely **non-modifiable** |
| **Jersey LLC (Art 47)** | Duty of good faith | **Modifiable**, subject to fraud / wilful default / gross negligence floor |
| **Delaware LLC** | Default fiduciary duties | **Modifiable** to a similar floor |
| **Cayman LLC** | Default fiduciary duties | **Modifiable**, similar floor |

This is the doctrinal foundation for using a Jersey LLC
as a **fund GP or LP** where the LLC agreement specifies
the precise duty framework rather than relying on
default common-law / statutory duties.

## Use cases

- **Fund GP** — LLC agreement contracts away fiduciary
  duties to LPs (other than the floor) so the GP's
  conflict-of-interest scope is defined by contract.
- **JV vehicle** — joint venturers can specify their
  duties to each other entirely in the LLC agreement.
- **Family office GP** — duties calibrated to family-
  internal expectations.
