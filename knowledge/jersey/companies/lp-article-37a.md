# LP Law 1994 Article 37A — Regulations

The **States** may make Regulations addressing:

- **Partner disqualification**;
- **Auditing**;
- **Continuance** (in / out);
- **Merger**;
- **Segregated portfolios** (cell-LP arrangements).

The segregated-portfolio enabling power is particularly
significant — it permits Jersey LPs to be configured as cell
structures akin to PCC / ICC companies, by Regulation rather
than primary legislation.
