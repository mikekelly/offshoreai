# CL 2014 Part 5 — Investigations and Powers

Part 5 sets out the Commissioner's **investigative and
remedial powers** for charity governance and asset protection.

## Article 39 — Initiating investigation

The Commissioner may **investigate**:

- A **suspected breach** of duties by trustees;
- **Misappropriation** of charity assets;
- **Misuse** of charity name or status;
- **Concerns** identified through annual reporting or
  complaints;
- **Themes** affecting the sector.

## Article 40 — Investigatory powers

In investigation, the Commissioner can:

- **Require** documents and records;
- **Require** information from trustees, beneficiaries,
  service providers;
- **Compel** attendance at examinations;
- **Inspect** charity premises and records;
- **Engage** specialist investigators;
- **Cooperate** with criminal investigators (AG, Police, JFSC).

## Article 41 — Interim directions

The Commissioner may issue **interim directions** pending
investigation:

- **Freeze** charity assets pending investigation;
- **Suspend** trustee from acting;
- **Limit** specific activities;
- **Require** specific operational measures.

## Article 42 — Substantive directions

Following investigation, the Commissioner may direct:

- **Specific corrective** action;
- **Restitution** of misappropriated assets;
- **Removal** of named trustees;
- **Appointment** of interim trustees;
- **Restructuring** of governance;
- **Closure** or wind-down of the charity in extremis.

## Article 43 — Asset protection

For **at-risk** charity assets:

- **Freezing orders** through the Royal Court;
- **Receiverships** appointed;
- **Asset realisation** under Court supervision;
- **Restoration** to legitimate charitable use.

## Articles 44-46 — Trustee disqualification

The Commissioner may **disqualify** a person from acting as
charity trustee:

- For **misconduct** in charity governance;
- For **dishonesty** or **fraud**;
- For **failure to comply** with directions;
- For **breach** of fiduciary duty;
- **Period** typically 3-10 years;
- **Subject** to appeal.

## Article 47 — Cooperation with other authorities

The Commissioner cooperates with:

- **JFSC** for regulated trustees (TCB firms acting as
  charity trustees);
- **AG** for criminal matters;
- **Comptroller of Revenue** for tax-status concerns;
- **UK Charity Commission** for cross-border charity
  concerns;
- **FIU** for AML concerns;
- **Foreign** charity regulators.

## Article 48 — Confidentiality

Information obtained in investigation:

- **Confidential**;
- **Specific exceptions** for sharing with other authorities;
- **Court process** for compelled disclosure;
- **Specific protections** for whistleblowers and complainants.
