# CL 2014 Part 8 — Miscellaneous

## Articles 64-66 — Regulations and Orders

The Minister may make **Orders**:

- **Forms** and **fees**;
- **Specific** procedural requirements;
- **Thresholds** (audit, restricted section, etc.);
- **Specific** Commissioner powers detail;
- **Updates** to implementing regulations.

The Commissioner may issue **guidance**:

- **Sector-wide** practical guidance;
- **Specific topic** guidance (governance, fundraising,
  international charity work);
- **Updates** following enforcement themes;
- **Not strictly** binding but treated as expected standard.

## Articles 67-68 — Transitional

Transitional provisions covered:

- **Pre-CL** charitable entities migrating to register;
- **Phasing** of registration deadlines;
- **Historic section** for pre-CL entities;
- **Specific** legacy arrangements.

## Article 69 — Tax status

The Commissioner's registration **establishes Jersey
charitable tax status**:

- Tax exemption under **ITL 1961 Article 115**;
- **GST** exemption;
- **Charity tax relief** for Jersey donors (limited);
- **International** recognition for tax exemption in some
  jurisdictions (subject to local rules).

A charity **must register** to obtain Jersey tax status — an
unregistered charity-purpose entity does not benefit from tax
exemption.

## Article 70 — Citation

The Law is cited as the **"Charities (Jersey) Law 2014"**.

It came into force in **phases from 2014 to 2018** as the
Commissioner office was established and the register
populated.

Amendments have addressed:

- **Procedural** refinements;
- **Specific** category clarifications;
- **Public-benefit** test refinements;
- **Cross-jurisdictional** coordination matters.
