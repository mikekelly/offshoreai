# Forfeiture Law 2018 Article 6 — Initial detention of seized cash for 96 hours

Cash seized under [Article 5](./foa-article-5.md) may be
**detained for an initial period not exceeding 96 hours**
for the purposes of investigation. Further detention
requires a Court order under
[Article 7](./foa-article-7.md).
