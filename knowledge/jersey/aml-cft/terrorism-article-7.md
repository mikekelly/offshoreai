# Terrorism Law 2002 Article 7 — Deproscription: application

A proscribed organisation (or a person affected by the
proscription) may **apply to the Minister** for removal
from Schedule 1. The Minister determines the application;
refusal opens the appeal route in
[Article 8](./terrorism-article-8.md).
