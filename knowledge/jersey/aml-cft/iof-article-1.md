# Investigation of Fraud Law 1991 Article 1 — Interpretation

Article 1 defines key terms — including **"the Attorney
General"**, **"document"**, and **"investigation"** —
used throughout the Law.

A reference to "serious or complex fraud" is the
threshold concept that gates the
[Article 2](./iof-article-2.md) investigative powers:
the Attorney General must be satisfied that there is good
reason to investigate, and that the matter falls within
the serious-or-complex-fraud threshold, before issuing
notices.
