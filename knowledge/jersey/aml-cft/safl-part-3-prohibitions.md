# SAFL 2014 Part 3 — Prohibitions (residual)

Articles 10 (asset freezing) and 11 (making available) have
their own deep files. This cluster covers Articles 12-14
addressing **sectoral and specific prohibitions**.

## Article 12 — Sectoral prohibitions

Beyond the personal designations, Jersey may impose **sectoral**
sanctions:

- **Country / region** sanctions (Russia, Iran, North Korea,
  Belarus, etc.);
- **Sector-specific** measures (oil and gas, technology, dual-
  use goods, defence);
- **Trade restrictions** on imports / exports;
- **Investment** restrictions in sectoral entities;
- **Service** restrictions (specific professional services
  for sanctioned sectors).

These typically mirror **UK sectoral sanctions** under SAMLA.

## Article 13 — Financial services prohibitions

Specific to **financial services**:

- **No** new financial-services relationships with designated
  persons or designated sectors;
- **No** new bank accounts;
- **No** new investment management;
- **No** new lending;
- **No** new insurance;
- **Restrictions** on settlement of pre-existing obligations
  beyond statutory exceptions;
- **Restrictions** on correspondent banking with sanctioned
  banks.

## Article 14 — Trade prohibitions

For **trade in goods**:

- **Export** restrictions on specific goods to specific
  destinations;
- **Import** restrictions from specific origins;
- **Dual-use goods** controls (items with civilian and
  military applications);
- **Specific** technology controls;
- **Coordination** with UK / EU trade-control regimes.

Jersey **rarely** acts independently on trade — Jersey
follows UK trade-control regimes through SAFL implementation.

## Specific regime examples

### Russia (post-February 2022)

Jersey implements **UK Russia sanctions** including:

- **Personal designations** of Putin-regime figures, oligarchs;
- **Oil price cap** measures;
- **Specific entity** sanctions (state-owned enterprises);
- **Investment restrictions**;
- **Specific service** prohibitions (legal services restrictions,
  trust services to sanctioned persons);
- **Reporting** obligations on frozen assets.

### Iran

Jersey implements UK Iran sanctions:

- **Nuclear-related** restrictions;
- **Ballistic missile** restrictions;
- **Terrorism-financing** restrictions;
- **Specific entity** and personal designations.

### Other regimes

- **DPRK** — comprehensive UN-based restrictions;
- **Syria** — selective sectoral and personal;
- **Belarus** — UK-led regime mirrored;
- **Myanmar** — UK-led regime mirrored;
- **Venezuela** — selective;
- **Various human rights** regimes (Magnitsky-style).

## Practical compliance

For Jersey businesses:

- **Country / region screening** in addition to person
  screening;
- **Sectoral checks** for specific industries;
- **Geo-restrictions** in IT systems;
- **Bank correspondent** relationships scrutinised;
- **Trade documentation** reviewed for sanctioned-origin /
  destination indicators;
- **Reporting** of suspected breaches.
