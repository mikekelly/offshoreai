# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 6 — Designation of supervisory bodies

The Minister may by **Order** designate further
supervisory bodies under this Law for specific classes of
supervised business — providing a path to bring
additional sectors or activities into the supervised
perimeter beyond the JFSC's primary supervisory remit.
