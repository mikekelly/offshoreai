# MLO 2008 Article 21 — Citation

Article 21 is the **citation** provision of the Money
Laundering (Jersey) Order 2008.

## The provision

The Order is cited as the **"Money Laundering (Jersey) Order
2008"**.

## Commencement

The Order came into force on **1 January 2008**.

## Amendments

The MLO has been amended on several occasions to align with:

- **FATF** Revised Recommendations;
- **Moneyval** evaluation feedback;
- **EU AML Directives** (selectively);
- **JFSC supervisory** experience;
- **Specific** sectoral updates.

## Related instruments

The MLO 2008 operates alongside:

- **Proceeds of Crime (Jersey) Law 1999** — the principal
  statute;
- **JFSC AML / CFT Handbook** — practical guidance;
- **Sanctions and Asset-Freezing (Jersey) Law 2014** —
  parallel sanctions regime;
- **Crime (International Co-operation) (Jersey) Law 2001**
  — MLA framework;
- **Specific** sectoral codes of practice.

## International standing

The MLO 2008 implements:

- **FATF Recommendations** 10-16, 22, 24;
- **Specific** Moneyval expectations;
- **EU AMLD** principles (where compatible);
- **OECD** transparency standards.
