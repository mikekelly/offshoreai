# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 8 — General powers of supervisory bodies

A supervisory body has all the **general powers**
necessary to discharge its supervisory functions —
including powers to monitor compliance, conduct
inspections, collect information, issue guidance and
codes, and take enforcement action. The Article 8 powers
are the **general functional grant** under which the
specific powers in Part 5 of the Law are exercised.
