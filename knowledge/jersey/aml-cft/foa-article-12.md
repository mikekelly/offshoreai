# Forfeiture Law 2018 Article 12 — Property restraint orders

The Royal Court may make a **property restraint order**
on the application of the Attorney General prohibiting
any person from dealing with specified property pending
forfeiture proceedings.

The restraint regime mirrors the POCL Article 15-style
restraint mechanism but operates within the civil
recovery framework.
