# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 31 — Investigations on behalf of a supervisory body

The supervisory body may **appoint investigators** to
conduct investigations on its behalf, with the
information-gathering powers under
[Article 30](./pocsb-article-30.md). Appointed
investigators (skilled persons / s166-style reviews)
report to the supervisory body and the supervised
business is required to cooperate.
