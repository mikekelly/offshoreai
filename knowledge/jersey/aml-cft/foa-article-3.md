# Forfeiture Law 2018 Article 3 — Property ceasing to be tainted property

Property ceases to be tainted property where it is
acquired by a **bona fide purchaser for value without
notice** of the taint. The Article protects innocent
third-party acquirers — the policy is that civil
forfeiture should reach the proceeds of crime in the
hands of the criminal or knowing recipients, not in the
hands of innocent counterparties.
