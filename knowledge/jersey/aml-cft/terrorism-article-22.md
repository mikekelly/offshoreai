# Terrorism Law 2002 Article 22 — Protected disclosures

A **disclosure that satisfies the conditions** for being a
protected disclosure is not actionable. Mirrors the POCL
protected-disclosure framework: persons (including
employees) who disclose suspicion of terrorist financing
to the FIU or internally to the regulated business's
MLRO are protected from civil liability.
