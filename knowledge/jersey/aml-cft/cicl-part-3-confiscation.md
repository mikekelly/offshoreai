# CICL 2001 Part 3 — Asset Realisation and Sharing (residual)

Articles 13 (foreign restraint) and 14 (foreign confiscation)
have their own deep files. This cluster covers Articles 15-22.

## Articles 15-17 — Asset realisation

The Viscount or appointed officer **realises** registered
foreign confiscation orders:

- **Takes possession** of Jersey property;
- **Sells** at market value;
- **Engages** professional advisers as needed;
- **Accounts** for proceeds;
- **Pays** subject to priority of legitimate third-party
  claims.

## Articles 18-19 — Asset sharing

Proceeds of recovery are **shared** between Jersey and the
requesting state:

- **Treaty arrangements** specify ratios;
- **Typical** sharing 80/20 or 50/50 depending on treaty
  and circumstances;
- **Specific cases** negotiated separately for major
  recoveries;
- **Compensation** to victims may be addressed.

## Article 20 — Third-party claims

Third parties may claim:

- **Pre-existing** interests in the property;
- **BFPV** protection;
- **Specific** beneficial interests in trust property;
- **Legitimate** business claims.

The Royal Court adjudicates competing claims.

## Article 21 — Ancillary orders

The Royal Court can make ancillary orders:

- **Information disclosure** requirements;
- **Asset tracing** orders;
- **Costs** of realisation;
- **Specific procedural** directions.

## Article 22 — Reciprocity

Jersey-issued restraint and confiscation orders are
**enforceable abroad** through:

- **UK** reciprocal arrangements;
- **Bilateral treaties**;
- **UN convention** frameworks;
- **Letters rogatory** to foreign courts.

The reciprocity provision supports Jersey's role in **cross-
border asset recovery**.

## Practical considerations

For Jersey-related asset recovery:

- **Engage Jersey AG** early;
- **Document** the foreign proceedings comprehensively;
- **Identify** specific Jersey assets and their current
  holders;
- **Consider** sanctions / freezing parallel action;
- **Coordinate** with Jersey civil-asset-recovery options
  (POCL Articles 42-45);
- **Plan** for asset sharing if recovery successful.
