# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 8A — Duty of supervisory body to use a risk-based approach

Article 8A is a **post-MoneyVal-era inserted duty**
requiring the supervisory body (the JFSC) to exercise its
supervisory functions **in accordance with a risk-based
approach** — concentrating supervisory resources on the
businesses, sectors and risks presenting the greatest
threat of money laundering and terrorism financing.

This is the statutory underpinning of the JFSC's
**risk-based supervisory model** and matches the FATF
Recommendation 1 / 26 / 28 expectation.
