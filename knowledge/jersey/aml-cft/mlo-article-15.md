# MLO 2008 Article 15 — External SARs

Article 15 requires the **MLRO** to make external SARs to
the FIU.

## When external SAR is required

The MLRO must make an **external SAR** to the FIU when:

- **Internal disclosure** has been received;
- The MLRO has **assessed** the matter;
- The MLRO **knows, suspects, or has reasonable grounds for
  suspecting** ML / TF;
- **As soon as practicable** after the MLRO's determination.

## SAR content

A SAR should contain:

- **Subject details** — name, identifiers, address;
- **Reporter** details — firm, MLRO, contact;
- **Nature** of the suspicion;
- **Activity** giving rise to the suspicion;
- **Source** of the information;
- **Documents** referenced;
- **Consent request** if needed for a specific transaction;
- **Other relevant context**.

## Format

The FIU prescribes **electronic SAR formats** through the
ELMER system (or equivalent):

- **Online** submission;
- **Specific** templates;
- **Manual** submissions accepted in exceptional cases.

## Consent procedure

Where the SAR relates to a transaction requiring consent:

- **Specify** the transaction;
- **Request consent** to proceed;
- **Provide** the FIU 7 working days (notice period);
- **Wait** for response or deemed consent.

If consent **refused**:

- Further **31-day moratorium** during which the prohibited
  act remains an offence;
- **No** transaction during moratorium;
- **Investigation** progresses with FIU.

## Quality

While Article 15 is about *making* the disclosure, MLROs
must ensure SAR **quality**:

- **Detail sufficient** for FIU to assess and act;
- **Identification** of all relevant parties;
- **Documents** referenced or attached;
- **Suspicion basis** clearly articulated;
- **Consent request** if needed.

A poor-quality SAR may be **insufficient** to discharge the
obligation.

## Timing

**"As soon as practicable"**:

- Allows **reasonable assessment** time;
- Does **not permit strategic delay**;
- Typically external SAR filed within **24-72 hours** of MLRO
  determination;
- Significant delay risks Article 34A POCL liability.

## Records

- **SAR log** maintained;
- **Internal disclosure log** referenced;
- **Decision-making documentation**;
- **FIU communications**;
- **Consent decisions**;
- **Retention** per Article 17 (5+ years).
