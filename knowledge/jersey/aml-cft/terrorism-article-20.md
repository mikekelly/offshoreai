# Terrorism Law 2002 Article 20 — Disclosure of information: immunity

A disclosure made under
[Article 18 or 19](./terrorism-article-18.md) is **not
to be treated as a breach** of any restriction on
disclosure imposed by statute, contract, or otherwise.
Persons making disclosures incur **no civil liability**
in respect of the disclosure if made in good faith.
