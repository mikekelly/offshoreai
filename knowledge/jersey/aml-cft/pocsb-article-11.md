# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 11 — Application for registration and deemed registration

Sets the **two registration routes**:

1. **Express application** under
   [Article 13](./pocsb-article-13.md) by a person seeking
   to carry on Schedule 2 business not already covered
   under another licence; and
2. **Deemed registration** — persons holding existing
   regulated-business licences under the
   [principal regulatory laws](../financial-regulation/index.md)
   are treated as registered without separate application.
