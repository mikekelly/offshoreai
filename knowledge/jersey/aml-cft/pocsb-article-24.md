# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 24 — Injunctions and remedial Orders

On the supervisory body's application, the **Royal Court**
may grant an **injunction** restraining a supervised
business from contravening AML/CFT legislation or a
direction, and may make **remedial Orders** requiring
restorative action.
