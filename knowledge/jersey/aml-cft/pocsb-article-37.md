# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 37 — Requirement to report certain matters to a supervisory body

Supervised businesses are required to **proactively
notify** the supervisory body of specified matters —
typically including changes in beneficial ownership,
principal persons, business activity, and material
compliance failings.

This is distinct from the SAR regime
([POCL Articles 34A](./pocl-article-34a.md) and
[Terrorism Articles 19 / 21](./terrorism-article-19.md))
— Article 37 reporting goes **to the supervisor** (JFSC)
rather than to the FIU.
