# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 12 — Determination of relevant supervisory body

Where more than one supervisory body could supervise a
person, Article 12 governs the determination of the
**relevant supervisory body** — typically the JFSC under
[Article 5](./pocsb-article-5.md) given the single-
supervisor architecture.
