# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 18 — Revocation of registration

The supervisory body may **revoke** a registration where
the registered person no longer satisfies the fit-and-
proper test or has materially breached AML/CFT
legislation or conditions of registration. Revocation
removes the person from the supervised perimeter and
prohibits continued Schedule 2 business.
