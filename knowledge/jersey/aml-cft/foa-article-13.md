# Forfeiture Law 2018 Article 13 — Effect of property restraint order

A property restraint order **prohibits all dealings**
with the restrained property (transfers, encumbrances,
diminutions in value) by any person served with the
order, until discharged.

Breach is contempt of Court and may also constitute the
[Article 31 obstruction offence](./foa-article-31.md).
