# Terrorism Law 2002 Article 18 — Co-operation with FIU or police

A person does **not commit an offence** under
[Article 15 or 16](./terrorism-article-15.md) if the
person makes a disclosure to the **Financial Intelligence
Unit (FIU)** or a police officer, and either:

- the disclosure is made **before** doing the act in
  question and the act is done with the **consent** of
  the FIU / police officer; or
- the disclosure is made **as soon as reasonably
  practicable after** doing the act and the person had
  no reasonable opportunity to disclose first.

This is the **CFT-side equivalent of POCL DAML** consent.
