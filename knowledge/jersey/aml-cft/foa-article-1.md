# Forfeiture Law 2018 Article 1 — Interpretation: general

Article 1 defines key terms — "cash", "property",
"unlawful conduct", "Viscount", and others — used
throughout the Law. The pivotal concept,
**"tainted property"**, is defined separately in
[Article 2](./foa-article-2.md).

The Law is a **civil-recovery** statute: forfeiture
proceeds without requiring a criminal conviction and on
the civil **balance-of-probabilities** standard.
