# Forfeiture Law 2018 Article 27 — Release of restrained etc. property

The Court must order **release** of restrained or
detained property where:

- forfeiture proceedings are not commenced within the
  prescribed period;
- forfeiture is refused; or
- the Court is otherwise satisfied release is just.
