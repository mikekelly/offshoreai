# Terrorism Law 2002 Article 61 — Crown servants, regulators, etc.

The Law applies, with appropriate modifications, to
**Crown servants** and to **regulators** (notably the
[JFSC](../financial-regulation/jfsc.md)) acting in
their official capacities.
