# Forfeiture Law 2018 Article 24 — Disclosure orders

The Royal Court may make a **disclosure order**
authorising the Attorney General (or persons authorised
by the AG) to require any person to **answer questions,
provide information, or produce documents** relevant to a
civil forfeiture investigation. Analogous in function to
the
[Investigation of Fraud Law 1991 Article 2](./iof-article-2.md)
notice but specific to civil-forfeiture investigations.
