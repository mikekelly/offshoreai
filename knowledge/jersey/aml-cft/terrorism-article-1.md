# Terrorism Law 2002 Article 1 — General interpretation

Article 1 defines the **general interpretive vocabulary**
of the Law — including "FIU" (the Financial Intelligence
Unit), "police officer", "Schedule 1" (the proscribed-
organizations list), and other terms used throughout.
Specialist terms (terrorism, terrorist property, terrorist
entity, terrorist) are defined separately in
[Articles 2–4](./terrorism-article-2.md) and
[Article 36](./terrorism-article-36.md).
