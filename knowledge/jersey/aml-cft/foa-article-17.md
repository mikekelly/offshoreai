# Forfeiture Law 2018 Article 17 — Bankruptcy

Interaction between forfeiture and **bankruptcy /
désastre** proceedings: where the property owner is the
subject of a désastre or other insolvency procedure, the
Court determines priority between forfeiture and the
insolvency creditors. Tainted property is generally
forfeit notwithstanding the insolvency.
