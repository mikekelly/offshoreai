# Forfeiture Law 2018 Article 5 — Seizure of cash

A police or customs officer may **seize cash** found in
the course of an Article 4 search where the officer has
**reasonable grounds for suspecting** the cash is tainted
property. "Cash" includes Jersey and foreign currency
notes and coins, postal orders, cheques, bankers' drafts,
bearer bonds, and bearer shares.
