# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 2 — Meaning of "supervisory functions"

"**Supervisory functions**" means the functions of a
supervisory body in monitoring, regulating, and enforcing
compliance by supervised businesses with **AML/CFT
legislation**. It frames the JFSC's (and any other
designated supervisory body's) mandate under this Law.
