# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 7 — Guidance, directions and accountability

Authorises **guidance** and **directions** from the
Minister to designated supervisory bodies regarding the
exercise of supervisory functions, and provides for the
**accountability** of designated bodies to the Minister.
