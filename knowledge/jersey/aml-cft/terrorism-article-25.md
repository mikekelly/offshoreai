# Terrorism Law 2002 Article 25 — Disclosure for purposes outside Jersey

Authorises **international information-sharing** for
terrorism-financing investigation, prosecution, and
regulatory purposes — providing the statutory gateway
for FIU-to-FIU and regulator-to-regulator information
exchange with foreign counterparts.
