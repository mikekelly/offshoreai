# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 28 — Notice period for public statements

The notice given under [Article 27](./pocsb-article-27.md)
must allow a **prescribed period** (typically 28 days)
for the affected person to make representations before
publication, save in cases where urgent publication is
justified.
