# Terrorism Law 2002 Article 49 — Port controls

Article 49 (with the supporting Schedule) confers
**port and border** examination powers — examining and
detaining persons at ports and the airport to determine
whether they are persons concerned in terrorism.
Operational analogue to the UK Schedule 7 power.
