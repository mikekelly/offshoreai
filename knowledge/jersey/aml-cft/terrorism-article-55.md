# Terrorism Law 2002 Article 55 — Terrorist bombing: jurisdiction

Extends Jersey criminal jurisdiction over **terrorist
bombing offences** committed overseas where the
defendant is a Jersey resident or where the offence is
otherwise connected with Jersey — implementing the
International Convention for the Suppression of Terrorist
Bombings 1997.
