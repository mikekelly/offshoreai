# Terrorism Law 2002 Article 33 — Account monitoring orders

The Royal Court may make an **account monitoring order**
requiring a financial institution to provide specified
information about a named account for a specified period
(not exceeding 90 days). Used for ongoing surveillance of
suspected terrorist-financing activity.
