# Terrorism Law 2002 Article 63 — Offences by body corporate, etc.

Where an offence under this Law is committed by a **body
corporate** with the **consent or connivance** of, or
attributable to **neglect** on the part of, a director,
manager, secretary or similar officer (or partner of a
partnership), that person also commits the offence.

Standard officer-attribution provision.
