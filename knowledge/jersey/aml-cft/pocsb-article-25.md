# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 25 — Powers of intervention

The supervisory body has **intervention powers** —
including appointing investigators, requiring changes to
operational arrangements, restricting business
activities, and (in serious cases) effectively removing
control from existing management pending resolution.

These powers mirror the JFSC's intervention powers under
the principal regulatory laws but apply to the AML/CFT
supervisory function.
