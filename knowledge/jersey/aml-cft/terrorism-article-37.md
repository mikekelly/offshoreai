# Terrorism Law 2002 Article 37 — Arrest and detention of suspected terrorists

A police officer may **arrest without warrant** a person
whom the officer reasonably suspects to be a
"**terrorist**" within
[Article 36](./terrorism-article-36.md). Extended
detention without charge is available — subject to
judicial authorisation under PPCE 2003 Codes — running
beyond the standard PPCE detention clock.
