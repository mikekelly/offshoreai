# Terrorism Law 2002 Article 24 — Disclosure for purposes within Jersey

Authorises onward disclosure of information obtained
under the Law for **purposes within Jersey** —
investigation, prosecution, regulatory supervision, and
related law-enforcement and oversight functions.
