# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 39 — Co-operation with relevant overseas supervisory authority

The supervisory body may **cooperate with overseas
supervisory counterparts** — exchanging information,
conducting joint examinations, and providing supervisory
assistance, subject to appropriate confidentiality and
gateway controls.

This is the **statutory gateway** for JFSC's
participation in cross-border AML/CFT supervisory
networks (Egmont-adjacent, FATF / MoneyVal-related).
