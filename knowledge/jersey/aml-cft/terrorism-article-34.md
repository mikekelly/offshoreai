# Terrorism Law 2002 Article 34 — Information about acts of terrorism

A person who has information that the person knows or
believes might be of material assistance in **preventing
the commission** of an act of terrorism or in securing
the apprehension, prosecution or conviction of another
person for an offence involving the commission,
preparation or instigation of terrorism, commits an
offence by failing to disclose to the police as soon as
reasonably practicable.
