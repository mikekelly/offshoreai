# Forfeiture Law 2018 Article 10 — Notice to be given of summary procedure

Before commencing the summary-procedure forfeiture under
[Article 11](./foa-article-11.md), the Attorney General
must give **notice** to the person from whom the cash was
seized and to any person reasonably believed to have an
interest in the cash.
