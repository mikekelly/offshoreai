# Forfeiture Law 2018 Article 23 — Account monitoring orders

The Royal Court may make an **account monitoring order**
requiring a financial institution to provide specified
information about an account for a period not exceeding
**90 days**. Used for ongoing surveillance of suspected
tainted-property flows.
