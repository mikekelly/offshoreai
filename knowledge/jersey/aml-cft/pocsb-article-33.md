# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 33 — Obstruction of investigations

It is an offence to **obstruct** a supervisory body or
its appointed investigator in the exercise of functions
under Part 5 — including failing to provide information
or documents without reasonable excuse, or knowingly
providing false information.
