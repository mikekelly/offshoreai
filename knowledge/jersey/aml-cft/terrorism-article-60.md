# Terrorism Law 2002 Article 60 — Evidence

Evidentiary provisions including **admissibility of
foreign material** and certified documents, and
inferences that may be drawn from a defendant's failure
to mention facts on questioning. Operates alongside the
PPCE 2003 evidence framework.
