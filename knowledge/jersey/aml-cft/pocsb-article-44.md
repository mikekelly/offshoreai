# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 44 — Regulations and Orders

The **States** (Regulations) and the **Minister** (Orders)
may make secondary legislation under this Law — including
fees, forms, time periods, Schedule 2 perimeter
adjustments, and related operational rules.
