# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 26 — Public statements

The supervisory body may issue a **public statement**
naming a supervised business or principal person and
detailing AML/CFT contraventions, sanctions imposed, or
findings of concern. Public statements are a **major
reputational enforcement tool** — used in JFSC public
enforcement releases and frequently the operative
consequence of significant supervisory action.
