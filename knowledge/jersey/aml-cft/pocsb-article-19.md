# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 19 — Procedure on refusal, revocation, or new / varied condition

Sets the **procedural protections** when the supervisory
body refuses, revokes, or varies registration: written
notice of reasons, opportunity to make representations,
and the right to **appeal to the Royal Court**.
