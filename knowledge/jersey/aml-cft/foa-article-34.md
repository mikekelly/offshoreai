# Forfeiture Law 2018 Article 34 — Consent to settlement

The Attorney General may **enter into a settlement** with
the person whose property is the subject of forfeiture
proceedings — agreeing to forfeit a portion of the
property in lieu of contested proceedings. Settlements
require the Court's approval.

The settlement mechanism is a key efficiency feature of
the civil-recovery regime, enabling resolution of
asset-recovery cases without full trial.
