# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 42 — Offences generally

Consolidates **penalty levels** for offences under this
Law, including the [Article 10](./pocsb-article-10.md)
unauthorised-business offence and the obstruction /
information offences. Officer-attribution applies in the
standard form.
