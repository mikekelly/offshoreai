# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 17 — Conditions of registration

The supervisory body may **impose conditions** on
registration — typically requiring specific compliance
arrangements, reporting, or operational restrictions.
Conditions may be **varied** or **revoked** subject to
the [Article 19](./pocsb-article-19.md) procedure.
