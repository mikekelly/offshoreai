# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 1 — Interpretation

Article 1 defines the **vocabulary** of the Law —
including "supervisory body", "supervised business",
"Schedule 2 business", "the Commission" (the JFSC), and
"AML / CFT legislation". The specialist defined terms
("supervisory functions"; "AML/CFT legislation") are
elaborated in
[Articles 2](./pocsb-article-2.md) and
[3](./pocsb-article-3.md).
