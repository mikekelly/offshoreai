# Terrorism Law 2002 Article 6 — Proscription

An organisation is **proscribed** if listed in
**Schedule 1** to the Law. The Minister may by **Order**
add an organisation to Schedule 1 if the Minister is
satisfied the organisation is concerned in terrorism (and
may remove a listed organisation).
