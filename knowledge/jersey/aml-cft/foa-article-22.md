# Forfeiture Law 2018 Article 22 — Orders to provide customer information

The Royal Court may make a **customer information order**
requiring a specified **financial institution** (or all
financial institutions) to provide
[customer information](./foa-article-21.md) on a named
person for purposes of a civil forfeiture investigation.

Failure to comply is an offence. The order overrides
banker secrecy but preserves legal professional
privilege.
