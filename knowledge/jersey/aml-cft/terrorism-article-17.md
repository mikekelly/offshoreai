# Terrorism Law 2002 Article 17 — Jurisdiction for Articles 15, 16, 16A

The financing offences under
[Articles 15](./terrorism-article-15.md),
[16](./terrorism-article-16.md), and
[16A](./terrorism-article-16a.md) have **extraterritorial
reach**: the offence is committed regardless of whether
the conduct or the terrorism takes place in Jersey or
elsewhere.
