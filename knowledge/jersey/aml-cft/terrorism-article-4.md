# Terrorism Law 2002 Article 4 — Meaning of "terrorist entity"

**Terrorist entity** means a person, group or
organisation that **carries out**, **is concerned in the
preparation of**, or **promotes / encourages** acts of
terrorism — including proscribed organisations listed in
**Schedule 1** to the Law.
