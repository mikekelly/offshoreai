# Forfeiture Law 2018 Article 7 — Orders authorising detention longer than 96 hours

A Magistrate may order further detention of seized cash
for periods not exceeding **3 months** at a time, up to a
total of **2 years** from the initial seizure, where there
are reasonable grounds for suspecting the cash is tainted
property and further investigation is justified.
