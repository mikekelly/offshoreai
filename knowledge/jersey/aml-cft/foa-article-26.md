# Forfeiture Law 2018 Article 26 — Nature of proceedings, and rules of court

Proceedings under this Law are **civil proceedings** —
the **balance of probabilities** standard applies and
civil rules of evidence and procedure govern. The
Royal Court may make Rules of Court for proceedings under
the Law.
