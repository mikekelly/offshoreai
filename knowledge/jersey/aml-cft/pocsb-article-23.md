# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 23 — Power to issue directions

The supervisory body may issue **directions** to a
supervised business requiring or prohibiting specified
conduct in connection with compliance with AML/CFT
legislation. Failure to comply is enforceable, including
via the [Article 24](./pocsb-article-24.md) injunction
power.
