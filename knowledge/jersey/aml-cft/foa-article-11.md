# Forfeiture Law 2018 Article 11 — Forfeiture of tainted property: summary procedure

Article 11 is the **fast-track civil-forfeiture
mechanism** for **cash** (and certain other prescribed
property): if **no person serves a notice of objection**
within the specified period after the
[Article 10](./foa-article-10.md) notice is given, the
Attorney General may issue a **forfeiture notice** and
the property is forfeit to the Crown **without
proceedings**.

The mechanism is designed to deal efficiently with
uncontested cases — typically small-to-medium cash
seizures from couriers — and reserves the contested
[Article 15](./foa-article-15.md) general procedure for
cases where ownership / status is disputed.

## Civil standard

Where the summary procedure proceeds, the AG must be
satisfied on the balance of probabilities that the
property is tainted property.
