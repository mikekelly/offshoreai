# Terrorism Law 2002 Article 23 — Restrictions on disclosure

> **Disambiguation.** This Article governs **onward use of
> information already disclosed to authorities** — the
> confidentiality envelope around an investigation. It is **not**
> the tipping-off offence. For the offence of disclosing to a
> client / suspect that an investigation is under way, see
> **[Article 35](./terrorism-article-35.md)**. The two are
> related but distinct: Art 23 is about onward channels; Art 35
> is the offence of warning the subject.

Restricts onward disclosure of information obtained
under the Law. Information disclosed for terrorist-
financing investigation purposes may only be used or
disclosed in accordance with
[Articles 24 and 25](./terrorism-article-24.md).
