# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 13 — Application for registration

An applicant must apply to the relevant supervisory body
in the **prescribed form** with the **prescribed
information** — typically including the applicant's
identity, beneficial ownership, business activity, fit-
and-proper information for principal persons, and AML/CFT
compliance arrangements.
