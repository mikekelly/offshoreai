# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 21A — Application of Part 5 to AML services provider

Article 21A extends the **Part 5 supervisory regime** —
codes of practice, directions, intervention, information
powers, and the rest — to a broader category of "AML
services provider", capturing outsourced compliance and
ancillary AML/CFT service providers that fall outside the
core regulated-business perimeter.
