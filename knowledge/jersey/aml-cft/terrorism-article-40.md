# Terrorism Law 2002 Article 40 — Authorization to stop and search to prevent acts of terrorism

A senior police officer may give a written
**authorisation** that constables may stop and search
pedestrians or vehicles in a specified area if the
officer reasonably considers it expedient to prevent
acts of terrorism.
