# Terrorism Law 2002 Article 3 — Meaning of "terrorist property"

**Terrorist property** is property that:

1. is likely to be used for the purposes of **terrorism**
   (including resources of a proscribed organisation);
2. is the **proceeds** of the commission of acts of
   terrorism; or
3. is the **proceeds of acts carried out for the purposes
   of terrorism**.

The dual-mode definition — both **prospective** (likely
to be used for terrorism) and **retrospective** (proceeds
of terrorism) — distinguishes the regime from the POCL
1999 framework, which is retrospective-only. References
to proceeds include any property representing proceeds in
whole or in part.
