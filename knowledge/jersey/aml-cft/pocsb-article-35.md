# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 35 — Restricted information

Information obtained by the supervisory body under this
Law is **restricted information** — subject to
confidentiality obligations and not to be disclosed
except under the permitted-disclosure gateways in
[Article 36](./pocsb-article-36.md) or the supply
provisions in [Article 34](./pocsb-article-34.md).
