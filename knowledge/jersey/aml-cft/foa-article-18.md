# Forfeiture Law 2018 Article 18 — Civil forfeiture investigations

Defines a **civil forfeiture investigation** as an
investigation into the question of whether property is
tainted property and is the **gateway** for the
investigative powers in
[Articles 19–25](./foa-article-19.md).
