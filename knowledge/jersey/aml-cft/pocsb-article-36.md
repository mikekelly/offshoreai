# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 36 — Permitted disclosures

Lists the **permitted disclosure gateways** for
restricted information — e.g. with consent of the
person concerned, for the purposes of criminal
proceedings, for proceedings under this Law, to other
designated regulators, to MoneyVal / FATF reviewers, to
courts.
