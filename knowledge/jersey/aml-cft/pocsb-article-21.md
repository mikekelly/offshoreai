# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 21 — Determination of fees and late payment

Fees are **determined by the supervisory body** in
accordance with Orders made under
[Article 44](./pocsb-article-44.md). **Late payment**
attracts surcharges and may be grounds for revocation
under [Article 18](./pocsb-article-18.md).
