# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 5 — Commission to be supervisory body for all supervised business

Article 5 is the **constitutional designation** of the
[Jersey Financial Services Commission (JFSC)](../financial-regulation/jfsc.md)
as the **supervisory body** for all **supervised
business** under this Law. The supervisory function spans
both regulated financial-services businesses
(banks / TCBs / fund managers / etc.) and the broader
**Schedule 2** businesses (high-value goods dealers, real-
estate agents, lawyers, accountants — the "designated
non-financial businesses and professions" / DNFBPs).

This single-supervisor architecture — JFSC as sole
AML/CFT supervisor for the supervised perimeter — is the
**structural choice** that distinguishes Jersey from
some peer jurisdictions (e.g. the UK's split between
FCA, HMRC, and professional-body supervisors).
