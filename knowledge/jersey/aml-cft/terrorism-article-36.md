# Terrorism Law 2002 Article 36 — Interpretation of Part 5: "terrorist"

For the purposes of Part 5 (counter-terrorist powers), a
"**terrorist**" is a person who has committed an offence
under Articles 12–16A, 50–55, or who is or has been
**concerned in the commission, preparation or
instigation** of acts of terrorism.
