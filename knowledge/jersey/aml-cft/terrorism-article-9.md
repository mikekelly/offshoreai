# Terrorism Law 2002 Article 9 — Further appeal

A party to a Royal Court appeal under
[Article 8](./terrorism-article-8.md) may, with leave,
appeal to the **Court of Appeal** and ultimately, again
with leave, to the **Judicial Committee of the Privy
Council**.
