# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 3 — Meaning of "anti-money laundering and counter-terrorism legislation"

"**AML / CFT legislation**" comprises the Jersey statutory
framework against money-laundering and terrorist
financing, including:

- the [Proceeds of Crime (Jersey) Law 1999](./proceeds-of-crime-law-1999.md);
- the [Money Laundering (Jersey) Order 2008](./money-laundering-order-2008.md);
- the [Terrorism (Jersey) Law 2002](./terrorism-law-2002.md);
- the [Sanctions and Asset-Freezing (Jersey) Law 2019](./safl-2019-index.md);
- this Law and Orders under it.
