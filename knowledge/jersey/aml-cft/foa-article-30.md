# Forfeiture Law 2018 Article 30 — Compensation where bad faith proven

The Court may order **compensation** to a person who has
suffered loss as a result of action under this Law where
the action was taken in **bad faith**. The bad-faith
threshold is a deliberately high bar — protecting the
AG's operational latitude while preserving a remedy for
genuine abuse.
