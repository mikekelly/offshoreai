# Investigation of Fraud Law 1991 Article 3 — Disclosure of information

Article 3 provides that **information subject to
obligations of secrecy** imposed by other enactments may
be **disclosed to the Attorney General** or to authorised
persons for the purposes of this Law. The information may
only be **further disclosed** for the purpose of
prosecution or otherwise as expressly permitted.

This is the **statutory gateway** that allows the JFSC,
banks, TCBs, and other holders of secrecy-protected
information to comply with an
[Article 2](./iof-article-2.md) notice without breach of
any other Jersey secrecy obligation.
