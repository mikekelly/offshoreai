# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 14 — Grant or refusal of application for registration

The supervisory body **grants or refuses** the
application — applying a **fit-and-proper test** to the
applicant and its principal persons, and assessing the
adequacy of the applicant's AML/CFT systems and controls.
