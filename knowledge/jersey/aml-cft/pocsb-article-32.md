# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 32 — Entry and search of premises

The Bailiff may grant a **warrant** authorising the
supervisory body (or its agents) to **enter and search**
business premises in connection with a supervisory
investigation. Used where information cannot be obtained
voluntarily or where there is risk of concealment.
