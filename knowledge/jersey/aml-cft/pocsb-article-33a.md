# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 33A — Communication with supervisory body by auditors and approved professionals

Article 33A provides a **protected channel** for
**auditors and approved professionals** (e.g. statutory
auditors of a supervised business) to communicate
concerns about AML/CFT compliance directly to the
supervisory body — without breach of professional duty
or contractual confidentiality.

Implements FATF Recommendation 32 / 33 on supervised-
business auditor cooperation.
