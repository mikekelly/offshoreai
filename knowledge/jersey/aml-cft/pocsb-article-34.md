# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 34 — Supply of information

The supervisory body may **supply information** it has
obtained under this Law to other Jersey enforcement and
regulatory authorities (the police, FIU, Attorney
General, Income Tax Office, Comptroller, other JFSC
divisions) where the supply is needed for the recipient
to perform statutory functions.
