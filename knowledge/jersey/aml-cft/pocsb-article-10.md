# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 10 — Prohibition of carrying on unauthorised Schedule 2 business

Article 10 is the **central registration gatekeeper** for
the supervised perimeter. A person must **not carry on
Schedule 2 business** (as defined in the
[POCL 1999 Schedule 2](./pocl-schedule-2.md)) unless
**registered** with the JFSC (or another designated
supervisory body) under this Law.

## The supervised perimeter

Schedule 2 covers — among others — the DNFBPs
(designated non-financial businesses and professions):
high-value-goods dealers, real-estate agents, lawyers,
accountants, virtual-asset service providers (VASPs).
Regulated financial-services businesses are
**deemed registered** by virtue of their existing
licensing under
[the principal regulatory laws](../financial-regulation/index.md).

## Offence

Carrying on Schedule 2 business without registration is
an **offence** with significant criminal penalties.
