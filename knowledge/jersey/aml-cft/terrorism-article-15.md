# Terrorism Law 2002 Article 15 — Use and possession of property for terrorism

Article 15 is the **core terrorist-financing offence**.
It is the CFT counterpart to the principal ML offences in
[POCL Articles 30–32](./pocl-article-30.md).

## The offence

A person commits an offence if the person:

1. **uses money or other property** for the purposes of
   terrorism; or
2. **possesses money or other property**, and **intends
   that it should be used, or has reasonable cause to
   suspect that it may be used**, for the purposes of
   terrorism.

The mental element ("reasonable cause to suspect") sets
the bar at an **objective** rather than purely subjective
standard — broadening reach beyond knowledge or intent.

## Penalty

Liable on indictment to imprisonment for a term not
exceeding **14 years** and / or a fine.
