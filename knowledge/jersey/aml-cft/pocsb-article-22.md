# PoCSB Law 2008 Article 22 — Codes of practice

Article 22 is the **statutory basis** for the JFSC's
issuance of binding **codes of practice** in respect of
supervised business — most importantly the
[JFSC AML/CFT Handbook](./aml-cft-handbook.md).

## Status of codes

A code of practice under this Article is **not strictly
binding law in itself**, but failure to comply with a
code:

- may be considered in any prosecution under AML/CFT
  legislation;
- is a relevant consideration in enforcement decisions;
- may result in directions, intervention, or public
  statements under the further Part 5 powers.

The Article is therefore the **doctrinal underpinning**
of the JFSC's principal regulatory toolset for AML/CFT
supervision.
