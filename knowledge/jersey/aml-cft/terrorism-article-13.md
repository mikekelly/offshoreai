# Terrorism Law 2002 Article 13 — Support

It is an offence to **invite support** (other than
financial) for a proscribed organisation; to **arrange,
manage or assist in arranging or managing** a meeting in
support of a proscribed organisation; or to **address** a
meeting whose purpose is supporting the organisation or
furthering its activities.
