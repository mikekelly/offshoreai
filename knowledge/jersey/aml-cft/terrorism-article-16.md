# Terrorism Law 2002 Article 16 — Dealing with terrorist property

Article 16 prohibits **dealing with terrorist property**
— entering into or becoming concerned in an arrangement
that facilitates the **retention or control** of
terrorist property by or on behalf of another person.

## The offence

A person commits an offence if the person enters into
or becomes concerned in an arrangement which facilitates
the retention or control by or on behalf of another
person of terrorist property by:

- concealment;
- removal from the jurisdiction;
- transfer to nominees; or
- in any other way.

The mens rea is **knowledge or reasonable suspicion**
that the arrangement relates to terrorist property.

## The "permitted arrangement" defence

It is a defence to show that the arrangement was made
with prior **JFCU consent** (the equivalent of POCL's
DAML consent regime), or that the defendant had a
reasonable excuse for not knowing.

## Penalty

Liable on indictment to imprisonment for a term not
exceeding **14 years** and / or a fine.
