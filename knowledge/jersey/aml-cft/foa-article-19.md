# Forfeiture Law 2018 Article 19 — Production orders

The Royal Court may make a **production order** requiring
a specified person to **produce material** (or give
access to it) in the possession or under the control of
the person, where the material is likely to be of
substantial value to a civil forfeiture investigation.

A production order overrides Jersey banker / professional
secrecy obligations but preserves **legal professional
privilege**.
