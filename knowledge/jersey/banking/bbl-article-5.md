# BBL 1991 Article 5 — Application for Registration

Article 5 specifies the **application requirements** for
Jersey banking registration.

## Application content

A complete BBL registration application includes:

### Applicant identification

- **Constitutional documents** (memorandum, articles);
- **Group structure** with ultimate beneficial owners;
- **Home regulator** confirmation and consent letter;
- **Specific** parent banking group details.

### Business plan

- **Proposed scope** of activity;
- **Target markets** (depositor base, geographic, sectoral);
- **Lending plans**;
- **Risk** appetite;
- **Volume** projections (3-5 years).

### Capital plan

- **Proposed capital base**;
- **Source** of capital;
- **Group capital** support arrangements;
- **Specific** Basel III compliance plan.

### Liquidity

- **Liquidity coverage** plan;
- **Net stable funding**;
- **Specific** stressed-liquidity scenarios.

### Principal persons

For each director, senior officer, controller:

- **Personal questionnaire**;
- **CV** and qualifications;
- **Regulatory history**;
- **Fit-and-proper** notification.

### Operational

- **Jersey premises**;
- **Staffing** plan;
- **IT systems**;
- **Outsourcing** arrangements;
- **Specific** AML / CFT programme.

### Governance

- **Board** composition;
- **Risk management** framework;
- **Internal audit**;
- **Compliance** function;
- **Specific** Jersey governance.

### Recovery / resolution

- **Recovery plan** summary;
- **Resolution** considerations;
- **Specific** Jersey resolution-regime alignment.

### Auditors

- **Auditor** appointment;
- **Internal audit** arrangements.

## Substantive

The application is **substantial**:

- **Typically months** of pre-application engagement;
- **Formal** application of 100+ pages;
- **Specific** JFSC engagement throughout;
- **Significant** fees and timeline.
