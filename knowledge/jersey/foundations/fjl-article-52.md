# FJL 2009 Article 52 — Fees and Forms

Article 52 provides for **fees and forms** prescribed by the
Minister.

## Fees

The Minister prescribes fees for:

- **Establishment** of foundation (initial registration);
- **Annual return** filing;
- **Amendments** (charter, name, registered office, council);
- **Continuance** in or out;
- **Dissolution** filings;
- **Copies and extracts** from the register;
- **Late penalties**;
- **Specific** other matters.

## Fee structure

Typical Jersey foundation fees (subject to current JFSC
schedule):

- **Establishment**: £150-£500 typical;
- **Annual return**: £150-£500 typical;
- **Name change**: specific fee;
- **Continuance**: higher fees reflecting complexity;
- **Specific** add-on services priced separately.

Practitioners check the **current JFSC fee schedule** for live
amounts.

## Forms

The Minister / Registrar prescribes **forms** for:

- **Application** for establishment;
- **Annual return**;
- **Amendments**;
- **Notice of changes** (council, registered office);
- **Continuance** application;
- **Dissolution** filing;
- **Specific** other procedural forms.

## Online forms

Most forms are **submitted online**:

- **JFSC online system**;
- **Login** required for registered users;
- **Specific** authentication for qualified-member firms;
- **Specific** procedural rules.

## Late filing fees

Late filings attract penalties:

- **Late fee** added to standard fee;
- **Scaled** by lateness;
- **Specific** Registrar discretion in some cases;
- **Compounded** for serial non-compliance.

## Fee waivers

In exceptional cases:

- **Specific** charitable / public-purpose waivers;
- **Specific** humanitarian considerations;
- **Subject to** Registrar discretion;
- **Rare** in practice.
