# FJL 2009 Article 51 — Inspection of Register

Article 51 establishes the **public inspection** rules for
the register.

## Public-inspection scope

The register is **public** in respect of:

- **Charter** (the public constitutional document);
- **Name**;
- **Registered office**;
- **Council members**;
- **Qualified member**;
- **Status**;
- **Date of establishment**;
- **Annual returns** (most content).

## NOT public

- **Regulations** (private constitutional document);
- **Detailed** beneficiary information (subject to specific
  BO register rules);
- **Internal** governance documents;
- **Specific** transaction terms.

## How to inspect

### Online

- **JFSC online registry** — primary access route;
- **Free** basic searches;
- **Paid** detailed information / certified copies;
- **24/7** availability.

### In person

- **JFSC Registry** at Hill Street, St Helier;
- **Office hours** access;
- **Specific** procedures and fees.

### Specific documents

- **Charter** copies on payment of fee;
- **Annual returns**;
- **Council membership** history;
- **Specific** filings.

## Fees

- **Search fees** for specific information;
- **Copy fees** for documents;
- **Certification fees** for certified copies;
- **Updated** annually per JFSC schedule.

## Transparency / privacy compromise

The Jersey foundation **register publicity** reflects a
**deliberate compromise**:

- **Charter public** — outlines foundation's existence,
  objects, governance structure;
- **Regulations private** — preserves confidentiality of
  family / specific arrangements;
- **Council public** — accountability for those running the
  foundation;
- **Beneficiaries private** — except in BO register
  (authority access).

This is **distinct from**:

- **UK company register** (more public);
- **Cayman foundation register** (different rules);
- **Specific** other jurisdictions.

## Specific use cases

### Counterparty diligence

- **Confirm** foundation exists;
- **Identify** council members for authorisation purposes;
- **Verify** registered office for service;
- **Specific** charter terms for authority understanding.

### Journalist / NGO research

- **Find** foundations by name search;
- **Identify** publicly-disclosed details;
- **Limited** access beyond public content.

### Foreign authority

- **Through proper channels** (MoU, treaty);
- **Specific** authority access to non-public content.
