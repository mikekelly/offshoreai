# FJL 2009 Article 34 — Annual Return

Article 34 requires every foundation to **file an annual
return** with the Registrar.

## The requirement

Every foundation must file an annual return:

- **By the prescribed date** (typically end of January for
  the preceding calendar year);
- **Containing** the prescribed information;
- **Accompanied by** the prescribed fee;
- **Signed by** a council member.

## Content

The annual return typically includes:

- **Foundation name** and registration number;
- **Current council members** (and any changes during year);
- **Qualified member** identification;
- **Registered office** address;
- **Confirmation** of charter / regulations status;
- **Confirmation** of compliance with relevant obligations;
- **Specific** financial / activity information per
  Registrar requirements;
- **Specific** beneficial-ownership information (per
  FS(DPI)(J)L 2020).

## Public

The annual return is **public**:

- **Accessible** through the JFSC online register;
- **One of** the few public-record elements of a Jersey
  foundation (alongside charter, name changes,
  dissolution);
- **Specific** items disclosed publicly.

## Late filing

Late annual return triggers:

- **Late-filing penalties** (scaled by lateness);
- **Risk** of strike-off (Article 44) for serious failure;
- **JFSC** action against qualified member;
- **Public** record of late filing visible.

## Filing process

In practice:

- **Qualified-member firm** prepares and files the annual
  return;
- **Council** reviews and signs;
- **Online** filing through JFSC system;
- **Fee** paid;
- **Confirmation** received;
- **Records** updated.

## Annual fee

Alongside the annual return:

- **Annual fee** paid to the Registrar;
- **Specific** amount per JFSC fee schedule;
- **Updated** annually;
- **Late payment** triggers penalties.

## Specific information for the BO register

The FS(DPI)(J)L 2020 also requires beneficial-ownership
information:

- **Identified** to the Registrar through specific filing;
- **Updated** for material changes;
- **Annual** confirmation;
- **Specific** access rules for authorities.

## Comparison with other entities

| Entity | Annual return required |
|---|---|
| Jersey company | Yes (Companies Law) |
| Jersey foundation | Yes (FJL Article 34) |
| Jersey trust | **No** (trusts are not registered) |
| Jersey LP | Yes (LP Law) |
| Jersey LLP | Yes |
