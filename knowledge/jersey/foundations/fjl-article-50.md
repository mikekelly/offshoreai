# FJL 2009 Article 50 — Register of Foundations

Article 50 establishes the **Register of Foundations**
maintained by the Registrar.

## Content

The register contains:

- **Foundation name** (current and former);
- **Date of establishment**;
- **Status** (active, dissolved, struck off);
- **Registered office** address;
- **Council members** (per annual return);
- **Qualified member** identification;
- **Charter** (current version) and amendments;
- **Annual returns** filed;
- **Name changes**;
- **Continuances** in / out;
- **Dissolution** events.

## What's NOT on the register

- **Regulations** (these are private — held by the
  foundation);
- **Beneficiary detail** (in regulations or BO register);
- **Internal** governance documents;
- **Specific** transaction details;
- **Distribution records**.

## Public access

The register is **accessible** through:

- **JFSC online registry** system;
- **Public** searches;
- **Specific** copies of public documents (charter, etc.)
  on payment of fee;
- **No** access to private content (regulations, beneficiary
  detail).

## Updates

The register is **updated** for:

- **New filings** (establishment, amendments, annual returns);
- **Material events** (council changes, registered office
  changes);
- **Status** changes;
- **Specific** other prescribed events.

## Searchability

The register is searchable by:

- **Foundation name**;
- **Registration number**;
- **Specific** other parameters per the online system.

## Records retention

Foundation records on the register:

- **Retained** during foundation's existence;
- **Retained** for prescribed period post-dissolution;
- **Specific** archival arrangements;
- **Available** for historical research within constraints.
