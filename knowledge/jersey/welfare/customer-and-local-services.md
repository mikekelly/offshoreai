# Customer and Local Services (CLS)

## What it is

**Customer and Local Services** is the States of Jersey
department that administers:

- **Social security contributions and benefits** under the
  SSJL 1974;
- **Income Support** under the ISL 2007;
- **Long-term care benefits** under the LTC Law 2012;
- **Specific** grants, allowances, and discretionary
  payments;
- **Employment support and back-to-work services**;
- **Many other public-facing services** for ordinary
  residents.

It is the successor to the historical "Social Security
Department" — broadened in scope and rebranded to
emphasise the customer-services role.

## What CLS does for residents

For an ordinary Jersey resident, CLS:

- **Collects** social security contributions (via Revenue
  Jersey for payroll);
- **Determines** Income Support eligibility;
- **Pays** benefits;
- **Maintains** contribution records and benefit
  entitlement statements;
- **Assesses** disability and incapacity claims;
- **Operates** employment support;
- **Provides** discretionary help in emergencies;
- **Reviews and reassesses** ongoing claims.

## How to engage CLS

Methods of contact:

- **In person** at CLS offices in St Helier;
- **Phone** for general queries and appointments;
- **Online** via gov.je for applications, calculators,
  and account information;
- **Written** correspondence for formal matters;
- **Through Citizens Advice Jersey** as a free
  intermediary.

## Application process for benefits

The typical flow:

1. **Eligibility check** — online calculator first;
2. **Application** with supporting documents (identity,
   residence, income, household);
3. **Interview** at CLS if required;
4. **Decision** — within prescribed timescales;
5. **Payment** by direct credit;
6. **Ongoing reporting** of changes of circumstances.

## Disputes and appeals

When a decision is contested:

1. **Internal review** by a senior officer at CLS;
2. **Tribunal appeal** to the Social Security Tribunal
   (an independent body);
3. **Further appeal** to the Royal Court on points of
   law.

See [`appeals.md`](appeals.md).

## Fraud and overpayment

CLS:

- **Investigates** suspected fraud;
- **Recovers overpayments** through deduction, debt
  recovery, or court action;
- **Prosecutes** serious fraud through the criminal
  process;
- **Conducts** routine audits.

## Other services

Beyond welfare, CLS provides:

- **Driver and Vehicle Standards (DVS)** — driving
  licences and vehicle registration;
- **Skills and back-to-work training**;
- **Specific** assistance with housing applications;
- **Some** parish-level coordination on shared services.
