# Sector-Specific Structuring — Jersey

## What this section is

A set of **sector-shaped structuring orientation files** —
cuts through the corpus organised by the **type of asset or
business** the client is structuring around, rather than by
the client's role
([use-cases](../use-cases/index.md)) or the client's home
jurisdiction
([client-cohorts](../use-cases/client-cohorts/index.md)).

This is the **third orthogonal navigation axis** alongside
role and cohort. For a wealth-management RM or adviser whose
client is "a PE founder with carry / pre-IPO stock / UK
property / crypto holdings", landing on the sector file is
often the fastest route to a structuring conversation.

The list is **representative, not exhaustive**. Additional
sector files (e.g. fund-of-funds, infrastructure assets, art
and collectibles, intellectual property) can be added by an
editor following the same shape.

## Common shape

Each sector file covers:

1. **What the sector is** — definitional scope and typical
   asset profile;
2. **Why a Jersey structure** — the load-bearing motivations;
3. **Jersey-structure menu** — the vehicle / regime options
   for the sector, with cross-references to the doctrinal
   corpus;
4. **Sector-specific risks and red flags** — things that
   recur in the sector and that an RM / adviser should know;
5. **Reporting and information-exchange overlay** — sector-
   specific reporting (e.g. CARF for crypto, ATED for UK
   property);
6. **Common cross-sector interactions** — e.g. a sports
   talent client may also have crypto holdings.

## How sector files interlock with role / cohort files

For a specific advisory conversation, an agent typically
combines:

- The **client's role** (e.g. founder-entrepreneur) →
  [use-case](../use-cases/index.md);
- The **client's home jurisdiction** (e.g. UAE expat) →
  [cohort](../use-cases/client-cohorts/index.md);
- The **asset / business type** (e.g. pre-IPO equity) →
  this section.

The three landings each surface different parts of the same
underlying doctrinal corpus, calibrated to the dimension that
matters most for the specific question.
