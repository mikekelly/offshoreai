# ITL Part 15 — Exemptions

Part 15 (Articles 115–120AD) provides various **exemptions**
from Jersey income tax for specified categories of income or
taxpayer.

## Categories of exemption

### Charitable bodies

- **Registered charities** under Charities Law 2014;
- **Income** applied for charitable purposes;
- **Specific** procedural requirements.

### Redundancy payments

- **Genuine redundancy** payments;
- **Up to specified** caps;
- **Excess** is taxable.

### Disability pensions

- **War-disability**;
- **Specific** medical pensions;
- **Exempt** from income tax.

### Non-resident income

- **Non-Jersey-resident** persons;
- **Specified income** not subject to Jersey tax;
- **Jersey-source rules** apply.

### International savings schemes

- **Specific** approved schemes;
- **Tax-exempt** treatment;
- **Detailed** qualification criteria.

### Consular exemptions

- **Consular and diplomatic** personnel;
- **Specific** treaty-based exemptions.

### Rent-a-room

- **Owner-occupier** renting room in main residence;
- **Modest** income threshold;
- **Exempt** up to limit.

### Article 118C (collective investment schemes)

- **Certified fund** explicit exemption;
- **Specific** conditions;
- **Service-provider** alignments;
- See [`itl-article-123.md`](./itl-article-123.md) and [`fund-counsel/fund-article-118c.md`](../use-cases/fund-counsel/fund-article-118c.md).

### Other specific

- **Specific** Jersey-policy exemptions;
- **Annual** budget reviews can add / remove categories;
- **Detailed** list in Articles 115-120AD.

## Practical considerations

### Claiming exemption

- **Identify** applicable provision;
- **Document** entitlement;
- **Claim** in tax return;
- **Maintain** evidence.

### Common pitfalls

- **Misclassification** of income as exempt;
- **Failure** to meet conditions;
- **Lapsing** of qualifying conditions;
- **Comptroller challenge**.

### Specific high-value scenarios

- **Article 118C** fund certification;
- **Charity** registration alongside tax exemption;
- **Non-resident** confirmation;
- **Pension scheme** approval.
