# ITL Part 7 — Collection and Repayments

Part 7 (Articles A39–49B) governs **payment of tax**, **deduction
at source**, **surcharges for late payment**, **recovery
procedures**, and **repayments**.

## Key provisions

### Tax payment timing

- **Standard deadlines** by category of taxpayer;
- **Annual** payments;
- **Instalment payments** for some categories.

### Income Tax Instalment System (ITIS)

- **Employer deduction** of tax from employee wages;
- **Monthly remittance** to Revenue Jersey;
- **Year-end reconciliation**;
- **Effectively the PAYE-equivalent** for Jersey.

### Building contractor deductions

- **Specific deduction** regime for construction industry;
- **Anti-evasion** measures;
- **Contractor responsibilities**.

### Late-payment surcharges

- **Automatic** for overdue tax;
- **Scaled** by lateness;
- **Interest** on overdue amounts.

### Recovery procedures

- **Civil debt** recovery for unpaid tax;
- **Court action** if needed;
- **Asset enforcement**.

### Repayments

- **Where overpayment** has occurred;
- **Comptroller** processes refund;
- **Time-limit** for repayment claims.

## Practical compliance

For taxpayers:

- **Pay** on time to avoid surcharges;
- **Use** ITIS efficiently for employed individuals;
- **Reconcile** annually;
- **Claim refunds** where overpaid.
