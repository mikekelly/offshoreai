# ITL Part 4 — Returns

Part 4 of the **[Income Tax (Jersey) Law 1961][itl]** governs
**tax-return filings** by individuals, companies, and other
persons.

## Scope

Part 4 (Articles A15 through 21C) covers:

- **Comptroller's duties** in administering returns;
- **Delivery requirements** for various taxpayers;
- **Employer registration** and employee-information returns;
- **Building contractor returns**;
- **Company returns**;
- **Benefit disclosures**;
- **Partnership notifications**;
- **Penalty provisions** for non-compliance.

## Key obligations

### Individuals

- **Annual personal tax return**;
- **Specific** forms by tax category;
- **Deadlines** prescribed annually.

### Companies

- **Annual corporate tax return**;
- **Filing through Revenue Jersey portal**;
- **Substance reporting** integrated;
- **Audit confirmation** where applicable.

### Employers

- **Registration** with Revenue Jersey;
- **Annual employee returns**;
- **ITIS** (Income Tax Instalment System) operation;
- **Year-end** reconciliation.

### Partnerships

- **Partnership return**;
- **Partner-allocation** information;
- **Each partner** files individually based on partnership
  return.

## Penalties

Non-compliance with return obligations attracts:

- **Late-filing fees** scaled by delay;
- **Civil financial penalties** for material non-compliance;
- **Criminal offences** for fraud / wilful failure;
- **Comptroller's** specific powers.

## Practical compliance

For Jersey tax compliance:

- **Engage** with Revenue Jersey online portal;
- **Maintain** records supporting returns;
- **File** on time;
- **Respond** to Comptroller inquiries promptly.
