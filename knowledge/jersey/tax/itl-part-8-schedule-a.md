# ITL Part 8 — Schedule A (Property Income)

Part 8 (Articles 50–55B) governs **Schedule A** — income from
Jersey **immovable property** (land, buildings).

## What Schedule A covers

- **Rental income** from Jersey real estate;
- **Property** owned for rental;
- **Trading property** treated differently (income tax under
  Schedule D);
- **Specific categories** of property income.

## Computation

### Income basis

- **Rents** received during YA;
- **Less** allowable deductions:
  - **Repairs and maintenance**;
  - **Insurance**;
  - **Property management** fees;
  - **Interest** on borrowings for the property;
  - **Other** allowable expenses.

### Capital vs revenue

- **Revenue** deductions allowable;
- **Capital improvements** typically not directly deductible
  (capital allowances rules apply where they do);
- **Distinction** important for tax outcome.

## Rate

- **20%** Jersey income tax on Schedule A income;
- **Individual or company** owners (different application).

## Specific provisions

### Article 50 — Schedule A charge

- **Charges Jersey property income**;
- **Owner** as the taxpayer.

### Articles 51-55 — Computation

- **Allowable deductions**;
- **Specific rules** for different property types;
- **Carry-forward** of losses.

### Article 55B — Rent treatment

- **Specific** rent computational rules;
- **Lease premiums** treatment;
- **Service charges** etc.

## Chargeable persons

- **Owner** of the property;
- **Beneficial owner** (some cases through trustees);
- **Specific** rules for shared ownership;
- **Non-resident landlords** — see Schedule 3A regime.

## Non-resident landlord

For non-Jersey-resident landlords:

- **Specific** withholding regime (Schedule 3A);
- **Jersey tenant / agent** deducts tax;
- **Annual** reconciliation.

## Practical considerations

- **Keep records** of rental income and expenses;
- **Claim** all allowable deductions;
- **Distinguish** capital from revenue;
- **File** Schedule A return component.
