# ITL Part 13 — Loss Relief

Part 13 (Articles 107–110B) governs **loss-relief** mechanisms
— how trading and other losses can offset income / be carried.

## Key provisions

### Loss carry-back

- **Trading losses** can be carried back against prior-year
  profits;
- **Specific time-limit** for carry-back;
- **Subject to** anti-avoidance.

### Loss carry-forward

- **Indefinite** carry-forward of unrelieved losses;
- **Against** future income of same trade typically;
- **Restrictions** for changes of ownership / activity.

### Capital allowance losses

- **Losses created** by capital allowances;
- **Specific** carry rules;
- **Articles 106A-106C** Part 12A interaction.

### Anti-avoidance

- **Change-of-ownership** restrictions on loss carry-forward;
- **Trade-cessation** rules;
- **Loss-buying** anti-avoidance.

## Mechanics

### Trade-loss carry-forward

- **Trading losses** offset against future profits of **same
  trade**;
- **Different-trade** losses generally not offsetable;
- **Specific** carry mechanics.

### Capital-allowance-driven losses

- **Capital allowances** create accounting losses sometimes;
- **Specific** Part 12A treatment;
- **Carry-forward** subject to restrictions.

### Group relief (limited)

- **Jersey doesn't have** UK-style group relief generally;
- **Specific** statutory provisions for some intra-group
  scenarios;
- **Less developed** than UK CTA 2010 Part 5.

## Specific company contexts

### Zero-ten companies

- **Most Jersey companies** at 0% rate;
- **Loss relief** less critical (no immediate tax to offset);
- **Important for** Article 123C 10%-rate financial-services
  companies.

### Foreign-source losses

- **Specific** rules for offsetting against foreign income;
- **Anti-avoidance** for loss-import.

## Practical considerations

- **Track** loss positions year on year;
- **Plan** trade-restructuring around loss-carry rules;
- **Document** loss origins for substantive carry-forward;
- **Engage** tax adviser on complex loss matters.
