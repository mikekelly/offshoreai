# ITL Part 19 — Pensions and Schemes

Part 19 (Articles 130–132) governs **pension-scheme approval**,
**contributions**, **distributions**, **annuities**, and tax
provisions.

## Key provisions

### Approved pension schemes

- **Specific** approval requirements;
- **Comptroller** approves schemes meeting criteria;
- **Tax-efficient** treatment for contributions and benefits;
- **Specific** structural requirements.

### Contributions

- **Tax deduction** for employee and employer contributions up
  to limits;
- **Annual limits** prescribed;
- **Specific** anti-avoidance provisions;
- **Carry-forward** of unused allowance in some cases.

### Distributions / benefits

- **Specific** tax treatment of pension benefits in
  retirement;
- **Annuity** taxation;
- **Lump-sum** treatment;
- **Specific** Jersey rules differ from UK pension regime.

### Annuities

- **Purchased life annuity** specific treatment;
- **Specific** capital / interest split;
- **Taxation** mechanics.

## Specific scheme types

### Approved occupational pension scheme

- **Employer-sponsored**;
- **Standard** Jersey corporate retirement vehicle;
- **Comptroller** approval.

### Retirement annuity trust scheme

- **Self-employed / individual** pension;
- **Trust-based** structure;
- **Specific** approval criteria.

### International pension scheme

- **Cross-border** pension provision;
- **Specific** Jersey rules for international schemes;
- **Tax-treaty** considerations.

### QROPS / QNUPS equivalents

- **UK pension transfer** schemes;
- **Jersey-recognised** qualifying overseas pension scheme;
- **Specific** Jersey rules.

## Tax mechanics

### Accumulation phase

- **Contributions** deductible up to limits;
- **Investment growth** within scheme tax-free typically;
- **Trustee** of scheme administers.

### Distribution phase

- **Retirement benefits** taxed when paid;
- **Lump-sum** specific treatment;
- **Annuity / drawdown** treatment.

## Cross-border

- **UK pension transfer** to Jersey scheme;
- **Specific** UK-Jersey DTA provisions;
- **HMRC recognition** of Jersey scheme important.

## Practical considerations

### For employers

- **Set up** approved scheme;
- **Deduct** contributions appropriately;
- **Report** contributions in payroll;
- **Comply** with scheme governance.

### For individuals

- **Maximise** contributions within limits;
- **Plan** retirement-phase distributions;
- **Consider** cross-border implications.

### For trustees / administrators

- **Scheme approval** maintenance;
- **Annual returns**;
- **Distribution** mechanics correctly applied.
