# ITL Article 150 — Citation

Article 150 — the **citation provision** — states the short
title by which the Law is cited.

## Citation

The Law may be cited as the **"Income Tax (Jersey) Law 1961"**.

## Common citations / shorthand

- **ITL 1961**;
- **Income Tax Law**;
- **1961 Tax Law**;
- **Jersey Income Tax Law**.

Citations to specific articles use **"Article X ITL 1961"** or
**"Article X of the Income Tax (Jersey) Law 1961"**.
