# ITL Articles 123E – 123S — Distribution Rules, Group Relief, etc.

Articles 123E through 123S (and intervening lettered articles)
of the **[Income Tax (Jersey) Law 1961][itl]** provide
**supplementary provisions** for the zero-ten regime:

- **Distribution rules** for Jersey-resident shareholders;
- **Deemed-distribution** anti-avoidance;
- **Group relief** (limited);
- **Cannabis industry** specific provisions;
- **Other specific** Part 17 sub-articles.

## Distribution rules (Article 81-onwards framework)

### Standard distributions to Jersey residents

- **0%-rate company** distribution to Jersey-resident
  shareholder: **20% personal tax** at receipt;
- **10%-rate company**: **withholding** to bring to 20%;
- **20%-rate company**: **further mechanics** as applicable.

### Deemed distributions

- **Excess retention** can be deemed distributed;
- **Loans to participators** can be deemed distributions;
- **Specific** anti-avoidance triggers;
- **Article 81 onwards** detailed regime.

## Group relief

Jersey doesn't have UK-style comprehensive group relief, but:

- **Specific intra-group** provisions in Articles 123E-S;
- **Loss surrender** in some categories;
- **Group restructuring** specific rules;
- **Less developed** than UK CTA 2010 Part 5.

## Cannabis industry

Specific Jersey provisions:

- **Cannabis-cultivation** and **cannabis-product** sector;
- **Specific rate** treatment;
- **Detailed** computational rules;
- **Reflects** Jersey's emerging cannabis-industry policy.
