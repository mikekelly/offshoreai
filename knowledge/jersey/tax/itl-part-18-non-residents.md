# ITL Part 18 — Temporary Absences and Non-Residents

Part 18 (Articles 126–129B) governs **residence determination
for individuals**, **non-resident taxation**, **rental income
collection**, and **apportionment rules**.

## Key provisions

### Residence determination

The three categories (Article 126):

- **Resident and ordinarily resident** — settled Jersey
  residence;
- **Resident but not ordinarily resident** — short-term
  residence;
- **Non-resident** — outside Jersey for tax purposes.

See [`residence-individuals.md`](residence-individuals.md) for
detail.

### Temporary absences

- **Jersey-resident** with temporary foreign presence;
- **Residence status** maintained or interrupted?
- **Specific** rules apply based on duration and circumstances;
- **Reasonable** temporary absences typically don't break
  residence.

### Non-resident charging

- **Non-resident** taxed only on **Jersey-source income**;
- **Schedule A** for Jersey property income;
- **Schedule D Case I/II** for Jersey trade;
- **Schedule D Case III** for Jersey employment income;
- **Foreign-source** income outside scope.

### Rental income (non-resident landlord)

- **Schedule 3A** of the Law;
- **Tenant or letting agent** deducts tax;
- **Annual** reconciliation;
- **Non-resident landlord scheme**.

### Apportionment rules

- **Mid-year arrival or departure**;
- **Time-apportioned** income for partial-residence years;
- **Specific** computational mechanics.

## Common scenarios

### Becoming Jersey-resident

- **Arrival** in YA;
- **Apportionment** of pre-residence income;
- **Full taxation** post-residence becoming ordinarily
  resident.

### Becoming non-resident

- **Departure** from Jersey;
- **Apportionment** for transition year;
- **Subsequent year** non-resident scope only;
- **Continuing Jersey-source** income still chargeable.

### Non-resident landlord

- **Foreign-resident** owner of Jersey property;
- **Tenant deducts** tax;
- **Annual return** by landlord;
- **Reconciliation** against actual liability.

### Non-resident with Jersey employment

- **Schedule D Case III** charging;
- **Specific** withholding obligations;
- **Annual return** by employee.

## Modern reform context

The traditional residence rules are gradually being modernised:

- **Specific** statutory criteria refined;
- **Day-count** tests becoming more prominent;
- **Comparable** with UK statutory residence test in some
  respects;
- **Specific** Jersey-policy considerations.

## Practical considerations

### For arrivals to Jersey

- **Confirm** residence status;
- **Apply** for relevant tax codes;
- **Plan** for full vs partial-year taxation.

### For departures

- **Document** departure clearly;
- **Specific** advisory engagement;
- **Track** continuing Jersey-source income.

### For non-residents

- **Identify** Jersey-source income;
- **Comply** with specific withholding obligations;
- **File** appropriate non-resident return.
