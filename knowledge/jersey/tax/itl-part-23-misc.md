# ITL Part 23 — Miscellaneous Provisions

Part 23 (Articles 142–149B) provides various **miscellaneous and
final provisions** — rate adjustments, regulatory powers, notice
delivery, transitional provisions.

## Key provisions

### Article 142 — Rate adjustments

- **Annual rate-setting** through Budget;
- **States Assembly** approval;
- **Specific** rate variations.

### Regulatory powers

- **Comptroller** powers for administering Law;
- **Specific** discretions;
- **Sub-delegated** powers in some cases.

### Notice delivery

- **Tax notices** to taxpayers;
- **Service** requirements;
- **Address** for service.

### Transitional provisions

- **Specific** transitions for new rules;
- **Pre-effective-date** treatment;
- **Phase-in** mechanics.

### Other miscellaneous

- **Specific** administrative provisions;
- **Cross-references** between Parts;
- **General** sweeper provisions.

## Annual budget

The States Assembly's annual budget:

- **Updates** rates, allowances, thresholds;
- **Specific** policy adjustments;
- **Becomes** part of the Law through amendments;
- **Effective** for specified YA.

## Comptroller's regulatory powers

- **Practical administration** of the Law;
- **Specific** technical rulings;
- **Concessions** in published guidance;
- **Discretionary** decisions in specific cases.
