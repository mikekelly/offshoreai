# ITL Article 123A — Standard 0% Corporate Rate

Article 123A of the **[Income Tax (Jersey) Law 1961][itl]**
sets the **standard 0% rate** for most Jersey-tax-resident
companies under the zero-ten regime.

## What Article 123A covers

- **Jersey-tax-resident company** is taxable at the **standard
  0% rate** on its income;
- Subject to **specific exceptions** (Articles 123C, 123D);
- The **default position** for most Jersey companies.

## Who pays 0%

Most Jersey companies, including:

- **Holding companies**;
- **Investment-management** companies (for own account);
- **General trading** companies (where Jersey-resident);
- **Family-business** vehicles;
- **Pre-IPO TopCos**;
- **Fund vehicles** (subject to specific Article 118C
  considerations);
- **Most operating subsidiaries**.

## Who doesn't pay 0%

- **Financial-services companies** under Article 123C: **10%**;
- **Utility companies** and **large corporate retailers**
  under Article 123D: **20%** or **10%**;
- **Specific Schedule A income** (Jersey real estate): **20%**;
- **Pillar Two MCIT** companies in large MNE groups: effective
  **15%**.

## Why 0%

The 0% rate is the **headline feature** of zero-ten:

- **Attracts** international business to Jersey;
- **Avoids** double taxation in foreign-tax models;
- **Substitutes** Pillar Two top-up for large groups;
- **Preserves** Jersey's competitive position.

## Compliance for 0%-rate companies

Even at 0%:

- **Annual return** filed;
- **Substance** compliance under 2019 Law;
- **CRS / FATCA** reporting;
- **Beneficial-ownership** filings;
- **Distribution-regime** anti-avoidance compliance.

## Distribution implications

Distributions from 0%-rate companies:

- **Non-resident shareholders**: no Jersey tax;
- **Jersey-resident shareholders**: Article 81 distribution
  regime applies — 20% personal tax at receipt.

## Pillar Two considerations

For 0%-rate companies in MNE groups (€750m+ revenue):

- **Pillar Two MCIT** applies;
- **15% minimum** effective rate;
- **Top-up to 15%** from 0%;
- See [`pillar-two.md`](./pillar-two.md).
