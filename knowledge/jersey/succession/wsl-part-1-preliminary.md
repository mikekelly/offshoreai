# WSL 1993 Part 1 — Preliminary

## Article 1 — Interpretation

Key definitions:

- **"Will"** — a testamentary document validly executed under
  the Law;
- **"Testator"** — a person making a will (gender-neutral);
- **"Movable property"** — personal property (chattels,
  intangibles, financial assets);
- **"Immovable property"** — real property (land and
  buildings, fixtures);
- **"Domicile"** — determined under conflict-of-laws rules
  (broadly: country of permanent home);
- **"Spouse"** — includes civil partner (post-amendment);
- **"Child"** — includes legitimate, illegitimate, and adopted
  children; specific rules for stepchildren and children of
  civil partnerships;
- **"Légitime"** — protected share for spouse / children;
- **"Intestate"** — without a valid will;
- **"Personal representative"** — executor (named in will) or
  administrator (appointed in intestacy / failed will).
