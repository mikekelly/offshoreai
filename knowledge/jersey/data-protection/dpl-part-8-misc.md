# DPL 2018 Part 8 — Miscellaneous

## Articles 88-93 — Specific exemptions and modifications

The DPL contains specific **exemptions or modifications** for:

### Journalism, academic, artistic, literary purposes

- **Specific** exemptions to enable freedom of expression;
- **Balanced** against data subject rights;
- **Subject to** specific safeguards.

### Research

- **Scientific or historical research** purposes;
- **Statistical** purposes;
- **Specific safeguards** under Article 39 / 91;
- **Lighter** rights regime in some respects.

### National security

- **Specific** carve-outs for national-security activities;
- **JOIC** oversight limited in this area;
- **Specific** Jersey arrangements.

### Crime and tax

- **Police** processing for criminal-justice purposes;
- **Tax authorities** processing for tax purposes;
- **Specific** modifications;
- **Generally** compatible with criminal-justice DP regime.

### Health

- **Medical** purposes;
- **Public health**;
- **Specific safeguards** under Article 9.

### Legal privilege

- **Legal professional privilege** preserved;
- **Specific exemptions** for legal advice and litigation
  preparation.

### Specific Jersey exemptions

- **Specific** Jersey statutory functions;
- **Historic Jersey** records and customary-law purposes.

## Articles 94-97 — Regulations and Orders

The Minister may make **Orders** for:

- **Forms** and **fees** (JOIC fees set under Order);
- **Adequacy decisions** for specific countries;
- **Specific exemption** clarifications;
- **Procedural** requirements;
- **Updates** to implementing rules.

The JOIC may issue **guidance**:

- **Sector-specific** guidance;
- **Practical** guidance on common compliance issues;
- **Statutory codes** in specific areas (e.g., AI, biometrics);
- **Updated** as practice evolves.

## Article 98 — Transitional

Transitional provisions:

- **Pre-2018** processing brought into compliance;
- **Specific** legacy arrangements;
- **Grandfathering** of pre-existing consents subject to GDPR-
  compliance review.

## Article 99 — Annual review

The Minister / JOIC review the DPL operation annually:

- **Effectiveness**;
- **Alignment** with international standards;
- **Adequacy** maintenance;
- **Areas** for legislative update.

## Article 100 — Citation

The Law is cited as the **"Data Protection (Jersey) Law
2018"**.

It came into force on **25 May 2018** (same day as EU GDPR)
and has been amended since to maintain alignment:

- **2020 / 2021** — post-Brexit alignment with UK ICO regime;
- **Specific** technical updates;
- **Schrems II** practical implementation;
- **EU-US DPF** integration.
