# DPL 2018 Part 6 — Information Commissioner / Authority

Part 6 establishes the **Jersey Office of the Information
Commissioner (JOIC)** as the supervisory authority.

## The office

The **Information Commissioner**:

- **Independent** office established by the Data Protection
  Authority (Jersey) Law 2018;
- **Appointed** by the States Assembly;
- **Reports** to the States;
- **Funded** by data-protection fees and States subvention;
- **Independent** of Council of Ministers in decision-making.

## Functions

The Commissioner:

### Compliance promotion

- **Educates** controllers and processors;
- **Issues guidance**;
- **Publishes** advice;
- **Supports** compliance.

### Complaints handling

- **Receives** complaints from data subjects;
- **Investigates**;
- **Decides** complaints;
- **Mediates** where appropriate;
- **Refers** to enforcement if needed.

### Investigations

- **Initiated** by complaints, intelligence, or themed
  reviews;
- **Information powers** under Article 64-65;
- **Inspections** under Article 66;
- **Specific** powers for surveillance / high-risk processing.

### Enforcement

- **Warning** controllers / processors;
- **Reprimands**;
- **Orders** to comply;
- **Bans** on processing;
- **Administrative fines**;
- **Public statements**.

### International cooperation

- **EU EDPB** observer / cooperation;
- **UK ICO** close cooperation;
- **Other** authorities through bilateral arrangements.

## Powers

The Commissioner can:

- **Require information** from controllers / processors;
- **Conduct audits**;
- **Inspect** premises and IT systems;
- **Issue directions**;
- **Suspend** processing in serious cases;
- **Impose fines** under Article 76+.

## Decision-making

Commissioner decisions:

- **Reasoned**;
- **Subject to** appeal to Royal Court;
- **Procedural fairness** required;
- **Transparent** processes.

## Accountability

The Commissioner is **accountable** through:

- **Annual report** to States Assembly;
- **Judicial review**;
- **Appeals** to Royal Court;
- **Specific** parliamentary scrutiny.
