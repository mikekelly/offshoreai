# DPL 2018 Part 2 — Principles (residual)

Articles 8 (lawful bases) and 9 (special category) have their
own deep files. This cluster covers Articles 10-15 — the
**principles of processing**.

These principles **mirror GDPR Article 5** principles.

## Article 10 — Lawfulness, fairness, transparency

Personal data must be processed:

- **Lawfully** (legal basis under Article 8 / 9);
- **Fairly** (no deception of data subjects);
- **Transparently** (data subjects informed of processing).

## Article 11 — Purpose limitation

Personal data must be:

- Collected for **specified, explicit, and legitimate
  purposes**;
- **Not further processed** in a manner **incompatible** with
  those purposes;
- **Specific exception** for archiving / research /
  statistical purposes in public interest.

## Article 12 — Data minimisation

Personal data must be:

- **Adequate**, **relevant**, and **limited** to what is
  necessary;
- **No more** data than required for the purpose;
- **Regularly reviewed** for continued necessity.

## Article 13 — Accuracy

Personal data must be:

- **Accurate** and **up to date**;
- **Inaccurate** data **rectified** or **erased**;
- **Verification** processes appropriate to the data type.

## Article 14 — Storage limitation

Personal data must be:

- Kept in a form permitting identification **no longer than
  necessary**;
- **Retention schedules** appropriate to purpose;
- **Deletion** at end of retention period.

## Article 15 — Integrity and confidentiality (security)

Personal data must be processed with:

- **Appropriate technical and organisational security**;
- **Protection against** unauthorised / unlawful processing,
  loss, destruction, damage;
- **Encryption, pseudonymisation, access controls**;
- **Resilience** to incidents.

## Accountability principle

The controller is **responsible for, and must be able to
demonstrate compliance with**, the principles:

- **Documentation** of processing;
- **Policies** and procedures;
- **DPIAs** for high-risk processing;
- **Training**;
- **Audits** and **review**;
- **Records of processing activities**.
