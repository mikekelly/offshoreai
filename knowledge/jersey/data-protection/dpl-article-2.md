# DPL 2018 Article 2 — Application

Article 2 governs **when the DPL applies** — the territorial
and material scope.

## The provision

The DPL applies to:

### Jersey-established processing

- Processing **in the context of activities** of a
  controller / processor established in Jersey;
- Whether the processing **takes place in Jersey** or not;
- The "establishment" test depends on **stable arrangements**
  for activities in Jersey.

### Jersey data subjects (offering goods / services)

- Processing of personal data of **Jersey-located data
  subjects** by a non-Jersey controller / processor;
- Where the activities relate to **offering of goods or
  services** to those data subjects (regardless of payment);
- **Targeted** at Jersey market.

### Jersey data subjects (monitoring)

- Processing of personal data of **Jersey-located data
  subjects**;
- Where the activities relate to **monitoring** of
  their behaviour (e.g., online tracking, profiling) **in
  Jersey**.

## Exclusions

The DPL does **not** apply to:

- **Purely personal / household** activities;
- **Specific** State activities (criminal-justice, national
  security — separately regulated);
- **Specific exempt** categories (journalism, art, literature
  with appropriate balancing);
- **Specific** legacy materials (subject to transitional
  rules).

## Practical scope

### Jersey businesses

Jersey-incorporated businesses processing personal data:

- **Banks** processing customer data;
- **TCBs / FSBs** processing client / BO / beneficiary data;
- **Law firms** processing client data;
- **Health-care providers** processing patient data;
- **Government** processing citizen data;
- **Retailers** processing customer data;
- All within DPL.

### Foreign businesses targeting Jersey

A non-Jersey business is subject to DPL if it:

- **Targets** Jersey customers (Jersey-specific marketing,
  Jersey-pricing, Jersey-language);
- **Monitors** Jersey customers' behaviour;
- Engages a **Jersey-established** processor.

### Jersey-incorporated entities serving abroad

A Jersey-incorporated entity providing services worldwide:

- **DPL applies** for Jersey-established activities;
- **GDPR also applies** for EU data subjects (parallel
  compliance);
- **Other foreign laws** apply for other jurisdictions.

## Comparison with GDPR

The DPL Article 2 mirrors **GDPR Article 3** with Jersey-
specific framing. The substantive scope is **broadly
identical**:

- Same establishment-based scope;
- Same targeted-service / monitoring extraterritorial reach;
- Same exclusions;
- This alignment **supports adequacy**.
