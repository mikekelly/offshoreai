# Article 61 — Citation

Article 61 is the **short title** provision. The Law is cited as the
"**Trusts (Jersey) Law 1984**".

(See the consolidated text at [jerseylaw.je][tjl].)

## Citation conventions in this corpus

When citing the Trusts (Jersey) Law 1984 in this corpus we use:

- the **full name** at first mention in a file — "Trusts (Jersey)
  Law 1984";
- the **shorthand** "Trusts Law" or "the Law" thereafter;
- **Article-specific citations** in the form "**Article X of the
  Trusts (Jersey) Law 1984**" or "**Article X**" where the context
  is clear;
- a **reference-style markdown link** to the consolidated text on
  [jerseylaw.je][tjl].

Other Jersey legislation cited in this corpus uses analogous
conventions — see the canonical sources in
[`../sources.md`](../sources.md).
