# DL 2013 Part 3 — Areas of Application

Part 3 specifies **where** the discrimination protections
apply.

## Articles 11-14 — Employment

Employment is comprehensively covered:

### Recruitment

- **Job advertisements** must not discriminate;
- **Application** procedures must be fair;
- **Interviews** must not discriminate (questions or
  treatment);
- **Selection** decisions must be made on non-discriminatory
  grounds;
- **Offers** must be on non-discriminatory terms.

### Terms and conditions

- **Pay** equality (specific Part 4 — equal pay);
- **Working hours**;
- **Holiday and leave**;
- **Benefits** and **promotion**;
- **Training** opportunities;
- **Disciplinary** and grievance procedures.

### Dismissal

- **Reason** for dismissal must not be discriminatory;
- **Procedure** must not be discriminatory;
- **Specific** automatic-unfair-dismissal grounds linked to
  protected characteristics.

### Post-employment

- **References** must not be discriminatory;
- **Victimisation** by former employer for protected acts;
- **Specific** post-employment claims possible.

## Articles 15-19 — Provision of goods, services, premises

### Goods and services

- **Sale** of goods;
- **Provision** of services (commercial, professional);
- **Public** services;
- **Access** to facilities (gyms, clubs, restaurants);
- **Subject to** specific exemptions.

### Premises

- **Sale** of premises;
- **Rent** of premises;
- **Terms** of tenancy;
- **Termination** of tenancy;
- **Use** of common parts of property;
- **Subject to** specific exemptions for owner-occupiers
  letting rooms.

## Articles 20-23 — Education

### Schools and educational institutions

- **Admissions** policies must not discriminate;
- **Treatment** of pupils within school;
- **Provision** of educational facilities;
- **Behaviour management** must not discriminate;
- **Exclusions** must not be discriminatory.

### Further and higher education

- **Admissions** and treatment;
- **Subject to** specific religious / single-sex exemptions.

### Specific exemptions

- **Single-sex schools** permitted in some configurations;
- **Religious schools** with specific exemptions on religious
  characteristics;
- **Specialist** schools for disabled pupils permitted.

## Article 24 — Public functions

Public bodies exercising **public functions** are subject to
DL:

- **Government** departments;
- **Specific** statutory bodies;
- **Public services**;
- **Subject to** specific exemptions for national security,
  judicial functions, etc.
