# DL 2013 Article 15 — Discrimination in Goods and Services

Article 15 prohibits **discrimination in the provision of
goods and services**.

## Scope

Discrimination prohibited in:

### Goods

- **Sale** of goods;
- **Specific** terms for sale;
- **Refusal** to sell on discriminatory grounds.

### Services

- **Provision** of services (commercial, professional);
- **Public** services;
- **Banking** and financial services;
- **Health** services;
- **Travel and hospitality** services;
- **Specific** other services.

### Facilities

- **Access** to facilities (gyms, clubs, restaurants);
- **Use** of facilities;
- **Specific** terms of access.

## Forms of discrimination

All standard forms apply (DL Article 4-10):

- **Direct** discrimination;
- **Indirect** discrimination;
- **Harassment**;
- **Victimisation**;
- **Reasonable adjustments** for disability;
- **Discrimination by association**;
- **Discrimination by perception**.

## Specific scenarios

### Refusal of service

- **Restaurant** refuses to serve customer based on race;
- **Bank** refuses account based on nationality;
- **Hotel** refuses room based on sexual orientation;
- **All** prohibited under Article 15.

### Differential terms

- **Higher prices** for protected-group customers;
- **Worse terms** for protected-group customers;
- **Subject to** specific justification defences for some
  cases.

### Inaccessibility

- **Failure** to make reasonable adjustments for disabled
  customers (Article 10);
- **Physical access** barriers;
- **Communication** barriers.

## Exemptions

Specific Part 5 exemptions:

- **Single-sex** services in specific contexts (women's
  refuges, etc.);
- **Religious** services in specific contexts;
- **Specific** age-related services;
- **Specific** insurance underwriting (subject to actuarial
  justification);
- **Specific** charity-related provisions.

## Service providers

The Article applies to:

- **Commercial** service providers (business-to-consumer);
- **Public** service providers;
- **Professional** service providers (lawyers,
  accountants, doctors);
- **Specific** voluntary / charitable providers.

## Remedies

Claims by aggrieved customers:

- **Compensation** (uncapped);
- **Injury to feelings**;
- **Declarations**;
- **Recommendations**;
- **Tribunal** primary forum;
- **Royal Court** for some claims.
