# DL 2013 Part 3 — Harassment in Specific Contexts

This cluster expands on Article 5 (Harassment) with
**context-specific** provisions.

## Article 25 — Workplace harassment

Workplace harassment is **specifically** addressed:

- **Employer liability** for harassment by employees;
- **Employer liability** for harassment by third parties
  (clients, customers) in some configurations;
- **Specific** defence: employer took **all reasonable steps**
  to prevent the harassment.

### Sexual harassment

- **Specifically** identified in Jersey law;
- **Workplace** sexual harassment particular focus;
- **#MeToo**-era awareness driving compliance focus;
- **Specific** Code of Practice for sexual harassment.

### Race harassment

- **Slurs**, **stereotyping** comments;
- **Cultural** insensitivity if pervasive;
- **Specific** treatment of religious-cultural overlap.

### Age harassment

- **"OK boomer"** generation-targeted comments;
- **Patronising** treatment of younger / older workers;
- **Stereotyping** comments.

### Disability harassment

- **Mockery** of disability;
- **Insensitive** language;
- **Specific** treatment of mental-health issues.

## Article 26 — Service-provision harassment

Harassment in **service contexts**:

- **Customer-facing** service providers;
- **Healthcare** settings;
- **Educational** settings;
- **Specific** rules for inhabited / inhabited services.

## Articles 27-30 — Specific harassment scenarios

### Educational harassment

- **Pupil-on-pupil** harassment with school liability;
- **Teacher-on-pupil** harassment;
- **Specific** protections during education;
- **Bullying** intersection with harassment.

### Premises harassment

- **Landlord harassment** of tenants;
- **Co-tenant** harassment;
- **Specific** rental-context rules.

### Online harassment

- **Workplace email**, **social media**;
- **Specific** employer policies;
- **Cross-jurisdictional** considerations.

## Employer "reasonable steps" defence

To defend against vicarious liability for employee harassment:

- **Anti-harassment policy** in writing;
- **Training** for staff;
- **Clear reporting** mechanism;
- **Prompt investigation** of complaints;
- **Appropriate action** in response;
- **Specific** Jersey Code of Practice followed;
- **Senior management** commitment demonstrated.

## Compensation

Harassment claims attract:

- **Compensation for injury to feelings** (Vento bands);
- **Aggravated damages** in some cases;
- **Specific** orders against employer to prevent future
  harassment;
- **No upper cap** on compensation.
