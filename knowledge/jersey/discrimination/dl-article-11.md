# DL 2013 Article 11 — Employment Discrimination

Article 11 covers **discrimination in employment**.

## Scope

Discrimination in employment includes:

### Recruitment

- **Job advertisements** must not discriminate;
- **Application** procedures must be fair;
- **Interviews** must not discriminate (questions or
  treatment);
- **Selection** decisions on non-discriminatory grounds;
- **Offers** on non-discriminatory terms.

### Terms and conditions

- **Pay** (alongside equal-pay rules in Part 4);
- **Working hours**;
- **Holiday and leave**;
- **Benefits**;
- **Promotion** opportunities;
- **Training** opportunities;
- **Disciplinary** and grievance procedures.

### Dismissal

- **Reason** for dismissal must not be discriminatory;
- **Procedure** must not be discriminatory;
- **Specific** automatic-unfair-dismissal grounds linked to
  protected characteristics (under EL 2003 Article 65+).

### Post-employment

- **References** must not be discriminatory;
- **Victimisation** by former employer for protected acts;
- **Specific** post-employment claims possible.

## Specific examples

- **"We don't hire women"** — direct sex discrimination
  in recruitment;
- **"You're too old for this role"** — direct age
  discrimination in recruitment / promotion;
- **"You need British nationality"** — potential nationality
  / race discrimination;
- **"Must work 60 hours per week"** — potential indirect
  discrimination (women, disabled, parents disproportionately
  affected);
- **Pay differentials** based on protected characteristic
  — direct or indirect.

## Employer obligations

The employer must:

- **Apply** non-discriminatory recruitment practices;
- **Train** managers on discrimination awareness;
- **Maintain** policies on equal opportunities;
- **Investigate** complaints promptly;
- **Specific** reasonable adjustments for disabled
  employees;
- **Comply** with sectoral codes.

## Defences

- **Occupational requirement** (Article 41);
- **Positive action** (Article 42);
- **Age justification** (Article 43);
- **Specific** other Part 5 exemptions.
