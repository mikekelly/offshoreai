# DL 2013 Part 1 — Preliminary

## Article 1 — Interpretation

Key definitions:

- **"Protected characteristic"** — characteristic identified
  by Order under Article 3;
- **"Direct discrimination"** — less favourable treatment
  because of a protected characteristic;
- **"Indirect discrimination"** — neutral provision /
  criterion / practice putting a protected group at
  disadvantage;
- **"Harassment"** — unwanted conduct related to a protected
  characteristic;
- **"Victimisation"** — adverse treatment because of a
  protected act;
- **"Reasonable adjustment"** — modification for a disabled
  person;
- **"Tribunal"** — Jersey Employment and Discrimination
  Tribunal.

## Article 2 — Application

The DL applies to:

- **Employment** (broadly defined);
- **Provision of goods and services**;
- **Education**;
- **Premises** (sale, lease, use);
- **Public functions** in specified areas;
- **Specific** other areas as added by Order.

Subject to:

- **Specific exemptions** (Part 5);
- **Specific** transitional provisions;
- **Specific** non-Jersey actors with limited Jersey nexus.
