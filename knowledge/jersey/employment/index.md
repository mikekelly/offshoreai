# Employment Law — Jersey

## What this section is about

This section covers **Jersey employment law** — rights and
obligations between employers and employees.

The principal statute is the **Employment (Jersey) Law 2003**
(EL), supplemented by:

- **Discrimination (Jersey) Law 2013** — equality / anti-
  discrimination ([../discrimination/](../discrimination/));
- **Health and Safety at Work (Jersey) Law 1989**;
- **Social Security (Jersey) Law 1974** — social-security
  contributions;
- **Pension legislation** — workplace pensions;
- **Control of Housing and Work (Jersey) Law 2012** — status-
  based employment access ([../immigration-residency/](../immigration-residency/)).
