# EL 2003 Part 6 — Unfair Dismissal (residual)

Article 64 (the principal right) has its own deep file. This
cluster covers procedural and remedial provisions.

## Articles 51-55 — Qualifying conditions

### Qualifying period

- **52 weeks** continuous service for general unfair-dismissal
  protection;
- **No qualifying period** for automatic unfair dismissal
  categories;
- **Continuity** preserved through transfer of employment in
  some cases.

### Excluded categories

- **Specific** categories of worker excluded (e.g., certain
  domestic workers, family members);
- **Specific** statutory exemptions.

## Articles 56-60 — Fair reasons

See [`el-article-64.md`](el-article-64.md) for the five
potentially fair reasons.

Each reason has detailed procedural and substantive
requirements developed through case law.

## Articles 61-63 — Procedural fairness

### ACAS-equivalent code

Jersey has a **Code of Practice** on disciplinary and
grievance procedures:

- **Issued by** the Employment Forum;
- **Not strictly binding** but Tribunal **expects** compliance;
- **Failure** to comply can be unfair-dismissal evidence and
  attract **uplift** in compensation.

### Investigation

- **Reasonable** investigation proportionate to the issue;
- **Time-limited** but not rushed;
- **Witness statements** where appropriate;
- **Documentary** evidence preserved.

### Disciplinary procedure

Typical procedure:

- **Informal** discussion or warning for minor issues;
- **Written warning** for escalation;
- **Final warning** before potential dismissal;
- **Dismissal** only after warning sequence (except gross
  misconduct);
- **Right of appeal** at each stage.

## Articles 65-70 — Specific dismissal grounds

### Pregnancy / maternity

- **Automatic** unfair dismissal protection;
- **No qualifying period**;
- **Specific** procedural protections during maternity leave.

### Family leave

- **Automatic** unfair dismissal for family-leave related
  reasons;
- **Specific** rules for paternity, adoption, parental leave.

### Trade union activity

- **Automatic** unfair dismissal for trade union activity;
- **Specific** Jersey trade-union landscape (limited
  unionisation but specific protections).

### Whistleblowing

- **Protected disclosure** to specified persons;
- **Automatic** unfair dismissal for whistleblowing;
- **No qualifying period**;
- **Specific** "good faith" and "reasonably believed"
  requirements.

### Health and safety

- **Automatic** unfair dismissal for health-and-safety
  activity;
- **Reporting** workplace hazards;
- **Refusing** unsafe work in serious cases.

## Articles 71-78 — Remedies

### Compensation calculation

**Basic award**:

- **Weekly pay** × weeks of service (capped) × age factor;
- **Capped at** the statutory maximum (similar to redundancy
  basic);
- **Reduced** by any redundancy payment received.

**Compensatory award**:

- **Actual loss** suffered;
- **Mitigation** required (look for replacement work);
- **Cap** at the statutory maximum (substantial);
- **Uplift** for procedural failures;
- **Reduction** for contributory conduct.

### Specific remedies

- **Reinstatement / re-engagement** considered first;
- **Compensation** if not practicable or not desired;
- **Specific** orders for serial offenders.

### Settlement

- **Compromise agreements** common;
- **Specific** Jersey requirements (independent legal advice,
  specific clauses);
- **ACAS-equivalent** Jersey conciliation through Employment
  Forum / JACS (Jersey Advisory and Conciliation Service).
