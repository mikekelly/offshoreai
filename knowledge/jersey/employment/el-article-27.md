# EL 2003 Article 27 — Holiday Entitlement

Article 27 establishes **statutory holiday** entitlement.

## Statutory minimum

- **2 weeks (10 working days)** statutory minimum annual
  leave;
- **Plus** Jersey bank / public holidays typically (per
  contract);
- **Pro-rata** for part-time workers;
- **Specific** Jersey contractual practice often enhances
  this.

## Accrual

Holiday accrues:

- **Pro-rata** through the employee's first year;
- **At start** of subsequent leave year (typically);
- **Specific** carry-over rules.

## Holiday pay

- **At average rate** of pay;
- **Include** regular overtime in some configurations;
- **Specific** calculation rules per the regulations;
- **Paid at time of leave** typically.

## Carry-over

- **Limited** carry-over of unused leave;
- **Specific** rules (typically not allowed unless
  exceptional circumstances);
- **Specific** sectoral practices.

## Pay in lieu of leave

- **Generally** payment in lieu only on termination;
- **Cannot** routinely pay in lieu instead of leave (defeats
  the rest purpose);
- **Termination**: pro-rata accrued leave paid out.

## Sickness during leave

- **Specific** rules for sickness during pre-arranged
  leave;
- **Holiday** can be reinstated if sick during it;
- **Specific** documentation requirements.

## Notice for taking leave

- **Required notice** per contract / regulations;
- **Refusal** of leave possible for legitimate business
  reasons;
- **Specific** Jersey practical rules.
