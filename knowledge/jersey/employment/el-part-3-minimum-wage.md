# EL 2003 Part 3 — Minimum Wage (residual)

Article 16 has its own deep file. This cluster covers
Articles 17-22.

## Article 17 — Rates and accommodation offset

- **Minimum wage rate** prescribed by Order;
- **Accommodation offset** prescribed separately;
- **Updated annually**;
- **Industry-specific** variations possible.

## Article 18 — Records

Employers must maintain **pay records** demonstrating
minimum-wage compliance:

- **Hours worked** by each worker;
- **Pay** received;
- **Itemised** as per Article 4;
- **Retained** for prescribed period.

## Article 19 — Enforcement

Enforcement routes:

- **Worker** can make Tribunal claim;
- **Employment Department** inspections;
- **Specific** prosecution for serious / persistent breach;
- **Compensation** to underpaid worker.

## Article 20 — Failure to pay

Where minimum wage not paid:

- **Recovery** of difference between paid amount and minimum;
- **Interest** on arrears;
- **Plus** specific penalties in egregious cases.

## Article 21 — Inspection powers

The Employment Department may:

- **Enter** business premises;
- **Inspect** records;
- **Interview** workers;
- **Require** information from employer;
- **Refer** matters for prosecution.

## Article 22 — Specific exemptions

Specific exemptions / modifications:

- **Family members** working in family business (limited);
- **Volunteers** for genuine charitable work;
- **Specific** trainee schemes;
- **Specific** apprenticeship arrangements.
