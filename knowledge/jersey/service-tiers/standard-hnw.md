# Service Tier — HNW Standard

## Client profile

Industry-typical: **£1m–£25m investable assets**. Often:

- Established HNW with multi-asset portfolios;
- International clients (UK non-dom, UAE expat, NRI, GCC
  national — see [client cohorts](../use-cases/client-cohorts/index.md));
- Family members of UHNW principal clients;
- Founders post-realisation but pre-UHNW scale;
- Professional services partners and senior executives.

This is the **bread-and-butter tier** for many Jersey wealth-
management businesses, where economics support full-human
service with light AI augmentation.

## Service menu — industry-typical

### Core services included

- **Discretionary or advisory portfolio management** with
  customised mandate;
- **Bespoke trust / company structuring** where appropriate;
- **In-depth tax overview and coordination** with the
  client's specialist tax advisers;
- **Suitability review** at semi-annual or annual cadence
  with full documentation;
- **Named relationship manager** (one RM typically serves 30–
  50 clients at this tier);
- **In-person meetings** on routine cadence (semi-annual or
  annual) and on demand;
- **Cross-border-structuring coordination** — Jersey trust /
  company structuring informed by the client's home-
  jurisdiction position;
- **AML/CFT discipline at EDD intensity** for many clients
  (PEP exposure, high-risk-country nexus, complex
  beneficial-ownership);
- **Family-coordination services** — succession-planning
  conversations, beneficiary information rights handling;
- **Reporting:** quarterly portfolio reporting, ad-hoc tax
  reporting, ad-hoc transaction reporting.

### Services typically light-touch / available on demand

- **Light family-office services** — limited concierge,
  occasional bill payment, vehicle / property coordination;
- **Lifestyle services** — typically limited;
- **Investment access to alternatives** — often via fund-of-
  fund or restricted-access vehicles rather than direct.

## Typical structures used

- **Jersey discretionary trust** with Article 9A reserved
  powers for succession;
- **Jersey holding company** below trust for asset-class
  segregation;
- **Cross-jurisdictional asset wrappers** (Jersey + Cayman /
  BVI underlying companies for specific assets);
- **JPF** for any private fund participations the client
  manages directly;
- **DIFC / ADGM will** for UAE-resident clients;
- **Bank account structures** — multi-currency, multi-
  custodian.

## Common conversations

- Forced-heirship defence for international clients;
- Succession planning across multiple jurisdictions;
- Pre-IPO equity structuring (for founder clients in this
  range);
- UK non-dom transitional planning;
- Inheritance / gifting strategy.

## Cross-tier movements

Promotion to UHNW Bespoke ([`./bespoke-uhnw.md`](./bespoke-uhnw.md))
typically triggered by:

- Realisation event taking total wealth above £25m;
- Multi-generational family conversation requiring full
  family-office service;
- Operating-business succession requiring trust-over-business
  structures;
- Substantial-property portfolio requiring concierge-level
  management.

Demotion to Mass Affluent rare in practice — wealth tends to
be sticky at this tier.
