# Service Tier — Mass Affluent / Standard

## Client profile

Industry-typical: **£100k–£1m investable assets**. Often:

- Professionals approaching peak earnings;
- Early-stage HNW (recently liquid through company sale,
  inheritance, or career progression);
- Cross-border employees / expatriates in the early phase of
  international tax-residence;
- Family members of HNW principal clients.

This tier is **AI-augmented service is the biggest commercial
opportunity**: traditional wealth-management economics often
make this tier unprofitable for fully-human service, but AI-
augmented light-touch service can make it viable.

## Service menu — industry-typical

### Core services included

- **Discretionary or advisory portfolio management** with a
  standardised mandate (typically risk-graded model
  portfolios);
- **Annual suitability review** with documentation;
- **Quarterly portfolio reporting** through a digital
  platform;
- **Telephone / video access to a relationship manager** (one
  RM may serve 100–200 clients in this tier);
- **Standard CRS / FATCA reporting** included;
- **Basic tax-overview** (not full tax advice — directional
  only).

### Services typically not included

- **Bespoke structuring** (trust / foundation / corporate
  vehicles) — typically over a £1m or specific-circumstance
  trigger;
- **In-depth tax advice** — directional only at this tier;
- **In-person meetings on demand** — typically annual
  in-person review only;
- **Family-governance services** — not in scope at this tier.

## Typical structures used

- **Direct portfolio account** with the wealth manager's
  custodian;
- **Trust / company structuring** rarely used at this tier
  due to cost / complexity ratio;
- **ISA / pension** wrappers used where home jurisdiction
  permits.

## Common conversations

- "I've just sold a business and want to invest the
  proceeds" → typically falls into this tier initially;
- "I'm a UK arriver and want to be tax-efficient" →
  FIG-regime planning at portfolio level;
- "My parents have given me an inheritance" → portfolio
  construction conversation.

## Cross-tier movements

This tier is often the **entry tier** for clients who
progress to HNW Standard ([`./standard-hnw.md`](./standard-hnw.md))
over time. Trigger events for promotion:

- Liquidity event (further sale of business);
- Inheritance event;
- Career progression to UHNW-adjacent earnings;
- Family-business structuring requirements emerging.
