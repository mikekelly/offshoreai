# CIFL 1988 Part 4 — Conduct and Disclosure

Part 4 governs **prospectus**, **advertising**, **valuation**,
**NAV**, and **redemption** — the operational rules of public
funds.

## Article 19 — Prospectus

A public fund must have a **prospectus** containing:

- **Fund description** — type, name, structure, objectives;
- **Investment policy** — what assets, geography, sectors;
- **Investment restrictions** — concentration, leverage,
  derivative use;
- **Risk warnings** — material risks;
- **Functionary identification** — manager, depositary,
  custodian, administrator, auditor;
- **Fee structure** — manager fees, expenses, performance
  fees;
- **Subscription / redemption** — minimum, frequency,
  pricing;
- **NAV** — calculation methodology, frequency;
- **Distribution** policy;
- **Tax** treatment summary;
- **Material** legal information;
- **Updates** required if the prospectus becomes materially
  inaccurate.

## Article 20 — Advertising

Public-fund advertising is subject to:

- **Truthfulness** — not misleading;
- **Risk balance** — not over-emphasising returns at the
  expense of risk;
- **JFSC approval** in some cases for new promotions;
- **Standards** for performance presentation (gross / net;
  fee impact; comparisons).

## Articles 21-22 — Valuation and NAV

**Valuation** of fund assets:

- **Methodology** specified in prospectus;
- **Fair-value** approach for non-quoted assets;
- **Mark-to-market** for quoted;
- **Independent valuer** for material illiquid assets in some
  cases;
- **Audit committee** oversight.

**NAV calculation**:

- **Frequency** per prospectus (daily, weekly, monthly,
  quarterly);
- **Calculation** by administrator (or in-house with
  independent oversight);
- **Reporting** to investors;
- **Pricing errors** remediated per CIFL Code rules.

## Articles 23-24 — Subscription and Redemption

**Subscription**:

- **Minimum** investment;
- **Application form** with CDD;
- **Settlement** rules;
- **Allocation** at NAV.

**Redemption**:

- **Frequency** (open-ended only);
- **Notice** periods;
- **Settlement** rules;
- **Gates** and **suspensions** in stressed liquidity;
- **Side-pockets** for illiquid assets.

For closed-ended funds, redemption is generally only at
**wind-up** or **secondary** transfer.

## Article 25 — Investor reporting

Public funds must provide:

- **Annual report** with audited financial statements;
- **Half-yearly report**;
- **NAV** statements;
- **Performance** information;
- **Material event** notifications;
- **AGM** materials (where applicable).
