# CIFL 1988 Part 5 — Investor Protection

Part 5 establishes **investor protections** — civil remedies
and special protective provisions.

## Article 26 — Misleading statements

Civil liability arises where:

- A **misleading statement** is made in connection with the
  fund (prospectus, advertising, periodic reporting);
- The statement is **relied upon** by an investor;
- The investor suffers **loss**.

The misled investor may recover from:

- The **manager / operator** of the fund;
- The **functionary** responsible for the statement;
- The **director** or **partner** who authorised the
  statement, in some cases.

Defences include:

- **Truth** of the statement;
- **Reasonable reliance** on professional advice;
- **No causation** of loss;
- **Statutory limitation**.

## Article 27 — Civil remedy

In addition to misleading-statement remedies, investors may
have civil claims for:

- **Breach** of constitutional document;
- **Breach** of fiduciary / contractual duty by manager,
  trustee, directors;
- **Breach** of CIFL Code obligations.

Standing is typically on **individual** basis (no formal class
action under Jersey law, but representative actions are
possible).

## Articles 28-29 — Redemption protection

When a fund is **suspended**, **gated**, or **in difficulty**:

- **Redemption rights** are protected to the extent possible;
- **Pro-rata** treatment among investors;
- **Liquidity priority** to retail investors over connected
  parties;
- **Disclosure** of suspension reasons;
- **JFSC notification** required.

## Article 30 — Special protective directions

The JFSC may, in extremis, direct:

- **Suspension** of redemptions;
- **Suspension** of subscriptions;
- **Specific** asset disposals or retentions;
- **Independent** trustee / receiver appointment;
- **Reporting** to investors of difficulties;
- **Wind-down** under JFSC supervision.

## Channel Islands Financial Ombudsman

For **eligible disputes** (retail investors, smaller cases),
the **Channel Islands Financial Ombudsman** provides:

- Independent **dispute resolution**;
- **Award** of compensation up to specified limits;
- **Free** to complainants;
- **Binding** on the regulated firm.
