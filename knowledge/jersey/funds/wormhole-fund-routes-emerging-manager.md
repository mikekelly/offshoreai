# Emerging PE manager, first sub-$100m fund — vehicle routes (compiled)

> **What this is.** A compiled, citation-complete distillation of the
> first-fund vehicle choice for an emerging PE manager. Derived from the
> four regimes' canonical files; `draft` status — verify the cited primary
> files. Wider matrix: [`../../CROSS-JURISDICTIONAL-MAP.md`](../../CROSS-JURISDICTIONAL-MAP.md).

## The four routes

| Route | Cap / threshold | Speed | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Jersey JPF** | **≤ 50 investors** | **48-hour** authorisation | Requires a **Designated Service Provider (DSP)** to make the regulatory confirmations; NPPR access for EU marketing |
| **Cayman ELP** | none | registration | The **dominant closed-ended PE vehicle**; the ELP registers under the **Private Funds Act 2020**; deepest LP familiarity |
| **BVI Approved Fund** | **≤ 20 investors, US$100m AUM ceiling** | light | **Permanent** regime (no incubator 2-year sunset); lowest-cost for a small, stable base |
| **Guernsey PIF** | **≤ 50 investors** (Route depending) | **1 business day** | **3 routes** — licensed-manager / family-relationship / qualifying-private-investor; NPPR access |

## Decision framework

- **Investor count is the first filter.** ≤ 20 and staying small →
  **BVI Approved Fund** (cheapest, permanent). Up to 50 → **Jersey JPF** or
  **Guernsey PIF**. Unbounded / institutional scale-up → **Cayman ELP**.
  ([`./jersey-private-fund.md`](./jersey-private-fund.md),
  [`../../bvi/incubator-and-approved-fund.md`](../../bvi/incubator-and-approved-fund.md),
  [`../../guernsey/private-investment-fund.md`](../../guernsey/private-investment-fund.md))
- **Marketing reach.** Targeting EU LPs via NPPR → **Jersey JPF** or
  **Guernsey PIF** (both have the NPPR route). US/global LP base with no EU
  marketing → **Cayman ELP**.
  ([`../../cayman/exempted-limited-partnership.md`](../../cayman/exempted-limited-partnership.md))
- **Manager substance.** A sub-threshold manager wanting a light-touch
  manager regime should look at the **BVI Approved Manager** (≤ US$400m
  open-ended / US$1bn closed-ended AUM) alongside the fund choice.
  ([`../../bvi/approved-manager.md`](../../bvi/approved-manager.md))
- **Economic substance** applies to the vehicle/manager in every one of
  these jurisdictions — factor the substance footprint into the cost.
  ([`../tax/economic-substance.md`](../tax/economic-substance.md))

## The decision in one line

≤20 and small → **BVI Approved Fund**; ≤50 with EU marketing → **Jersey JPF**
or **Guernsey PIF**; institutional scale-up → **Cayman ELP**.
