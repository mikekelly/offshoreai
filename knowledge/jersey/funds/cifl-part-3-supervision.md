# CIFL 1988 Part 3 — Supervision

Part 3 provides the JFSC with **supervisory powers** over
CIFL public funds and functionaries.

## Articles 13-14 — Continuing obligations

Holders of certificate or permit must:

- **Maintain** authorisation conditions;
- **Submit** prescribed returns:
  - **Annual** audited financial statements;
  - **Annual** prudential / compliance returns;
  - **Quarterly** management accounts (larger holders);
  - **Investor reporting** as prospectus requires;
- **Notify** material changes (principals, scope, ownership,
  significant events);
- **Cooperate** with JFSC supervision;
- **Conduct** business in line with CIFL Code of Practice.

## Article 15 — Information powers

The JFSC may require:

- **Documents** produced;
- **Information** provided;
- **Explanations** of records or transactions;
- **Confirmation** of specified facts;
- Within **specified timeframes**.

Failure to comply is an offence and grounds for supervisory
action.

## Article 16 — Inspections

JFSC may inspect:

- **Premises** of certificate / permit holders;
- **Records** and IT systems;
- **Staff** (interviews);
- **Outsourced** functions.

Inspections include:

- **Routine** (1-3 years);
- **Themed** (sector-wide on specific topics);
- **Cause-based** (specific concerns);
- **Pre-launch** (for new permitted activities).

## Article 17 — Directions

JFSC may issue **directions**:

- **Cease** specified activities;
- **Take** specified steps (strengthen controls, capital,
  audit);
- **Limit** specified exposures or activities;
- **Provide** specified information;
- **Cease writing new business / accepting new investors**;
- **Wind-down** orderly in some cases.

Directions are **enforceable** through Court order and subject
to appeal.

## Article 18 — Cooperation with foreign regulators

The JFSC cooperates with:

- **UK FCA** for AIFMD passporting and other matters;
- **US SEC / CFTC** for Jersey funds with US distribution;
- **Other regulators** through MoUs;
- **IOSCO** for international standards.

Information sharing is subject to confidentiality safeguards.

## CIFL Code of Practice

The Code applies in detail to:

- **Governance** of funds and functionaries;
- **Conduct of business**;
- **Custody / depositary** standards;
- **Valuation** methodologies;
- **NAV calculation** and pricing-error remediation;
- **Outsourcing**;
- **Cross-border** marketing.
