# Expert Fund

The **Expert Fund** is a Jersey fund regime designed for **expert
investors** — sophisticated parties meeting **net-worth** and
**investment-threshold** criteria.

## Headline features

- **Expert investor only** — net-worth or institutional-investor
  criteria;
- **More flexible** investor cap than JPF;
- **Streamlined** JFSC registration but with more disclosure than
  a JPF;
- **Regulated administrator** required;
- **Marketing latitude** to expert investors in jurisdictions
  permitting private placement.

## Expert investor definition

An **expert investor** is broadly:

- A person investing at least a **minimum amount** (typically
  US$ 100,000 or equivalent);
- A person with **net assets** above a prescribed threshold;
- A **professional client** under regulated-investment definitions;
- An **institutional investor** (pension fund, insurer, fund-of-
  funds);
- Certain other prescribed categories.

The threshold is materially **higher** than retail-investor
standards.

## Comparison with JPF

| Feature | JPF | Expert Fund |
|---|---|---|
| Investor cap | 50 | No specific cap (subject to expert criteria) |
| Investor qualification | Professional / eligible | Expert |
| Authorisation time | ~48 hours | Few days to weeks |
| Disclosure document | Light PPM | More detailed offering memorandum |
| Marketing scope | Limited private placement | Wider expert-investor marketing |

## When to use an Expert Fund

- **Single-strategy hedge funds** with multiple expert investors;
- **Large private-equity** vehicles exceeding JPF investor count;
- **Liquid alternative** strategies marketed to family offices
  and institutions;
- Funds requiring **AIFMD NPPR** compliance for EU marketing.
