# CIFL 1988 Article 19 — Prospectus Requirements

Article 19 requires public funds to have a **prospectus** with
prescribed content.

## Required content

A prospectus must contain:

### Fund description

- **Fund name**, type, structure;
- **Date of establishment**;
- **Governing law** (Jersey);
- **Specific** other identification.

### Investment policy

- **Investment objective**;
- **Asset classes** permitted;
- **Geographic** scope;
- **Sectoral** focus.

### Investment restrictions

- **Concentration** limits;
- **Leverage** limits;
- **Derivative use** rules;
- **Specific** quantitative limits.

### Risk warnings

- **Material risks** of the fund;
- **Specific** risk categories;
- **Specific** investor categories warnings.

### Functionary identification

- **Manager / investment manager**;
- **Custodian / depositary**;
- **Administrator**;
- **Auditor**;
- **Specific** other service providers.

### Fees

- **Manager fees** (percentage of NAV typically);
- **Performance fees** if any;
- **Expenses**;
- **Other** charges to the fund;
- **Specific** fee structures.

### Subscription / redemption

- **Minimum** subscription;
- **Dealing frequency**;
- **Subscription / redemption** procedures;
- **Specific** pricing rules.

### NAV calculation

- **Methodology**;
- **Frequency**;
- **Valuation principles**.

### Distribution policy

- **Income** treatment;
- **Capital** distributions;
- **Tax** treatment summary.

### Material legal information

- **Constitutional documents** referenced;
- **Specific** legal status;
- **Specific** rights of investors.

### Updates

- **Updates** required if the prospectus becomes materially
  inaccurate;
- **Specific** Jersey rules on revisions.

## Investor receipt

Investors must:

- **Receive** the prospectus before / on subscription;
- **Acknowledge** receipt;
- **Have time** to consider before commitment;
- **Specific** subscription confirmation procedures.
