# Jersey Private Fund (JPF)

The **Jersey Private Fund (JPF)** is Jersey's **light-touch
private-fund regime**, designed for **closed-investor, sophisticated-
investor** structures.

## Headline features

- **Up to 50 offers / investors** at any time;
- **Professional / eligible** investors only — not for retail
  public offer;
- **Fast-track JFSC authorisation** — typically issued within
  **48 hours** of application;
- **Reduced ongoing supervision** compared to public funds;
- **JFSC-regulated administrator** (a "designated service
  provider") with substantive responsibility for the JPF.

## When to use a JPF

JPFs are the **default Jersey fund vehicle** for:

- **Private equity** funds and **co-investment** vehicles;
- **Closed-ended real estate** funds;
- **Family-and-friends** investment partnerships;
- **Single-LP** or **club deal** structures;
- **Carry vehicles** for fund-management teams.

## Structural flexibility

A JPF can be structured as:

- A **Jersey company** (often a no-par-value private company);
- A **Jersey limited partnership** (the most common structure);
- A **Jersey unit trust**;
- A **cell company** (PCC / ICC under Part 18D Companies Law).

The choice depends on **investor tax preferences** and
**operational considerations**.

## The designated service provider (DSP)

A JPF must have a **Jersey-licensed administrator** acting as the
**designated service provider**:

- The DSP carries out **CDD and AML / CFT** on investors;
- The DSP maintains the JPF's **books and records**;
- The DSP provides **administration services**;
- The DSP is **JFSC-supervised** under FSB registration;
- The DSP **substitutes** for direct JFSC oversight of the JPF.

This **DSP-substitution** is what makes the JPF light-touch from
the fund's perspective while preserving regulatory accountability.

## Investor protection

Investors in a JPF receive:

- A **Private Placement Memorandum (PPM)** disclosing the fund's
  terms;
- **Eligible-investor self-certification**;
- **Limited liability** through the fund vehicle's structure;
- **DSP regulatory cover** for administration.

Investors do **not** receive:

- A **prospectus** in the public-fund sense;
- The full **JFSC scrutiny** that applies to public funds.
