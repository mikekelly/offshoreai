## Related fund regimes

The CIFL governs **public funds** (those marketed to the
public). For **non-public** funds:

- **Expert Fund** regime — accredited investors only
  ([expert-fund.md](./expert-fund.md));
- **Eligible Investor Fund** regime — sophisticated
  investors ([eligible-investor-fund.md](./eligible-investor-fund.md));
- **Jersey Private Fund** regime — closed-circle investors
  ([jersey-private-fund.md](./jersey-private-fund.md));
- **Listed Fund** — exchange-listed
  ([listed-fund.md](./listed-fund.md));
- **Unregulated Funds** — Unregulated Eligible Investor Fund
  / Unregulated Exchange Traded Fund
  ([unregulated-funds.md](./unregulated-funds.md)).

Each regime has its own constitutive Order(s) under the CIFL
or under the FSL 1998.
