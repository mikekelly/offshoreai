# Funds — Jersey

Jersey is a major offshore funds domicile, with vehicles ranging from
the lightly-regulated **Jersey Private Fund (JPF)** at the
professional-investor end through to the **public funds** regulated
under the [Collective Investment Funds (Jersey) Law 1988][cif]. The
overall framework is graduated by the **sophistication of the
investor** and the **number of investors** in the fund.
