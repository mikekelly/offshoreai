# Source Index — Jersey / CI / CD Industry History

## What this is

The reference bibliography underpinning the `history/`
time-horizon documents. Every claim in those documents
should cite back to one of the sources below. The list is
deliberately weighted toward **high-reputation third-party
material** — supranational regulators, top-tier strategy
consultancies, Big 4, named research houses — rather than
industry promotion. Industry-promotion sources are included
but flagged as such.

Each entry is dated; the corpus convention is
`accessed: 2026-05-19` for all sources confirmed via
WebFetch / WebSearch on that date.

## How to cite from these

When a `history/` time-horizon document makes a quantitative
or strategic claim, the convention is:

- Inline reference to the source (publisher + year, e.g.
  *"per Bain Global PE Report 2026"*);
- Source entry appears in the file's own `sources:` frontmatter
  with `title`, `url`, `accessed`, `kind`;
- Cross-reference to this index for the full bibliography.

## Source hierarchy

The corpus uses the source hierarchy from
[`KNOWLEDGE-BASE-PRINCIPLES.md`](../../../../KNOWLEDGE-BASE-PRINCIPLES.md):

1. **Primary** — statutes, regulators (JFSC, IMF, MoneyVal,
   OECD), official statistics (Statistics Jersey, BoE)
2. **Authoritative third-party** — top-tier consultancies
   (McKinsey, Bain, BCG), Big 4, named research houses
   (Capital Economics, Cebr, Monterey Insight, Z/Yen)
3. **Industry-commissioned** — Jersey Finance, Guernsey
   Finance, TheCityUK (commissioned but methodologically
   defensible)
4. **Counterweight perspectives** — Tax Justice Network
   (offshore-skeptic; useful for triangulation)

Higher tiers preferred for substantive claims. Lower-tier
sources used for industry narrative and trend articulation.

## Coverage by time horizon

The historical narrative docs are built on these sources
indexed by the horizon they primarily support:

### Last 2 years (2024-2026) — *abundant coverage*

A1, A3, A5, A6-A9, B1-B6, C1, C5-C7, D1-D9, E1-E6, F1-F2,
G1-G2, H1-H2, I1-I2.

This horizon has the densest source base — current
regulator outputs, Big 4 2025/2026 reports, and Bain /
McKinsey / BCG annuals.

### Last 5 years (2021-2026)

A6-A7, B4, C1, C4, D3-D4, D8, G1-G2, I1, plus the 2024
MoneyVal vs 2016 comparison (A1 vs A2).

The Cebr 2021 Jersey-Global-Value-Chains report (C4) is
the cleanest 5-year-horizon anchor.

### Last 10 years (2016-2026)

A1 vs A2 (MoneyVal round-on-round), B4 (Statistics Jersey
decade), C2 (Capital Economics 2016 baseline), E1 (Bain
PE Report runs since 2010), F1-F2 (Monterey 10-year
series), G1 (GFCI long-run).

The MoneyVal 2016 → 2024 transition is the cleanest 10-year
narrative arc for the corpus (FATF compliance evolution).

### Last 25 years (2001-2026)

A4 (IMF 2009 baseline), B2 (JFSC annual reports back to JFSC
founding 1998), B4 (Statistics Jersey series), E3, E5 (BCG /
Capgemini 25-year annual series), F1-F2 (Monterey 31-year),
G1 (GFCI since 2007 — close to but not quite 25 years).

For the pre-2007 portion of the 25-year horizon, the
foundational regulatory milestones (FSL 1998, Zero-Ten 2009,
Substance Law 2019) appear in the doctrinal corpus and can
be cross-referenced.

## Gaps and flagged items

- **Capital Economics post-2016 update on Jersey** — no
  evidence of a standalone post-2016 Capital Economics
  Jersey report; the 2026 TheCityUK report (C1) and 2021
  Cebr report (C4) appear to have superseded the series.
- **Preqin / PitchBook PE-domicile data** — Preqin Global
  Reports exist but jurisdiction-specific Jersey / Guernsey
  / Cayman breakdowns are subscription-gated. Not freely
  citable; available behind paywall.
- **IFI Global Channel Islands report (standalone 2025)** —
  IFI Global publishes thematic work and partnered with
  Jersey Finance on C6, but no standalone 2025 Channel
  Islands report verified.
- **Jersey FSAP update post-2009** — Jersey is a voluntary
  FSAP participant; no newer edition published. The 2009
  report remains the most recent IMF FSAP for Jersey.
- **Isle of Man 6th round MoneyVal** — scheduled 2026;
  latest published is 5th round, Jan 2017.
- **GFCI 39 Jersey-specific rank** — full ranking table
  requires direct visit to https://www.longfinance.net/programmes/financial-centre-futures/global-financial-centres-index/gfci-39-explore-the-data/gfci-39-rank/
  to capture the exact 2026 rank/rating; the headline PDF
  shows cluster commentary only.
