# FSL 1998 — Schedules

The FSL 1998 has four principal Schedules.

## Schedule 1 — Fit and Proper Criteria

Schedule 1 sets out **detailed criteria** for the fit-and-
proper assessment under Article 9:

### Honesty, integrity, reputation

Factors include:

- **Criminal convictions** (especially financial / fraud
  offences);
- **Regulatory sanctions** (in any jurisdiction);
- **Civil judgments** for fraud, dishonesty;
- **Adverse findings** by professional bodies;
- **Conduct** in business / personal dealings;
- **Truthfulness** with regulators.

### Competence and capability

Factors include:

- **Qualifications** (formal and continuing);
- **Experience** in similar regulated activity;
- **Track record** of competent performance;
- **Knowledge** of Jersey regulatory environment;
- **Ongoing development** through CPD.

### Financial soundness

Factors include:

- **Personal bankruptcy** history (for principals);
- **Material judgments** unsatisfied;
- **Adverse credit** events;
- **Capital adequacy** of corporate applicant;
- **Source of capital**.

### Time and commitment

- **Sufficient time** to perform the role;
- **Other commitments** (other regulated roles, directorships);
- **Geographic** availability;
- **Conflicts** management.

## Schedule 2 — Investment Business Activities

Schedule 2 lists the **specific activities** within
Investment Business:

- **Investment advice** (specific subcategories);
- **Discretionary investment management**;
- **Dealing as principal** in investments;
- **Dealing as agent** in investments;
- **Arranging deals** in investments;
- **Safeguarding and administering** investments (custody);
- **Other** prescribed activities.

Each activity has **specific scope** rules and **exemptions**.

## Schedule 3 — Trust Company Business Activities

Schedule 3 lists the **specific activities** within TCB:

- **Acting as trustee**;
- **Acting as foundation council member / guardian**;
- **Acting as company director / secretary on commercial
  basis**;
- **Acting as registered office provider on commercial
  basis**;
- **Acting as nominee** for commercial purposes;
- **Administration** of trusts, foundations, companies;
- **Other** prescribed activities.

## Schedule 4 — Fund Services Business Activities

Schedule 4 lists the **specific activities** within FSB:

- **Acting as manager** of a fund;
- **Acting as administrator** of a fund;
- **Acting as custodian / depositary** of a fund;
- **Acting as registrar / transfer agent**;
- **Acting as investment manager / adviser** to a fund;
- **Acting as trustee** of a unit trust fund;
- **Other** prescribed fund-related services.

## Other Schedules

Additional Schedules cover:

- **GIMB activities** (insurance mediation);
- **MSB activities** (money services);
- **Specific exemptions**;
- **Transitional** and supplemental matters.

## Updates to Schedules

Schedules are **updated by Order** of the Minister:

- **New activity types** added as the market evolves;
- **Scope** clarifications based on JFSC experience;
- **Alignment** with international standards;
- **Carve-outs** introduced or refined.
