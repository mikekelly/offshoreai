# Code of Practice for Deposit-taking Business — Index

Agent-facing entry point for the **JFSC Deposit-taking Business
Code of Practice** (current version effective **1 January
2025**), the binding conduct standard for Jersey businesses
registered to take deposits under the
[Banking Business (Jersey) Law 1991](./banking-business-law-1991.md).

The 2025 edition is the most recently updated FSL/BBL-class
Code. For the statutory framework — registration, supervision,
prudential and resolution — read
[`banking-business-law-1991.md`](./banking-business-law-1991.md)
and the per-Article files in
[`../banking/`](../banking/index.md).
