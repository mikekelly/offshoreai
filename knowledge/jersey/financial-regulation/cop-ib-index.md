# Code of Practice for Investment Business — Index

Agent-facing entry point for the **JFSC Code of Practice for
Investment Business** (current version effective **17 January
2022**), the binding conduct standard for every Jersey business
registered to carry on investment business under the
[Financial Services (Jersey) Law 1998](./financial-services-jersey-law-1998.md).

This is the **wealth-management conduct surface** — the Code
that an RM giving advice, a discretionary investment manager, or
an investment dealer in Jersey supervises against. For the
perimeter of the **Investment Business regulated activity class**
read [`fsl-class-investment-business.md`](./fsl-class-investment-business.md)
first.
