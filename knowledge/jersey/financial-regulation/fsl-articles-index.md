# Financial Services (Jersey) Law 1998 — Articles Index

Systematic scaffold for the **Financial Services (Jersey) Law
1998 (FSL)** — the umbrella regulatory statute covering Trust
Company Business (TCB), Fund Services Business (FSB),
Investment Business (IB), General Insurance Mediation Business
(GIMB), and Money Service Business (MSB).

The Law has **~50 active Articles** in multiple Parts, with
Schedules listing the regulated activities by class.
