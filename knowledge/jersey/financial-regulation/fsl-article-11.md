# FSL 1998 Article 11 — Variation of Registration

Article 11 enables a registered firm to **vary** its
registration.

## Types of variation

A registered firm may apply to vary:

### Scope of class

- **Add** activities to existing class registration;
- **Remove** activities;
- **Add new class** registrations (e.g., TCB adding FSB);
- **Specific** sectoral scope changes.

### Conditions of registration

- **Modify** existing conditions;
- **Add** specific conditions;
- **Remove** conditions where matured / no longer needed;
- **Specific** Article 10 conditions detail.

### Principal persons

- **Approved** principal persons (directors, controllers,
  senior officers, MLRO, Compliance Officer);
- **Specific** fit-and-proper procedures;
- **Specific** Article 13 notification overlap.

### Other registration details

- **Registered office** address;
- **Specific** administrative changes.

## Application

A variation application:

- **Submitted** to JFSC;
- **Form** and content per JFSC requirements;
- **Specific** information depending on variation type;
- **Fee** prescribed.

## JFSC review

The JFSC:

- **Reviews** the variation;
- **Assesses** under same principles as initial registration;
- **Material variations** receive full assessment;
- **Administrative variations** streamlined;
- **Decision** with reasons.

## Material vs minor variations

### Material variations

- **Expansion** of scope;
- **New product** launches;
- **New customer types**;
- **Principal person** changes;
- **Substantial capital** changes;
- **Full JFSC assessment**.

### Administrative variations

- **Address changes**;
- **Minor wording** of conditions;
- **Specific** administrative updates;
- **Streamlined** approval.

## Refusal

The JFSC may refuse variation:

- **Grounds** similar to initial-registration refusal;
- **Reasoned** decision;
- **Right of appeal** to Royal Court.

## Notification vs variation

Some changes require **notification** rather than variation:

- **Material changes** under Article 13 notified;
- **Variations** require active approval;
- **Specific** Jersey rules on which is which.
