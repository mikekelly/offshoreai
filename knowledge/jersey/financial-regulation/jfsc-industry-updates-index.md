# JFSC Industry Updates — Index

JFSC **industry updates** are the periodic communications the
regulator sends to the regulated population — communicating
policy direction, supervisory priorities, operational
reminders, deadline notifications, and guidance clarifications.

This is the **per-surface index** for these updates. Entries
below are seeded as the editor populates them from JFSC
publications.

## What this surface includes

- **Quarterly industry updates** — recurring publications
  consolidating recent developments.
- **Targeted updates** — sector-specific communications
  (e.g. updates to TCB businesses, fund-services businesses,
  banks).
- **Consultation announcements and feedback statements** —
  on Code or Handbook updates.
- **Operational reminders** — filing deadlines, fee changes,
  new submission processes.
- **Public-policy communications** — international
  developments affecting Jersey supervision (FATF, OECD,
  Moneyval).

## Why this matters operationally

For a regulated business:

- **Subscription / monitoring** — every supervised business
  should track industry updates (typically via JFSC mailing
  list or website monitoring);
- **Policy direction-of-travel** — updates flag forthcoming
  Code amendments and shifts in supervisory priorities;
- **Operational deadlines** — missed filing deadlines or
  fee-payment dates have practical consequences;
- **Cross-sector intelligence** — what's happening in
  parallel sectors (banking, funds, insurance) informs the
  TCB / IB business reading the update.

## How entries should be structured

Each update entry (when populated) should carry:

- **Date** of publication;
- **Type** — quarterly update / targeted update / consultation
  / operational reminder;
- **Summary** of contents;
- **Items requiring action** by regulated businesses (with
  deadlines);
- **Cross-references** to the relevant Code or Handbook
  surface;
- **`as_of`** date.
