# FSL 1998 Part 3 — Registration (residual)

Articles 7-10 have their own deep files. This cluster covers
Articles 11-15.

## Article 11 — Variation

A registered firm may apply to **vary** registration:

- **Scope** of class (add / reduce activities);
- **Conditions** of registration;
- **Principal persons** approved;
- **Other** registration details.

Material variations engage **full assessment** (similar to
new application for material changes). Administrative
variations (address change, minor admin matters) are
streamlined.

## Article 12 — Refusal

If the JFSC **refuses** to grant or vary registration:

- **Notice** of intention with grounds;
- **Right** to make representations;
- **Reasoned** decision;
- **Right of appeal** to the Royal Court within prescribed
  period.

Refusal grounds include failure of any Article 9 conditions,
adverse fit-and-proper of principals, inadequate resources,
or public-interest concerns.

## Article 13 — Notification of changes

A registered firm must **notify** the JFSC of:

- Changes in **principal persons** (proposed and actual);
- Changes in **ownership / controllers**;
- Material changes in **business** (scope, products, customer
  types);
- Material changes in **outsourcing**;
- **Adverse events** affecting fit-and-proper or operations;
- **Material breaches** of conditions or rules.

Notifications are typically required **promptly** or within a
prescribed period (e.g., 14 days for principal-person changes).

## Article 14 — Continuing fit-and-proper

Continuing obligation:

- **All principal persons** must remain fit and proper;
- **The firm** must remain fit and proper;
- **Adverse changes** notifiable;
- **JFSC** can require updated fit-and-proper information at
  any time.

## Article 15 — Public register

The JFSC maintains a **public register** of registered firms:

- **Available** through the JFSC website;
- **Includes**: firm name, class of registration, scope,
  registration date, status;
- **Updates** for material changes;
- **Searchable** by name, registration number, class;
- **Free access**.

The public register supports:

- **Counterparty verification** (banks, customers, foreign
  regulators);
- **Investor / customer** due diligence;
- **Market transparency**;
- **JFSC supervisory** transparency.
