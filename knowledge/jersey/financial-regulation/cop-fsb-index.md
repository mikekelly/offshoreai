# Code of Practice for Fund Services Business — Index

Agent-facing entry point for the **JFSC Code of Practice for
Fund Services Business** (current version effective **15 July
2021**), the binding conduct standard for every Jersey business
registered to carry on fund services business under the
[Financial Services (Jersey) Law 1998](./financial-services-jersey-law-1998.md).

For the perimeter of the **FSB regulated activity class** — what
counts as fund services business, who must register, who is
exempt — read [`fsl-class-fsb.md`](./fsl-class-fsb.md) first.
This file covers the **conduct surface** the JFSC supervises
against once a person is registered.
