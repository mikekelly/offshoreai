# Code of Practice for Money Service Business — Index

Agent-facing entry point for the **JFSC Code of Practice for
Money Service Business** (current version effective **1 January
2021**), the binding conduct standard for businesses registered
to carry on money services business (currency exchange, money
transmission, cheque cashing) under the
[Financial Services (Jersey) Law 1998](./financial-services-jersey-law-1998.md).

For the perimeter of the **MSB regulated activity class** read
[`fsl-class-msb.md`](./fsl-class-msb.md).
