# FSL 1998 Part 1 — Preliminary

## Article 1 — Interpretation

Article 1 defines terms used throughout the Law:

- **"Class"** — one of the regulated activity classes
  (Investment Business, TCB, FSB, GIMB, MSB);
- **"Code of Practice"** — guidance issued by the JFSC under
  Article 19 setting out conduct expectations for a class;
- **"Commission"** — the Jersey Financial Services Commission
  (JFSC);
- **"Financial service business"** — defined in Article 2 by
  reference to the regulated activity classes;
- **"Fit and proper"** — the standard for registration and
  continuing authorisation, with criteria in Schedule 1;
- **"Investment"** — defined widely (securities, units in
  collective investment schemes, derivatives, certificates,
  contracts for differences, etc.);
- **"Principal person"** — directors, controllers, senior
  officers, MLRO, Compliance Officer, and other defined roles;
- **"Registration"** — the JFSC authorisation under Article 9
  required to carry on financial service business.
