# FSL 1998 Article 7 — Registration Required

Article 7 is the **operative prohibition** of the FSL — no
person may carry on financial service business (any of the
five classes) without JFSC registration.

## The provision

A person **shall not** carry on financial service business in
or from within Jersey unless they are **registered** under the
Law for that class of business.

## Territorial scope

The prohibition applies to:

- Activity **in Jersey** (Jersey-based operations);
- Activity **from within Jersey** (Jersey operations with
  off-shore clients);
- **Jersey-incorporated** entities providing services
  internationally with Jersey nexus.

## Per-class registration

Registration is **class-specific**:

- A TCB-registered firm **cannot** carry on Investment Business
  without separate Investment Business registration;
- Many firms hold **multiple class** registrations (e.g., TCB
  + FSB; Investment + GIMB);
- **Conditions** can be specified per class.

## Penalty for breach

Unregistered financial service business:

- Is a **criminal offence**;
- Substantial **fines**;
- **Imprisonment** up to **7 years** for individuals;
- **Civil consequences** (contracts voidable, disgorgement of
  fees);
- **JFSC public statements** naming unauthorised providers.

## Application

Application under **Article 8** with:

- **Identity** of applicant (corporate or other);
- **Constitutional** documents;
- **Business plan** specific to class and scope;
- **Capital** sufficient for class;
- **Principal persons** — fit-and-proper notifications;
- **Resources** — Jersey premises, qualifying staff,
  IT systems;
- **AML / CFT** programme;
- **Code of Practice** acknowledgement;
- **Compliance and risk** functions;
- **Outsourcing** arrangements;
- **Group** information for affiliated entities;
- **Fees** prescribed.

## Substantive grant criteria (Article 9)

The JFSC grants registration if satisfied:

- **Fit and proper** of the applicant and principal persons;
- **Adequate** resources;
- **Adequate** systems and controls;
- **Acceptable** group structure;
- **Public interest** considerations met.

## Exemptions

The Minister may **exempt** specific persons / activities:

- **Specific exempt categories** — e.g., PTCs for related-
  party trusts, certain professional services not on
  commercial basis;
- **De minimis** thresholds;
- **Group treasury** activities;
- **Specific orders** for limited carve-outs.

These exemptions are carefully bounded — unauthorised
reliance on supposed exemptions creates Article 7 liability.

## Notification before commencement

A common practice:

- **Pre-application** engagement with JFSC for material new
  business;
- **Formal application** months ahead of intended
  commencement;
- **Phased** application for complex businesses;
- **Conditions precedent** to authorisation (e.g., capital
  injection, system implementation).
