# Glossary Extension — Additional Terms

This file **extends** the main [`glossary.md`](./glossary.md)
with terms introduced in subsequent corpus expansions (new
statute wikis, adjacent jurisdictions, customary law topics).

## A

**Article 47 mistake / hastiness.** Statutory regime under
Articles 47B-J of the Trusts (Jersey) Law 1984 allowing the
Royal Court to set aside trustee dispositions and exercises
of fiduciary power for mistake or failure to take relevant
considerations into account. Wider than the English
*Pitt v Holt* common-law jurisdiction. See
[trusts/article-47-set-aside](./trusts/article-47-set-aside.md).

## B

**Bailiff of Guernsey.** The head of judiciary in Guernsey,
parallel role to the Bailiff of Jersey. See
[`../guernsey/legal-system.md`](../guernsey/legal-system.md).

**BBL.** Banking Business (Jersey) Law 1991. See
[banking/bbl-articles-index](./banking/bbl-articles-index.md).

**BC.** BVI Business Company under the BVI Business
Companies Act 2004. The standard offshore corporate vehicle
in the BVI. 400,000+ active BCs globally. See
[`../bvi/business-company.md`](../bvi/business-company.md).

**BDL.** Bankruptcy (Désastre) (Jersey) Law 1990, Jersey's
principal insolvency statute. See
[insolvency/bankruptcy-desastre-law-1990](./insolvency/bankruptcy-desastre-law-1990.md).

**Bermuda Monetary Authority (BMA).** Bermuda's financial-
services regulator since 1969. Oldest offshore regulator.
**Solvency II equivalent** for EU and **NAIC qualified
jurisdiction** for US. See [`../bermuda/bma.md`](../bermuda/bma.md).

**BOSS (Beneficial Ownership Secure Search System).** BVI's
central register of beneficial owners with authority access.

**BVI.** British Virgin Islands, a British Overseas Territory
in the Caribbean. World's leading offshore corporate
jurisdiction by volume (400k+ BCs). See
[`../bvi/index.md`](../bvi/index.md).

## C

**Cause.** A civil-law concept — the motive or reason for a
contract. Historically required for Jersey contract validity;
modern position contested. See
[contract/cause](./contract/cause.md).

**Cayman.** Cayman Islands, British Overseas Territory.
World's leading offshore jurisdiction for hedge fund and PE
fund domiciliation. See [`../cayman/index.md`](../cayman/index.md).

**Charity Commissioner.** Statutory independent supervisor of
Jersey charities under the Charities (Jersey) Law 2014. See
[charities/cl-part-3-commissioner](./charities/cl-part-3-commissioner.md).

**CHWL.** Control of Housing and Work (Jersey) Law 2012. The
status-based housing / employment access regime. See
[immigration-residency/chwl-articles-index](./immigration-residency/chwl-articles-index.md).

**CICL.** Crime (International Co-operation) (Jersey) Law
2001. Jersey's framework for cross-border MLA, evidence,
asset freezing, and confiscation. See
[aml-cft/cicl-articles-index](./aml-cft/cicl-articles-index.md).

**CIFL.** Collective Investment Funds (Jersey) Law 1988. See
[funds/cifl-articles-index](./funds/cifl-articles-index.md).

**CIMA.** Cayman Islands Monetary Authority. Cayman's
integrated financial-services regulator. See
[`../cayman/cima.md`](../cayman/cima.md).

**Contract passing.** The Jersey conveyancing procedure
whereby real-property transactions are completed by the
parties (or their lawyers as attorneys) appearing before the
Royal Court. The Greffier records the transaction. See
[property/contract-passing](./property/contract-passing.md).

**Coutume reformée.** The modernised (1583) version of the
Norman customary law that historically applied in Jersey.
Cited in modern Royal Court cases. See
[legal-system/normandy-and-jersey-law](./legal-system/normandy-and-jersey-law.md).

## D

**DAC6.** EU Directive 2018/822 — Mandatory Disclosure
Rules for cross-border tax arrangements. Jersey is **not** an
EU Member State and does not implement DAC6 directly, but
Jersey practitioners often engage with DAC6 through EU
counterparties. See
[international/dac6](./international/dac6.md).

**Dégrèvement.** The Jersey court process for enforcement of
a **hypothec** over real property — equivalent in function to
English mortgage foreclosure but distinct in procedure. See
[property/hypothec](./property/hypothec.md).

**Délit.** Civil-law term for an intentional civil wrong (a
form of tort). Pothier framework distinguishes délit (intent)
from quasi-délit (negligence). See
[contract/delit-tort](./contract/delit-tort.md).

**Désastre.** Jersey insolvency status declared by the Royal
Court. The principal Jersey insolvency procedure for both
individuals and companies. See
[insolvency/bankruptcy-desastre-law-1990](./insolvency/bankruptcy-desastre-law-1990.md).

**DL.** Discrimination (Jersey) Law 2013. See
[discrimination/dl-articles-index](./discrimination/dl-articles-index.md).

**DPL.** Data Protection (Jersey) Law 2018. Jersey's GDPR-
equivalent. See
[data-protection/dpl-articles-index](./data-protection/dpl-articles-index.md).

**DPO.** Data Protection Officer. Required for some
controllers under DPL Article 42. See
[data-protection/dpl-part-4-controller-processor](./data-protection/dpl-part-4-controller-processor.md).

## E

**eGaming.** Online gambling, a major Isle of Man specialty.
Regulated by the Gambling Supervision Commission. See
[`../isle-of-man/egaming.md`](../isle-of-man/egaming.md).

**EL.** Employment (Jersey) Law 2003. See
[employment/el-articles-index](./employment/el-articles-index.md).

**Enforcer.** Required for non-charitable purpose trusts
(Jersey Article 12, Cayman STAR, BVI VISTA). Acts in place of
beneficiaries to enforce trustee duties.

## F

**First Deemster.** Head of Isle of Man judiciary, parallel
role to Bailiff of Jersey. See
[`../isle-of-man/legal-system.md`](../isle-of-man/legal-system.md).

**FJL.** Foundations (Jersey) Law 2009. See
[foundations/fjl-articles-index](./foundations/fjl-articles-index.md).

**FS(DPI)(J)L.** Financial Services (Disclosure and Provision
of Information) (Jersey) Law 2020. Jersey's central
beneficial-ownership register legislation. See
[registries/fs-dpi-law-2020](./registries/fs-dpi-law-2020.md).

**FSC (BVI).** BVI Financial Services Commission. See
[`../bvi/fsc.md`](../bvi/fsc.md).

**FSL.** Financial Services (Jersey) Law 1998. The umbrella
regulator statute for TCB, FSB, Investment Business, GIMB,
MSB. See
[financial-regulation/fsl-articles-index](./financial-regulation/fsl-articles-index.md).

## G

**GFSC.** Guernsey Financial Services Commission. See
[`../guernsey/gfsc.md`](../guernsey/gfsc.md).

**GSC.** Gambling Supervision Commission (Isle of Man). See
[`../isle-of-man/egaming.md`](../isle-of-man/egaming.md).

**Guernsey.** Sister Crown Dependency to Jersey. Europe's
largest captive insurance jurisdiction. PCC originated 1997.
See [`../guernsey/index.md`](../guernsey/index.md).

## H

**Holograph will.** A will entirely handwritten, dated, and
signed by the testator with **no witnesses**. Recognised
under WSL Article 6. See
[succession/wsl-article-6](./succession/wsl-article-6.md).

**HVR.** High-Value Residency — Jersey Licensed status with
specific tax treatment under Article 135A of the Income Tax
(Jersey) Law 1961. See
[immigration-residency/hvr-2-1-e](./immigration-residency/hvr-2-1-e.md).

**Hypothec.** Jersey form of security over real property —
similar in function to English mortgage but distinct in
mechanism (no transfer of legal title, registered in Public
Registry, enforced through dégrèvement). See
[property/hypothec](./property/hypothec.md).

## I

**IBL.** Insurance Business (Jersey) Law 1996. See
[insurance/ibl-articles-index](./insurance/ibl-articles-index.md).

**ILS.** Insurance-Linked Securities. Bermuda speciality —
financial instruments derived from insurance risk
(catastrophe bonds, sidecars). See
[`../bermuda/ils.md`](../bermuda/ils.md).

**IoM.** Isle of Man, third Crown Dependency. eGaming and
life insurance specialty. See
[`../isle-of-man/index.md`](../isle-of-man/index.md).

## J

**JOIC.** Jersey Office of the Information Commissioner. The
data protection supervisory authority under DPL 2018. See
[data-protection/dpl-part-6-authority](./data-protection/dpl-part-6-authority.md).

## L

**Le Geyt, Philippe (1635-1716).** Jersey customary law jurist
who wrote the principal codification of Jersey customary law.
Cited in modern Royal Court cases. See
[legal-system/code-le-geyt](./legal-system/code-le-geyt.md).

**Le Marchant, Pierre.** Guernsey customary law jurist
equivalent to Le Geyt. Cited in Guernsey customary law cases.

**Légitime.** Statutory protected share under WSL 1993 for
**surviving spouse / civil partner** (Article 14) and
**children** (Article 15) of the deceased's **movable
estate**. A Jersey form of forced heirship. Distinct from
English testamentary freedom. See
[succession/wsl-part-3-legitime](./succession/wsl-part-3-legitime.md).

**Loi (1880) sur la propriété foncière.** Jersey's principal
real-property statute. Despite its age, remains foundational.
See
[property/loi-1880-sur-la-propriete-fonciere](./property/loi-1880-sur-la-propriete-fonciere.md).

## M

**MCIT.** Multinational Corporate Income Tax — Jersey's
Pillar Two implementation from 2025. 15% on in-scope
multinationals (€750m+ revenue). See
[international/pillar-two-mcit](./international/pillar-two-mcit.md).

**MLI.** Multilateral Instrument — OECD's mechanism to modify
bilateral tax treaties to implement BEPS treaty-related
measures. Jersey signed 2017. See
[international/mli](./international/mli.md).

**MLO.** Money Laundering (Jersey) Order 2008 — the operative
AML compliance regulation. See
[aml-cft/mlo-articles-index](./aml-cft/mlo-articles-index.md).

**MTT.** Multinational Top-up Tax — Guernsey, Cayman, BVI
equivalents of Jersey MCIT for Pillar Two implementation.

## N

**Negotiorum gestio.** Civil-law concept (from Pothier) —
managing another's affairs without their authority but for
their benefit. Quasi-contractual recovery for the manager. See
[contract/unjust-enrichment](./contract/unjust-enrichment.md).

**Norman customary law.** The legal heritage of Jersey, Guernsey,
and historic Norman territories. Significant in real property,
succession, contract. See
[legal-system/normandy-and-jersey-law](./legal-system/normandy-and-jersey-law.md).

## O

**OFSI.** UK Office of Financial Sanctions Implementation —
the principal authority for UK sanctions (and through
extension, Crown Dependencies / BOTs). See
[aml-cft/safl-article-5](./aml-cft/safl-article-5.md).

## P

**PIF.** Private Investment Fund — Guernsey's closed-circle
investor fund regime, similar to Jersey JPF. Also BVI Private
Investment Fund.

**Pothier, Robert-Joseph (1699-1772).** French jurist whose
treatises on obligations, sales, partnerships, and other
areas remain authoritative in Jersey customary law. See
[legal-system/pothier-and-the-coutume](./legal-system/pothier-and-the-coutume.md).

**POCL.** Proceeds of Crime (Jersey) Law 1999. See
[aml-cft/pocl-articles-index](./aml-cft/pocl-articles-index.md).

## Q

**Quasi-contract.** Civil-law category of obligations arising
without agreement (negotiorum gestio, unjust enrichment).
Jersey recognises through Pothier framework. See
[contract/unjust-enrichment](./contract/unjust-enrichment.md).

**Quasi-délit.** Civil-law term for negligent civil wrong.
Pothier framework distinguishes from intentional délit. See
[contract/delit-tort](./contract/delit-tort.md).

## R

**Rente.** Historic Jersey perpetual rent charge — land
burdened with annual payment. Largely converted to modern
hypothec arrangements. See
[property/real-property-overview](./property/real-property-overview.md).

## S

**SAC.** Segregated Accounts Company — Bermuda's equivalent
of PCC. See [`../bermuda/companies.md`](../bermuda/companies.md).

**SAFL.** Sanctions and Asset-Freezing (Jersey) Law 2014. See
[aml-cft/safl-articles-index](./aml-cft/safl-articles-index.md).

**Servitude.** Jersey term for easement — a right over one
parcel of land in favour of another. See
[property/servitudes](./property/servitudes.md).

**SIL.** Security Interests (Jersey) Law 2012 — Jersey's
personal-property security framework (not real property). See
[property/security-interests-law-2012](./property/security-interests-law-2012.md).

**SPC.** Segregated Portfolio Company — Cayman's equivalent
of PCC. See [`../cayman/companies.md`](../cayman/companies.md).

**SPI.** Special Purpose Insurer — Bermuda vehicle for ILS /
cat bond transactions. See
[`../bermuda/ils.md`](../bermuda/ils.md).

**STAR trust.** Special Trust — Alternative Regime, Cayman's
distinctive non-charitable purpose trust regime under the
STAR Act 1997. See
[`../cayman/star-trusts.md`](../cayman/star-trusts.md).

## T

**TGL.** Trusts (Guernsey) Law 2007 — Guernsey's principal
trust statute, similar to Jersey TJL 1984 with specific
Guernsey features.

**TISE.** The International Stock Exchange, based in
Guernsey but used for Jersey-domiciled listings too. See
[`../guernsey/financial-regulation.md`](../guernsey/financial-regulation.md).

## V

**VISTA trust.** Virgin Islands Special Trusts (Act 2003) —
BVI distinctive trust regime where trustee holds shares of
underlying company without active management duties. See
[`../bvi/trusts.md`](../bvi/trusts.md).

**Voisinage.** Jersey customary tort concept — neighbour
obligation not to cause unreasonable harm to neighbouring
property. Strict-liability elements. See
[contract/delit-tort](./contract/delit-tort.md).

## W

**WSL.** Wills and Successions (Jersey) Law 1993. See
[succession/wsl-articles-index](./succession/wsl-articles-index.md).
