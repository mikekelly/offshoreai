# Jersey Real Property Law — Overview

## The distinctive Jersey approach

Jersey real-property law is **fundamentally different** from
English law:

- **Norman customary law** foundation;
- **Civil-law** influences;
- **No "estates"** in the English-law sense;
- **Contract passing** before the Royal Court;
- **Hypothecs** (not mortgages);
- **Rentes** (not standard rent charges);
- **Servitudes** (not English-style easements);
- **Specific** customary inheritance rules.

## Concepts of ownership

### Pleine propriété (full ownership)

The **closest** Jersey equivalent to English freehold:

- **Outright** ownership of the land and buildings;
- **Subject to** servitudes, hypothecs, statutory burdens;
- **Most common** form for residential property.

### Other ownership concepts

- **Bail (lease)** — possessory interest for a term;
- **Usufruit** — right to use and enjoy property of another
  (civil-law concept);
- **Specific** historic / customary forms.

## How property transfers

### Contract passing

Jersey real-property **conveyancing** is **distinctive**:

- **Contract of sale** is drafted;
- **Contract passed** before the **Royal Court** (the
  Samedi Division typically);
- **Royal Court Greffier** records the transaction;
- **Transfer effected** by the Court act;
- **Registered** in the Public Registry.

This is **public** — Jersey property transactions are visible
in Royal Court records (though specific terms typically not).

### What "passing" means

- **Bailiff** or **Greffier** confirms the contract;
- **Parties** appear (or by proxy);
- **Acts** of the Court record the transfer;
- **No** separate "completion" event in the English sense.

## Specific Jersey concepts

### Hypothec

A **hypothec** is the Jersey **security interest** over real
property:

- **Different from** English mortgage;
- **No transfer of legal title** to the lender;
- **Charge** registered in the Public Registry;
- **Enforceable** through specific Court process (dégrèvement);
- See [`hypothec.md`](hypothec.md).

### Rente

A **rente** is a **historic perpetual rent charge**:

- **Pre-modern** financing mechanism;
- **Land** burdened with annual payment to rente-holder;
- **Largely converted** to modern mortgage / hypothec
  arrangements;
- **Historic** importance, less current use.

### Servitude

A **servitude** is a **burden** on one piece of land in
favour of another:

- **Right of way**;
- **Right of light**;
- **Drainage easements**;
- **Specific** customary servitudes;
- See [`servitudes.md`](servitudes.md).

### Privilège

A **privilège** is a **specific statutory or customary
priority** in respect of property:

- **Maritime liens**;
- **Specific** workmen's privileges;
- **Subject to** statutory rules.

## Sales — Loi (1991) sur la Vente

The **Loi (1991) sur la Vente** modernised aspects of sale
of real property:

- **Contract of sale** requirements;
- **Specific** procedural rules;
- **Coordinates** with traditional customary procedures.

## Foreign ownership

Jersey real property ownership by **non-Entitled** persons:

- **Restricted** under the Control of Housing and Work
  (Jersey) Law 2012;
- **Specific consents** required for non-Entitled buyers;
- **HVR** route for high-net-worth foreign buyers;
- **Specific** rules for commercial property (less
  restrictive than residential).

See [`../immigration-residency/chwl-article-10.md`](../immigration-residency/chwl-article-10.md).

## Planning

Property transactions are subject to **planning law**:

- **Planning consent** required for development;
- **Specific** Jersey planning legislation;
- **Designations** for protected areas;
- **Coordinated** with CHWL and conveyancing.

## Modern practice

For practitioners:

- **Engage** Jersey advocates / solicitors for any real-
  property transaction;
- **Royal Court process** for contract passing;
- **Title** investigation through Public Registry;
- **Specific** Jersey conveyancing procedures.

For foreign lawyers:

- **Jersey** real property cannot be transferred by English-
  style conveyancing;
- **Royal Court process** essential;
- **Engage** Jersey counsel from the outset.
