# Employee Share Schemes for Jersey TopCos

## The question

You want to incentivise employees / executives with **share-based
compensation**. What schemes work for a Jersey TopCo?

## The short answer

Jersey TopCos can implement most common share schemes:

- **Stock options** (employee stock option plans, ESOPs);
- **Restricted Stock Units (RSUs)**;
- **Restricted shares**;
- **Phantom stock / share appreciation rights (SARs)**;
- **Performance share plans**;
- **All-employee schemes** (SAYE, EMI-equivalent — UK rules don't
  directly apply but Jersey companies can offer to UK employees
  under UK rules).

Key considerations:

- **Foreign-tax characterisation** in employee jurisdictions;
- **Jersey company law** flexibility for share issuance;
- **UK / US employer tax rules** if relevant employee
  populations.

## The proper answer

### Jersey company law flexibility

Jersey company law supports:

- **Multiple share classes** (different rights for different
  schemes);
- **Treasury shares** (Article 58A — buy-back without
  cancellation);
- **Variable-rights** shares;
- **Convertible** instruments;
- **Conditional / vesting** mechanics.

This is **flexible architecture** for share-scheme designs.

### Common schemes

#### Stock options

- **Grant** option to acquire shares at specified strike price;
- **Vest** over time / on conditions;
- **Exercise** during exercise window;
- **Tax**: employee jurisdiction's option-tax rules.

For UK employees: **Enterprise Management Incentive (EMI)**
options are tax-advantaged but require UK-tax-qualifying status.
Jersey company offering to UK employees can qualify under specific
conditions.

#### Restricted Stock Units (RSUs)

- **Promise** to deliver shares on vesting;
- **No exercise** required — automatic delivery;
- **Vest** on time / performance / mixed;
- **Tax**: typically taxed at vesting in employee jurisdiction.

Common for **public companies** (post-IPO) but can be used for
private.

#### Restricted shares

- **Grant** of actual shares subject to forfeiture / vesting;
- **Section 83(b)** election in US;
- **Earlier tax point** than RSUs but less common in modern
  practice.

#### Phantom / SAR

- **Cash-settled** based on share-value appreciation;
- **No actual shares** issued;
- **Simpler** governance but no equity outcome;
- **Tax**: ordinary income at payment in most jurisdictions.

### Foreign-tax considerations

#### UK employees

- **EMI options** if Jersey company qualifies;
- **CSOP** (Company Share Option Plan) — UK-tax-advantaged;
- **SAYE** — all-employee scheme;
- **SIP** — Share Incentive Plan;
- **Unapproved options** at unfavourable tax.

#### US employees

- **ISO** (Incentive Stock Options) — generally requires
  US-corporation, so Jersey TopCo doesn't qualify;
- **NQSO** (Non-Qualified Stock Options) — works for Jersey
  company;
- **RSU** — works;
- **PFIC** considerations for US-person holders of Jersey shares.

#### Other jurisdictions

Each has specific employee-share-scheme rules — **engage local
tax advisers**.

### Implementing for a Jersey TopCo

1. **Choose** scheme type(s);
2. **Engage** Jersey counsel for share-issuance / amendment of
   articles;
3. **Engage** employee-jurisdiction tax advisers for
   tax-optimisation;
4. **Draft** scheme rules and grant documents;
5. **Issue** shares / grant options / RSUs;
6. **Administer** vesting, exercises, departures.

### Treasury shares for ongoing schemes

Article 58A allows treasury shares:

- **Buy-back** shares without cancellation;
- **Hold** in treasury;
- **Issue** to scheme participants;
- **Efficient** for ongoing scheme administration without
  diluting outside scheme.

### Vesting and forfeiture

Standard provisions:

- **Time-based** vesting (e.g. 4-year ratable);
- **Performance** conditions;
- **Good leaver / bad leaver** distinctions;
- **Acceleration** on change of control;
- **Forfeiture** on dismissal for cause.

### Listed-company considerations

For listed Jersey TopCos:

- **Listing-rule disclosure** of major-shareholder schemes;
- **Remuneration committee** approval for executive schemes;
- **Pre-emption** considerations for new-share schemes;
- **Insider-trading** rules during open / closed windows.

### Common scenarios

#### Pre-IPO option plan

- **Stock options** at low strike price (early-stage);
- **Significant vesting** over years;
- **Pre-IPO** value crystallisation potential.

#### Public-company RSU plan

- **RSUs** standard for senior executives;
- **Performance vesting** tied to share price / metrics;
- **Disclosure** under listing rules.

#### Private-company carry-style scheme

- **Phantom equity** linked to share value;
- **Cash settlement** on liquidity event;
- **Simpler** than actual shares.

## What to do next

1. **Choose** scheme architecture;
2. **Engage** Jersey counsel;
3. **Engage** employee-jurisdiction tax advisers;
4. **Implement** with proper documentation.

## Pitfalls

- **Foreign-tax surprise** for employees;
- **UK-tax-qualifying** failures (EMI eligibility);
- **PFIC** for US employees holding Jersey company shares;
- **Listing-rule** misalignment for public companies.
