# PEPs in My Customer Base — Handling

## The question

Your customer book includes **PEPs and PEP-associates**.
What's the operational management?

## The short answer

Maintain a **PEP register** of customers in PEP status with:

- **Onboarding-time** EDD;
- **Annual review** (vs typical 3-yearly);
- **Enhanced ongoing monitoring**;
- **Adverse-media** scanning on an ongoing basis;
- **Senior-management oversight** of the PEP population;
- **Periodic risk-committee** review.

For programme-level: **integrate PEP management** into AML
programme rather than treating PEPs separately.

## The proper answer

### Identify

#### At onboarding

- **Screen** against PEP-list providers (Refinitiv, Dow Jones,
  WorldCheck);
- **Manual review** for senior positions in customer's
  background;
- **PEP self-declaration** in customer documentation.

#### Ongoing

- **Automated re-screening** on schedule;
- **News alerts** for political-event changes;
- **Customer-disclosure** of new PEP-relevant positions.

### Classify

PEP categories:

- **Foreign PEPs**: senior figures in foreign governments;
- **Domestic PEPs**: equivalent Jersey figures;
- **International organisation PEPs**: senior UN / WTO / similar;
- **Family members and close associates** of any of the above.

### EDD for PEP customers

For each PEP customer:

- **Senior-management approval** of admission / continuance;
- **Enhanced source-of-wealth** documentation;
- **Enhanced ongoing monitoring**;
- **Adverse-media screening** ongoing;
- **Annual review** (more frequent than standard).

### Maintenance

#### PEP register

Maintain a central register:

- **PEPs and PEP-associates** in the book;
- **Specific PEP role / category**;
- **Risk classification** (within PEP, sub-grading);
- **Senior-approver** for each;
- **Review schedule**.

#### Population reviews

Periodically (e.g. quarterly):

- **Review** the PEP population aggregate;
- **Identify** trends (new admissions, exits, risk-rating
  changes);
- **Report** to senior management / risk committee.

#### Adverse-media monitoring

- **Automated tools** scanning for adverse news;
- **Specific PEP names** in customer base;
- **Family / associate** monitoring;
- **Action** on material adverse media (review, exit
  consideration).

### Specific scenarios

#### PEP wins a new political role

- **Re-screen** automatically;
- **Risk-reassess** the relationship;
- **Senior-management** notification;
- **Refresh** EDD as appropriate;
- **Continue** with enhanced oversight.

#### PEP leaves office

- **PEP status persists** for prescribed period after leaving
  (typically 12–18 months);
- **Continue** EDD treatment during this period;
- **Reduce** to standard after the period if otherwise low-
  risk.

#### Regime change in PEP's home country

- **Significant risk** event;
- **Re-screen** for new sanctions (regime change often triggers
  designations);
- **Reassess** relationship;
- **Possibly exit** if risk profile no longer acceptable.

#### Adverse media about PEP customer

- **Assess materiality**;
- **Internal review** by MLRO;
- **Discussion** at risk committee;
- **Decide** to retain, exit, or otherwise modify the
  relationship;
- **Document**.

### Common issues

#### Family-office layers obscuring PEP

- **Look through** to ultimate beneficial owner;
- **Identify** PEP family members in BO structure;
- **Apply** PEP treatment.

#### "PEP fatigue"

- Repeated false-positive PEP screens can desensitise staff;
- **Calibrate** screening tools;
- **Maintain** discipline on genuine matches.

#### Banking-relationship pressure

- Correspondent banks may refuse the firm if PEP exposure is
  high;
- **Calibrate** PEP appetite against banking constraints;
- **Document** firm-wide PEP risk tolerance.

## What to do next

For programme:

1. **Build** PEP register;
2. **Implement** EDD onboarding flow;
3. **Set up** annual review schedule;
4. **Train** staff on PEP identification;
5. **Monitor** population at senior level.

For an individual PEP case:

1. **Apply** EDD onboarding;
2. **Set up** enhanced monitoring;
3. **Review** annually;
4. **Escalate** material events.

## Pitfalls

- **Mechanical PEP handling**: should be substantive risk
  assessment;
- **Family-PEP gaps**: PEP-association captures family;
- **Stale PEP screening**: refresh as political events occur;
- **Documentation thinness**: PEP files need depth.
