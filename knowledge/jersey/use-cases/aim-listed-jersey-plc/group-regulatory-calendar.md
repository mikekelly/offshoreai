# Group Regulatory Calendar — Jersey AIM-Listed Wealth Group

## What this file is

A **calendar reference** for the company secretary or group
compliance officer of an AIM-listed Jersey-incorporated wealth-
management group. Maps the recurring filing, reporting, and
notification obligations across the dual-jurisdiction
governance framework.

This is **for orientation, not for compliance reliance** —
specific deadlines and filing windows vary by issuer
circumstance and update over time. Verify against the relevant
primary source for any specific filing.

## The four obligation streams

A Jersey-incorporated AIM-listed wealth-management group
typically operates under **four parallel regulatory streams**:

1. **AIM / LSE** — listing-segment obligations (AIM Rules,
   MAR, DTR);
2. **Jersey corporate** — CJL 1991 statutory filings;
3. **Jersey regulatory** — JFSC obligations for any
   regulated subsidiary (TCB, FSB, IB, etc.);
4. **AML/CFT** — Handbook + MLO 2008 cross-sector
   obligations.

## Year-at-a-glance calendar

The calendar below assumes a **31 December financial year-end**
(the most common pattern). Adjust dates for actual year-end.

### Q1 (January – March)

| Obligation | Source | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| MAR inside-information surveillance | [MAR](../../external-rules/mar.md) Article 17 | Continuous |
| PDMR closed period (30 days before annual results) | [MAR](../../external-rules/mar.md) Article 19(11) | Annual; aligned to results announcement |
| AML/CFT business-wide risk assessment refresh planning | [AML Handbook](../../aml-cft/amlh-risk-assessment.md) | Annual; BWRA updated at least annually |
| Codes of Practice — annual compliance assertion (where required for any regulated subsidiary) | Relevant [Code](../../financial-regulation/codes-of-practice.md) | Class-specific; check Code |

### Q2 (April – June)

| Obligation | Source | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Annual report and audited accounts | [AIM Rule 19](../../external-rules/aim-rules.md) | Within 6 months of year-end (so by 30 June for 31 December year-end) |
| Annual financial report dissemination | [DTR 4](../../external-rules/dtr.md) (premium-listed only) | Within 4 months of year-end |
| Companies Annual Return (Jersey) | CJL 1991 | Annual; calendar year basis; due end of February but variation by company |
| Beneficial-ownership register update / confirmation | [FSDI Law 2020](../../registries/fs-dpi-law-2020.md) | Annual confirmation + trigger-driven updates |
| QCA "apply and explain" governance update | [QCA Code](../../external-rules/qca-code.md) | Annual; alongside annual report |

### Q3 (July – September)

| Obligation | Source | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| AGM | CJL 1991 | Within 18 months of incorporation; annually thereafter with no more than 18 months between AGMs |
| Interim (half-yearly) report | [AIM Rule 18](../../external-rules/aim-rules.md) | Within 3 months of period-end (30 September for 30 June period-end) |
| Half-yearly report | [DTR 4](../../external-rules/dtr.md) (premium-listed) | Within 3 months of period-end |
| MLRO annual report to board | [AML Handbook](../../aml-cft/amlh-internal-controls.md) | Annual; usually mid-year |

### Q4 (October – December)

| Obligation | Source | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Board AML/CFT review / risk-appetite reaffirmation | [AML Handbook](../../aml-cft/amlh-internal-controls.md) | Annual |
| Group budget / business-plan approval | Various | Internal but interacts with regulatory expectations |
| FATCA / CRS reporting cycle | [FATCA](../../international/fatca.md) / [CRS](../../international/crs.md) | Annual; reporting to Comptroller of Revenue by 30 June following year-end (so prepare in Q4–Q1) |
| Pillar Two / MCIT impact assessment (if in scope) | [MCIT](../../international/pillar-two-mcit.md) | Annual; for groups with EUR 750m+ revenue |

## Continuous obligations

Beyond the periodic cycle, the following operate **continuously**:

| Obligation | Source | Trigger |
|---|---|---|
| Inside-information disclosure | [MAR](../../external-rules/mar.md) Article 17 | When inside information arises |
| AIM Rule 11 disclosure | [AIM Rules](../../external-rules/aim-rules.md) | When price-sensitive information arises |
| AIM Rule 26 website update | [AIM Rules](../../external-rules/aim-rules.md) | When website-disclosed information changes |
| DTR 5 vote-holder notification | [DTR](../../external-rules/dtr.md) | When 3% / integer-above-3% threshold crossed |
| PDMR notification | [MAR](../../external-rules/mar.md) | Within 3 business days of transaction |
| Related-party transaction (AIM Rule 13) | [AIM Rules](../../external-rules/aim-rules.md) | At transaction trigger |
| SAR reporting | [POCL Article 34](../../aml-cft/pocl-article-34.md) | When suspicion arises |
| JFSC material-event notification | Relevant [Code](../../financial-regulation/codes-of-practice.md) | When material event occurs at regulated subsidiary |
| Beneficial-ownership change | [FSDI Law 2020](../../registries/fs-dpi-law-2020.md) | Trigger-driven |

## Event-driven obligations

Certain events trigger their own obligation cycles:

- **M&A or change of control** — see
  [`../m-and-a-acquirer/index.md`](../m-and-a-acquirer/index.md)
  for the acquirer's view; the target equivalent applies
  reciprocally for any sell-side process.
- **Capital raise** — additional disclosure under
  [DTR](../../external-rules/dtr.md),
  [AIM Rules](../../external-rules/aim-rules.md),
  and (for Main Market) the Prospectus Regulation.
- **Reverse takeover** — [AIM Rule 14](../../external-rules/aim-rules.md)
  with re-admission process.
- **Takeover bid received** — [UK Takeover Code](../../external-rules/uk-takeover-code.md)
  + [Takeovers Panel Law 2009](../../companies/takeovers-panel-2009-index.md).
