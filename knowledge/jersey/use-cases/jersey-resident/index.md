# Jersey Resident — Use Cases

## Who this section is for

You **live in Jersey**. You're not a fund counsel or a trust
officer — you're a person dealing with everyday Jersey legal
questions: housing, work, vehicles, family, public services,
small disputes, healthcare, parishes, civic life.

This persona exists to correct a deliberate bias in the rest
of the corpus. Most of this wiki was built outward from
professional-finance personas, which produced deep coverage
of trusts, AML, regulatory, and tax law and **almost nothing
on the law that ordinary Jersey residents actually deal
with**. See [`../../COVERAGE-AUDIT.md`](../../COVERAGE-AUDIT.md)
for the full audit that surfaced this gap.

This index sits **above** four more specific resident-facing
personas — tenant/landlord, driver/motorist, parent/family,
and employee/worker — and catches the cross-cutting
"ordinary-life" questions that don't fit neatly in any of
them.

## Canonical questions

### Civic and constitutional

- Am I entitled to vote in Jersey? What categories of
  election am I eligible for?
- What is my parish, and what does it do for me?
- How does the rates bill work? Who do I pay and what for?
- Can I make a Freedom of Information request to a States
  department?

### Small disputes and money

- I'm owed money by a tradesperson / former friend / business.
  How do I sue them?
- What's the Petty Debts Court and when do I use it vs the
  Royal Court?
- How do I challenge a parking fine, a traffic offence, a
  States decision?

### Healthcare, capacity, end of life

- How does the Jersey healthcare system work — what's free,
  what isn't?
- What's a Lasting Power of Attorney in Jersey?
- What happens if I lose capacity? Who decides for me?
- How do I make a will in Jersey?

### Welfare and benefits

- Can I claim Income Support? How does it interact with my
  partner / household?
- What's Long-Term Care and how is it funded?
- What pension contributions am I paying, and what do I get
  for them?

### Police, criminal procedure, civil liberties

- I've been arrested — what are my rights under PPCE?
- A police officer has stopped me — what can they ask?
- How do bail and remand work in Jersey?

### Consumer

- I bought something defective — what are my statutory
  rights?
- A trader has misled me — what can I do?
- What's the position on online / cross-border consumer
  contracts in Jersey?
