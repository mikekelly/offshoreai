# What Does FATF / Moneyval Say About Jersey?

## The short answer

Jersey is evaluated by **Moneyval** (Council of Europe's AML/CFT
evaluation body) under **FATF Recommendations**:

- **Mutual Evaluation Reports** published periodically;
- **Compliance** generally rated high;
- **Specific recommendations** for improvement in each cycle;
- **Effectiveness** measured against 11 Immediate Outcomes;
- **Followup** until improvements implemented.

Latest evaluation rating Jersey as **largely compliant** with
ongoing improvements expected. Check **current state** on
Moneyval website.

## The proper answer

### FATF / Moneyval framework

#### FATF (Financial Action Task Force)

- **Global standard-setter** for AML / CFT;
- **40 Recommendations** + 11 Immediate Outcomes;
- **Methodology** for mutual evaluations.

#### Moneyval

- **Council of Europe** AML body;
- **Evaluates** Council of Europe members including Crown
  Dependencies;
- **Same FATF methodology**;
- **Peer-review** model.

#### Jersey's role

- **Moneyval member** since 2003;
- **Evaluated** periodically through Mutual Evaluation Reports;
- **Cooperates** with international AML standards.

### Mutual Evaluation Reports (MERs)

#### Components

- **Technical compliance** with 40 Recommendations
  (Compliant / Largely Compliant / Partially Compliant /
  Non-Compliant);
- **Effectiveness** of the 11 Immediate Outcomes (High / 
  Substantial / Moderate / Low);
- **Detailed analysis** of Jersey's AML / CFT regime;
- **Specific recommendations** for improvement.

#### Recent evaluations

- **Most recent** published on Moneyval / FATF website;
- **Earlier rounds** archived;
- **Follow-up reports** track improvements;
- **Check current status** at moneyval.coe.int.

### What Jersey is typically rated on

#### Strong areas

- **Legal framework** for AML / CFT (POCL 1999, MLO 2008, AML/
  CFT Handbook);
- **Regulatory supervision** through JFSC;
- **International cooperation**;
- **Sanctions implementation**;
- **Financial-intelligence-unit** function.

#### Areas for improvement

- **Beneficial ownership** transparency (historic critique;
  central register now operating);
- **Effectiveness** of enforcement (sometimes flagged);
- **DNFBP supervision** (lawyers, accountants);
- **Specific** technical-compliance gaps.

### Why Moneyval evaluations matter

For Jersey:

- **International reputation** depends on positive evaluations;
- **Correspondent banking** access dependent on AML standing;
- **Foreign-regulator cooperation** based on FATF standing;
- **EU lists** influenced by Moneyval ratings.

For investors / customers:

- **Risk assessment** of Jersey as jurisdiction;
- **AML compliance** confidence;
- **Reputational** dimension.

### FATF lists

#### Grey list (Increased Monitoring)

- **Jurisdictions** with strategic AML deficiencies;
- **Action plans** to address;
- **Reputational impact**.

Jersey has **not been** on FATF grey list.

#### Black list (Non-Cooperative)

- **Most serious** AML deficiencies;
- **Countermeasures** required.

Jersey has **not been** on FATF black list.

### Comparison with peers

Jersey typically rates similarly to:

- **Guernsey, Isle of Man**: similar Crown Dependency profile;
- **UK**: more comprehensive (larger jurisdiction);
- **Cayman, Bermuda, BVI**: comparable offshore peers;
- **Some** weaker offshore jurisdictions historically below.

### Specific Jersey AML areas

#### POCL 1999 + MLO 2008 + AML/CFT Handbook

- **Comprehensive** framework;
- **Risk-based approach**;
- **Generally well-implemented**.

#### Beneficial ownership

- **Central register** under FS(DPI)(J)L 2020;
- **Improved** transparency for authorities;
- **Pressure** for further public access continues.

#### Supervised business (Schedule 2)

- **Expanded** over time;
- **DNFBP** coverage improved;
- **VASPs** added.

#### Cooperation

- **Egmont Group** (FIU cooperation);
- **MoUs** with foreign regulators;
- **Information exchange** mechanisms.

### Where to find reports

#### Moneyval

- **moneyval.coe.int**;
- **Search for "Jersey"**;
- **MER and follow-up** reports;
- **Free public access**.

#### FATF

- **fatf-gafi.org**;
- **General** FATF assessments;
- **Cross-reference** with Moneyval for member-state detail.

### For investigators / NGOs

#### What's authoritative

- **Moneyval MERs** are the primary authoritative AML / CFT
  assessment of Jersey;
- **FATF lists** and methodology underlie;
- **EU Code of Conduct Group** assessments separate.

#### Limitations

- **Periodic** rather than continuous;
- **Snapshot** of state at evaluation time;
- **Improvement** between evaluations possible;
- **Some** detail confidential.

### Critical perspectives

#### NGO assessments

- **Tax Justice Network** Financial Secrecy Index;
- **Transparency International** Corruption Perception Index;
- **Different methodologies** and conclusions;
- **Useful** as alternative perspectives but not authoritative
  same as Moneyval.

#### Industry critiques

- **OECD reports** on specific issues;
- **EU Code of Conduct Group** specific assessments;
- **Foreign-regulator** perspectives in specific cases.

## What to do next

For research:

1. **Check** moneyval.coe.int for current Jersey MER;
2. **Read** authoritative international assessments;
3. **Cross-reference** with NGO and academic sources;
4. **Report** with appropriate context and sourcing.

## Pitfalls

- **Outdated information** — check current state;
- **Confusing** different evaluation frameworks;
- **Cherry-picking** from MERs without context;
- **Overstating** specific findings.
