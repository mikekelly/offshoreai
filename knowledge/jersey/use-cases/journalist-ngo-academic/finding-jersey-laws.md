# Where Are Jersey Laws and Regulations Published?

## The short answer

Jersey laws and regulations are published at:

- **jerseylaw.je** — the official Jersey legal repository
  (statutes, regulations, orders);
- **statesassembly.je** — the States Assembly (legislative
  proceedings, Projets de Loi);
- **gov.je** — the Government of Jersey (specific departmental
  rules, guidance);
- **jerseyfsc.org** — JFSC (financial-services regulations,
  codes of practice, guidance);
- **Various** specialist sources for specific regulatory
  categories.

All free, searchable.

## The proper answer

### Primary legislation

#### jerseylaw.je

- **Authoritative** source for Jersey statutes;
- **Consolidated** versions of current Laws;
- **Historical** versions sometimes available;
- **Free** access;
- **Search** by topic, title, year.

Key Laws on the site:

- **Companies (Jersey) Law 1991** (l_30_1991);
- **Trusts (Jersey) Law 1984** (l_11_1984);
- **Foundations (Jersey) Law 2009** (l_23_2009);
- **Income Tax (Jersey) Law 1961** (l_29_1961);
- **Financial Services (Jersey) Law 1998** (consolidated index);
- **Bankruptcy (Désastre) (Jersey) Law 1990** (l_4_1990);
- **Many others**.

### Subordinate legislation

#### Regulations

- **States Assembly** enacts;
- **Published** on jerseylaw.je;
- **Subordinate** to principal Laws.

#### Orders

- **Made by Ministers**;
- **Published** on jerseylaw.je or department websites;
- **Specific** matters.

#### Court Rules

- **Royal Court Rules** published;
- **Court-procedure** specific.

### Codes of practice and regulatory guidance

#### JFSC Codes of Practice

- **jerseyfsc.org/industry/codes-of-practice**;
- **Sector-specific** (TCB, FSB, banking, insurance, etc.);
- **Mandatory effect** for regulated entities.

#### JFSC AML / CFT Handbook

- **jerseyfsc.org** primary source;
- **Updated** periodically;
- **Practical** application of AML rules.

#### Revenue Jersey guidance

- **gov.je/TaxesMoney**;
- **Practical** tax-administration guidance;
- **Specific** rulings sometimes published.

### Court judgments

#### Royal Court

- **jerseylaw.je** publishes Royal Court judgments;
- **Free** access;
- **Searchable** by topic, party, year.

#### Court of Appeal

- **jerseylaw.je** for Jersey Court of Appeal;
- **Free** access.

#### Privy Council

- **jcpc.uk** for Privy Council judgments;
- **bailii.org** also hosts;
- **jerseylaw.je** for Jersey-relevant decisions.

### States Assembly

#### statesassembly.je

- **Projets de Loi** (Bills) in progress;
- **States debates** and proceedings;
- **Lodged questions** and answers;
- **Committee reports**.

### Specific specialist sources

#### Population / immigration

- **Population Office** at gov.je;
- **Control of Housing and Work** Law and regulations.

#### Charities

- **Jersey Charity Commissioner** at charitycommissioner.je;
- **Register of charities**.

#### Public Registry

- **Land transactions**;
- **Hypothecs**.

### Commentary and analysis

#### Practitioner commentary

- **Major Jersey law firms** publish briefings:
  - Mourant briefings;
  - Carey Olsen insights;
  - Ogier publications;
  - Bedell Cristin commentary;
  - Walkers publications;
  - Appleby briefings.

#### Academic

- **Limited academic** commentary specific to Jersey;
- **English-law commentary** often applied with Jersey
  adjustments;
- **JIE (Jersey & Guernsey Law Review)** publishes academic
  articles.

### International / comparative

- **Trust Law International** (UK-based) covers Jersey
  developments;
- **Step Journal** (Society of Trust and Estate Practitioners);
- **Tax Journal** and similar publications.

### For journalists / researchers

#### Free primary sources

- **jerseylaw.je**: laws, regulations, court judgments;
- **statesassembly.je**: legislative process;
- **gov.je**: government policy and departmental information;
- **jerseyfsc.org**: financial-services regulation.

#### Paid resources

- **Westlaw / LexisNexis**: Jersey law databases;
- **PLC (Practical Law Company)**: practitioner guidance;
- **Tax journals**: specialist tax coverage.

#### Searching effectively

- **Statute names** with year (e.g. "Companies Jersey Law
  1991");
- **Specific articles** (e.g. "Article 51 Trusts Jersey");
- **Topic-based** through jerseylaw.je search;
- **Cross-references** in commentary and judgments.

### Currency

- **jerseylaw.je consolidated** versions updated as laws change;
- **Always check** version date;
- **Recent amendments** sometimes lag in consolidation.

### Historical Jersey law

- **Pre-1991 Loi sur les Sociétés** (the predecessor companies
  Law) at jerseylaw.je;
- **Older statutes** may need archival access;
- **Norman customary law** at specialist libraries.

## What to do next

For research:

1. **Start with** jerseylaw.je;
2. **Identify** relevant primary law;
3. **Read** in current consolidated form;
4. **Check** subordinate legislation;
5. **Consult** Royal Court judgments;
6. **Review** practitioner commentary for context.

## Pitfalls

- **Outdated** versions when consolidation lags;
- **Foreign-jurisdiction** sources mistakenly applied to Jersey;
- **English law** vs Jersey-law divergences missed.
