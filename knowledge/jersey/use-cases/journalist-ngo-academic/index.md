# Journalist / NGO / Academic — Use Cases

## Who this section is for

You are a **journalist, NGO researcher, or academic** working
on Jersey-related questions. You may be:

- **Investigating** financial-crime, beneficial-ownership, or
  tax-transparency stories;
- **Researching** the offshore finance industry academically;
- **Campaigning** on tax-justice or financial-transparency
  policy;
- **Verifying** Jersey-corporate-history information.

You need to know **what information is publicly available**,
**where to find it**, and **what the regulatory and legal context
is** — without necessarily having (or wanting) practitioner-level
detail.
