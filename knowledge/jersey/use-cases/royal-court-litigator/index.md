# Royal Court Litigator — Use Cases

## Who this section is for

You are an **Advocate or Solicitor of the Royal Court of Jersey**
running civil litigation. Your day involves drafting pleadings;
making applications; preparing witness evidence; appearing
before the Bailiff and Jurats; navigating procedural rules;
managing costs.

This section focuses on the **procedural** dimensions of Royal
Court practice. Substantive trust and company law lives in the
topic graph; this persona handles **how to do it in court**.
