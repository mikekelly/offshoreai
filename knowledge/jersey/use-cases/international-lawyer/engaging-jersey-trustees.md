# Engaging Jersey Trust Companies — Practical Tips

## The question

You're a foreign lawyer engaging a **Jersey trust company** —
to act as trustee, qualified-member, or service provider on a
specific client matter. Practical tips?

## The short answer

- **Choose** the trust company based on **scale**, **subject-
  matter expertise**, and **reputation**;
- **Engagement-letter** clarity from the outset;
- **Respect** trustee fiduciary independence — Jersey trustees
  are not your agents;
- **Brief thoroughly** on the foreign-client context;
- **Establish** communication cadence and decision-protocols;
- **Coordinate** AML / CFT diligence efficiently.

For most matters: **established major Jersey firm** — Mourant
trust services, Carey Olsen-affiliated, Ogier Global, Bedell
Trust, etc.

## The proper answer

### Choosing the trust company

#### Major firms

- **Mourant Trust**, **Carey Olsen Trust**, **Ogier Global**,
  **Bedell Cristin Trust**, **Walkers**, **Appleby Trust**;
- **Scale infrastructure**;
- **Multi-jurisdictional** reach;
- **Strong reputation**;
- **Higher cost** typically.

#### Specialist independents

- **IQ-EQ**, **Vistra**, **Apex Group** (Sanne legacy),
  **Hawksford**, **Suntera Global**, **Maples**;
- **Often** specialist sectors (funds, family offices);
- **Mid-tier** pricing;
- **Different** service models.

#### Boutique

- **Personalised** service;
- **Smaller** capacity;
- **Lower-cost** for simpler matters;
- **Subject-matter** focus.

### First contact

- **Initial inquiry** through partner referral or direct
  approach;
- **Conflict check** for the prospective relationship;
- **Initial AML / CDD** on parties;
- **Preliminary scope** discussion.

### Engagement letter

#### Key terms

- **Scope** of services (trustee / qualified-member / admin /
  combinations);
- **Pricing** structure (typically fixed-fee + activity);
- **Director / officer** identities;
- **Term** and **termination** rights;
- **Confidentiality**;
- **Indemnities** and limitations of liability;
- **Communication** protocols.

#### Pricing

- **Annual trustee fee**: 0.25–1%+ of trust assets depending on
  service level;
- **Transaction fees** for specific events (sales, restructures);
- **Hourly rates** for ad-hoc work;
- **Total cost** can range £15k–£200k+ annually.

### Coordinating with the trustee

#### Communication

- **Single point of contact** typically (relationship officer);
- **Escalation path** to senior person;
- **Regular meetings** (quarterly typically);
- **Documented decisions**;
- **Reporting cadence**.

#### Substantive issues

- **Brief** the trustee thoroughly on the client matter;
- **Provide** background documents;
- **Engage** on family dynamics, sensitivities;
- **Update** as matter evolves.

### Respect for trustee independence

This is the **single most common foreign-lawyer mistake**:

- **Trustees are fiduciaries** — not your client's agent;
- **Distribution decisions** are trustees' to make, not yours
  to direct;
- **Letters of wishes** are guidance, not instruction;
- **Pressure tactics** counter-productive.

Treating the trustee with appropriate respect produces better
outcomes.

### AML / CFT diligence

#### What the trustee needs

- **Settlor identity** and verification documents;
- **Beneficiary identification**;
- **Source of wealth / funds**;
- **PEP / sanctions screening**;
- **Background** on the structure and purposes.

#### Streamlined diligence

- **Provide** pack of documents efficiently;
- **One-time submission** rather than serial requests;
- **Trustee will verify** independently regardless;
- **Don't be precious** — full disclosure is in client's
  interest.

### Specific scenarios

#### Establishing a new trust

- **Choose trustee** with appropriate expertise;
- **Engage** Jersey counsel to draft deed;
- **Coordinate** CDD with trustee;
- **Brief** trustee on family / purposes;
- **Execute** with trustee accepting office.

#### Adding to existing trust

- **Brief trustee** on new contributions;
- **AML** on new funds source;
- **Document** addition;
- **Coordinate** trustee acceptance.

#### Restructuring existing trust

- **Engage trustee** in planning;
- **Article 47 / 51** considerations;
- **Court applications** if needed;
- **Implementation** with trustee cooperation.

#### Trustee removal / replacement

- **Discuss** with trustee respectfully;
- **Identify** successor trustee;
- **Retirement deed** with indemnity;
- **Handover** of records and assets.

### Cultural considerations

#### Foreign-lawyer-Jersey-trustee dynamic

- **Jersey trustees** are professional fiduciaries with
  long-standing tradition;
- **Foreign lawyer's role** is advisory and coordinating;
- **Mutual respect** essential;
- **Don't condescend** based on home-jurisdiction perspective;
- **Build relationship** for long-term value.

#### Family dynamics

- Trustee may know family better through ongoing relationship;
- **Listen** to trustee on family considerations;
- **Integrate** trustee insight with your legal analysis.

## What to do next

For new engagement:

1. **Identify** appropriate Jersey trust company;
2. **Make** introduction;
3. **Negotiate** engagement letter;
4. **Onboard** with thorough briefing;
5. **Establish** ongoing communication;
6. **Build** working relationship.

For existing relationship:

- **Periodic review** of relationship;
- **Refresh** scope and pricing;
- **Address** concerns constructively.

## Pitfalls

- **Wrong-tier** trust company for the matter;
- **Pressure tactics** undermining fiduciary independence;
- **Inadequate briefing** producing poor trustee decisions;
- **Communication breakdowns** through lack of structure.
