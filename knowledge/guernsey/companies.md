# Guernsey Companies — Overview

## Principal statute

The **Companies (Guernsey) Law 2008** is Guernsey's modern
company law statute, replacing earlier legislation.

The Law is **structurally different** from Jersey's CJL 1991
but **substantively similar** in many respects.

## Company types

### Company limited by shares

The standard commercial vehicle:

- **Limited liability** of shareholders;
- **Share capital** (with or without par value);
- **Standard governance** rules;
- **Most common** Guernsey corporate vehicle.

### Company limited by guarantee

For **non-profit** and **specific** purposes:

- **No share capital**;
- **Guarantee** by members on winding-up;
- **Used** for membership organisations, specific charities.

### Unlimited company

- **No limited liability**;
- **Specific** uses;
- **Limited** modern practice.

### Cell companies

#### Protected Cell Company (PCC)

- **Originated in Guernsey** (1997);
- **Single legal entity** with multiple cells;
- **Cell assets** statutorily segregated;
- **Widely used** for captive insurance, structured products,
  fund umbrella vehicles.

#### Incorporated Cell Company (ICC)

- **Multiple incorporated cells**, each its own body
  corporate;
- **Stronger** segregation than PCC;
- **More complex** governance.

Jersey has equivalent PCC and ICC regimes — but **Guernsey
originated** the concept.

## Key features

### Constitutional documents

- **Memorandum** and **Articles**;
- **Share capital** (or not, for guarantee companies);
- **Specific** Guernsey provisions on constitutional
  documents.

### Directors

- **At least one director**;
- **Standard** fiduciary duties;
- **Specific** Guernsey CGL provisions on duties;
- **Broadly similar** to Jersey CJL 1991 framework;
- **Different** numbering and structure.

### Share capital

- **Par-value** or **no-par-value** shares possible;
- **Different** specific rules from Jersey;
- **Specific** Guernsey share-capital flexibility.

### Capital maintenance

- **Solvency-based** test for distributions and capital
  reductions;
- **Specific** Guernsey test;
- **Solvency statement** by directors;
- **Similar** in principle to Jersey but specific
  differences.

### Mergers

- **Statutory** merger procedure;
- **Specific** Guernsey CGL provisions;
- **Specific** approval requirements;
- **Different** from Jersey scheme of arrangement (though
  both available).

### Schemes of arrangement

- **Available** in Guernsey;
- **Specific** procedural rules;
- **Royal Court of Guernsey** approval.

### Migration / continuance

- **Continuance in** to Guernsey from other jurisdictions;
- **Continuance out** to other jurisdictions;
- **Specific** Guernsey procedural rules;
- **Cross-jurisdictional** with Jersey continuances facilitated.

## Registration

Companies are registered with the **Guernsey Companies
Registry**:

- **Online** registration system;
- **Public register** of companies;
- **Annual filings**;
- **Specific** Guernsey filing requirements.

## Insolvency

Guernsey insolvency:

- **Désastre-equivalent** specific Guernsey regime;
- **Winding-up** under CGL 2008 Part XXI (equivalent);
- **Specific** Guernsey insolvency procedures;
- **Cross-border** cooperation with Jersey.

## Specific differences from Jersey

### Numbering

- **Different** section numbering and structure;
- **No** direct mapping to Jersey CJL articles.

### Specific provisions

- **Specific** Guernsey provisions on:
  - Member protections;
  - Director duties;
  - Capital flexibility;
  - Cell company features;
  - Migration.

### Practice

- **Specific** Guernsey filing procedures;
- **Specific** Royal Court of Guernsey practices;
- **GFSC** rather than JFSC for regulated subsidiaries.

## When to use Guernsey companies

Common scenarios:

- **Existing** Guernsey group with new subsidiary;
- **Specific** Guernsey advisory relationships;
- **Captive insurance** vehicles (Guernsey PCC market);
- **Specific** fund vehicles;
- **Cost** considerations in some configurations.
