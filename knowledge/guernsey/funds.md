# Guernsey Funds — Overview

## Principal legislation

Guernsey funds are governed by:

- **Protection of Investors (Bailiwick of Guernsey) Law
  2020** — investor-protection / fund services framework;
- **Specific** fund regime Rules issued by GFSC;
- **Companies (Guernsey) Law 2008** — for corporate fund
  vehicles;
- **Limited Partnerships (Guernsey) Law 1995** — for LP
  vehicles.

## Fund categories

Guernsey has multiple fund regimes:

### Authorised funds

#### Class A schemes

- **Retail** open-ended schemes;
- **UCITS-equivalent** regime;
- **Highest** regulatory standards;
- **Public** investor base.

#### Class B schemes

- **Non-retail** open-ended schemes;
- **Sophisticated investor** base;
- **Lighter** regulatory regime than Class A;
- **Wider** investment freedoms.

#### Class Q schemes

- **Qualifying** professional investor schemes;
- **Most restricted** investor base;
- **Lightest** regulation;
- **High** investment freedoms.

### Registered funds

Funds **registered** rather than authorised by GFSC:

- **Notification** rather than full authorisation;
- **Faster** to market;
- **Lighter** initial scrutiny;
- **Subject to** ongoing GFSC supervision.

### Private Investment Funds (PIF)

The **Guernsey PIF regime**:

- **Closed-circle** investors;
- **Light** GFSC scrutiny;
- **Fast** establishment (days);
- **Similar to** Jersey Private Fund;
- **Specific** Guernsey rules.

### Closed-ended investment schemes

- **Listed** typically on TISE or other exchanges;
- **Different** from open-ended schemes;
- **Specific** regulatory requirements.

## Comparison with Jersey

| Feature | Jersey | Guernsey |
|---|---|---|
| Public funds | CIFL 1988 | Class A, B schemes |
| Expert funds | Expert Fund Guide | Class Q schemes |
| Private funds | Jersey Private Fund (JPF) | Private Investment Fund (PIF) |
| Listed funds | Listed Fund Guide | Closed-ended schemes |
| Unregulated | UEIF, UETF | Unregulated funds |
| AIFMD passporting | NPPR | NPPR |

Both jurisdictions have **competitive fund regimes**;
specific differences make one or the other better suited to
specific configurations.

## Service providers

Guernsey funds use:

- **Authorised manager** (or AIFM for AIFMD purposes);
- **Administrator**;
- **Custodian / depositary**;
- **Auditor**;
- **Specific** GFSC-licensed providers.

## Investment policies

Each fund category has prescribed:

- **Investment restrictions** appropriate to investor base;
- **Concentration** limits;
- **Leverage** limits;
- **Liquidity** requirements;
- **Specific** Guernsey rules.

## AIFMD / cross-border

Guernsey funds:

- **No EU passporting** (Guernsey outside EU);
- **NPPR** route for EU distribution;
- **GFSC opt-in** AIFMD regime for managers seeking EU
  compatibility;
- **Specific** AIFMD-compliant rules.

## TISE

The **The International Stock Exchange (TISE)** is based in
Guernsey:

- **Listed** funds often use TISE;
- **Lower** cost than mainland exchanges;
- **Specialist** offshore listing venue;
- **Jersey-listed** funds also use TISE in some cases.

## Substance

- **In-scope** for Guernsey Substance Law (mirror of Jersey
  Substance Law 2019);
- **Manager** typically substance-relevant;
- **Annual** substance return.
