# Guernsey — Adjacent Jurisdiction

## Why this section exists

Jersey practitioners frequently encounter **Guernsey** in
cross-jurisdictional matters. Guernsey is:

- The **other principal Channel Island** jurisdiction;
- A **Crown Dependency** like Jersey;
- A **major offshore finance centre** with substantial trust /
  fund / banking / insurance / captive markets;
- A **competitor** and **complementary** jurisdiction to
  Jersey.

This section provides **high-level** Guernsey coverage to
support Jersey practitioners encountering Guernsey matters.
For full Guernsey practice, engage **Guernsey advocates**.

For completeness, Jersey practitioners also encounter:

- **Isle of Man** — separate Crown Dependency, parallel to
  Jersey / Guernsey for many purposes;
- **Cayman Islands** — British Overseas Territory, major
  offshore finance centre;
- **BVI** — British Overseas Territory;
- **Bermuda** — British Overseas Territory;
- **Bahamas, Malta, Mauritius, Singapore, UAE** — various
  competing / complementary jurisdictions.
