# Guernsey — Overview

## What Guernsey is

**Guernsey** is a **Crown Dependency** in the **Channel
Islands**, comprising:

- **Guernsey island** (largest);
- **Alderney**, **Sark**, **Herm**, **Jethou**, **Brecqhou** —
  smaller islands within the **Bailiwick of Guernsey**;
- **Sark** has its own distinctive legal system;
- **Alderney** has its own States and specific legal regime.

For commercial purposes, "Guernsey" usually means the
**Bailiwick** legal system based on the main island.

## Constitutional position

Like Jersey:

- **Crown Dependency** — not part of UK, not part of EU;
- **Self-governing** for domestic matters;
- **UK Government** acts for defence and external relations;
- **Privy Council** as final appeal court;
- **States of Deliberation** as legislature;
- **Bailiff** as judicial / civic head.

## Population and economy

- **Population**: approximately **63,000**;
- **Capital**: **St Peter Port**;
- **Currency**: **Guernsey pound** (par with GBP);
- **Economy**: dominated by **financial services** (similar
  to Jersey) plus tourism, agriculture.

## Financial-services sector

Guernsey is a **major offshore finance centre**:

- **Banking**: smaller than Jersey but significant;
- **Insurance**: **largest captive insurance domicile** in
  Europe (larger captive market than Jersey);
- **Funds**: substantial fund-services industry;
- **Trusts**: well-established trust jurisdiction;
- **TCB**: similar to Jersey.

## Regulator

The **Guernsey Financial Services Commission (GFSC)**:

- **Independent** statutory regulator;
- **Established** by the Financial Services Commission
  (Bailiwick of Guernsey) Law 1987;
- **Regulates** all Guernsey financial-services activity;
- **Equivalent role** to Jersey's JFSC;
- **Memorandum** of cooperation with JFSC.

## Distinctive Guernsey features

### Insurance / captive market

- **Larger** captive sector than Jersey;
- **PCC** (Protected Cell Companies) **originated in
  Guernsey** (1997) — Jersey followed;
- **Largest** captive insurance jurisdiction in Europe by
  number of vehicles;
- **Specialist** insurance services industry.

### Trust law

- **Trusts (Guernsey) Law 2007** — modern statute;
- **Different in detail** from Jersey TJL 1984;
- **Specific** Guernsey trust concepts;
- **Generally similar** practical outcomes.

### Companies law

- **Companies (Guernsey) Law 2008** — modernised company
  law;
- **Different** numbering and structure from Jersey CJL 1991;
- **Specific** Guernsey company law concepts.

### Funds

- **Different** fund regimes from Jersey;
- **Authorised funds** vs **registered funds**;
- **Open-ended** vs **closed-ended** treatment;
- **PCC / ICC** widely used.

### Customary law

- **Norman customary law** roots like Jersey;
- **Specific** Guernsey customary differences;
- **Le Marchant** Guernsey customary jurist;
- **Specific** Guernsey case law.

## Jersey vs Guernsey

| Feature | Jersey | Guernsey |
|---|---|---|
| Size | Larger (population ~107k) | Smaller (~63k) |
| Banking | Larger sector | Smaller sector |
| Funds | Significant | Significant |
| Insurance | Smaller | Larger captive market |
| Trust law | TJL 1984 | TGL 2007 |
| Companies law | CJL 1991 | CGL 2008 |
| Tax regime | Zero-ten | Zero-ten |
| Regulator | JFSC | GFSC |
| Currency | GBP-par Jersey pound | GBP-par Guernsey pound |
