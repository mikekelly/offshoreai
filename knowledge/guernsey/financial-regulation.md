# Guernsey Financial Regulation — Overview

## Regulatory architecture

Guernsey's financial regulation rests on:

- **GFSC** as principal regulator;
- **Multiple sectoral statutes** (different from Jersey's
  single FSL 1998 umbrella);
- **Specific** Codes of Practice / Rules;
- **Specific** AML / CFT framework.

## Principal statutes

### Banking

- **Banking Supervision (Bailiwick of Guernsey) Law 2020** —
  modernised banking supervision law.

### Investment business

- **Protection of Investors (Bailiwick of Guernsey) Law
  2020** — investment business, fund services, advisers,
  dealers.

### Trust / fiduciary

- **Regulation of Fiduciaries, Administration Businesses and
  Company Directors etc. (Bailiwick of Guernsey) Law 2000** —
  fiduciary regulation (TCB equivalent).

### Insurance

- **Insurance Business (Bailiwick of Guernsey) Law 2002**;
- **International Insurance Business (Bailiwick of Guernsey)
  Law 2002**;
- **Insurance Managers and Intermediaries Law 2002**.

### Money services

- **Specific** money services law.

### AML / CFT

- **Specific** AML / CFT statute (equivalent to Jersey POCL
  1999);
- **Money laundering regulations** (equivalent to Jersey MLO
  2008);
- **GFSC AML / CFT Handbook**.

## Comparison with Jersey

| Aspect | Jersey | Guernsey |
|---|---|---|
| Umbrella statute | FSL 1998 (covers TCB, FSB, Investment, GIMB, MSB) | Multiple separate statutes per sector |
| Banking | BBL 1991 | Banking Supervision Law 2020 |
| Insurance | IBL 1996 (Category A / B) | Multiple insurance laws (domestic / international) |
| Funds | CIFL 1988 plus regimes | PoIL 2020 plus regimes |
| TCB equivalent | FSL 1998 Article 9 | RFCB Law 2000 |
| Regulator | JFSC | GFSC |

**Substantively**, both regimes are aligned with international
standards (FATF, IOSCO, IAIS, Basel). The **structural**
approach differs.

## Authorisation process

Common features:

- **Application** to GFSC;
- **Fit and proper** assessment;
- **Capital** requirements;
- **Systems and controls** assessment;
- **Local presence** required;
- **Annual** ongoing requirements;
- **Specific** sectoral rules.

## Enforcement

GFSC enforcement tools:

- **Civil penalties**;
- **Public statements**;
- **Conditions on licence**;
- **Suspension** of licence;
- **Revocation** of licence;
- **Criminal** prosecution for serious offences;
- **Individual** accountability for senior managers.

## Codes of Practice

GFSC issues sectoral codes:

- **Code of Practice for Banking**;
- **Code of Practice for Fiduciaries**;
- **Code of Practice for Investment Business**;
- **Code of Practice for Insurance Business**;
- **Specific** topic-area codes;
- **AML / CFT Handbook**.

## TISE

The **The International Stock Exchange (TISE)**, headquartered
in Guernsey:

- **Listing** venue for bonds, funds, equities;
- **Specialist** offshore listings;
- **Used** by Jersey-domiciled vehicles too;
- **Lower** cost than mainland exchanges;
- **TISE** has its own listing rules and oversight.

## Industry engagement

Key Guernsey industry bodies:

- **Guernsey Finance** — sector promotion;
- **Guernsey Bankers Association**;
- **Guernsey Investment & Funds Association (GIFA)**;
- **Guernsey International Insurance Association (GIIA)**;
- **Guernsey Association of Trustees (GAT)**;
- **Channel Islands Securities Exchange (TISE)**.
