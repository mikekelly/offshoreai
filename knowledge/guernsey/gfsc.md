# GFSC — Guernsey Financial Services Commission

## What the GFSC is

The **Guernsey Financial Services Commission (GFSC)** is
Guernsey's **independent statutory regulator** of financial
services:

- **Established** by the Financial Services Commission
  (Bailiwick of Guernsey) Law 1987;
- **Independent** of Guernsey government;
- **Funded** through industry fees;
- **Reports** to the States of Deliberation.

## Functions

The GFSC:

- **Licenses / authorises** financial-services businesses;
- **Supervises** ongoing compliance;
- **Investigates** breaches;
- **Enforces** regulatory standards;
- **Issues** Codes of Practice and guidance;
- **Operates** the Guernsey Registry (companies, foundations,
  partnerships);
- **Cooperates** with international regulators.

## Areas of regulation

The GFSC regulates:

### Banking

- **Banking Supervision (Bailiwick of Guernsey) Law 2020**;
- **Licensed** banks;
- **Basel III** aligned prudential rules.

### Insurance

- **Insurance Business Law 2002** (domestic);
- **International Insurance Business Law 2002** (captive /
  international);
- **Insurance Managers and Intermediaries Law 2002**.

### Investment business / funds

- **Protection of Investors (Bailiwick of Guernsey) Law
  2020**;
- **Licensed** managers, advisers, dealers;
- **Authorised** and **registered** funds.

### Trust company business

- **Regulation of Fiduciaries, Administration Businesses and
  Company Directors etc. (Bailiwick of Guernsey) Law 2000**;
- **Licensed** fiduciaries;
- **Similar** scope to Jersey TCB.

### Other regulated activities

- **Money services**;
- **Designated** non-financial businesses;
- **VASPs** (under specific regime);
- **Specific** other sectors.

## Cooperation with JFSC

The GFSC and **JFSC** have:

- **Memorandum of understanding**;
- **Joint working** on cross-jurisdictional matters;
- **Information sharing** within proper channels;
- **Coordinated** approaches to international standards
  (FATF, IOSCO, IAIS).

## International standards

GFSC participates in:

- **IOSCO** — international securities;
- **IAIS** — international insurance;
- **Basel Committee** — banking supervision (observer);
- **FATF / Moneyval** — AML / CFT;
- **GIFCS** — Group of International Finance Centre
  Supervisors.

## Comparison with JFSC

| Feature | JFSC | GFSC |
|---|---|---|
| Established | 1998 | 1987 |
| Independence | Independent of Ministers | Independent of Ministers |
| Funding | Industry fees | Industry fees |
| Sectors regulated | TCB, FSB, Investment, GIMB, MSB, Banking, Insurance | Equivalent sectors |
| Statute approach | Single FSL 1998 umbrella plus sectoral laws | Multiple sectoral laws |
| Registry function | Yes | Yes |

Both regulators are **substantively aligned** in approach;
**structural** differences in legislative architecture.

## Engagement

For practitioners:

- **GFSC** authoritative for Guernsey-licensed entities;
- **Specific** GFSC procedures;
- **Different** from JFSC processes in specifics;
- **Industry engagement** through trade associations.
