# Guernsey Legal System — Overview

## Structure

Guernsey's legal system mirrors Jersey's in structure with
specific differences:

### Royal Court of Guernsey

- **Principal civil and criminal court**;
- **Presided over by** the Bailiff of Guernsey (and Deputy
  Bailiff);
- **Jurats** as lay judges (same Channel Islands tradition);
- **Multiple Numbers** (Ordinary, Full, Plenary) for
  different jurisdiction levels;
- **Similar** structure to Jersey Royal Court.

### Magistrate's Court

- **Lower court** for minor matters;
- **Magistrate** appointed for the role;
- **Equivalent** to Jersey Magistrate's Court.

### Court of Appeal

- **Guernsey Court of Appeal**;
- **Same** judges effectively as Jersey Court of Appeal
  (shared bench);
- **Appeals** from Royal Court.

### Privy Council

- **Final** appeal (with leave);
- **Same** as Jersey.

## Sark / Alderney

The Bailiwick includes:

### Sark

- **Distinct** Court of the Seneschal;
- **Specific** Sark customary law;
- **Appeals** to Royal Court of Guernsey.

### Alderney

- **Court of Alderney** for local matters;
- **Specific** Alderney statutes;
- **Appeals** to Royal Court of Guernsey.

## Sources of law

Same hierarchy as Jersey:

1. **Statute** (Bailiwick of Guernsey laws);
2. **Royal Court of Guernsey** case law;
3. **Customary law** (Norman-derived, with Guernsey-specific
   features);
4. **English law** as persuasive authority;
5. **Privy Council** decisions binding.

## Customary law

Guernsey customary law is **distinct from Jersey**:

### Le Marchant

The principal Guernsey customary jurist:

- **Pierre Le Marchant** — Guernsey jurist;
- **Wrote** "Remarques et animadversions sur l'Approbation
  des Lois et Coutumes de Normandie";
- **Authoritative** for Guernsey customary law;
- **Cited** in modern Royal Court of Guernsey cases.

### Specific Guernsey customary differences

- **Different** specific inheritance rules from Jersey;
- **Different** property law specifics;
- **Different** contract / obligations rules in some areas;
- **Same** broader Norman tradition.

## Legal profession

### Advocates of the Royal Court of Guernsey

- **Members** of the Guernsey Bar;
- **Specific** admission requirements;
- **Different** from Jersey advocates;
- **Distinct** Bar with own rules.

### English / UK admission

- **UK-qualified** lawyers can practise in some areas
  subject to specific recognition;
- **Specific** routes to Guernsey qualification;
- **Major firms** typically have lawyers admitted in both
  Jersey and Guernsey.

## Procedure

The **Royal Court of Guernsey Rules** govern procedure:

- **Different** specific procedures from Jersey;
- **Similar** in principle;
- **Specific** Guernsey procedural terminology.

## Statute publication

Guernsey statutes are available at:

- **guernseylegalresources.gg**;
- **Specific** Guernsey legal databases;
- **Different** from Jersey's jerseylaw.je but similar
  approach.
