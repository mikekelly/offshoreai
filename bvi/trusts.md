# BVI Trusts — Overview

## Principal legislation

BVI trust law is governed by:

- **Trustee Act** (principal statute, amended);
- **Virgin Islands Special Trusts Act 2003** — VISTA trusts;
- **Specific** other trust legislation.

## Standard BVI trusts

### Express trusts

- **Common-law** foundation;
- **Specific** BVI statutory provisions;
- **Used** for standard wealth structures.

### Charitable trusts

- **Recognised** under common law and statute;
- **Standard** BVI charity rules.

### Discretionary trusts

- **Standard** discretionary regime;
- **Specific** BVI provisions.

## VISTA trusts (the distinctive feature)

The **Virgin Islands Special Trusts Act 2003** is the BVI's
distinctive trust offering:

### Key feature

- **Trustee holds shares** in an underlying company;
- The **trustee is NOT required to actively manage** the
  company's affairs;
- The **directors** of the underlying company manage;
- **Settlor's** preferences for company management can be
  expressed and given effect;
- **Resolves** the issue where settlors want to retain
  business decision-making while having trust structure for
  wealth purposes.

### How VISTA works

Under VISTA:

- **Trustee** holds shares of company;
- **Office of director** filled per the **wishes** the
  settlor has expressed;
- **Trustee's role** is limited to specific matters;
- **Business decisions** taken by directors (whom the settlor
  has nominated or specified);
- **No** "duty to intervene" obligation on trustee
  regarding the company's business.

See [`vista-trusts.md`](vista-trusts.md) *(to be created)*.

### Why VISTA matters

For **family businesses**:

- **Settlor** wants to settle business shares into trust;
- **But** wants family / chosen managers to continue running
  the business;
- **Standard trust** would impose duty on trustee to oversee
  business;
- **VISTA** removes this duty.

### Comparison with Jersey Article 9A

- **Jersey 9A** = settlor reserves powers (revocation,
  distribution direction);
- **BVI VISTA** = trustee's duty regarding underlying company
  is modified;
- **Different mechanism** but similar objective (settlor /
  family-business control).

## Firewall

BVI has firewall provisions:

- **Foreign forced-heirship** claims excluded;
- **Foreign matrimonial** claims limited;
- **Specific** BVI statutory provisions.

## Reserved powers

BVI law allows **settlor reserved powers**:

- **Specific** BVI statutory provisions;
- **Similar to** Jersey Article 9A in principle.

## Duration

- **Specific** BVI duration rules;
- **VISTA trusts** can be **indefinite** typically.

## Trustee duties

- **Common-law** fiduciary duties;
- **Specific** BVI statutory provisions;
- **Standard** offshore trustee approach.

## Comparison with Jersey

| Feature | Jersey TJL 1984 | BVI |
|---|---|---|
| Statute | TJL 1984 | Trustee Act + VISTA Act |
| Reserved powers | Article 9A | Specific provisions |
| Firewall | Article 9 | Specific provisions |
| Purpose trusts | Article 12 | Specific provisions |
| **VISTA-style** | No equivalent | **Distinctive offering** |
| Customary law | Norman | Common law |

## When to use BVI trusts

Common scenarios:

- **VISTA trust** holding family business shares;
- **Trust** holding BVI BC (common combination);
- **Specific** BVI structural advantages;
- **Existing** BVI-based wealth or counterparties;
- **Cost** considerations.
