# Isle of Man — Adjacent Jurisdiction

## Why this section exists

The **Isle of Man** is the **third British Crown Dependency**
alongside Jersey and Guernsey. Jersey practitioners encounter
Isle of Man matters in:

- **Trust structures** across Crown Dependencies;
- **Captive insurance** (Isle of Man has significant captive
  market);
- **eGaming** (Isle of Man is a leading online gaming
  jurisdiction);
- **Specific** corporate / fund structures;
- **Cross-jurisdictional** family-office matters.

This section provides **high-level** Isle of Man coverage. For
full Isle of Man practice, engage **Isle of Man advocates**.
